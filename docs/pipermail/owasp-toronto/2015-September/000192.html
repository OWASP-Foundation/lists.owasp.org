Dr. Ann Cavoukian
While some believe that fundamental privacy protections will be challenged
by the operation of Big Data analytics, Dr. Cavoukian dispels the notion
that privacy acts as a barrier to analytics and the innovations they can
spark. She argues that the limiting paradigm of “zero-sum” – that you can
either have privacy or innovation, but not both – is an outdated, win/lose
model of approaching the question of privacy in the age of Big Data.
Instead, a “positive-sum” solution is needed in which the interests of both
sides may be met, in a doubly-enabling, “win-win” manner though Privacy by
Design (PbD). read more
