Hi Everyone,

For OWASP's ten year anniversary, OWASP would like to make a "Chapters Around the World" video for the keynote speech at AppSecUSA. They have asked each chapter to, if possible, make a short video message saying "Hello from the *** OWASP chapter!"

We are thinking of making this video in front of one of the city's landmarks, the CN Tower. It would be great if OWASP Toronto members could come out in great numbers!

Here are the details:

Location:
CN Tower, 301 Front Street West, Toronto, Ontario M5V 2T6<http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=en&geocode=&q=301+Front+Street+West,+Toronto,+Ontario+M5V+2T6+Canada>
(Note: We will meet at the passenger drop-off location of the CN Tower South Entrance, right on Bremner Boulevard)

Date:
Wednesday, August 17th

Time:
6:00 PM

If you are planning to come, please RSVP at owasp-rsvp at securitycompass.com<mailto:owasp-rsvp at securitycompass.com>, so we can plan out how many people will be in the video.
