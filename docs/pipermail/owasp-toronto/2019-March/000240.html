Hello everyone,

Just a few notes about tomorrow night's event:

This is a Capture The Flag (CTF) event, so if you wish to play and get
hands-on experience, bring your laptop! Otherwise, you are also welcome to
join us to just spectate and learn from others.

If you can no longer attend, please update your RSVP status on Meetup
<https://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Toronto/events/259127655/>! Getting an
accurate number of attendees allows us to better plan for the evening.

See you tomorrow!
