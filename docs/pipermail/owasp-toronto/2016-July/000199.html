Hello everyone!

We are planning to have another panel discussion for our July 2016 chapter
event. This time, the discussion will be around one of OWASP's flagship
projects, the OWASP Top 10.

*Please RSVP here <http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Toronto/events/232455360/>
if you wish to attend!* Again, space is limited, so I ask that you please
confirm your presence ASAP, but also that you release your spot if you can
no longer attend. This will everyone to get the most out of the session.

---
*Date/Time: July 20, 2016, 6:00 - 8:00 PM EST*
*Location: Suite 500, 257 Adelaide St. W., Toronto, ON*

*Panel Discussion: OWASP Top 10*

*We invite you to join us at our next chapter event, where panelists will
discuss one of OWASP's flagship projects, the OWASP Top 10 application
security flaws. The discussion will include:*


   - *Roundtable on select items on the OWASP Top 10 (e.g. injection flaws,
   security misconfigurations, CSRF, etc.)*
   - *Thoughts on candidates for the 2016/2017 release of the OWASP Top 10
   (e.g. what should be added? what should be removed?) *


*Come prepared to learn, discuss, share and ask questions!*

*We are still accepting panelists from the OWASP Toronto community. If you
are interested in being a candidate, please send me a note at
yuk.fai.chan at owasp.org <yuk.fai.chan at owasp.org>!*

---

Looking forward to seeing you all.
