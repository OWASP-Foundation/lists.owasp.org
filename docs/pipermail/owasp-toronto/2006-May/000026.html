Hi Everyone,

Just a reminder, that the Toronto OWASP Chapter meeting is going to be
held on Wednesday (tomorrow, sorry for the last minute e-vite), May 10th
at 6:00 PM.

Our meeting is going to be held at:

D&T, 20th floor, the TLC Room (signs will be provided on the floor)
TD Centre, TD Waterhouse Tower
79 Wellington Rd. W. Toronto
(same location as last time).

The Agenda for the meeting is:

6:00 - 6:10 : Registration and introductions
6:15 - 7:30 : Web Service Policy Management and Runtime Governance


Description of talk: The talk will be provided by Joe Bate of ADP. He
will cover the traditional verse industry trends in web service policy
management, web service standards and their relationship to each other
and the importance and/or benefits of runtime SOA governance verse
traditional web service policy management.
