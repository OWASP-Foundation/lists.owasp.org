Hi everyone,

Please note that SecTor's first round selection has now ended. The 2nd
round speaker selection is open, and the deadline for submissions is August
16, 2015.

-----

SecTor <http://www.sector.ca/> is searching for a few keynotes as well as
speakers for their upcoming event Oct. 19-Oct. 21, 2015 in Toronto,
Ontario, Canada and has asked for our help.  Last year the event drew just
over 2,000+ attendees and they expect to exceed that number this year.



Below is the information they have sent regarding both keynote and speaker
talks.  To be included in the 2nd round speaker selection, your proposal
submission must be submitted
<http://www.sector.ca/Program/Speakers/Call-for-Speakers> *prior to August
16, 2015*.



==============================================================



*If you are submitting a speaker/paper please note:*

   1. *SecTor’s *second round speaker selection deadline is Aug 16th.
   2. SecTor has 3 technical, 1 management and 1 fundamentals tracks. * If
   selected for the technical or management tracks, SecTor will cover travel
   (flight and hotel) costs for your speaker. *
   3. *Absolutely no marketing or product pitches please*.
   4. We always tell prospective speakers to submit more than one paper as
   it is the content that is selected and not the company submitting it.  It
   is possible to have more than one speaking opportunity at SecTor.
   5. Selection is made by majority vote by SecTor's advisory committee.
   6. For more information on past speaking presentations please visit:
   http://www.sector.ca/Media/Presentations.


*Submission requirements:*



Proposals should consist of the following information in the body of an
email:

   1. Presenter and contact info (country of origin and residence, e-mail,
   postal address, phone, fax).
   2. Employer and/or affiliations.
   3. Brief biography.
   4. Topic synopsis, Proposed paper title, and a one paragraph description.
   5. Reason why this material is innovative or significant and/or how you
   think attendees will be able to apply the knowledge.
   6. Optional: List of publications, papers or other speaking
   engagements.  Give us an idea of your qualifications.
   7. Optional: Samples of prepared material or outlines ready.
   8. Optional: Let us know if you plan a whitepaper or tool release with
   the talk.
   9. Please list any other publications or conferences where this material
   has been or will be published/submitted.



Please identify what track you believe your submission best fits:

   - Technical talk (60min) - travel covered
   - Management talk (60min) - travel covered
   - SECurity FUNdamentals talk (60min) - travel not covered
