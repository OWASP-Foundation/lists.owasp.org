Hi All,

Our September meeting for this year is coming up on the 23rd.  Joe Bate a Security Architect, for a major Canadian Financial Institution is going to present on "Corporate Identity Considerations for Cloud and ASP Applications".

Hope you can attend this session, please RSVP to the address below

Location:  285 Victoria Street<http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=en&geocode=&q=285+Victoria+Street,+toronto&sll=37.0625,-95.677068&sspn=42.987658,93.076172&ie=UTF8&z=16&iwloc=A>, 4th Floor (Room number VIC405).
Date/Time: September 23rd, 2009, 6:00-7:30 PM EST
Title: Corporate Identity Considerations for Cloud and ASP Applications
Speaker: Joe Bate

Description and Bio:

As the Senior Security Architect, for a major Canadian Financial Institution, Joe Bate is responsible for assisting in increasing the security posture of the organization by enhancing the security embedded in the architecture of the systems, solutions, processes and ensuring the appropriate controls are implemented to maintain the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the customer, employee and business information.

At this informative session, Joe, will talk to you about Corporate Identity Management and the key areas of considerations for both Corporations and ASP Vendors, including Cloud Computing Vendors. These key areas of consideration being  Provisioning/De-provisioning, Entitlement and Attestation Reporting and Access Control (including SSO, Authentication and Authorization).

Approaching Corporate Identity Management using these key areas, Joe will present the traditional approach to identity management, the issues associated with the traditional approach, industry trends, and then the benefits (from a corporate prospective) and the considerations (from a ASP prospective) of potential solutions, such as federation.
