6:00 - 6:10 : Registration and introductions
6:15 - 7:30 : Secure SDLC
 
Description: Review some of the common "Secure SDLC" employed or proposed by
the industry. Hui will emphasize on a few key steps in the SDLC that will
significantly improve the security of the application. 
Presenter : Hui Zhu
About The Presenter: Hui works in Information Security at PWC and has
presented on Authentication and various authentication attacks in previous
OWASP meetings.
