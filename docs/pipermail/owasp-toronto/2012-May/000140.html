There will be a free SANS at Night event on May 16th . OWASP Toronto has been

INVITATION FOR DISTRIBUTION:

 

You are invited to join SANS, OWASP and members of your local security
community on Wednesday, May 16th for what promises to be an informative and
interactive evening in conjunction with SANS Toronto 2012
(http://www.sans.org/toronto-2012/).

 

Yuk Fai Chan and Maxim Veytsman will deliver their exciting presentation,
"Bust a Cap in a Mobile App."

As enterprises realize the efficiency in which employees operate by using
mobile apps and allowing employees to bring their own smartphones, a merging
of enterprise and consumer use presents real risks to the enterprise. We'll
discuss a number of mobile attack scenarios that may compromise enterprise
data and present to you how attackers may try to get at information within
smartphones, with live demos.

 

There is no cost to attend this Community Of Interest for Network Security

(COINS) Event, but you must RSVP at

(https://www.sans.org/coins_event.php?eventid=405).

 

Event Details:

Date:     Wednesday, May 16, 2012

Time:      6:00pm-8:15pm

Where:  Hyatt Regency Toronto

               370 King Street

               Toronto, ON M5V - IJ9 CA

 

Schedule of Events:

6:00pm - 7:15pm           Networking Reception (snacks and beverages

provided)

7:15pm - 8:15pm           Presentation by Yuk Fai Chan and Max Veytsman,

"Bust a Cap in a Mobile App"

 

Seating is limited so RSVP today at:

(https://www.sans.org/coins_event.php?eventid=405).

 

If you haven't registered for SANS Toronto 2012 yet there are still seats

Enter discount code OWASP-JS at the time of registration
