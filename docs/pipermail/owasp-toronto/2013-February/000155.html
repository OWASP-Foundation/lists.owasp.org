*OWASP Toronto Chapter - May Meeting
*
This is an advanced announcement for an upcoming in May. Sherif Koussa, the
OWASP Ottawa chapter leader, will be visiting us and giving a talk on
secure code review. Here are some preliminary details and a preview of his
presentation:

*Date: May 8th
Location: TBD*

*Secure Code Review* is the best approach to uncover the largest number
ofsecurity flaws in addition to the most stealth and hard to uncover
security vulnerabilities. During this session, you will learn how to
perform security code review and uncover vulnerabilities such as OWASP Top
10: Cross-site Scripting, SQL Injection, Access Control and much more in
early stages of development. You will use a real life application
"SecureTickers" pulled from SourceForge. You will get an introduction to
Static Code Analysis tools and how you can extend PMD (
http://pmd.sourceforge.net/), the open source static code analysis tool, to
catch security flaws like OWASP Top 10. Expect lots of code, tools, hacking
and fun! *

Stay tuned for more details!

*OWASP Kitchener-Waterloo Chapter*

If you are in the Kitchener-Waterloo area, check out the next event of your
local chapter. Colin Delaney and Chris Howell are planning a general meet &
greet with a short presentation. Here are the details:

*Tuesday February 26th 2013, 6:00-8:00pm*
*Location: Morty's Pub (Basement) 272 King Street North, Waterloo Ontario*
Please RSVP to Colin Delaney <colin.delaney at owasp.org> OR Chris
Howell<Chris.Howell at owasp.org>To Confirm your presence (
Chris.Howell at owasp.org or Colin.Delaney at Owasp.org).

Agenda:
    1.    Introductions
    2.    OWASP Mission & Meetings
    3.    Guest Presentation & Discussion (by Colin Delaney)
    4.    OWASP Materials
    5.    OWASP Membership

Colin Delaney is the product & mobile security manager for McAfee Anti
Virus based out of Waterloo Ontario. He has been with McAfee for 7 years
now and is responsible for ensuring that the consumer product is free from
security vulnerabilities&  that could bring harm either to McAfee or our
customers. He is an exceptional penetration tester and his expertise &
relentless pursuit of secure products & systems protect McAfee consumers
from those that would do them harm. He is a valuable resource for the
security aware developer as well as the QA security tester.
