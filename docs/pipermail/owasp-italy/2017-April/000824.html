*What to Expect at AppSec Europe 2017*


We're now less than 2 months way from AppSec Europe
<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/f07cc1b107> conference
and here's some of the things we can look forward to in Belfast this May:
 

*Talks*

  *  Sessions have been selected and include topics such as breaking
    CSPs, threat modeling, hacking configuration management systems,
    hacking contactless payments, GDPR, quantum safe crypto, securing
    Azure Cloud, Oauth2, bypassing XSS controls, pentesting voice
    biometrics and even printer security! 
  *  In addition to that we'll have panels on diversity and hacking
    elections, along with interactive sessions allowing everyone to get
    out their laptop and hack a few websites.
  * While this year's tracks are aimed at developers, ethical hackers,
    CSIOs and devsecops, we will also have 'Hallway Tracks' allowing
    anyone to setup their own talk or discussion session, as well as
    HackPra AllStars
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/cf0b7a4c28> and
    lightning talks.


*Training*

  * Training sessions are available
    from https://2017.appsec.eu/program/training
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/4bd0a446f9/url=https%3A%2F%2F2017%2Eappsec%2Eeu%2Fprogram%2Ftraining&urlhash=y3pY&_t=tracking_anet> and
    include Mario Heiderich on exploiting websites, Robert Seacord on
    secure Java, mobile, python, machine learning, IoT and SSO
    exploitation, web app security with Fabio Cerullo, and Docker and
    DevOps security sessions.
  * ​Of course we are offering 100 euro off if you purchase training
    with your conference ticket.  Spots are limited so register now
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/ef665c783b>. 

*Social Events*

  * AppSec EU Belfast comes with a full social calendar, with the main
    conference dinner taking place in the Titanic Suite of the Titanic
    Visitor Center, voted the best worldwide tourist attraction in 2016,
    including drinks, music and a bit of unique entertainment.
    This venue looks out onto the Game of Thrones studios here in Belfast.
  *  In addition there'll be a Pre-Conference drinks event on the
    Wednesday night, allowing everyone to settle in and mingle at the
    conference venue.


*The Usual*

  * AppSec wouldn't be AppSec without CtFs, University Challenges
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/001f4b1e97>,
    and project
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/6c9b4683b6>
    and developer summits
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/b1fdaf45d4>.
    Many of which are free to attend, view our website
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/3d08cf9ffb>
    at to see the free events or Register
    <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/da5dd3f93d/9902a62c04/633323210e/EventID=1882450>
    today.
