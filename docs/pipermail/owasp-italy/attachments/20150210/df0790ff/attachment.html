                                        <div><b>Discussione generale sul
                                            progetto (obiettivo,
                                            possibili attivita', ...)</b></div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div>Il possibile sviluppo del
                                          progetto dipende dal livello
                                          di partecipazione e dal numero
                                          di interessati.</div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div>L'obiettivo sembra chiaro -
                                          facilitare la collaborazione
                                          tra security pro e developers
                                          di progetti opensource.</div>
                                        <div>Durante la chiacchierata,
                                          abbiamo parlato
                                          dell'importanza che ci sia
                                          interesse da entrambe le parti
                                          (es: alcuni progetti OSS non
                                          vogliono nemmeno questo genere
                                          di collaborazione). </div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div>Con un numero di volontari
                                          > 4, possiamo pensare di
                                          creare una piattaforma per il
                                          "matching" tra progetti
                                          OpenSource e Volontari. In
                                          questo caso, il ruolo del
                                          progetto e' principalmente
                                          quello di gestire la
                                          "logistica" della
                                          collaborazione. Con meno
                                          volontari, possiamo
                                          semplicemente sperimentare il
                                          modello - fornendo
                                          direttamente noi aiuto a
                                          progetti OSS.</div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div>Abbiamo anche parlato di
                                          competenze per i volontari.
                                          Per aiutare il progetto "Adopt
                                          OSS" stesso non serve troppa
                                          competenza tecnica, mentre e'
                                          necessario abbinare skill
                                          specifici in base al progetto
                                          OSS che si vuole aiutare. </div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div><b>Progetto OWASP globale
                                            vs iniziativa indipendente</b></div>
                                        <div><b><br>
                                          </b></div>
                                        <div>Visto il numero di persone
                                          online (sad panda), abbiamo
                                          pensato di iniziare con
                                          un'iniziativa limitata ad
                                          OWASP Italy. Una cosa del
                                          tipo: OWASP Italy adotta OSS
                                          "Italiani".</div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div>L'idea e' di identificare
                                          un numero limitato di progetti
                                          OSS noti a livello globale, in
                                          cui almeno uno dei mantainer o
                                          referenti del progetto sia
                                          Italiano. Qualche esempio:
                                          Ntop, Redis, LibreOffice, ..</div>
                                        <div>A questo punto cerchiamo
                                          volontari e facciamo partire
                                          la collaborazione. Dopo circa
                                          sei mesi, raccogliamo un
                                          feedback tra entrambi le parti
                                          e vediamo come espandere il
                                          progetto.</div>
                                        <div><br>
                                        </div>
                                        <div><b>Lista della spesa</b></div>
                                      </div>
                                      <div>
                                        <ol>
                                          <li>Identificare i progetti
                                            OSS (Alessio mandera' una
                                            nuova email sul tema)</li>
                                          <li>Creare un piccolo
                                            documento contenente alcune
                                            linee guida, da condividere
                                            con progetti e volontari.
                                            L'idea e' di avere qualcosa
                                            che spieghi il funzionamento
                                            dell'adozione (Luca,
                                            Alessio)</li>
                                          <li>Creare Google form con
                                            domande per raccogliere i
                                            potenziali volontari
                                            (Alessio)</li>
                                          <li>Pagina sul wiki di OWASP
                                            Italy in cui avere qualche
                                            informazione e la lista
                                            delle adozioni "attive"
                                            (Luca) </li>
                                        </ol>
                                      </div>
