Good Afternoon,

Planning is in the final stages for the 1st OWASP Tampa Day. The event will
run from 9:30 AM to 3 PM on Monday, June 20th at Tampa International
Airport. The event will feature 4 speakers, and will include lunch and
refreshments. Thanks to the event sponsors (WhiteHat Security, Qualys, and
Stratum Security), the event will be COMPLETELY FREE! There is a limited
number seats (60), and registration will be on a first come, first serve
basis.

I will send out the Eventbrite page once everything is finalized so that you
can reserve your spot, but I wanted everyone to block their calendar as soon
as possible. Also, please start spreading the news. This is a great way for
folks to get a 1/2 day of Application Security training for free. We are
focussing on developers and INFOSEC managers.

Stay tuned and let me know if you have any questions.

Thanks!
