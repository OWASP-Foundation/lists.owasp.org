On the heels of my previous email, everything has been finalized and
registration for OWASP Tampa Day 2011 is now officially open! You can
register at,

   - http://owasptampaday2011.eventbrite.com

Registration is limited to only 60 attendees, so I recommend registering as
soon as you know you can attend. The event is completely free!

I ask that you please let your friends and colleagues know about the event.
We need a good showing to keep the interest of sponsors who make events like
this free. Please try and invite at least two new people to the event
(developers and management types are an easy target).

Please contact me with any questions you may have and I hope to see you all
at the event on June 20th.

A big thanks to Kate Mullin @ Tampa Airport for securing the facilities for
us. Thanks Kate!

See you all on the 20th.

Justin
