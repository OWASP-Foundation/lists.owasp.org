*Guest Speaker:  Doug Morato*

*Title*: AppSec Pipeline: Application Security in a world of Agile
Development, Continuous Change and DevOps

*Abstract*: In this talk, we will go over some ideas on how to implement
security process and testing automation to support the new paradigm of
continuous change, agile efforts, leveraging open-source and free tools.

*Bio*: Passionate about AppSec and Automation, Doug Morato has been working
with InfoSec for 8 years. Currently a Senior Manager on the AppSec team @
PwC, prior roles include HP, MasterCard, Disney and consulting for Fortune
500 companies. On his free time, Doug enjoys contributing to OWASP WebGoat,
a project where he is a Core Contributor, and spending quality time with
his family in Boca Raton, Florida.


*LinkedIn profile*: https://www.linkedin.com/in/dougmorato
