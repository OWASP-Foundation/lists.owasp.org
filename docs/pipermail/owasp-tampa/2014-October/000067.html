Good Evening,

Our next meeting will be the annual OWASP Tampa Day for 2014. It will be
held on Monday, November 3, 2014 from 10AM EDT to 4PM EDT at WellCare
Health Plans Inc, located at 8725 Henderson Rd Tampa, FL 33634. Registration
is free and tickets are available from
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-tampa-day-2014-tickets-13541995473.


This year we are doing a formal Call For Papers (CFP) for the
presentations. If you are interested in presenting this year, please
complete the CFP form located at http://bit.ly/owasptd2014cfp.


Check the site registration page and OWASP Tampa page for regular updates!


I hope to see everyone there!


Thanks,
