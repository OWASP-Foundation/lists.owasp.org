Carlos, thank you for your energy and enthusiasm.  From what I have heard,
there is quite a bit of momentum in this area and it seems like a great time
to restart this chapter.



I have updated the chapter page on the wiki to reflect that you have assumed
the leadership role.



I would only request that at your first meeting you solicit a board to help
you manage the chapter.  There is quite a bit involved in running a
successful chapter and the more people you have involved, the stronger your
community will become.  Most of our successful and most active chapters are
managed by a chapter leader (who is the liaison with the foundation) and a
board of at least 3 other persons to help you manage membership, venue,
sponsorship, materials, and relationships with other chapters.



I have copied Fabio Cerullo on this email.  Fabio is very active in the
foundation and I’m confident he can help you grow a successful chapter.
Please reach out to him if you need help.  If he doesn’t have the answer for
you, he can find someone who does.



Thank you for your support and I look forward to working with you.
