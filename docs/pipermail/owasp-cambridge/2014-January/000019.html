Dear All,

Now that we’re all back from our holidays etc, I thought I’d post a reminder to make sure you’re all aware that the OWASP AppSec Europe conference for 2014 is going to be held at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge, from the 23rd to the 26th of June 2014 (23-24 Training, 25-26 Conference days).

The conference website at can be found here: https://2014.appsec.eu. (*)

If your organisation is interested or you know of a company who maybe interested in Sponsoring AppSec Europe, the Sponsor pack is available at:https://2014.appsec.eu/sponsorship/

Make sure you put it in your planners, and Call for Papers and Call for Training announcements are due to come out shortly.
