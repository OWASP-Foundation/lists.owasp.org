> The Cambridge Cyber Security Cluster has been formed, under the umbrella of the UK Cyber Security Forum, by a number of cyber security focused organisations in and around the Cambridge (Silicon Fen) & Peterborough area’s (but also including East Anglia as a whole) including Anglia Ruskin University & its Cyber Security Research Team 
>  
> 
> The aims of the Cluster are two-fold:-
> 
>  
> 
> To support the members of the cluster by communicating National and International initiatives and trade opportunities, providing a networking platform to share ideas and best practice, encouraging collaboration and identifying partnership opportunities so that small cyber security specialist businesses in the East of England can find new ways to grow.
>  
> 
> To support the newly proposed devolved Greater Cambridgeshire area and the East of England’s commitment to Cyber Security (and UK Government's Cyber Security Strategy) by building cyber security knowledge, skills and capabilities in the Region, to make businesses more resilient to cyber attacks and the East of England Region, one of the most secure places in the world to do business.
>  
> 
> These meetings are free to attend for any company actively working in Cyber Security.
> 
>  
> 
> If you, as an individual or an organisation, are interested in exploring what you can offer to support the UK Cyber Security Strategy in East Anglia then please contact us.
