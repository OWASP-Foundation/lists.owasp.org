Dear All

Hope everyone is well and ready for a well deserved break for Christmas

Just a reminder we have one of our sponsored events at Canterbury Christ
Church University in the New Year

The Second Annual Cyber conference organised by the Cyber Innovation Hub of
Canterbury Christ Church University., entitled "Emerging Cyberspace
Challenges and Solutions” is being held at Canterbury Christ Church
University,  Friday 11 January 2019 and is being supported by OWASP
Cambridge (more details in the attached PDF's and please contact
cyberconference at canterbury.ac.uk if you’re interested)

We're also looking to put on a couple of day events in Cambridge as well in
the New Year (more details to follow later)


   - Tuesday 15th January  "Reverse Engineering Workshop” (supported by,
   ARU Cyber Research Group, BCS Cybercrime Forensics Specialist Group & OWASP
   Cambridge) @ ARU in Cambridge – we’re still looking for a couple more
   speakers/demonstrators, please let me know if you know anyone who might be
   interested.
   - Thursday 24th January “Cyber Threat Intelligence Day” (supported by,
   ARU Cyber Research Group, BCS Cybercrime Forensics Specialist Group & OWASP
   Cambridge) @ ARU in Cambridge – possibly looking for one more speaker.


More details early in the New Year

Wishing all members a Merry Christmas and a Prosperous New Year
