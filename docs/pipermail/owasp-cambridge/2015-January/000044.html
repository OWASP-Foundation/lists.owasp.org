On the 28th-30th of January the OWASP London Chapter is helping host the Cyber Startup Summit in Central London, which is an event series designed to promote, highlight and bring spotlight to cyber security innovation and new cyber startups in the UK. 

Secure Startup Event: 
Talks/workshops for generic startups to better understand how to develop secure products, secure existing products and secure the business assets/IP/Data.

Cyber Innovation Event: 
Talks and security leader discussions on key topics discussing the now and future of cyber security innovation and how new cyber startups may have a part to play. 

Hackathon Event:
A two day hackathon for developers, students and the security community so work on new ideas that will either create a cyber security product or a product that has security at core.

We invite you to connect and discuss the hot topics within the world of cyber security. For all details and (free) tickets please view the website: http://www.cyberstartupsummit.com <http://www.cyberstartupsummit.com/>
