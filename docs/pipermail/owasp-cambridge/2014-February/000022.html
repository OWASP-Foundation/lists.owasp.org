The AppSec Europe 2014 Selection Committee is accepting papers, presentations and training submissions for the OWASP event in Cambridge, June 23rd-26th.

We invite all practitioners of application security and those who work or interact with all facets of application security to submit speaker, research  and trainer proposals.

For more information, read the appropriate document:

Call for Presentations (http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Call-for-Presentations-v1-1.doc)
Call for Training (http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Call-for-Training-v1.1.doc)
Call for Papers (http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Call-for-Papers.doc)

All submissions should be sent via EasyChair (https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=appseceu2014)

(Select the appropriate track once you have registered.)

Important Dates:
     CFP/CFT Open: Feb 1st
     CFP/CFT Closes: March 21st
     Acceptance Notification: April 25th
     Conference Schedule Publication: May 16th

Please help us spread the word by sending this e-mail to all your work colleagues and associates
