Dear All

Further to my recent announcements on this list and at recent chapter
meetings, we are now confirming the following two events on Thursday 17th
May 2018.

   - Between 12:00 - 17:30, we are organising a* Student Team “Capture the
   Flag” e*vent with guest speakers for teams of 4-10 members from
   college’s or universities to compete in jeopardy challenges against each
   other.  One of our OWASP partners Avatao will be hosting the CTF
   challenges.  There will be prizes for the winning teams.  Lunch and
   refreshments will be provided.

Ed Williams Director for EMEA Trustwave/SpiderLabs talking on Red Teaming &
Sgt Andy Baldwin (ERSOU) talking on Cyber Prevent

Feel free to come along if only for the talks

Registration Link: https://goo.gl/uzvdao

   - Between 17:30 - 21:30, we are organising an additional *OWASP
   Cambridge CTF event* (with guest speakers) for industry and students
   alike on an individual challenge basis.  Avatao will be kindly hosting the
   OWASP based challenges for individuals to compete against each other.
   There will be prizes for winning individuals.  Pizza and beer will be
   available during the evening.

Guest Speaker - Goher Mohammad – Head of Security Engineering, Photobox,
“Scaling Security Workflows & How to be Hired for a Crowd Sourcing Security
Team”

Registration Link: https://goo.gl/azYnp3

Please remember to bring your laptop and charger
If anyone is willing to sponsor the event(s) or willing to donate any
prizes, please let me know.

Please feel to distribute to other colleagues and lists

Further details attached to this email.

Many thanks

Adrian
