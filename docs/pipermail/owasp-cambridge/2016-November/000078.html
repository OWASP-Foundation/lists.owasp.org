Dear All

Just an early reminder that our next OWASP Cambridge  Chapter Meeting is set for the Tuesday 13th December 2016 5:30 - 8:30 pm

We have 3 brilliant speakers lined up for the evening & Sonatype are kindly sponsoring the refreshments 

We have Ken Munro from Pentest Partners doing what he does best. (Ken is the guy who regularly breaks IoT devices for a living in the National Press to demonstrate just how poorly we build security into devices nowadays.

We have Chris Dye, VP Glasswall who will share how files are being manipulated and show the trends in attacks and techniques that can be deployed to eliminate document based attacks.

Rounding up the evening we have Iikka Turunen from Sonatype delivering a topical talk of Secure DevOps.

In the next few days, I’ll be posting round the official flyer and Eventbrite details.

Look forward to seeing you there
