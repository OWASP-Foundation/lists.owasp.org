It is often difficult to undertake automated threat risk assessments or
attempt to quantitatively define the potential impact of attacks due to
lack of data. It is hard to keep track of publicised attacks, but the
following report includes useful data:

http://info.shapesecurity.com/rs/935-ZAM-778/images/Shape_Credential_Spill_Report_2018.pdf

There is information on the number of accounts compromised per successful
known attack, effects on retailer website traffic, lifecycle of attacks,
exploitation of validated credentials (including OAT-012 Cashing Out and
OAT-011 Scraping), and some estimates of the typical costs OAT-008
Credential Stuffing attacks have by sector.
