The UK's Digital Economy Act 2017 came into force this month, and includes
measures to make it illegal to attempt to purchase multiple tickets for a
recreational, sporting or cultural event in the United Kingdom, when they
are purchased online and limits per person are stated:

http://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2017/30/section/106/enacted


See also summary information in this news article:

https://www.theguardian.com/money/2018/jul/05/ticket-
touts-face-unlimited-fines-for-using-bots
<https://www.theguardian.com/money/2018/jul/05/ticket-touts-face-unlimited-fines-for-using-bots?CMP=Share_iOSApp_Other>


and a report on secondary ticketing:

http://researchbriefings.files.parliament.uk/documents/SN04715/SN04715.pdf
