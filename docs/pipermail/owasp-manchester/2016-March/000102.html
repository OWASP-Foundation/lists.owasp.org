Hello everyone,

We will have a new OWASP meeting at 17th March 2016, starting at 18:00 in
SpacePortX, Manchester. So far we have one talk by Scott Helme. The
abstract of his talk is:


Turning over a new Leaf – How the world’s bestselling electric car, the
Nissan Leaf can be accessed remotely to activate the climate control and
spy on details of the driver’s journeys simply by knowing or guessing the
VIN of the vehicle. This may seem like a harmless prank but could be used
to void warranties or drain batteries remotely, with Nissan looking to add
GPS tracking to the vehicles this issue could have become a whole lot worse.

You can book your tickets at
https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-manchester-meeting-17th-march-tickets-22527547501

We are still looking for presenters. If you would like to present or you
know someone who can prepare presentation (or already have ready one) for
17th, please let us know, since we would love to hear from you.

Best regards,
