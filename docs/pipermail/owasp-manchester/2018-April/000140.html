Hello all,

As I have already announced the next chapter meeting will be next Thursday
(03.05.2018) at the University place (room 1.218), on Oxford road (M13 9PL
).

Please register here for the event:
https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-manchester-chapter-meeting-tickets-45532107760

We're starting with something those of us 'in' the industry may take for
granted - how to get started in Information Security/Cyber
Security/Breaking Stuff as a Service/Fixing as a Service. We've lined up
two speakers who you may know, but you may not know how they found their
feet in the industry:

Daniel Dresner (@danielgdresner) is starting us off with his experiences of
pragmatically addressing risk across his career to date from industry and
academia. And possibly why bow ties matter.

John Denneny of Pentest Limited will be speaking to how he's seen his
career and role change in starting up a firm. Those of you with an
entrepreneurial spirit take notes....John knows what it takes to build your
own career and a £10m business around it.

QA will be after each speaker due to scheduling commitments. Also, the
event will be accompanied with some snacks and beers.

Hope to see you there.
