Hi folks,

Immo Huneke has forwarded the details of the BCS meeting thats being held in
Manchester which he mentioned last Wednesday:

BCS Software Practice Advancement specialist group
==================================================

Experience some of the most popular sessions from the recent SPA2011
conference, for free, at miniSPA2011 on Monday, October 3rd at Manchester
Conference Centre. (Yes, Manchester, not London, for a change!)

The miniSPA2011 one-day programme features six sessions, in two streams,
that give an excellent guide to the variety and quality you'll find at
every SPA conference.

If you attended SPA2011 you might find that miniSPA2011 allows you to
catch up with sessions you missed at the event.

We hope that attending miniSPA will encourage you to submit a session
proposal for SPA2012, which will be taking place from July 1-4 (London).

For more information visit:
http://minispa2011.eventbrite.com/

Booking is essential and places are limited, so reserve yours now.

Cheers,

Simon
