Howdy,

On Saturday the 15th of October we will be hosting a Cyber Security Challenge: Capture the Flag Event.

Unfortunately the competition is only open to non cyber security professional and students, as its purpose is to try to get more talent into the area.
So if your work in IT or generally have an interest in security but don't officially work in it, then please send an email to:

universities at cybersecuritychallenge.org.uk or email me back directly

NOTE: Teams are made up of 4 people but if you are either an individual or don't quite make a 4 still email your details and we will make the teams up to 4's.

-----
Here is the official blurb and here is a link to the poster - https://digi.ninja/files/cyberchallengeposter.pdf

"Sheffield Hallam, The Cyber Security Challenge and Raytheon, will be hosting a CTF event on Saturday the 15th of October.

We invite teams of up to 4 individuals to enter a jeopardy style CTF, so come along, have some fun, develop your skills and meet other like minded people.

Lunch will be provided courtesy of the BCS.

We will have talks from Robin Wood AKA Digi Ninja and Steve Wilson from BT.

Prizes have been donated by Satisnet for the top 3 teams.

We will be based in the Cantor Building in the City Campus. - https://goo.gl/maps/bWCN38zESAK2
PC's to access the CTF will be provided on the day.


There is a car park which costs £4 for all day, here are the directions.

https://goo.gl/maps/KGk1Jjc171H2

We are also based right next to the train station:

http://goo.gl/dCLyF6.

"
