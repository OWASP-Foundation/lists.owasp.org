Hi All,



The next OWASP Manchester meeting will be at 6pm on Wednesday 15th June at
UK Fast's offices in Hulme [1]. UKFast will be sponsoring the venue and
bar. NCC Group will sponsor the pizza.



*We are still seeking speakers for this event. *We have one speaker so far
and want more - please email stuartw1 at gmail.com with a brief outline of
what you want to speak about. It doesn't have to be web-app related
(anything to do with IT security will be fine) but presentations around
web-apps will generally get priority.



A more detailed agenda including speakers will be sent out in the next few
weeks.



The first wave of 100 tickets will be released around 12pm midday on Friday
15th May. A link to the eventbrite page will be sent out immediately
before. A smaller wave will follow closer to the event for anyone who
missed out.
