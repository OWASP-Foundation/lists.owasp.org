The *OWASP AppSec-Eu/Research 2015 *is coming close:
http://2015.appsec.eu

For the first time, the OWASP BeNeLux-Chapters are to organize the*
AppSec-Eu/Research 2015* conference at the RAI Amsterdam!
Don't be later to register!

With two* training days, May 19th and 20th* with 12 high value 1 and 2 day
trainings:
http://2015.appsec.eu/trainings/
At both training days, we host a *BugBash *event together with BugCrowd,
from 18:00 to 23:30: http://2015.appsec.eu/bugbash/
This is open and free for all, no conference registration/ticket
required!Please register here (for catering service, pizzas will be served):
http://www.eventbrite.com/e/bugcrowd-owasp-appsec-eu-bug-bash-tickets-16764293453

And two *conference conference days, May 21st and 22nd*:
+ >30 talks in  4 tracks of* Dev, Ops, Hack and CISO* talks are lined up:
http://2015.appsec.eu/talks/
+ high valuable *hands-on sessions* are available at both days:
http://2015.appsec.eu/hands-on/
+ *HackPra Allstars* add's even more great speakers in an additional
session at day one, May 21st: http://2015.appsec.eu/hackpra-allstars/
+ a great line-up of *international recognized keynote speakers* add even
more value to the program: http://2015.appsec.eu/keynote-speakers/

The social event at Thursday eve is included in the ticket, a guided boat
trip over the Amsterdam channels to the NEMO Science Center!
http://2015.appsec.eu/social-events/ i

See the *whole conference schedule* at:
http://2015.appsec.eu/conference-program/
