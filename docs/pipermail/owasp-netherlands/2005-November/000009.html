Dear Geert,

English is the preferred language on the mailing list. There are a
couple of reasons for this:

- archive function of the mailing list
The mailing list serves as an information archive, for the whole OWASP
community

- participation of OWASP chapters, companies and other individuals
outside the Netherlands
Participation on discussions, projects, development, etc. etc.

- companies grow outside national borders, employees frequently speak
English

But if somebody has difficulties writing in English, please write in
Dutch, instead of not writing at all!

Regards,

Bert Koelewijn
