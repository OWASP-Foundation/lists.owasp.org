*Jim Manico, known as host of the OWASP Podcast series will be in the
Netherlands to speak at the HITB conference. *

*We where able to set up a chapter meeting for May 19th, see the OWASP NL

Program:

18:30 to 19:00 registration and dinner

19:00 to 19:30 OWASP Global and NL chapter update

19:30 to 20:30 Presentation of Jim Manico (see below)

20:30 to 21:00 Discussion about OWASP NL 2011 plans


Jim Manico is a managing partner of Infrared Security with over 15 years of
professional web development experience. Jim is also the chair of the OWASP
connections committee, one of the project managers of the OWASP ESAPI
project, a participant and manager of the OWASP Cheatsheet series, the
producer and host of the OWASP Podcast Series, the manager of the OWASP Java
HTML Sanitizer project and the manager of the OWASP Java Encoder project.
When not OWASP'ing, Jim lives on of island of Kauai with his lovely wife
Tracey.


*The Ghost of XSS Past, Present and Future. A Defensive Tale.*

This talk will discuss the past methods used for XSS defense that were only
partially effective. Learning from these lessons, will will also discuss
present day defensive methodologies that are effective, but place an undue
burden on the developer. We will then finish with a discussion of future XSS
defense mythologies that shift the burden of XSS defense from the developer
to various frameworks. These include auto-escaping template technologies,
browser-based defenses such as Content Security Policy, and Javascript
sandboxes such as the Google CAJA project and JSReg


If you want to attend, please send an email to: owasp-netherlands at owasp.org
Please register before May 18th, due to the necessary catering arrangements.
