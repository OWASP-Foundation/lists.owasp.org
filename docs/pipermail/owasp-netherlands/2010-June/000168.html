Dear Benelux members,

I wanted to inform everyone that the schedule of BruCON 2010 is online. A
small non-profit community-driven security conference in Brussels featuring
various topics including application security. While there are no specific
discount codes, normal tickets are only 150 euro.

Participants are welcome to contribute their own topics to the conference
and you can still register for a lightning talk. Only rule is:
non-commercial, ethical and legal topics only. ;-)

http://2010.brucon.org/index.php/Lightning_Talks
