Hello OWASP Netherlands followers,

The next OWASP NL Monthly Meetup is scheduled for the 3rd of November
in restaurant De Branding in Utrecht.

The Meetup will start at 18:30.
During the Meetup we will chat, brainstorm and play Cornucopia (
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Cornucopia).
To play Cornucopia some card decks will be available and the rules will be
explained.

For more details, see:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_NL_Monthly_Meetup
https://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Chapter-Netherlands-Utrecht-Meetup/events/228632193/
