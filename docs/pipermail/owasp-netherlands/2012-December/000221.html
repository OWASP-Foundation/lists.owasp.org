Dear OWASP Netherlands Chapter Mailing list subscriber,

As a not-for-profit organization we are constantly looking for venues
suitable for a Chapter meeting.

We would like to have a list of venues from which we can select one for
every meeting.

Prerequisites are:
- Capacity between 20 and 30 attendees;
- Close to public transport
- Ample parkingspace
- Open in the evenings
- Preferably equipped for presentations (beamer & screen)

If you can help us in providing such a venue, please fill in the form of
which we have supplied a link below.

https://docs.google.com/a/owasp.org/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dEp4UXhxUUFodXdkN2pETVlFY1pNQ2c6MQ#gid=0

Many thanks in advance and hope to meet you at one of our Chapter meetings.
