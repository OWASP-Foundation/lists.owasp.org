Next April BruCON organizes their Spring training for a 2nd time in Ghent.

OWASP members get a 10% discount on these trainings.
(This applies even on top of the early-bird registration which is available
until the 21st of March !)

They have some excellent trainers and topics:
* Practical Malware Analysis: Rapid Introduction by Michael Sikorski (3
days)
* Tactical Exploitation and Response by Russ Gideon (3 days)
* Red Team Testing by Ian Amit and Chris Nickerson (3 days)
* Hacking web applications – case studies of award-winning bugs in Google,
Yahoo, Mozilla and more by Dawid Czagan (2 days)
* Wireshark WiFi and Lua-Packet Class by Dieder Stevens (2 days)

For more information and registration, please visit the BruCON training
page - http://2015.brucon.org/index.php/Training
