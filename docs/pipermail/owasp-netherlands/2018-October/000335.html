Dear,

I kindly invite you to our OWASP BeNeLux Days 2018. The event will take
place on 29 and 30 November 2018, in Mechelen (Belgium).


On *Thursday 29 November*, three *1-day trainings*
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_BeNeLux-Days_2018#tab=Training_Day> are
offered (*in parallel - only pick 1*):

   -

   Kubernetes security (by Andrew Martin)
   -

   OWASP Zap Training (by David Scrobonia)
   -

   Android security workshop (by Jeroen Beckers & Stephanie Vanroelen)


On *Friday 30 November*, the *1-day conference*
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_BeNeLux-Days_2018#tab=Conference_Day>
features
a line-up of various regional and international speakers:

   -

   Lessons from the legion *by Nick Drage*
   -

   OWASP Zap* by David Scrobonia*
   -

   When Serverless Met Security… Serverless Security &
   Functions-as-a-Service (FaaS) *by Niels Tanis*
   -

   Leaky Processors: Stealing Your Secrets with Foreshadow* by Jo Van Bulck*
   -

   Weaknesses in our voice communications network: from Blue Boxing to
   VoLTE *by Ralph Moonen*
   -

   Juice Shop: OWASP's most broken Flagship *by Björn Kimminich*
   -

   Fast forwarding Mobile Security with the MSTG *by Jeroen Willemsen*
   -

   Fast, Furious and Insecure: Passive Keyless Entry and Start in Modern
   Supercars *by Lennert Wouters*



Attendance to the conference is free of charge, but registration is
required. If you register for a training, please consider to become an
OWASP member <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership>.

We have a limited number of seats available, so *first come first serve*.



More information on the program and venue can be found here
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_BeNeLux-Days_2018>.
Kind regards,
