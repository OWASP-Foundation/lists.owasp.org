The meeting 9 march is definitive.

When reading all the subgroup initiatives, I think we should reserve time for forming the subgroups on the general meeting.
For every subgroup the following questions has to be answered:
1. Which specific topic is to be addressed?
2. What are the deliverables?
3. What is the relation to OWASP? (Current projects, materials, expertise and knowledge interchange, etc.)
4. Who is the central contact of the subgroup?

Currently we are looking for a venue somewhere around Utrecht and 1 speaker.

It would be nice to have a bigger and more diverse group, compared to the first meeting. So let's recall: "Please, bring at least one friend, next time."

The agenda:
18.00 - 18.30 Check-In (bread & drinks)
18.30 - 18.45 Opening
18.45 - 19.30 Presentation
19.30 - 20.00 Inventarisation of subgroup initiatives
19.45 - 20.00 Coffee break
20.00 - 21.00 Form subgroups

If you want to attend, please send an email to owasp at irc2.nl

All OWASP chapter meetings are free! There are never vendor pitches or sales presentations at OWASP meetings.
