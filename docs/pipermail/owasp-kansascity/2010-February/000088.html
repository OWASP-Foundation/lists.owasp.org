 6:00-7:30: "*Closing the TLS Authentication Gap*" presented by *Steve
Dispensa*
 7:30 - 8:00: Chapter Business to Discuss the 2010 Calendar Items and
Networking


Primary presentation by Steve Dispensa, Phone Factor
Title: Closing the TLS Authentication Gap


About the Speaker:

Steve Dispensa is co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of
PhoneFactor. Steve is responsible for the architecture and development of
their
award winning authentication and remote access platforms. His many
accomplishments include designing one of the world’s first broadband
wireless internet networks for Sprint, as well as being a five time winner
of
the Microsoft Most Valuable Professional award. He was also instrumental in
PhoneFactor’s recent discovery of the SSL/TLS authentication gap.

Further details at:
 http://www.phonefactor.com/sslgap
 http://www.phonefactor.com/blog


After the primary presentation, there will be opportunity to discuss future
directions of the chapter, meeting location plans, and upcoming speakers for
the coming year.
