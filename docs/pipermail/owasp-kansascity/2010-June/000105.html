KC-OWASP Chapter Members and Attendees:

The June Kansas City OWASP meeting was held on June 17th.

Special thanks to James Connolly for making the trip from Des Moines to
present to our chapter.
Also, Balance Point provided pizza and a great conference room location for
us.  THANK YOU!

Slides from James Connolly's presentation have been posted on the Kansas
City OWASP page:
   http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Kansas_City_June_2010_Meeting

Direct link to the presentation for those unable to attend is here:
   http://www.owasp.org/images/e/e5/RoleOfPenTestingAndAppScanning.pdf

Keeping on track with our plan to have bi-monthly chapter meetings, the next
OWASP chapter meeting will take place August 26.
Location and speaker details are pending and will be announced on this list.

Related, upcoming event:
The Kansas City Infragard will be hosting a "cyber warfare" event Sept 16,
2010 at the downtown Marriott.
Further information is available at:   http://cyber-raid.com/

We'd like to have another local speaker in August, so if you'd like to
present, let's get it lined up!

Thanks!
