Greetings:

We're now beginning to plan OWASP-KC events for the summer.

This email is a call for meeting speaker-presenters for chapter
meetings in the months ahead.

Previous presenters have hailed from: FishNet, PhoneFactor
pre-Microsoft-acquisition, Veracode, F5, Fortify, Imperva among many
others, although no affiliation is required.   In fact, we'd really
love to hear from any KC area security hackers who'd like present on a
topic of general interest.

So, if you are a hacker looking to showcase your talent, a security
company looking to advise the general public on the goods your company
can provide, or a consultant who's been there and done that, we'd like
to have you on board to help us build our chapter meetings.  Email me
directly or reply to the list for full consideration and a discussion
about best time to schedule.

Cheers,
