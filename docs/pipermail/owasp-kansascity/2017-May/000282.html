OWASP Kansas City will meet on May 23rd, 2017, at the usual location.  
We will be discussing the future of the Cowtown Day Of AppSec, and continuing our discussion on the OWASP Top Ten 2017 Release Candidate.  
Speaker: 
Justin Ferguson
"OWASP Top Ten Topics - Authentication: Letting the Right Users In and Keeping the Wrong Users Out"
Authentication is pretty much a requirement for any web application ... but there are a lot of moving parts to an auth system, and a lot of ways to mess it up.  This will be an overview of a number of aspects of web app authentication, from a fundamentals point of view, and from an AppSec point of view.  We'll touch on:
* Identity Federation - SAML, OAuth, OpenID, and more* Secure Password Storage* Multi-Factor Authentication (Tokens, SMS, Code Generators)* PKI and Certificates* Session Management* And more... 
We hope you'll be able to join us.  As always, RSVPs are appreciated, but not required.  Share this event on Facebook
                    and Twitter
                    We hope you can make it!Cheers,OWASP-KC
