Hi, all - First off, apologies for the late announcement - things (as
usual) have been a bit crazy.

You are invited to the June meeting of the Kansas City Chapter of the Open
Web Application Security Project!

This month's speaker will be yours truly (Justin Ferguson) - I will be
giving an hour-long overview of the OWASP Top 10.  Many people have
mentioned wanting an entry-level discussion of application security for
developers - this is the meeting!

As usual, the meeting will be from 6:30 to 8:00pm at the Kauffman
Foundation Conference Center, just East of the Plaza.  Please RSVP if
possible
<https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-kc-june-2016-tickets-26199132310?ref=etckt>
so we can provide an accurate count to the facility, but don't let a lack
of RSVP discourage you from attending!

If you are a developer, or have developers in your organization who you
believe might benefit from this, please forward the invite along.

Also, wanted to re-send the information about the INTERFACE-Kansas City
event - they are offering free admission for KC User Group members, OWASP
KC included - just sign up at the link below.

http://www.interfacetour.com/evites/kcm/owasp-kcm.htm

Looking forward to seeing everyone next Tuesday, and anyone who's at KCDC
tomorrow and Friday!

JF
