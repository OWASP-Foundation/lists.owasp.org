Kansas City OWASP chapter:

Individual sponsorship memberships to OWASP are $50

Why become a supporting individual member?

   - As a member of the internet community do you agree with the ethics and
   principals<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/About_The_Open_Web_Application_Security_Project>of
OWASP Foundation?
   - Do you want to underscore your awareness of web application software
   security?
   - Do you want to continue to increase your knowledge and expand your
   skills when attending OWASP conferences at a discount?
   - Do you want to expand your personal network of contacts? OWASP
   Linked'In Group <http://www.linkedin.com/e/gis/36874>
   - *A 40% of your membership fee directly supports the local chapter of
   your choice. *
   - You get a @OWASP.ORG email address submit a
request<https://spreadsheets.google.com/a/owasp.org/viewform?hl=en&formkey=dFN1R2NIMTNROXN3dml4ZEcxXzJQYXc6MQ#gid=0>
   - Have (1) individual vote in elections and on issues that shape the
   direction of the community.


The paid membership of the Kansas City is rather painfully low (less than 10
members in the greater KC area?)
     https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership
