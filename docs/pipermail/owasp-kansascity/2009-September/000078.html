Hi all:

This is a reminder of our next meeting this Thursday, September 17 at JCCC in Room 270 of the Regnier Center. We have a change in our agenda.  Stephen Craig Evans of Fishnet Security has recently moved to the KC area and will be presenting at our next meeting. He is well known for his 2008 Summer of Code project, Securing WebGoat using ModSecurity  and is also on the  OWASP Global Membership Committee. He will be an excellent resource to answer any general OWASP questions that somebody might have.

This is not a meeting you want to miss!

Agenda:

6:00 -6:15: "A very quick  overview of OWASP tools and guides" presented by Rohini Sulatycki, Trustwave
6:20-7:20: "An overview of WebGoat and Securing WebGoat using ModSecurity" presented by Stephen Craig Evans Fishnet Security
7:20 - 8:00: Networking
