Hi Stephen:

The understanding I have from Rohini is that for the remainder of this
term, I will be acting president to ensure a smooth transition.  This
will enable full process for the coming year.

Among the agenda items are to establish a way to grow up a board -
secretary, treasurer, etc. since this has been a 100% volunteer type
situation by a very small group of people and as we grow the OWASP
community here in Kansas City their roles will become increasingly
more important.  Right now these roles are not filled.  In sum, we can
use all of the help we can get, particularly from companies such as
FishNetSecurity who operate in this competitive space.  Would FishNet
like to sponsor the December OWASP Kansas City meeting?

We have some short term needs that seem as important as leadership,
such as filling speaking slots to maintain a purpose for bi-monthly
meetings.  There is no current speaker for November or December, for
example.

However, this shakes out, your contributions, and those of your
colleagues, will be most welcome.

Please give me a call and we can discuss how best to collaborate,
