OWASP Leaders,

This is the LAST CALL for AppSec DC Call for Papers that closes
tomorrow 8/31/2010.  You all work really hard for the organization and
it's important that that work be recognized.  The best way to do so is
to present your project at an OWASP conference!  If you are a project
leader and would like to present at AppSec DC this year please go to
https://www.easychair.org/account/signin.cgi?conf=appsecdc2010
(account creation for EasyChair Required) and submit a talk about all
the great work your already doing at OWASP.  Additional CFP
information can be found at
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSec_DC_2010#tab=CFP.

This year we are partnering with elements from DHS, NSA, DoD and NIST
to collaborate on content and it's important that OWASP have a strong
showing of our capebilities and products.  I had a conversation with
Joe Jarzombek on Friday and he is espeially looking forward to
introducing all his friends in the federal governemnt to the exciting
work done by OWASP!

You don't need to have a full abstract ready to submit.  If your
interested simply signup and link to your project page in the CFP
abstract.  That's all we'll need to get you on the schedule and into a
great OWASP conference!

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me, or CFP at appsecdc.org.

Thanks,
