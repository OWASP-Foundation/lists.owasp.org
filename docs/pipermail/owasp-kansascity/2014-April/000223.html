Directions from North of the Grandview Triangle

   - Take I-435 South
   - Take the Bannister Road exit - make a right turn onto Bannister Road
   Turn
   - Left at second stop light onto Marion Park Drive
   - Continue straight on Marion Park Drive to the security gate
   - Guest parking is on the right.
   - Enter the main lobby through the glass doors.

Directions from South of the Grandview Triangle

   - Take I-435 North
   - Take the Bannister Road exit
   - Left turn onto Bannister Road Turn
   - Left at third stop light onto Marion Park Drive
   - Continue straight on Marion Park Drive to the security gate
   - Guest parking is on the right.
   - Enter the main lobby through the glass doors.



This is a correction from earlier instructions which suggested an Overland
Park location.
