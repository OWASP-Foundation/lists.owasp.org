>> Greetings:

>> On June 25, in southern Kansas City, Kansas (at community college JCCC)
>> the OWASP chapter was present and staffed a booth at an annual conference of
>> area developers known as KCDC.   (  http://kcdc.info/  )
>>
>> We took the opportunity to discuss SQL injection, cross site scripting,
>> and database security with conference attendees who had questions on these
>> topics.  Approximately 12 people were emailed copies of the .NET remediation
>> resources from the OWASP wiki web site.
>>
>> OWASP notepads and lanyards, ordered specially for the occasion, were a
>> big hit and we gave away all that we came with.
>>
>> Special thanks to Chris Flowers and August Johnson who displayed
>> leadership in fielding questions and staffing the booth throughout the day.
>> A quick note of thanks also to the conference organizers and sponsoring
>> vendors who made this happen, Lee and Boon.
>>
>> See attached picture of the OWASP table at the conference.
>>
>> We hope to have the opportunity to be present next year.  The OWASP
>> mission is specifically targeted at the software development process, so it
>> was a particularly useful venue for our chapter to reach the audience for
>> whom software security is the message.
