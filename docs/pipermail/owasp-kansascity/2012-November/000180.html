Hello All,

Jenny Hoff (http://jennyhoff.com/), has started posting interviews she
conducted at AppSec USA.   She kicked it off with an interview with Jeff
Williams on "What is OWASP?".

http://www.youtube.com/user/owasptv

The purpose of the interview series is an outreach program - to capture the
voices of OWASP and continue to make appsec more visible!

Jenny is an international journalist who has been on CNN, Deutsche Welle,
and did political reporting for several years at KXAN in Austin, Texas.

Incidentally, she has been covering appsec for a while now - Here's an
older interview she did with RSnake back in the day:

reach out to Jenny (cc'ed).
