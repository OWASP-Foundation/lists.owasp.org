Anti-farming is a defensive strategy to stop attackers from scraping 
data off your webservices in high volume.

For example, attackers may scrape pricing data from a e-commerce sire or 
chip prices from a wholesales chip manufacturer.

The bottom line is - if you are listing prices in non-authenticated 
pages or resources, there is nothing you can do to effectively stop data 
farming. You need these resources protected under good authentication to 
even get started in an anti-farming strategy.
