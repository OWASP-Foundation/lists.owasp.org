                        <p><b>Description:</b>Passwords are the most
                          common way to authenticate to systems,
                          applications, services, etc. Authentication is
                          how a person or system proves their identity.
                          Three methods of authentications are: provide
                          something you know, something you have, or
                          something you are. Passwords fulfill the first
                          condition, something you know. People and
                          systems authenticate by providing something
                          only they should know, therefore proving their
                          identity. Weak password handling
                          vulnerabilities are weaknesses in the
                          handling, storage, and use of passwords. <br>
                        </p>
                        <p><b>Threats:</b>The exposure of passwords
                          through mishandling or improper storage could
                          allow discovery and use by a third party. <br>
                        </p>
                        <p><b>Impact:</b>Weak password handling can
                          result in the unauthorized access and
                          compromise of data or systems. <br>
                        </p>
                        <p><b>Recommendations:</b><br>
                        </p>
                        <ul>
                          <li>Use Multi-factor Authentication,
                            especially on important accounts <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Use a Password Manager <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Use Strong Passwords <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Avoid using the same password across
                            different accounts <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Do not answer security questions with
                            easily identifiable or enumerable answers <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Do not allow browsers to store passwords <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Do not share your passwords <br>
                          </li>
                          <li>Change default passwords <br>
                          </li>
                        </ul>
