Hi,



Attending DevTalks Bucharest comes with many perks! Our 2016 Agenda
has *meaningful
content* and the *Expo Area* is specially tailored so you can find out more
about local and international projects that didn’t fit into the Agenda
(Hey, it’s just a one-day event!).



DevTalks is a conference that focuses on the content that you can use for
future plans or analyze present projects, so be present and interact with
the speakers and other attendees! Our awesome speaker lineup includes: *Adrian
Furtuna – *Founder and ethical hacker at VirtualStorm Security, *Karina
Popova* – DevOps/Systems Engineer at whatever mobile GmbH, *Tobias Baldauf*
– Senior Web Performance Solutions Architect at Akamai Technologies, *Badi
Sudhakaran* – Competence Leader at IQ Business, *Paul Ardeleanu* – Mobile &
Web Software Engineer, Hello24 t/a Lupo, *Anto Joseph* – on behalf of the
OWASP Mobile Project and many more.



This year we also introduced a Startups Area - for more details about how
you can apply contact us at contact at devtalks.ro. As a participant in the
Startups Area you can interact with the attendees and company
representatives in order to promote yourself and get valuable advice. We
also organize meetup sessions with the speakers during the conference.



DevTalks Bucharest will take place on *June 9th* at *Romexpo Bucharest
(C1&C2)*. In order to get in the mood for the event we recommend that you
keep an eye on our blog <http://www.devtalks.ro/bucharest/blog/> for
articles and interviews with our speakers.



Thanks!
