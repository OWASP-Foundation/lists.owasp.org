Call for papers it is then! :)

Regardless the name, I have still got zero "applications". We can accommodate pretty much any presentation since we have none yet, but I suggest having no more than 15-20 minutes per presentation, 4 presentations max for the first meeting.

Please have a look over the formalities at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Speaker_Agreement . I intend to briefly go over the basics of OWASP since this is the first meeting, and start some discussion on what chapter members see as immediate and long term goals.

The presentations do not need to be the latest and greatest; they just have to be of interest to security specialists, so stuff that appeals to you may appeal to the audience.
Some example topics include:
-          Brief presentation on an OWASP project that you used, or that you are enthusiastic about (here I can cover something on webscarab if no one else wants, since I've used it quite a lot);
-          If you plan to contribute on something, or you are just thinking you may at some point, tell us a few words about it maybe you co-opt some other members too!
-          If you just discovered something on a particular security area, do share;
-          You may also show us how to do something hands on;
-          Do you own the truth on <insert conspiracy theory here> (stuxnet anyone? :) )

For further meetings I'll try to get a speaker from OWASP, but we'll have to do on our own for the first one.
