Unit tests are wonderful things, but they are painful to add to a mature
project that doesn’t have enough of them. We would love to have more ZAP
unit tests, and we are therefore launching a Unit Test Bounty program,
where we pay for unit tests for specific areas of the ZAP codebase.

For full details see: http://zaproxy.blogspot.com/
2016/08/announcing-zap-unit-test-bounties.html

Please spread the word!
