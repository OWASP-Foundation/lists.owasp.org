Hello,

Capitolul OWASP din Ungaria ne invita la urmatorul lor eveniment ce va avea
loc pe *17 Martie*. Evenimentul va fi *online* (voi reveni cu un link,
google hangouts), va incepe la ora *19.30* iar prezentarile vor fi in limba
engleza.
Mai multe detalii gasiti aici: http://meetu.ps/2GGLhn

Agenda evenimentului cuprinde prezentari pe tema *Internet of Things*:

1. *Picking an IoT Platform is Risky Business*, Attila Török, Security
engineering manager / LogMeIn
2. *Secure by design*, Aaron Guzman, OWASP LA / board member, Sr
penetration tester / Belkin, @scriptingxss
3. *IoT Top 10*, Jürgen Grieshofer, OWASP AT / board member, Awarity
Training / director
4. *Identity, Security and Privacy in the IoT era*, Gregory Estrade, a
software dev and a self-taught hw hacker, @torlus
5. *Know Thyself – Managing Identity in the IoT*, Sean Lorenz, Sr product
marketing manager / LogMeIn
6. *Where are the Humans in IoT?* - Usability, trust, and privacy, Márk
Vinkovits, Security engineer / LogMeIn

O seara frumoasa!
