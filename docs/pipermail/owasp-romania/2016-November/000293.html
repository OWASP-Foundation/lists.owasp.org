Dear OWASP Leaders,

Hello from LASCON in Austin, Texas!

Colin Watson and I would like to announce the availability of version 1.1
of OWASP Automated Threat Handbook. The handbook is a product of OWASP
Automated Threats to Web Application project
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Automated_Threats_to_Web_Applications>,
and Colin and I have been working on the update together over last few
months.

Key updates in this version are ​enumeration of countermeasure classes,
application of countermeasures to specific threats and more examples of
symptoms for each threat.

You can download the PDF book here
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/File:Automated-threat-handbook.pdf>. It
should be available on Lulu Press <http://www.lulu.com/spotlight/owasp> in
a few days.

We are now onto v1.2 of the book and please let us know of your comments,
feedback and opinion.
