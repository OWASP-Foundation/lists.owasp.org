Hello!

We are thrilled to announce the first annual *BSides Bucharest* *Conference*
 *made by **security enthusiasts for security enthusiasts.*

*BSides Bucharest* is part of the *Security BSides* phenomenon, a worldwide
community-driven framework for building events for and by information
security community members.

The idea of creating a *BSides Bucharest Conference* was born in early 2018.
 The team already organized 2 meet-up events with a huge success in the
community and now they are ready to launch the first annual conference.

The goal of the *BSides Bucharest Conference* is to *’’organize a
sales-pitch free Information Security community where professionals,
experts, researchers, and InfoSec enthusiasts come together to discuss*’’.
It is an intense event with discussions, demos, and interaction from
participants. It is where conversations for the next-big-thing are
happening.

This year’s edition of *BSides Bucharest Conference* is a 1 full day
*Security *Conference with 1 *Capture the Flag* play-day contest, both
taking place on 23rd of November 2018 at *Romanian-American University
<https://www.google.ro/maps/place/Universitatea+Rom%C3%A2no-American%C4%83/@44.4733941,26.063302,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x40b20239830b10f3:0x11e08e39c69aebbe!8m2!3d44.4733903!4d26.0654908>
*
Bucharest.

Further information about the talks is available *here
<https://bsidesbucharest.org/>*. Registration is *FREE *so you just need to
book your seat using this *link
<https://www.eventbrite.com/e/security-bsides-bucharest-conference-tickets-50528737815?aff=ebdssbdestsearch>*
.

Come and join us on 23rd of November 2018 because *YOU* make *BSides
Bucharest Conference* great!
