Hello,

Urmatoarea intalnire OWASP va avea loc *joi 18 iunie* de la ora *19.00 in
sediul Dell
<https://www.google.com/maps/place/Dell+International+Services/@44.481324,26.122221,297m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m2!3m1!1s0x0:0x98c76bed5db07a1d?hl=en>*
(B-dul
Dimitrie Pompei, nr. 10A, Conect Business Park, Cladirea C3)

Il vom avea prezent pe *Johnny Wachter
<https://www.linkedin.com/in/johnnywachter>* ce va sustine o prezentare
despre:

*Dynamic Malware Analysis* – A hands on guide for quickly studying malware
behavior and implementing mitigating controls:

   - This will demonstrate the manual analysis of malicious samples
   commonly distributed via Phishing Emails and Exploit Kits.
   - Common Tools and Techniques for Safely and Efficiently identifying
   Indicators of Compromise will be covered.
   - A practical guide and hands-on labs will be made available in case
   attendees wish to “practice” at home.

Daca doriti sa contribuiti la aceasta intalnire cu o prezentare sau tema de
discutie imi spuneti si o adaug pe agenda :)

Ca de obicei, va rog sa nu uitati sa confirmati prezenta.
Multumesc!
