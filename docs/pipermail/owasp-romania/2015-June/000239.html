Urmatoarea intalnire OWASP va avea loc joi 18 iunie de la ora 19.00 in sediul Dell (B-dul Dimitrie Pompei, nr. 10A, Conect Business Park, Cladirea C3)
Il vom avea prezent pe Johnny Wachter ce va sustine o prezentare despre:
Dynamic Malware Analysis – A hands on guide for quickly studying malware behavior and implementing mitigating controls:   
   - This will demonstrate the manual analysis of malicious samples commonly distributed via Phishing Emails and Exploit Kits.
   - Common Tools and Techniques for Safely and Efficiently identifying Indicators of Compromise will be covered.
   - A practical guide and hands-on labs will be made available in case attendees wish to “practice” at home.   

Daca doriti sa contribuiti la aceasta intalnire cu o prezentare sau tema de discutie imi spuneti si o adaug pe agenda :)
