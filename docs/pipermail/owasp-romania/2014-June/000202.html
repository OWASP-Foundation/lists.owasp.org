> *19:15 - 20:00* Presentation: *Jack Mannino* (remote): *Building Secure
> Android Apps*
> *Building Android applications in a secure way requires a touch of common
> sense combined with an understanding of the underlying security model and
> framework. In this presentation, we will examine the Android security model
> and common security flaws introduced by developers, including the OWASP
> Mobile Top 10 Risks. We will see these issues exploited and more
> importantly, we will see them remediated.*
> *20:00 - 21:00* Presentation: *Cosmin Huruiala*: *Risk scoring for
> penetration testing*
>
> *This short presentation tries to point out why I believe is important to
> calculate risk and some of the metrics I consider relevant for calculating
> risk after a pentest. Also tries to outline common pitfalls for some of the
> most used risk calculation methodologies and how can they be successfully
> avoided.*
