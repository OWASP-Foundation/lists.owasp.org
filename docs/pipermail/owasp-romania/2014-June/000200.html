Salut,

OWASP va fi prezent la DevTalks <http://devtalks.ro> 2014. (
http://devtalks.ro/exhibitors/)
Sunteti invitati la acest eveniment dedicat programatorilor si
specialistilor IT din Romania, miercuri, pe 11 iunie la Radisson Blu Hotel.

Ne vedem acolo! Nu ratati acest eveniment!

Mai multe detalii si invitatia oficiala sunt mai jos:
**********************************************************************************

Hello,

We are back with the last update about *Dev Talks*
<http://devtalks.ro/agenda/?utm_campaign=DevTalks&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter_wi&utm_content=30>
-
the largest IT event for 350 developers & IT professionals.

First of all, if you haven’t got the chance to register, please do so
because there *are a few more days to register *for the newest IT event. As
we have mentioned before, we have launched this event for the developer’s
community and our purpose is to bring deep technical content with insights
for the contemporary professionals.

In case you didn't read the agenda, the whole event experience will cover
Web, Mobile, Cloud Computing & Big Data areas. Specially tailored formats
such as *keynotes, labs, panel discussions or exhibitors* bring along the
way various technologies, which are a valuable demand in the community. *“The
Web is Dead. Long live the Web!”, “One bug per month”, “Big Data, now or
never”, “Cloud API”, “Yes, you need Agile architecture”, “Mobile Wars:iOS,
Android & Windows”* are just a few examples of what *Dev Talks* will
represent.

Moreover, during our exhibitors space, you can talk with IBM, XL Team,
IMWorld, Brainspotting, 3D printing, Artworks with Augmented Reality (AR)
technology, Google Glasses brought by our speakers, Multytouch surfaces and
a networking area that will generate valuable talks and great contact
exchanges.

Register here
<http://devtalks.ro/register/?utm_campaign=DevTalks&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter_wi&utm_content=30>
 and see you at the event. We offer OWASP Members a 20% discount off the
general event registration fee.

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact us at
andreea.balaci at catalyst.ro.

We look forward to seeing you at the event. We promise great networking &
company!
