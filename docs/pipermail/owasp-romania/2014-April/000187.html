> Nu uitati de intalnirea de maine: ora *19.00* in Politehnica sala *EG306.* Acestea
> sunt prezentarile si descrierile lor:
>
> *Simon Bennetts - "An introduction to OWASP ZAP"*
>
> The Zed Attack Proxy (ZAP) is now one of the most popular OWASP projects.
> It is designed to be used by people with a wide range of security
> experience and as such is ideal for developers and functional testers who
> are new to penetration testing as well as being a useful addition to an
> experienced pen tester's toolbox.
> In this talk Simon will give an overview of the functionality ZAP provides
> as well as an insight into some of the enhancements that will be available
> in the forthcoming 2.3 release
>
> *Ionut Popescu - "Introduction to shellcode development" *
>
> Ce sunt shellcode-urile, care e diferenta dintre un executabil si un
> shellcode, cand se folosesc, cum functioneaza, principiile scrierii
> shellcode-urilor, limitari, obfuscare si evitarea detectiei de catre
> antivirus.
