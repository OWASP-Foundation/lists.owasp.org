Leaders,

The Needs Assessment Report for the Website Project
<https://tracking.cirrusinsight.com/94fd0309-1156-46c5-9a97-a523315ef120/owasp-org-index-php-owasp-initiatives-global-strategic-focus-website-project>has
been completed.  Before the board votes on how to go forward it is
imperative that we receive input from you and the community.  The report is
available here
<https://tracking.cirrusinsight.com/94fd0309-1156-46c5-9a97-a523315ef120/owasp-org-images-b-b2-owasp-needs-assessment-report-pdf>
please
look through it and leave your comments on the Discussion Page
<https://tracking.cirrusinsight.com/94fd0309-1156-46c5-9a97-a523315ef120/owasp-org-index-php>.
We will also be announcing a Twitter and FB chat later today to discuss the
project.

This is a major investment for OWASP in terms of funds, time, and
importance.  The decisions made here will have lasting impacts on the OWASP
website and experience.  Please talk about this with your chapter and
project teams on mailing lists, chapter/team social media, and in your
meetings should you have them.  Please STRONGLY ENCOURAGE people to comment
on the discussion page
<https://tracking.cirrusinsight.com/94fd0309-1156-46c5-9a97-a523315ef120/owasp-org-index-php1>
and
join the Twitter/FB chat. Please also reach out to other leaders to ensure
they know what is happening and feel free and encouraged to post from your
personal social media accounts.
