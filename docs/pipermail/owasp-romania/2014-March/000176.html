Folks,

The wiki cleanup project is going to begin in earnest over the next few
months. For starters:

1) If you see any area of the OWASP wiki that needs improvement, please
send me email at jim at owasp.org or email the wiki-cleanup list via
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-wiki-editors
2) If you are interested in helping be a wiki curator/editor, please join
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-wiki-editors. We have not
even decided for sure if we will have a formal wiki editing staff, but we
are considering it at this point.
3) If you have any other wiki cleanup advice or suggestions, please send
them my/our way.
