Buna,

A fost extins pana pe 25 iunie call-ul pentru speakeri la "OWASP InfoSec
India Conference 2013".

Cei interesati pot gasi mai multe detalii despre beneficii si criteriile de
evaluare aici <http://2013.owasp.in/category/cfp/> si in mesajul de mai jos.

***********************************************************************************************


Just wanted to update all, who are interested in participating and
presenting their papers / researches at forthcoming "OWASP InfoSec India
Conference 2013" happening at Hotel Crowne Plaza, Gurgaon, New Delhi NCR,
India from August 30-31, 2013. The CFP for the conference has been extended
for a few more days now.****

With few great papers submitted already, would like to take this
opportunity to extend the CFP submission dates to 7 more days in order to
allow maximum participation.

CFP will now close on June 25th, 2013, more details can be found at:
http://2013.owasp.in/category/cfp/ ****

Don't miss out the opportunity to present your papers to a vibrant
information security community of CIOs, CISOs, investigators, policy
makers, INTs, defense, CERTs in the cyber city and national capital region
of India.


With regards,
