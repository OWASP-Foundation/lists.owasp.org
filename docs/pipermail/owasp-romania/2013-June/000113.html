OWASP Leaders -


There have been a lot of announcements and updates flying around from our
Global AppSec Events, which serve as both outreach opportunities as well as
fundraisers for the OWASP Foundation, bringing in 40% of our annual
revenue.  Here is a summary of the latest news from each event! (Please
share with your chapter lists).


*AppSec Research 2013 - August 21-24, Hamburg Germany <https://appsec.eu/>*



   - Registration is OPEN and early bird registration prices available
   until July 1* *https://appsec.eu/registration/
   - Schedule has been published here:  sched.appsec.eu
   - 10 great pre-conference training classes are being offered August
   21-22, 2013 - https://appsec.eu/trainings/


*
**AppSec Latam 2013 - October 1-4, Lima Peru <http://www.appseclatam.org/>*



   - Call for Training and Call for Papers are now open (deadline August 2,
   2013) - visit http://www.appseclatam.org to submit your training or talk!



*AppSec USA 2013 - November 18-21, New York City, NY<http://appsecusa.org/2013/>
*



   - Full schedule of Talks and Training Classes is now online:
   http://appsecusa.org/2013/schedule/
   - Sponsor opportunities are filling up fast - if you want to be a part
   of our exhibit hall or career fair, contact us ASAP!
   http://appsecusa.org/2013/sponsors/
   - A number of great activities are planned for the conference: CTF,
   Lockpick Village, Career Fair, OWASP Project Summit, Project and Chapter
   Workshops, 3k for Charity, and more.
   http://appsecusa.org/2013/activities/


*
**CONGRATS to the following chapters that will be hosting our 2014 events:*

*
**AppSec APAC 2014 - March 17-20 in Tokyo Japan hosted by the OWASP Japan
Chapter <https://owasp.org/index.php/Japan>*

*
**AppSec Research 2014- Late June 2014 - at Anglia Ruskin University
(Cambridge) hosted by the Cambridge, UK
Chapter<https://owasp.org/index.php/Cambridge> with
support from the other UK chapters.*

*
*and last but not least...*AppSec USA 2014, September 2014, Denver, CO -
hosted by the OWASP Denver <https://owasp.org/index.php/Denver> and OWASP
Boulder Chapters <https://owasp.org/index.php/Boulder> *who have put on
FROC (Front Range OWASP Conference) for the past 5 years.


Thanks to all the volunteers, trainers, speakers, sponsors, attendees, and
owasp community members that continue to make these events a great success
for OWASP.


Do you want to get involved in one of the events listed above or have a
