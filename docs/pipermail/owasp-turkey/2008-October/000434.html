Accept-Encoding: gzip,deflateAccept-Charset: ISO-8859-9,utf-8;q=0.7,*;q=0.7Keep-Alive: 300Connection: keep-aliveCookie: comment_author_xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx63=O%C4%9Fuzhan; ...
