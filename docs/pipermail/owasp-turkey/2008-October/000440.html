Verdigin linkte (http://developer.yahoo.com/performance/rules.html#opt_images) asagidaki gibi bi bilgi de var;
 
"Run jpegtran on all your JPEGs. This tool does lossless JPEG operations such as rotation and can also be used to optimize and remove comments and other useless information (such as EXIF information) from your images."jpegtran -copy none -optimize -perfect src.jpg dest.jpg 
 
Hem performansi hem de guvenligi saglayan yegane teknolojilerden biri PreparetStatements (Bind Variables, v.b.) diye ornek verirdim. Ama image optimizerlar da oyleymis.
