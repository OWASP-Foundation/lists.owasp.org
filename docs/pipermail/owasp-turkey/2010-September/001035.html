Ilgilenebileceginizi dusundugum bir egitim icin atiyorum bu emaili.

SANS Organizasyonu'nun her yıl Londra' da gerçekleştirmekte olduğu SANS
London Eğitimi bu sene de 27 Kasım - 6 Aralık 2010 tarihleri arasında
Londra'da gerçekleşecektir. Bu konferansda bizi ilgilendiren Web Application
Penetration Testing (Sec. 542) dersi de verilecektir. SANS'ın
http://www.sans.org/london-2010/ sayfasından detaylı bilgiye
ulaşabilirsiniz.

Eğitim hakkında daha fazla bilgi sahibi olmak isterseniz benimle irtibata
geçebilirsiniz.

Teşekkürler

Ibrahim Saruhan







PS: SANS Londra ile ilgili bilgiler aşağıdadır.

SANS London is Europe's largest training event for information and security
professionals, which is celebrating its 5th anniversary this year with one
of the most comprehensive programs to date. Last year around 300 students
attended SANS London, making it the largest SANS Institute event outside of
the US of the year as well as the single largest independent gathering of
information security professionals in the UK.

This year, SANS announces 14 courses covering software and audit, management
and compliance as well as new additions around security within virtualized
environments, network forensics and ethical hacking. The sessions, course
materials and deep interaction with peers, instructors and subject matter
experts extend past the classroom.

SANS helps students to build practical skills allowing students to meet the
goals of their organizations, but also to enhance their individual value in
what is still a highly competitive market. Many students, who will be
returning to SANS London understand that the community of likeminded
individuals provides an atmosphere and possibilities for interaction, and
for first time students, SANS can assist them in deciding which training
streams can best help them to achieve their own personal goals, career
development path and top tier industry qualifications.
