Howdy application security community!


After the first chapter meeting in Ankara, Turkey on 6th of May, 2007, we
proudly announce the second meeting, which happen to be a small size
conference* packed mainly with technical presentations including security
best practices, fresh new attack and of course testing tools and a demo of
relatively old but perhaps unappreciated vulnerability.

 Having Gelisim Platformu <http://www.gelisimplatformu.org/> as the sponsor,
this time the *free* "small conference" will be held in Istanbul, Turkey on
14th of July, 2007 between 11:00 and 18:00.

 Above all, OWASP chief evangelist Dinis Cruz will be presenting the OWASP
world to Turkish application security community.

 For OWASP-Turkey chapter page and for meeting details go to:
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Turkey

 For OWASP-Turkey upcoming events and community interaction register at:

http://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-turkey

For further inquiries please contact:

Ferruh Maviruna <ferruh at mavituna.com>


* most of the talks will be in Turkish, however, we are sure there will be
participants willing to help in translation in person, so don't be shy and
participate J
