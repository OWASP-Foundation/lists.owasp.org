Dallas OWASP Chapter,

Just a friendly reminder that next week is our monthly meeting and we'll have Peter Perfetti speaking on "Breaking the Paradigm of AppSec as a Disruptive Cost Center".

Peter will share his experience and lessons learned to ease the burden of performing enterprise-wide application security programs, while still maintaining the same or better level of results.

We will meet at SMU on 4/6/2011 from 11:30am - 1:00pm in the Atrium A&B rooms of the Hughes-Trigg Student Center (Campus Map at http://smu.edu/maps/flash/)

If you plan to attend please register at:  http://owaspdallasmtg.eventbrite.com/

Please Note:  We have not been able to secure a sponsor for the lunch this month, so please bring a sack lunch if you would like.  If you or your company would be interested in sponsoring this meeting, or a meeting in the future please contact me directly.  


Thanks, and I hope to see many of you there
