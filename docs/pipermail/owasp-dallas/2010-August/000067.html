Please RSVP: OWASPDallas at utdallas.edu<mailto:OWASPDallas at utdallas.edu>



Date:  Tuesday, September 21, 2010 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM



Topic:  Securing the Smart Grid's Software



Presenter: Andy Bochman is Energy Security Lead for IBM's Rational division, where the focus is on securing the software that runs the Smart Grid. Location:  CoreLogic, 1 CoreLogic Drive, Westlake, TX 76262  Map to follow.
