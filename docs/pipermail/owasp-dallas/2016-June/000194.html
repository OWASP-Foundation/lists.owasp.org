Hi all

I wanted to send out another reminder about how you can help the OWASP
Dallas chapter:

   1. *Volunteer to speak.*  We love to have folks from the local chapter
   talk share their security knowledge with the rest of us.  I'm sure that
   there is a lot that we can learn from one another... don't be shy!
   2. *Volunteer to host a meeting. * If your company has room to
   accommodate about 30 people (perhaps even more based on our last meeting),
   a projector, and a willingness to support the local app sec community, then
   please let us know.  This is an easy way to expose developers to security
   that otherwise might not come out to a meeting.
   3. *Sponsor meeting food & drinks.*  We typically order pizza and sodas
   for our meetings and it is usually around $250.  A fed OWASP community is a
   happy one :)

Please take a moment to consider if you can help in any of the
above-mentioned ways.  We are also open to comments and suggestions, so
please feel free to send those along if you have any too.

I will be sending out the July meeting announcement tomorrow.

Thanks!
Denis
