A quick note to share some recent announcements:


   - Our meeting this month will be on Tuesday, June 20th at Toyota's

   https://www.meetup.com/Dallas-The-Open-Web-Application-Security-Project-Meetup/events/240487098/

   Analysis" present by Mark Szewczul.  Please RSVP here:
   https://www.meetup.com/Dallas-The-Open-Web-Application-Security-Project-Meetup/events/240488143/


   - The slides from the April meeting presentation "AWS Security: Staying
   on Top of the Cloud" have been posted to the OWASP Dallas wiki.  Here's the
   URL: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Dallas

   same security issues.  I've gone the past 2 years and it's been a blast.
   Full details here: https://2017.appsecusa.org/


   - I'm working filling out the meeting schedule for the end of the year.
