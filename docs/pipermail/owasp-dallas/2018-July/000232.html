Hi everyone.  A friend of my has a remote information security job
opening.  Below is the information.  If you are interested in the position
tell Chris that you are from OWASP Dallas and Matt sent you.

Matt

*Top Skills:*

1)      Splunk experience – Event monitoring, experience designing and
managing Splunk dashboards, reports, lookup tables and summary indexes.
Regular expression experience as well.

2)      Broad experience across Operating Systems (OS) - specifically with
Windows AND Linux, must have both not one or the other, experience
maintaining and supporting them

3)      Broad experience across networking appliances, specifically with
Firewalls, Routers, Proxies, and Networking gateways, implemented,
maintained and supported these

4)      Knowledge and understanding of data security controls including
malware protection, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, content
filtering, Internet proxies, encryption controls, and log management
solutions

5)      DPI (Deep Packet Inspection) and/or full packet capture solutions
experience



*Other nice to have skills:*

- Experience writing and supporting any sort of software, website, or other
custom programming base. These specifically: web (php, jsp, .net, html),
and/or scripting (python, vbscript, perl), or any regular programming
(Java, C#, C++, C, Visual Basic).
