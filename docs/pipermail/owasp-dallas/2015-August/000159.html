Hi all

We are excited to announce a September meeting on Application Security
Trends hosted at the Goldman Sachs office in Irving.  We have another
meeting scheduled for October (more details can be found at the OWASP
Dallas site <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Dallas>).  We are hoping to
squeeze in 1 more event -- most likely a social gathering -- before the end
of the year too.

There will be pizza served at the September meeting so *please RSVP via our
Meetup page
<http://www.meetup.com/Dallas-The-Open-Web-Application-Security-Project-Meetup/>*
so we can get an accurate headcount.

Full details for the September meeting can be found below.

Thanks!
Denis Sheridan

===

*SEPTEMBER OWASP DALLAS MEETING*

*When:* Thursday September 17th, 2015 from 5:30PM – 6:30 PM

*Topic: *Application Security Trends

*Who:* Stephen Pasco is a Vice President of Application Risk at Goldman
Sachs based in Irving where he is currently focused on vulnerability
testing. Prior to joining Goldman Sachs Stephen was employed at Disney on
the Security and Compliance Team where he performed security/application
vulnerability assessments. He also architected and developed security
tools, wrote policies, and managed software security fixes. One aspect of
that job was to frequently visit Disneyland parks searching for WiFi
vulnerabilities.

*Where:* 6011 Connection Drive, Irving, TX 75039. Volunteers will be onsite
to escort you from the lobby to the meeting room.
