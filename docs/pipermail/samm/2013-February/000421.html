> The Network of Excellence on Engineering Secure Future
> Internet Software Services and Systems (NESSoS) is an on-going EU project
> aiming  at constituting and integrating a long lasting research community
> on engineering secure software-based services and systems.
> This project (see http://www.nessos-project.eu/) is now entering in its
> third year and we are looking for new members of industrial advisory board
> (IAB).****
>
> ** **
>
> Description of NESSOS IAB:
>
> The members of the Advisory Board will receive periodically (approx once
> every 3 months) an e-mail with an update of the status of the project,
> relevant events, etc.
>
> The members of the Advisory Board are welcome, although not obliged, to
> comment on issues such as the research roadmap, strategy or potential
> direction of the project.
>
> The members of the Advisory Board will be invited to NESSOS Annual Forum
> events and their travel and hotel costs will be reimbursed by the project
> coordinator.
>
> The members of the Advisory board act with the knowledge and experience of
> the positions they occupy in their companies/institutions, but their
> comments and opinions will only be interpreted as their personal ones (and
> not reflecting their current companies/institutions).****
