Dear all,

As a result of the Hamburg workshop a list of actions was created.
I have turned these in a list of work packages where you can contribute
according to your available cycles.

The list is online at:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AocY8G2SHaWKdHhKZXdnek1hcFFRTWhzN1NRQWd0d1E&usp=sharing#gid=0

You can also find this link on the wiki via:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Category:Software_Assurance_Maturity_Model#tab=Project

The first sheet "Work Packages" contains the current list of activities.
The second sheet "Guidance" provides some guidelines on the Work Package
follow-up.

A separate list of potential SAMM changes, improvements and fixes is still
to be created (WP.7).
Once we decide on the implementation of proposed SAMM changes, these will
be transferred to the Work Package list.

As you will see, there are quite some Work Packages that need some love and
an owner, ranging from 4 hours to 5 days of estimated work.

If you want to take ownership of one of these items, do NOT hesitate to do
so and in parallel contact me if you need any help to get you started.
Completed work packages will result in you being recognised as a
contributor to the next SAMM version 1.1.

Do not hesitate to contact me - or any of the other co-project leaders -
with your questions and/or remarks!

Thank you,

Kind regards,

Seba
