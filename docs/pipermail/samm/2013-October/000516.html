Regularly I get asked: "who actually uses OpenSAMM"?

>From my personal experience I know quite a lot of organisations that do,
but it would be great if we could point interested people to our list of
OpenSAMM adopters:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OpenSAMM_Adopters

Currently the page lists 6 organisations that testify their usage of SAMM.

I am sure there are more, much more!
The latest stats from the opensamm.org web site show more than 150 unique
visitors per day.
That is more than 50,000 unique visitors per year, not counting the OWASP
SAMM wiki page.

Therefor I ask YOUR help.

If your organisation uses SAMM, or you know of an organisation that uses
SAMM:
ask them to come forward and be listed on the OpenSAMM adopter page?

All we need is:
1) Organization Name
2) Contact
3) Role
4) Organization Type
5) Region
6) Testimonial (optional, but would be great)

If they agree, you can add them to the wiki page yourself.
If you do not have a wiki account, you can also forward the information to
me: I will post it for you.

If each of us adds one or more SAMM users, we will grow this list to
hundreds of public references!

Thank you,
