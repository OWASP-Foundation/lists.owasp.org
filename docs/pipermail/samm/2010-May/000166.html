Hi,

 

The point of Rugged is to make appsec into something that developers
*want* to know and practice.  In my experience, organizations that have
achieved this mindset have dramatically more successful programs.
Programs that primarily focus on making developers feel bad are doomed
to failure.

 

I respectfully suggest that changing the culture and the mindset of an
organization is a critical component of an appsec maturity model. There
are certainly activities and processes that can be put in place to help
achieve this.  If that's something you'd like to work on in SAMM, I'm
ready to help.

 

--Jeff
