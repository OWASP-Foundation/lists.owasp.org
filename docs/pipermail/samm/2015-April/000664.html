Jerome,

there is no such thing as a standard process template within OpenSAMM. It
often depends on the level of granularity that you need, and what your (or
the target) company is already using in development.

If you have a clean sheet to start from, you might want to look into (from
higher level to more detailed): BPMN, Data flow diagrams, UML collaboration
diagrams, or similar. Google, Visio or other modeling tools will be able to
support you in finding a way to create them.

HTH,
Bart.
