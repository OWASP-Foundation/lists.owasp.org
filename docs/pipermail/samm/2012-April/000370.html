Actually this is a great presentation - and I would love to discuss it
more; I tried to download your models - but they don't work on linux or mac.

I always assume 100% compromise. This is because *zero* day exploits are
for sale, and attackers have them. You can not defend against the future
nor uncertainty. Thus the question isn't "if you get hacked", its "when you
get hacked" can you detect it and how do you manage it.
