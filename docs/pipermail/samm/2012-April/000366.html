I'm not sure I followed that entirely, but you're muddling the issues of what's the right granularity of *collection* of data vs *presentation* of data. Most of my comments concern the former as this is, in my opinion, far more fundamentally important since if you have garbage data, no degree of presentation magic makes it useful.
