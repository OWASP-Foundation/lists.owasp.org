I am in agreement completely on the presentation of (specific) data which
we should define as its "view" as each audience is not presented with the
entire dataset e.g. "Executive view", "Application Development Manager
view", "Developer view", "Application Security view", etc

For processes that are purely technical, such as the fuzzing example I used
below, we need to specify how they can be measured objectivity and
not subjectivity (at least not without a disclaimer of the subjective
measurement and how that may skew the objective measure).
