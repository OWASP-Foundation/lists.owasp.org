EG3 and PC3 are considered "Related Levels" i.e. p45 EG3 is related to
"Policy & Compliance - 2 & 3" and p41 PC3 is related to "Education &
Guidance - 3"

I believe the reason as they are listed as different "Security
Practices" is that conformance to a regulatory authority (e.g. PCI
DSS, SOX, etc) is not required by every organization.
