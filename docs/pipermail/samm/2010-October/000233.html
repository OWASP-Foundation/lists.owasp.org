James

Dinis Cruz spoke with people from ENISA and the Cloud Security
Alliance at OWASP AppSec Iberia 2009, and subsequentlyt OWASP were
asked to contribute to the development of a "Common Assurance Maturity
Model (CAMM)".  This is an ongoing effort:

   http://www.common-assurance.com/

   http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Industry:ENISA_Cloud_Computing_Common_Assurance_Metrics

We were asked to provide objectives and controls for the "development
and acquisition" sub-domain linked to other standards such as
ISO27001:2007, PCIDSS 1.2, COBIT 4.1 and SANS Top 25.  We actually
based our input primarily around SAMM, and also looked at BSIMM and
SAFEcode for any gaps.  The structure, wording and actual focus is
subject to change and is being decided by others (reviewers and
steering committee), but we have made a start at integrating SAMM.
