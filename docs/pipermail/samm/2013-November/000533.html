The quickstart guide isn't meant to describe a different kind of
implementation - its purpose is to give a reader some guideposts to build
on.

I imagine a person coming to SAMM not knowing where to begin. The
Quickstart guide is meant to help them get a quick understanding of what
SAMM offers. Once they know have this basic understanding, they would go on
to the more in-depth materials.
