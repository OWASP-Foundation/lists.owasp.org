There are several of us who may use SAMM to evaluate projects produced
by OWASP organization in the near future.  As part of that effort, I
created a SAMM Interview Template.  This template uses the original SAMM
self-assessment questions as a base, but expands on these items to
include detailed assertions providing further validation of software
security activities or processes.

Please let me know if you have any feedback or suggestions.

The template can be found on the following page:
http://nickcoblentz.blogspot.com/2009/06/samm-inteview-template-version-10.html
