Hi all, we've put together an audit framework based on the efforts of
a couple of others on this mailing and SAMM as well as our own
experience. We wanted to frame it in the language of controls auditing
so that we can start to push it to the people who have the power to
require it (i.e.controls auditor). We liked and built off the efforts
of Nick Coblentz's SAMM Assessment Interview Template
(http://spreadsheets.google.com/pub?key=rYpVqQR3026Zu4DNg8LBIwg&gid=0)
and Christian Frichot's SAMM-Assessment Worksheet -  both available
int the archives of this mailing list.

Ours is slightly more targeted to an external auditor and is based on
our experience of assessing SDLCs in the real world.

You can find a short write-up and link to the actual framework here:
http://labs.securitycompass.com

I'd love to hear any feedback you have
