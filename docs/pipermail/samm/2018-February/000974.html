Hi,

The OWASP Northern Virginia donated 5.000 USD to the SAMM project.
We would like to thank the chapter leaders Abdullah Manuwar and Ben Pick
for sponsoring us!

We will put this to good use to further develop SAMM towards V2 and make it
available to the community. All proceeds from the sponsorship support the
mission of the OWASP Foundation and the further development of SAMM.
Supporting the project drives the funding for research grants, SAMM
hosting, tools, templates, documents, promotion, and more.

You can also sponsor SAMM. You will not only support an important and
flagship OWASP project, you will also get visibility during the next SAMM
Summit (part of the Open Security Summit 2018), recognition on the OWASP
SAMM project web site and the next release of SAMM (version 2.0).

For more information: Download our SAMM2 sponsorship brochure here:
https://www.owasp.org/images/f/fb/OWASP_SAMM2_Sponsorship_Form_v20170212.pdf

Activate your sponsorship by contacting me (seba at owasp.org +32.478.504.117)

Thank you,

Kind regards,

Seba
