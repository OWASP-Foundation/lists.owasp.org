Hi,


The monthly calls are really used to track & discuss the V1.2 progress.


In the mean time we will schedule extra SAMMv2 calls, every 4th Wednesday
of the month.

We will start with discussing all the security practices of the core model.

Objective is to update the core model towards SAMMv2.

First call is next Wednesday!

I have created a work document for improvements on the first practice
(Strategy & Metrics):

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dWa8z-qVYGiOQBto5eOL03I4LqAVmK9r-2lnJiDm-vw/edit?usp=sharing
It contains the notes of our discussions during the NY summit.

I have expanded this with:

   - Guidance on how to review & improve the practice
   - Some general principles to keep in mind:
   - Improvement placeholders


Here is your homework :-)

1) first READ the current practice
2) REVIEW the summit notes
3) THINK :-) and review how you apply this practice in your
team/organisation
4) if you have improvement suggestions: ADD them to the working document
(use the template)
5) if you can make it: JOIN our call next Wednesday (28-Sep at 21h30 CEST /
3:30pm EST.)

After the call, we will share our notes for review and work towards an
improved practice for the next SAMMv2 call (and at the same time start
working on the next practice ...)

This is your change to influence and improve our core SAMM model!

Thank you,

Kind regards,
