Dear all,

A couple of weeks ago Bart De Win and myself delivered a well-received
OWASP SAMM 1-Day training at AppSec Europe 2014 in Cambridge:
Slide deck download:
https://www.owasp.org/images/d/df/OpenSAMM_Training_vFINAL.pptx
Training description download:
https://www.owasp.org/images/7/7c/Training_-_Bootstrap_and_improve_your_SDLC_with_OpenSAMM.docx

We had 6 attendees with very good feedback on the training.
We encourage you to reuse the material and hope you contribute back any
improvements you might have.

Furthermore Bart and I have donated our cut of the training profit (1500
EUR) to the OWASP SAMM project. These funds are most welcome to improve
SAMM to version 1.1 and organize a dedicated SAMM summit (planned for Q1
2015, detailed timing and venue to be announced).

Lastly, if you like or use SAMM: please vote it back to Flagship status by
filling in the following form:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?fromEmail=true&formkey=dHBMYjdpZzVXaFJjTWgwVzdOdTJCbEE6MA
(Vote for Flagship - OWASP Document Projects)

This will take you less than 1 minute!

Thank you very much,

Kind regards
