Dear,

SAMM 1.0 provides four Template Roadmaps and one 12-page Case Study

The 1-Page Template Roadmaps cover example roadmaps for the following
organisations:

   - Independent Software Provider
   - Online Service Provider
   - Financial Services Provider
   - Government Organisation


The 12-page Case Study details the phased approach of a SAMM based
assurance program for a Medium-sized Independent Software Vendor.

One of the SAMM 1.1 improvements is to add extra guidance with additional
use cases and/or templates.
We have 2 questions for you:

1) Your input/requirements - deadline 10-Dec-2014
What are your expectations with regards to the content and format of
additional use cases?
Do you want more/less detail in a use case?
Would you benefit from additional templates, lessons learned?
What is your challenge with regards to starting or maintaining a software
assurance program?

2) Your contribution - donation for SAMM 1.1 - deadline 27-Mar-2015
Can you share your experience with SAMM for your assurance program? What
worked or what did not work?
We can anonymise your input if necessary.
Would you be willing to share this as a testimonial or in a roundtable
session at the upcoming SAMM Summit in Dublin (27-Mar-2015)?

Please respond to the mailing list or myself?

Thank you!

Kind regards
