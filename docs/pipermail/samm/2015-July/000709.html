You have probably seen the first communications around OpenSAMM Benchmark (
http://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/samm/2015-April/000657.html)

additional data sets to include in the first release.

following template:
https://github.com/OWASP/opensamm/blob/master/v1.1/OpenSAMM_DataCollectionSpreadsheet_v3.xlsx

You can send this to me or Dan Cornell (dan AT denimgroup DOT com)

or on the process, do not hesitate to contact Dan or me.
