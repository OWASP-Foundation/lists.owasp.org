Hi Kimberly,

To my knowledge, nobody has combined everything under one roof, but I 
think that's largely because sw assurance would be a sub-component of an 
overall framework. As such, it should be straightforward to embed SAMM 
directly within the program.

As an aside, if you don't mind my saying, C2M2 is an odd choice for a 
security vendor to choose since it was specifically written for the 
energy sector (I was a contributor to the original version). NIST CSF 
directly derives from the work that went into C2M2 and is in general a 
much better match for non-energy orgs (Robb I would think knows this, 
too). Unfortunately, CSF is almost completely silent on appsec/sw 
assurance and isn't perhaps as good a match.

Anyway, fwiw, tldr; is that you should be able to stick SAMM into 
whatever framework you choose, then would just have to figure out how to 
align scoring.

cheers,

-ben
