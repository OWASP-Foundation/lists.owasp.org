I am working for Ping Identity on a project to implement maturity models
for our organization. I have selected SAMM to use to assess the secure
coding practices of each of our products and then have adapted the C2M2
cybersecurity maturity model into the SAMM toolkit for evaluating the
maturity of our enterprise security program. This is allowing me to
integrate the data from both systems for reporting and analysis purposes.

I'm wondering if anyone has attempted something similar? Since our
department is ultimately responsible for enterprise, infrastructure and
product security, we needed a way to show the maturity of our programs as a
whole as well as break thing down. Consequently, a consistent scoring
system across models was important.

Has anyone combined models before? Or customized models to fit their
organization's unique security programs? I've also adapted the toolkit to
provide retrospective reporting in addition to the roadmap, and wondered
whether anyone has also made such modifications?

Thank you for the toolkit materials - very well done! I am new to this list
and looking forward to being an active part of the community.
