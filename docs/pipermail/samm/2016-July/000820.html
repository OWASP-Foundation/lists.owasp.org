>hi,
>
>One of the summit outcomes was the following list of improvements to
>incorporate in v1.2 of SAMM:
>
>Focus is on updated scoring:
>
>   1. Recover source and move it to an asciidoctor based document for
>   better reproduction capabilities
>   2. Clarification of maturity levels (syntactic changes to keep the text
>   consistent)
>   3. Not change activities but try to impose the current scoring system on
>   existing activities, i.e. move from binary yes/no to the multi-tiered
>   questions/answers of the current proposal.
>   4. Show improvements with every activity introduced
>   5. Adapt documents/toolbox for the new scoring method
>   6. Update questions for 4-tiers
>   7. Review and where necessary clarify current questions
>   8. Consider v1.1 remarks/comments that were not withheld for the
>   previous release
>
>Targeted completion date: end of september in time for Appsec USA (October
>11, 2016)
>
>Are you ok with this list?
>Any strong feelings to include/drop requirements you want to share?
>
>Your input will be taken into account for the next monthly call on 13-July
>
>Thanks
