We start a series of preparation calls to prepare the SAMM Summit (part of
the upcoming OWASP Summit in June - owaspsummit.org)

Main objective of these preparation calls is to discuss & prepare our
summit working sessions to efficiently use our face to face time during the
summit.

One of the first topics is the Core model - and what to update for v2.0.

I have listed the main points of our core model discussions here:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1kWYlVoOY99W4MUyEimWXSV-wi77LaESxmTb6DEHf0mE/edit

I invite you to read these before our extra call on 26-April (in 2 weeks).

discuss this in our mail list
