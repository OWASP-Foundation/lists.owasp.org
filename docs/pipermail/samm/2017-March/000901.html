Hello Board members

After talking a couple of times with the OWASP summit organizers, and
seeing the huge interest in companies to implement and improve their DevOps
Security,  I mentioned why not rebrand the summit as

*OWASP DevOps Security Summit: *
*OWASP projects meets DevOpsSec*

The major reason is that for many people OWASP projects are an unknown
thing, For most of the projects, with exception of a few like  ZAP , many
people do not know how to apply these projects or use them in their SDLC or
even better, creating DevOps Security pipeline.

Rebranding it in this way will allow the summit to create more interest and
awareness, as people attending the summit but also for Project leaders to
be able to share their project within these process and show hands on
implementation.

This branding is neutral as this is not a DevOpsCon, but how to apply OWASP
projects in a DevOps process to create continuos delivery.

*Please, could you let us know your opinion on this, as we then can rebrand
the summit and market it in a way that is also appealing to folks looking
on how to put DevOps in practice?*

The concept consist in having tracks and Villas that actually match the
DevOps security process/pipelines where projects leaders attending are set
