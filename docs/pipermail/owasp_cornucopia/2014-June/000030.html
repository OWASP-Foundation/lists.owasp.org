RSVP by 24 June

As a shortlisted entrant you are entitled to three free tickets for

All proceeds from additional ticket sales will be donated to Nominet's

Information needed from you

1.       Please can you send us a 50 word “elevator pitch”  that sums

on the evening.  Please send no later than Friday 27 June.

2.       If you have a twitter account if would be great if you could
promote the awards and provide us with your details so that we can
follow you and re-tweet. We can be found @Nominet and are using the
hash tag #nia2014.

3.       Please check your initiative entry details and logo are

brochures post event.

Parliamentary reception – 9 July (4pm-6pm)

As part of the celebrations of this year’s awards we will also be
holding a reception in Parliament on 9 July. This event will provide

varied work they do online and celebrate the internet as an important
force for good. Drinks and food will also be on hand at what we are
sure will be an exciting and enjoyable event.
