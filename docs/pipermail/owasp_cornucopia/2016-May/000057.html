The current printed decks available from the OWASP Merchandise Store
are v1.04 - apart from a few minor wording changes, the primary
difference between 1.04 and 1.10 is the version of ASVS in the
mappings. The latest printed decks available from OWASP are apparently
on better quality card, and can be obtained in bulk (300 in stock now,
but going quickly). Individual packs of v1.10 are also available from
Blackfoot free of charge, but they do not have the OWASP branded box.

   https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Cornucopia#tab=Get_the_Cards

We expect printed decks of v1.20 in both English and Spanish to be
available from the OWASP Merchandise Store later in 2016.
