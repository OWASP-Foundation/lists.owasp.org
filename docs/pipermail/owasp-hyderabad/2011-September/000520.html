Dear Chapter Members

As part of our chapter building program and efforts, we have thought it
prudent that all presentations are to be uploaded on the local chapter wiki
(after due diligence by Core Team Members), to serve the following
purposes:-

1. Giving a bird's eye view of happenings @ the local chapter;
2. Giving the community back by putting the presentations online;
3. Visibility to the presenters who take the pain of preparing and giving
presentations to the community, thereby sharing the knowledge.

We request all the members who have talked @ our chapter meetings, to mail
their presentations to the following email ID (
owasp-core-team-hyd at googlegroups.com), along-with a short introduction of
the author. We will, after some small checks, upload the same onto the OWASP
Hyderabad Wiki.

Regards
M S Sripati
