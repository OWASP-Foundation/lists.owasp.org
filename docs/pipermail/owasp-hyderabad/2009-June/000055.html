null proudly introduces  it's first WildHack Contest "wh[0x01]"

-------
who
-------
null is a non-profit, open security community which strives to research and
spread security awareness.
website: http://null.co.in


--------
what
---------
wh[0x01] (WildHack Contest) is the first in the series of null hacking
challenges and contests.

Rush in your submissions at submit_at_null.co.in and win exciting
prizes/null credits.

The best of the lot will also be considered for the soon  to be held
security conference - nullcon.

For details about contest:
http://null.co.in/2009/06/14/wh0x01-wildhack-contest/

mailing list on 20th July 09.
