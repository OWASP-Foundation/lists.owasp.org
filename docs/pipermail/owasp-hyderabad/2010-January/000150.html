Dear Chapter Members,

It  is  indeed  a  pleasure  to  inform  you  that  Shri R. Chandrashekhar,
Secretary,   Department   of  Information  Technology  (DIT),  Ministry  of
Communications  and Information Technology (MCIT), Govt. of India, released
the  report  of the recently conducted Survey on Data Security and Privacy.
The survey was conducted by DSCI through KPMG, in association with CERT-In,
under the Cyber Security Awareness Project sponsored by DIT, MCIT, Govt. of
India.  The  survey  covered  153  leading companies from sectors including
Banking  &  Financial  Services,  Manufacturing,  E-Commerce,  IT  &  ITES,
Telecom, and PSUs.

The objective of Data Security survey was to:

      1.       Assess the importance given to information security and data
      privacy
      2.        Capture  the leading practices and/or standards adopted for
      Information Security and Data Privacy in organizations
      3.        Identify the major sources of data leakage, compromising of
      data privacy, in organizations
      4.        Study  the  status  of  Information  and  Physical Security
      Convergence.
      5.        Awareness about the Information Technology (Amendment) Act,
      2008, and interface with Cert-In when incidents happen

You can view or download the survey report from the following link:
http://www.dsci.in/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=76&Itemid=95
. Please feel free to get in touch with us for any clarification that you
may require.
