Below is a short intro about our Managing Director Isaac Rajkumar.

Isaac Rajkumar is Managing Director, OpenText, India. Currently his
responsibilities include product development and India operations general
management.

During his career spanning over 24 years, he has been involved in
technology development, management and leadership roles in Enterprise
Software areas. He has extensively worked at both India and the US, and was
instrumental in setting up OpenText Hyderabad Centre and in developing a
strong ecosystem in Enterprise Information Management (EIM).

Isaac has an Executive MBA from ISB, Hyderabad; MS in Computer Science from
Temple University, Philadelphia and B.E. (Honours) in Electrical and
Electronics Engineering from BITS, Pilani. Isaac is a co-inventor in 7
patents awarded by the United States Patent Office.
