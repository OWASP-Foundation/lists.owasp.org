Hi!
I’m at Monterrey, México and i want to contribute with something...
I think it’s time to get up to do something, like security chats, sharing
our experiences... what do you have in mind people?

About the PHP topic: I know one of the leaders of one of the PHP user groups
here in Monterrey, and they are organizing PHP chats and hands-on training
courses here in Monterrey as a part of the FLISOL Mty 2011... it is going to
be a huge event... also in the same FLISOL, the AMSI Group it’s going to
give some chats in the Information Security area.

And it is not even the end. We, the “Grupo de Usuarios Linux de la
Universidad Regiomontana" (we are the guys behind this project)  are pushing
the initiative of integrate the participation of the biggest universities of
the north of the country, Universidad Autonoma de Nuevo Leon, Instituto
Tecnologico de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey, Universidad De Monterrey
and La Universidad Regiomontana, to inject more human resources, fresh
ideas, and the most important thing, to break the walls who for years had
been separating our integration as a information technologies professionals.
Also, we’re setting up an TEDx to bring a space to all those people who want
a place to share their projects, so I think it is going to be a worthwhile
weekend.

Hope we can do something like that or join the FLISOL to make it even
bigger.
