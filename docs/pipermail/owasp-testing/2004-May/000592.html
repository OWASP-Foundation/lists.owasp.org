> Hi folks,
> 
> i was working through the items in the pen-test list, and ran across the 
> following contradiction:
> 
> OWASP-AD-002 Application Lockout
> 
> Ensure that the application does not allow an attacker to reset or lock 
> out users accounts.
> 
> and
> 
> OWASP-AUTHN-007 Password lockout
> 
> Ensure that the user's account is locked out for a period of time when 
> the incorrect password is entered more than a speciifc number of times.
> 
> Seems to me you can have either/or, but not both.
> 
> I would be inclined to remove OWASP-AD-002, because it should really be 
> covered by OWASP-AUTHN-004 (non-trivial/wallet usernames) and 
> OWASP-AUTHN-008 (login and recovery process does not allow for user 
> enumeration).
> 
> Thoughts?
