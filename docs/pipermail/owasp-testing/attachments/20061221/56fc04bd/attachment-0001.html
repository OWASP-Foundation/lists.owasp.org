<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">I'm all for a very simple system, but a one-size-fits-all model is unrealistic. One simple example is that corporate LAN applications are frequently the most critical applications, but the model below minimizes their importance.&nbsp; I believe we should develop an OWASP framework with a way for organizations to tailor it – so that it produces the right risk ratings for them.
</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">I suggest we start with the standard risk model….</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;Risk = Likelihood * Impact</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">There are a number of factors that go into likelihood and other factors that go into impact.&nbsp; Organizations can customize a standard risk ranking framework by weighting these factors according to their business.
</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Likelihood factors generally break down into information about the threat agent, and information about the vulnerability:</span></font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 24pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><span>-<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font>
<font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Threat agent</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Skill level required</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Threat agent motivation (attractiveness of target)</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Threat agent access level</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Threat agent size</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">…</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 24pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><span>-<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font>
<font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Vulnerability</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Ease of discovery</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Awareness</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Tools available</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Mitigating controls in place</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Accountability (not logged, logged w/o review, logged and reviewed)</span></font></p>
<p style="MARGIN-LEFT: 60pt; TEXT-INDENT: -0.25in"><font face="Courier New" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><span>o<font face="Times New Roman" size="1"><span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></font></span></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2">
<span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">…</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Impact is generally calculated based on annualized loss expectancy (ALE). Businesses should create a standard for what dollar amounts are significant to their business and establish some levels for Impact. Understanding the assets and functions involved, and the importance of confidentiality, integrity, and availability to the business is critical to getting good estimates of the real business impact. Reputation damage is frequently the driver here.
</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Since none of these factors are very easy to measure, it's best to have enumerated lists (like accountability above) for each factor that make sense for the particular business.
</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">The real tailoring comes from weighting these factors according to your business. &nbsp;Having a risk ranking framework that's customizable for a business is critical for adoption. &nbsp;Otherwise, you'll spend lots of time arguing about the risk ratings that are produced.
</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">&nbsp;</span></font></p>
<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial">Based on the factors and weights, you calculate whether the likelihood is L, M, or H and whether the impact is L, M, or H. &nbsp;Then you can calculate the overall risk with a 9-box.
</span></font></p>
