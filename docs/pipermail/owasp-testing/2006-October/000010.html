Sounds very promising but take into account that the Autumn of Code project is of a finite time.

It is important to consider:

In order to re-write all the chapters AND add the required new content shall take some time. The completion date is the 31st December 2006 which gives us about 11 weeks, not much time from my experience of developing the existing Testing guide. 

It would be better not to take too much on and miss the completion date. The guide shall be ever evolving and the aim of this AoC project is to consolidate the existing guide and NOT to perform a complete rewrite.
