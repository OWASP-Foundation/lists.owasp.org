Hi,

I started looking at Chapter 4 in line with Dave's original "release1.zip"
mail of 21/3 & the release deadline of today.  Luckily, you guys are 5 to 8
hours behind, so my 11th hour response is a little earlier in your day!

I agree that chapters 3 and 4 have some crossover - particularly in choosing
the appropriate testing methodology.  

For example, an external audit driven requirement may require specific
targeted areas to be assessed from an operational perspective in order to
attain a certification or licence.  An application assessment driven by a
due-diligence desire may have a much broader scope.

Perhaps Chapter 3 should evolve into 'why assess?' and 'how to assess'
sections.  "How" would clearly be derived from "Why", and then lead into
"How" in the next chapter - "The OWASP methodology".

However the structure settles, I have attached updated drafts of the
existing Chapter 4 - The OWASP Methodology.

The intent with this draft is to provide a structure for future debate,
based on the existing guide draft, whilst allowing for plenty of fleshing
out as time goes on.  ** It is destined and expected to change in response
to debate in the list **

For example, the "Application Implementation Review and Testing" is clearly
a broad subject at this stage, and will undoubtedly yield as many
sub-headings as the preceding phases put together.

Comments craved,
Glyn.

PS. Apologies for the multi-format post, but there seem to be a few
different user requirements out there!
