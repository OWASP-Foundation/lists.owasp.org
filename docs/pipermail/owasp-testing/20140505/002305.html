>
> So I gave a workshop to the teams on using ASVS and mentioned OWASP RRM for use in reporting. RRM is great for us because our reports are mostly used internally or to help the 'risk' discussion with customers when a vuln is in third party software.
>
> However a participant noticed that the risk was lower when the required skill level was high, and low when the required skill level was low. This seems very strange as it's almost certain that there are far more low skilled than highly skilled attackers.
