 Hi,
So far, so good as the man says....

The OWASP Testing guide is shaping up real well.
Many documents are of high quality and new sections have been introduced
such as the Oracle testing section complements of NGS (David Litchfield),
thanks to our  tech lead Matteo!!!

We now have a final push to complete the guide in its current form by the
31/12/2006. This is the end date for the AoC project.
So,.....
If there is any documentation left to do can you dedicate a couple of hours
to finish your doc off?

If you have a couple of hours can you read an unreviewed document? Review
status can be found here:
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Testing_Guide_v2_Review_Panel

I'd like to thank Matteo for his great work so far in the Autumn of
Code lead role and also all of you who have taken time and effort to
contribute and update to the new Testing guide.

Once the AoC is over please feel free to continue to contribute to the guide
as this is an ever changing/organic document.

In the new year we hope to integrate the guide more closely with some of the
new projects, one idea is to integrate the testing guide with the ORG (OWASP
Report Generator).
