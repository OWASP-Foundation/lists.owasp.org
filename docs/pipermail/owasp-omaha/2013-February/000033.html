Good morning, and welcome to the OWASP Omaha chapter reboot!

During our first "official" meeting we will introduce chapter leadership and share the vision for Omaha's OWASP.


Thu Mar 7, 2013 - Welcome to OWASP Omaha!
-Presenters, OWASP Omaha Chapter Leadership
-Thursday, March 7th, 12:00 noon - 1:00 P.M., Bellevue University
-Durham Student Center (building #6). You can park in Lot D.  Check out the map here: http://www.bellevue.edu/about/content/images/map-of-main-campus.jpg.

This event is open to everyone!
RSVP (for numbers only) at http://www.eventbrite.com/event/5509906278.


Directions to the meeting room:

-          Go directly in the doors from Lot D

-          Head straight into the main area of the building

-          Room will be directly on the left.

Food:
There is a cafeteria directly opposite the room which serves a mix of fast food style options with a few healthy choices as well.
