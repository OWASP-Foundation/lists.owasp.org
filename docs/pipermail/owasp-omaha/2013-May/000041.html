Good evening OWASP Omaha!

Our next chapter meeting is coming up, and it will be a good one!  We'll go over some of the threats, demo some tools, and talk about how the OWASP lists and resources can help educate you or your teams in the world of web application security.

                "So  Many Tools, So Little Time" - John Rogers

                12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
                Peter Kiewit Institute on UNO's campus,
                Room 279
Map: http://www.google.com/maps?q=the+peter+kiewit+institute,+omaha&hl=en&ll=41.24743,-96.016447&spn=0.003449,0.008256&sll=41.291859,-96.043406&sspn=0.441107,1.056747&hq=the+peter+kiewit+institute,&hnear=Omaha,+Douglas,+Nebraska&t=m&z=18&iwloc=A


John Rogers is a Senior Application Security Engineer working in the Security Assurance department at Lincoln Financial Group.  Previously John worked as a Lead Security Engineer at ACI Worldwide, Inc.  John is one of the unique Application Security Professionals with over 20 years of experience in all aspects of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) for the Banking, Payment and Financial Services industries.  John is also a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and serves as President of InfraGard Nebraska.

                Food: You are encouraged to brown-bag it, or come a little early and grab something from the Scott Café on the first floor before we start.

                Parking: You can park in the visitor lot (circle drive) out front or in the student lot to the north. (Turn right from the circle drive.)

Can't attend? We are going to try to set up the presentation online again if you can't attend.  Keep watching your email or twitter for more as the event gets closer!



Enjoy the holiday weekend, and see you on June 6th!
