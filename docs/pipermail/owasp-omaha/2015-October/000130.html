Mark your calendars.  Omaha chapter is hosting a joint social / networking

*Oct 29, 4-7pm, Scriptown Brewery, 3922 Farnam St, Omaha, NE 68131*

October is National Cyber Security Awareness month.  OWASP is all about
awareness, so help spread the word about how important those backups and
2-factor authentication methods are to large and small businesses alike.
