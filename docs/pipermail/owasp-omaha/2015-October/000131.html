Good Day OWASP Omaha,

I have an Internship opportunity to pass on. Rob Baldi is looking for an
Intern for IT Internal Audit at ACI Worldwide. The job details are below.

Job# 11530BR



Job Title: IT Internal Audit – Intern

Job Description: The IT Internal Audit - Intern will assist the Director of
IT Internal Audit in evaluating and testing information security controls.
The successful applicant will document the findings of network assessments,
database reviews, information security, application development, backup
processes and other information technology controls to ensure they are
working properly. The intern will come away from this position having
experience conducting information technology audits focused on compliance
and regulatory requirements such as Payment Card Industry (PCI) and
Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX)..



Work Location: Omaha, NE



Minimum Qualifications



• Must be working toward a 4 year degree in Information Technology or
related field

• Must be a Sophomore/Junior/Senior at an accredited college

• Will consider candidates with a 3.0 GPA or above

• Must have good oral and written communications skills

• Must have attention for detail

• Must be able to work independently and in a team environment

• Strong Microsoft Excel familiarity

• Solid background and familiarity with basic Internet Protocol (IP)
networking commiserate with Network+ certification.



Highly Desired Qualifications



• Pursuing BS in computer, cyber, or information security fields.

• Familiarity with computer-assisted audit tools and techniques (CAATTs);
systems used to automate the audit process, i.e. basic office productivity
software and more advanced software packages involving statistical analysis
and business intelligence software



More information can be found here:
