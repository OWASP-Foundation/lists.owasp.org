Please share the message below with your groups / students / friends /
colleagues/ clients. *BBB will waive the $49 charge to any of your contacts
that are not BBB Accredited Businesses. Students can also attend at no
charge!*

On October 6, 2016, the Better Business Bureau is hosting a cybersecurity
program in conjunction with National Cyber Security Awareness Month The
event is from 8 am -10 am at Mammel Hall on UNO’s Aksarben campus at 6708
Pine St. The presenter is Bill Fanelli, Chief Security Officer at  the
Council of Better Business Bureaus.  *5 Steps to Better Business
Cybersecurity**will provide training* to help small and medium size
businesses understand how best to identify and protect their business’s
vital data and technology assets, and how to detect, respond to and recover
from a cybersecurity incident. It was created by Mr. Fanelli with the National
Cyber Security Alliance (NCSA).
