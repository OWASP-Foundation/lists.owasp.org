After long and faithful service, the Mailman service which powers this list is being retired on March 22, 2019.

All is not lost!  The Owasp-modsecurity-core-rule-set list has a new home in Google Groups.

One-click link to join the new group:
https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/#!forum/modsecurity-core-rule-set-project/join

Or send an email to modsecurity-core-rule-set-project+subscribe at owasp.org

The new Group is also available online at:
https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/#!forum/modsecurity-core-rule-set-project

Detailed information on the different ways to join group is at https://drive.google.com/open?id=1TBzgvB8Tb0aAZnEy2qYzLnrzJuRzK_T91Em09DVf5YA

NOTE:  You are NOT required to have a Google account, an @owasp.org email address, or to provide your phone number to join and participate in the new Group at modsecurity-core-rule-set-project at owasp.org - just an email address.

Finally, full details on the plan to retire Mailman, links and FAQs are  at: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Staff-Projects/Mailman-EOL

Thanks for your past and future contributions to our community!
