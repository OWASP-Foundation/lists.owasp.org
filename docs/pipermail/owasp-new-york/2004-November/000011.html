Following up on the well attended first OWASP NYC Local Chapter meeting (October 20th), we are in the process of planning the follow-up.
 
For those planning your 2005 calendar already; the targeted date for the next meeting is Tuesday, January 25th at 6PM.  Thanks to the generosity of Time Inc., this meeting will be hosted at the Time Inc Building (1271 6th Avenue; between 50th & 51st Streets).
 
Please watch the NYC OWASP for the offical meeting announcement and details.
http://www.owasp.org/local/nyc.html
 
Thinking beyond the second meeting, we are looking for future topics of interest and speakers.  We would like our meetings to reflect the interests of our chapter participants.  
 
Therefore, please feel free to email to me topics of interest and if you might like to volunteer to be a presenter.  
 
Thanks for your participation.
