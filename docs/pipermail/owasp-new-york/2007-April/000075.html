April 17th starting at 6:00pm SHARP will be the next NY/NJ Metro OWASP
meeting located at UBS. This is a open-meeting to OWASP members and
non-members!  Doors open at 5:45pm

Confirmed Speakers & Topics Include:

Marc Maiffret, Founder/CTO & Chief Hacking Officer eEye Digital Security
Maiffret will explain the evolution of attack vectors and their impact
on the nature of security, the challenge facing software vendors and
the overall impact this cycle of events has on network security
professionals today

Jeremiah Grossman, Founder/CTO - White Hat
Grossman will demonstrate: How a user is first infected or attacked
using a malicious Web page or Cross-Site Scripting vulnerability; How
a Web browser can be completely controlled or monitored remotely. How
a Web browser can be used as a stepping stone to perform network
reconnaissance on internal networks; and, How to exploit internal
machines using a compromised Web browser

& More!

Full details, directions are at the NY/NJ Metro website

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_Jersey

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_York
