Last nights OWASP NY/NJ Metro meeting was a success, we had over 100
attendees and great content.  We would like to thank the speakers our
host UBS and other co-sponsors for supporting the mission (dedicated
to finding and fighting the causes of insecure software) of OWASP.

Our goal is to always provide educational information that provides
new techniques, skills, and insight into security and last nights
meeting reinforced that concept.

The next meeting is scheduled for JUNE in New York City and the call
for speakers and sponsors is officially NOW OPEN with a deadline of
May 11th.
