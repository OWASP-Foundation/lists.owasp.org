OWASP SunSec - **Wednesday** Nov 3rd  2010 6:30 PM

*SunSec at SunUp*

Please join OWASP Phoenix for an end of year Happy Hour at SunUp Brewing.
It's been a great year for OWASP Phoenix. For November we are going to wind
down with a happy hour celebration. Come out and meet others in the local
security community. Everyone is welcome. 6:30-10:00pm.


*Please note this event is on Wednesday.  **(There is no OWASP meeting on
Tuesday in November.)*

*
*

(Just West of 7th St. and Camelback)
http://www.sunupbrewing.com/location.html



Define: *SunSec* [suhn-sek]

-noun

1) An atmospheric and scenic phenomena accompanying the consumption of adult
beverages and discussion of Information Security.

2) An informal meetup in Greater Phoenix of people interested in
information security. Unlike other meetings, there are no dues, no signup,
and no PowerPoint. Expect to chat about security or anything else over
drinks.
