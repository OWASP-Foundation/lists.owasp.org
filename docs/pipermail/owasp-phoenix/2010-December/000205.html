*Phoenix SecureWorld Expo – a great agenda, a local event and a perfect way
to earn affordable CPE’s!*



We are pleased to support the Phoenix SecureWorld Expo, December 7th & 8th,
2010, at the Phoenix Convention Center.  The agenda is strong; please click
here to view: http://www.secureworldexpo.com/events/index.php?id=285



There are three great options to take advantage of our partnership with
SecureWorld:



1.       *Two-Day Conference Pass* – Phoenix OWASP Members are offered a
$100 discount off the $265 two-day conference pass which includes access to

Certificate of Attendance. Register at www.secureworldexpo.com using code –
PhxOWASP



2.       *SecureWorld+ Training Pass* – Phoenix OWASP Members receive a
special $200 discount off the $695 SecureWorld+ which includes access to the
Conference Sessions, Keynotes, Lunch, Exhibits, Open Sessions and 16 CPE
Certificate of Attendance.  This also includes 4 ½ hours of extended
training on topics such as:

·         Assessing Your Current Security Program

·         Defense Against Social Engineering

Register at www.secureworldexpo.com using code – PhxOWASP



3.       *Exhibits Pass* – Phoenix OWASP Members can also receive
complimentary admission to Keynotes, Exhibits and Open Sessions. Register
at www.secureworldexpo.com using code - PhxOWASP



We look forward to seeing you all at this local and affordable conference.
