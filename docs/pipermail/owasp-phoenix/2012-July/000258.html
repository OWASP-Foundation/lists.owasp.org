OWASP Phoenix invites you to it's August meeting!

Tuesday August 7th  6:30 - 7:30 PM

University of Advancing Technology 2625 W. BASELINE RD. TEMPE, AZ 85283

The meetings are always free, unfortunately the drinks after aren't.

*Defcon Panel Talk*

Every year Defcon some of the greatest talks in the industry, Defcon 20 was
no exception.  However, even though thousands show up, not everyone gets a
chance to go.  This upcoming OWASP is going to feature a panel of locals
who attended Defcon.  They will be reviewing some of the talks the saw and
things they did.  For those who couldn't make it out to Vegas-- this is a
great chance to learn about some new things released this year.


If you attended and would be willing to participate, please let me know
(privately) and I will get you slated for a few minutes to talk.
