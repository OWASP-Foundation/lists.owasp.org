August's HackFest Lab at the Foundation for Blind Children will be a
linux lab demonstration or PuppetFest Theatre.

We will have a one-ish hour of presentation, a puppet test lab
including a fully fleshed out securely setup puppet configuration and
at least two servers. This PuppetFest Theatre will show how easy it is
to pull strings to maintain configurations and standards in any server
farm comprising two or more systems.

Please see http://www.linuxgazette.com for my July "Linux Layer 8
Security" article for lab presentation materials.

Bring your recipes to wow us? Let's see what we can't make puppet do?

All PLUG events are full duplex, allowing for individual expansion and
enrichment as we follow our critical thought, rarely constrained by
anything but time.

"Don't learn to hack; hack to learn".

There is an open call for presentations and participants. Take the
black pill and leave forever the Security Matrix; HackFest with PLUG!

Phoenix.  Entrance is via the North Door, follow the Desert Penguins
signs.
