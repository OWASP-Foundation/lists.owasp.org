OWASP Phoenix invites you to it's JULY meeting!

Tues July 7th at 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM with a social/happy hour at a local
watering hole afterward. The meetings are always free, unfortunately the
drinks afterward aren't.

The meetings are now being generously hosted by ExecuTrain and QuietMove, at
Executrain's training facility.


This month's meeting:

Introduction, chapter business. web application security news update by Adam
Muntner, Phoenix OWASP Chapter Lead

Presentation:

Andrew Wilson will be presenting on how to use Microsoft's threat modeling
process and tools to build more secure software.

Andrew Wilson has been programming and interested in computer security for
over 10 years. He is a senior consultant for Neudesic.


The location is: Executrain 3600 E University Dr # A1400 Phoenix, AZ 85034
The entrance is on the West side of the building. Look for the suite number
on the door.
