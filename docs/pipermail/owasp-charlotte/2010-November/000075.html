Hi.  My name is Larry Crump and I am a System Architect at Duke Energy.   I work with online billing and payment applications and other miscellaneous internal customer service applications.  I am responsible for the architecture and design of our systems in the customer service domain, with emphasis on ensuring adherence to standards and integration with both internal and external applications.  In addition to those responsibilities, I am also the representative for our division to our company-wide team that reviews security strategy, architectures and standards across the enterprise.
