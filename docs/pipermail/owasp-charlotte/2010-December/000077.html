Welcome aboard!  Things are a little slow for the holidays right now,
but we're planning on having our next meeting in late January or early
February.  We also have a couple of folks working on an elevator pitch
so we can talk up the group with other local groups to get some
additional interest.

restarted, is what we hope to accomplish as a group.  We've talked
about a couple of existing OWASP projects we might try to get involved
