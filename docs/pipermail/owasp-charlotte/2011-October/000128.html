Manager, Application Security

Description	
Responsible for all aspects of application security program, including vulnerability assessment, source code analysis, ethical hacking, and application developer training.   Position will be also responsible for influencing application architecture, engineering infrastructure, and application development resources and processes to create and maintain secure applications.  

Essential Duties and Responsibilities
*	Manages the personnel, tools and processes involved in application vulnerability assessment and penetration testing.
*	Planning and managing the delivery of scanning and assessments of high risk applications.
*	Develops information security strategies, conducts security assessments, and implements security solutions to assist business with the assessment and improvement of their applications.
*	Leads team members to generate a vision, establish direction and motivate members, create an atmosphere of trust, leverage diverse views, coach staff, and encourage improvement and innovation.
*	Develops metrics and reporting to demonstrate application security posture, and the company's ability to defend against threats to the application portfolio. 
*	Manages team personnel, continuously develops and optimizes / improves processes, develops process measures and controls, tests and reports on process controls, integrates with IT and Corporate control and Compliance processes.
*	Provides expert assistance to application development and infrastructure teams concerning application security.
*	Supports the Information Security program by participating in or leading efforts requiring application security subject matter expertise.	
	 

Qualifications	
*	3-4 years object-oriented application development experience
*	5-10 years working within the Information Security field, with at least five years hands-on technical experience testing applications with industry leading tools, augmented by manual verification.
*	2-3 years minimum managerial experience
*	Knowledge of different application architectures and platforms, their development challenges, their control configurations, and their inherent security strengths and weaknesses
*	Strong understanding of application, network, operating system, and core infrastructure security concepts and concerns.
*	Demonstrated experience building and improving technology security programs within responsibility areas described above. 
*	Current understanding of best practices, management techniques and industry trends within responsibility areas described above. 
*	Superior communication and influence skills, ability to gain agreement and support at all levels in the organization. 
*	GIAC, CISSP, CCIE, CCSE, CEH certifications a plus.
