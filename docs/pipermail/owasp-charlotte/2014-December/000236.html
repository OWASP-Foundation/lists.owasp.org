When you are talking dues do you mean ask the people that came from meet up to help with the meet up fee's, or OWASP dues?

We should certainly encourage everyone to pay the OWASP dues and assign to the local chapter, or if they came from meet up, help with that. Perhaps holding the CISSP credits at ransom for OWASP members or “charitable donations” towards the local funds - just a thought.

To recap OWASP dues are: $50 annually, $95 for 2 years or Lifetime for $500. 40% of dues go to a local chapter. So $20/year, $38 for 2 years and $200 once for lifetime members which would be the same as 10 years at the annual cost could go to our chapter.

Perhaps we should reach out to some organizations and see if they are willing to do the corporate OWASP membership. I haven’t looked at the by-laws on company sponsorship of a local chapter, but would question if that is allowed.

Those are my thoughts.
