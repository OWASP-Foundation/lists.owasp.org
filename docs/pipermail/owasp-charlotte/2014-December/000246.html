Not to toot my own horn or anything, but I renewed my membership back
around September. And I made sure to designate OWASP Charlotte as the
40% local chapter recipient. I'm guessing at least a couple others have
as well based on the current number in our bucket.

Corporations can sponsor similarly. Information about it here
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership.

I agree too about the meetup fees being worth it. We haven't been using
that money for anything else. As Will Stranathan mentioned, I do recall
a discussion about the use of meetup.com on the owasp-leaders list. I
missed the details about meetup for non-profits though. I'm searching
the list archives trying to find it. I will post again if and when I can
locate it.
