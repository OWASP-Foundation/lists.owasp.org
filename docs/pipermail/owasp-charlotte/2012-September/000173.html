All,

Our academic chair, Bill Chu, has been selected to present at this
year's AppSec USA being held in Austin Texas next month. Be sure to
congratulate him.

- 2012 Cyber Security Symposium is October 10th. More information and
registration is found at this URL
http://www.cvent.com/events/2012-fall-unc-charlotte-cyber-security-symposium/event-summary-592d564e732c4ac18946bddfaaaba9dc.aspx.
Scroll all the way to the bottom in order to register.

- By registering as a member of OWASP you can not only show your support
for the organization but you can allocate a portion of your membership
funds to the local chapter (OWASP Charlotte) or to a project of your
choosing like AppSensor or ASIDE. Both AppSensor and ASIDE are projects
being actively worked on by local chapter members John Melton and Bill
Chu respectively. Please show your support by making a donation at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership.

If you have any questions please ask.
