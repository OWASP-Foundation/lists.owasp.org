Hello All,
Just a reminder meeting is next week and SnowFROC is at the end of the
month.

Its time to get registered so we can plan on the right amount of food!

You are invited to our monthly meeting that feature current topics in cyber
security.. We look forward to seeing you.

March OWASP events:


Here is the information for our Monthly event:
You can RSVP at:  http://1303owaspdenver.eventbrite.com
or at: http://www.meetup.com/Denver-OWASP/

You are invited to Denver OWASP's March 20th Meeting.

This month we welcome Jim Manico who will be presenting on secure coding
techniques.  Jim is a global board member for the OWASP organization and VP
of Security Architecture at WhiteHat Security.  It is our understanding
that some giveaways will be presented to some lucky attendees.


South Seas Corporation will be providing our food for Wednesday's event.
We thank South Seas Corporation (http://southseascorp_com.htm) for so
generously sponsoring food for all of our meetings this year!


Also SnowFROC



Front Range OWASP Conference

Join 300+ developers, security professionals, and cyber
security<http://www.meetup.com/Denver-OWASP/events/105016462/#>
industry
leaders for a day-and-a-half of world-class presentations, hacking
competitions, expert training, and collaborative Birds of a Feather
sessions.

SnowFROC 2013 offers four tracks designed to tackle cyber security from all
angles: technical, deep-dive, management, and legal. For the hands-on
crowd, a capture the flag hacking tournament will run in parallel to
conference proceedings.

Protection, NSA.
