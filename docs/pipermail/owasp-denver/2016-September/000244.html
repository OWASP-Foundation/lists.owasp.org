CALL FOR SPEAKERS for the SQUAD Conference 2017!

We are inviting you to participate in the SQUAD Conference, September 12th
and 13th 2017 at the PPA Event Center in Denver CO.

We have 3 tracks for the conference and are looking for both 50 minute
presentations and half or full day workshops that relate to the following
topics:

1.    *Testing in an Agile world*:   Presentations that speak to testing
successes and challenges in Agile environments.  How are you incorporating
test in your agile environment? What's working? What are some of the
obstacles and difficulties?

2.    *Test Tools and Test Automation*: Presentations that speak to using
test tools and test automation to effectively test in your environment?
How are you incorporating test automation?  What's working?  What are some
of the challenges?  What tools are you using?

3.    *Test Strategy and Approach:* Presentations on issues related to test
strategy and test approach.  How does test integrate in your overall
organization?  How do you manage the test life cycle, test artifacts, test
data, test coverage,etc... What strategies do you use in your environment
to test effectively and what challenges do you face?

If you are interested in presenting at the SQUAD conference 2017 on one of
these topics please reply to this message or contact Melissa Tondi directly
at melissa.tondi at gmail.com.

Thanks and we hope to see you all at the SQUAD conference next September!

Bev Berry,
