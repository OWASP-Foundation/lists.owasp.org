Veracode has two available seats to our private dinner with Veracode CTO and Co-Founder, Chris Wysopal,
along with a viewing of the Denver Broncos game at Elways Downtown at the Ritz Carlton on Thursday, December 6th.

These seats will be given to the first two people to respond to this email by emailing sthayer at veracode.com<mailto:sthayer at veracode.com>.

Executive Roundtable & Dinner:
Confronting Cyber Risk from Insecure Software


[cid:image001.png at 01CDD3BD.2C607110]



Invitation-Only Moderated Discussion with
Chris Wysopal, CTO & Co-Founder, Veracode.
Thursday, December 6th - 5:00pm | Elway's Downtown, The Ritz-Carlton

[cid:image003.jpg at 01CDD3BD.2C607110]Cybersecurity is a rapidly growing global concern, and firewalls and defensive technologies are no longer enough to keep your enterprise safe from attack. You've done a lot to secure the network but the cyber threat is quickly moving beyond this perimeter to the application layer.

Join speaker Chris Wysopal, CTO and Co-Founder Veracode, author of The Art of Software Security Testing and contributing author to the SANS/CWE Most Dangerous Software Errors, for an exclusive, invitation-only dinner and moderated discussion on how enterprises can better understand and reduce security risks associated with insecure software.

Topics for discussion:

 *   The State of Software Security
 *   Managing the Risk from Vendor-Supplied Software
 *   Mobile Security Strategies BYOD and Beyond

The roundtable discussion will be followed by a private dinner along with a screening of the Denver Broncos vs. Oakland Raiders game in a private dining room.

RSVP: For more information and to RSVP, please contact Stacy Thayer at sthayer at veracode.com<mailto:sthayer at veracode.com>. Space is limited, so please RSVP by November 16th to reserve your seat.
