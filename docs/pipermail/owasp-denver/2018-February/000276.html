SnowFROC 2018 will be held March 8, 2018 at the Cable Center Denver.
SnowFROC is Denver’s premier Application Security Conference of the year.
We will host over 450 developers, business owners, and security
professionals for a day of presentations, training and bonding.   John
Strand, Owner of Black Hills Information Security and renowned SANS

at www.snowfroc.com
