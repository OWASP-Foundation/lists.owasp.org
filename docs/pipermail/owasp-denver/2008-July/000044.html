Folks,

Apologies for that last post.  This list is created primarily for the 
distribution of information related to Denver OWASP meetings and 
conferences.

We have occasionally supported the submission of other information, 
including job postings, but only if they are specifically focused on 
app-sec.

We have enabled moderation on the list, and for future reference, the 
only job postings appropriate for submission here are positions with a 
focus on app-sec, posted by principal OWASP members (i.e. not recruiters).

Thanks for your patience.
