SANS invites you to be our guest in Denver for our COINS (Community Of
Interest in Network Security) SANS at Night Event on Monday, June 27th at the
Westin Tabor Center in Downtown Denver, during SANS Rocky Mountain
(https://www.sans.org/rocky-mountain-2011).  

SANS Instructor Bryce Galbraith will deliver his exciting presentation, "Why
Your End Users Are Your Weakest Link".

We must understand the true risk we face in today's threatscape if we are to
have a chance to defend ourselves.
This talk will highlight some of the most salient threats our end users face
both in and out of the office.

There is no cost to attend this event, but we do need you to register
at: (https://www.sans.org/coins_event.php?eventid=371)


*Event Details*
Date:		Monday, June 27, 2011
Time:     	7:00pm - 8:45pm

Schedule:	7:00-7:45pm Networking Cocktail & Snack Reception
		7:45-8:45pm Presentation

Location:	The Westin Tabor Center
		1672 Lawrence Street
		Denver, CO 80202 US
		Tel: 303-572-9100

Seating is limited so RSVP today at:

 (https://www.sans.org/coins_event.php?eventid=371)
