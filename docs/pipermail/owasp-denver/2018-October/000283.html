Abstract:
NowSecure recently performed a groundbreaking benchmark analysis of
hundreds of thousands of public apps found in Apple App Store® and Google
Play™. NowSecure found that 85% of mobile apps have 1 or more vulns that
violate the OWASP Mobile Top 10. From leaking employee/customer data to
interceptable mobile app communications, the threats are real but
identifying and addressing these vulns are difficult.

In this session, Brian Reed, Chief Mobility Officer from NowSecure, will
review the OWASP Mobile Top 10 framework and present this eye popping
benchmark data enumerating threats and risks by OWASP categories and CVSS
scores. Then he will provide sample tools, policies and vetting procedures
to deal with this pandora's box of mobile app risk. Finally he will pull up
the benchmark database live and crowdsource names from the audience to
checkout whether they are safe or sorry!

Brian C Reed Speaker bio:

As NowSecure Chief Mobility Officer, industry veteran Brian Reed brings
over a 15 years experience in mobile, apps, and security including Good
Technology, BlackBerry, ZeroFOX, BoxTone (from Columbia, MD), MicroFocus
and INTERSOLV (from Rockville, MD) working with Global 2000 customers,
Mobile-First companies and government agencies. With more than 25 years
building innovative products and scaling operations, Brian is a dynamic
speaker and compelling storyteller who brings unique insights and global
experience. Brian is frequent speaker at industry and private events
including Mobile World Congress, OWASP, Cloud Security Alliance, JavaOne
and more. Brian is a graduate of Duke University.
