February OWASP meeting this Wednesday at Dave and Busters.  RSVP at

Also, we're filling up fast for our SNOWFROC conference on March 16th.
Full day of speakers including:

   1. Robb Reck (CISO Ping Identity)
   2. Deborah Blyte (CISO State of CO)
   3. Jim Manico (Author, International AppSec speaker / expert)
   4. Dan Cornell (International AppSec speaker)
   5. and more ....

   - Tech Track
   - Management Track
   - Hands-on track (bring your laptops!)

parking and immediately next door to light rail stop.

here:  https://www.eventbrite.com/e/snowfroc-2017-tickets-31073466575).

great talks!!
