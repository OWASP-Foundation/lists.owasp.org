The Denver OWASP chapter is looking to host a training day tentatively
scheduled for November 17th.  This will be a one day training with 2 or 3
simultaneous classes going on.  Cost per student is targeted to be $500
with a class size cap at 25 or 30 persons.  Funds raised from the training
will be split 40/60 (trainer/OWASP) where some funds go to the local
chapter and some funds go to the OWASP Foundation.  OWASP will arrange the
venue, food, bring all AV in, and conduct marketing for the event.

We are looking for hot current training.  So if you have or can develop a
one day training class, please submit your proposed class along with a bio
to me and I will forward this onto our selection committee.

Submissions must be received by September 9th.

Thanks!

Steve Kosten
OWASP Denver Chapter Leader
