OWASP-Denver,

As you are likely aware, Denver will be hosting the national OWASP
conference (witht the Boulder OWASP Chapter) in Denver in 2014.  We have
the downtown Marriott reserved for the conference and are meeting weekly
planning for this event.  The event will be Sept 16th - 19th, 2014 where
the 16th and 17th are training days and the conference portion will be held
on the 18th and 19th.

We are currently reaching out to engage potential keynote speakers for this
event.  Bruce Schneier (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruce_Schneier) has
already committed to being a keynote and we are looking for other strong
keynotes.  Please email me if you have suggestions (and contacts) with
someone you think would make a strong keynote for this event.

Also, if you are at all interested in volunteering for this event, please
feel free to reach out to me and we can look to get you engaged.

Thank you.

Steve Kosten
OWASP Denver Chapter Leader
