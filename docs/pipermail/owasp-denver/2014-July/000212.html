Calling all brewers: OWASP’s AppSec USA's homebrew competition needs your
help!

Join us for an evening of all things homebrewed and celebrate one of
Colorado’s most beloved traditions. With the state’s rich history of
brewing and esteemed reputation in the national craft beer scene, no
Denver-based conference would be complete without an homage to this
longstanding local pastime. Anticipate fermented creations of all
persuasions made by brewers from near and far alike in the name of honor,
Rocky Mountain pride, and some really cool prizes.

Interested in brewing something for the contest? Check out our Call for
Brewers page for sign-up info and contest guidelines:

http://appsecusa.org/events/cfb/

We’ll be giving awards for Best of Show, People’s Choice, Sponsors Choice,
and more, so show up to sample some delicious one-of-a-kind brews, vote for
your favorite, and enjoy the company of visitors from across the nation.

Brewers are not required to attend AppSec USA in order to participate in
Code Brew, but we do offer AppSec USA discounts to all brewers. Non-brewer
admittance to Code Brew is restricted to conference attendees only. See the
AppSec USA site for conference registration details.
