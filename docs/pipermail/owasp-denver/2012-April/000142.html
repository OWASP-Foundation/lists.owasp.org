OWASP is back in Boulder! The inaugural meeting is Thursday, April 19th at
17th and Pearl. To introduce the newcomers, our first meeting is geared
entirely toward OWASP mainstays: WebScarab, WebGoat, and the top-10. This
will be the same presentation the Denver chapter sees during their April
meeting, so save yourself the drive and catch the Boulder encore instead!
One word of caution: seating is limited. To reserve your spot, RSVP through
MeetUp.com:

http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Boulder/

*
*

*Hands-on Introduction to WebScarab & WebGoat*
Tim Van Cleave with TVG Network will introduce a couple of OWASP classics:
WebScarab and WebGoat. Tim will also cover some of the OWASP Top 10 issues
as he demonstrates proxy-based web application security testing.


This will be an interactive meeting. If you want to play along, bring a
laptop with wireless capability as well as WebScarab and Firefox installed.

WebScarab:
http://sourceforge.net/projects/owasp/files/WebScarab/20070504-1631/
Firefox: http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/products/download.html
Java: http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html


I am very excited to kick off the Boulder chapter, and I hope to see you
all there!

--
Mark Major
