This is the last call for registrations for the HP Enterprise Security Summit this Thursday, April 19th at Colorado National Golf Club in Erie, CO.

There are a only a few spots available so please act quickly  to secure a seat at this high impact security conference.

As a reminder the keynote address will be given by Tom Reilly, EVP and GM<http://www.hp.com/hpinfo/newsroom/press_kits/2011/risk2011/Tom_Reilly_Bio.pdf> of HP Enterprise Security and form CEO of ArcSight.

Final registrations will be accepted on a first come, first serve basis.
