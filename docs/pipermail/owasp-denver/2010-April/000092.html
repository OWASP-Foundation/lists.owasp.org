Registration is Now Open for the 2010 Rocky Mountain Information Security
Conference

Co-Hosted by the Denver Chapters of ISACA and ISSA


Wednesday, May 05, 2010
7:00 a.m. to 6:30 p.m.
Marriott Denver Tech Center
4900 South Syracuse Street, Denver , Colorado 80237

With the increasing set of information security threats facing today's
businesses, it is vital to stay up to speed on current technology,
successful security models and the most high-profile compliance issues.
Managers and CIOs want to stay compliant and prevent security breaches that
might put their company in the paper. Audit professionals want to make sure
they can prove compliance, within budget, while technical-level
professionals just want to know it's going to work.

The 4th Annual Rocky Mountain Information Security Conference is the only
conference of its kind in the Rocky Mountain region. A convenient,
affordable one-day knowledge-builder for IT security, audit and compliance
professionals at all levels. Learn from the best and network with peers!
See the agenda at:  http://www.isaca-denver.org/rmisc/agenda/index.php


As a member of OWASP, you are eligible to receive a 20% discount off the
Non-member pricing.  To receive this discount, please follow these
instructions:


1. Go to http://www.isaca-denver.org/rmisc/

2. Click on "Registration" on the top menu bar.

3. Click on the "Non-member" link in the registration fees table.

4. Enter "OWASP2010" in the discount code in the discount code box.

5. Enter contact information and hit next.

6. Select the breakout sessions you are interested in attending and hit
next.

7. Enter payment information and hit submit.



Early Bird Fees available until April 12, 2010 - register now!

Go to http://www.isaca-denver.org/rmisc/ to register!


For more information, visit the RMISC Website
