Volunteer Opportunity - Rocky Mountain Collegiate Cyber Defense
Competition

 

Are you looking for an excellent VOLUNTEER opportunity to earn some
CPE's while meeting and help train our next generation of cyber
defenders?

 

Check out this opportunity - the Rocky Mountain Collegiate Cyber Defense
Competition, March 2nd- 3rd.  This competition tests each team's ability
to operate, secure, manage, and maintain a corporate network.  Volunteer
assignments may include judging and ensuring team compliance.   

 

Rocky Mountain Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition

 

CCDC is a two day event open to all college student teams and focuses on
the operational aspect of managing and protecting an existing
"commercial" network infrastructure.  Regis University's Center on
Information Assurance Studies is participating in this competition and
is looking for volunteers.  If you or anyone you know is interested,
contact the center at rmccdc at regis.edu   See more detail on the
competition here http://www.nationalccdc.org/.
