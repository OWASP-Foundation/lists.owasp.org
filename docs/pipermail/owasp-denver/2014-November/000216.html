We are actively seeking a skilled Web Application Security Engineer to work as a member of our Cyber Operations team performing web application vulnerability and access operations.  This full time salaried position will be located at our root9B state of the art office locations in Colorado Springs, CO (preferred) or San Antonio, TX (for exceptional talent).  The individual must be able to think outside the box to deliver tailored operations and reporting for fortune 500 companies. The individual will also be responsible for researching, developing, and maintaining expert-level proficiency of current trends in web based exploitation and vulnerability analysis.

Functional Responsibility:
Responsible for

  *   Collecting and analyzing customer requirements to implement the most effective tactics, tools, and procedures for an engagement,
  *   Providing proper direction, guidance, and training to supporting personnel,
  *   Executing operations against client networks and ensure all deadlines are achieved and in accordance with company and industry standards,
  *   Providing quality assurance / quality control during post-op reporting, and
  *   Actively working with program management and senior staff to promote dynamic development of root9B's cyber operations and capabilities.
Minimum/General Experience:

  *   Minimum of seven (7) years education and/or technical experience performing full-scale web application vulnerability assessments.
  *   Extensive knowledge of OWASP exploitation methodology and web application vulnerability standards.
  *   Experience with industry standard web application frameworks such as Burp Suite, Acunetix, and Nikto but more importantly can think outside-the-box to develop tailored solutions for non-standard problems.
  *   Experience developing custom tools and/or scripts to accomplish desired goals.
  *   Expert knowledge of web-based programming, protocols, best practices, and cryptography.
  *   Expert knowledge of common computer operating systems, networking protocols, and host-based / network security products and capabilities.
  *   Experience with applicable programming/scripting languages such as JavaScript, jQuery, Python, Ruby, etc.
  *   Experience performing mobile application security assessments would be considered a plus.
Minimum Education/Experience:

  *   Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering or other scientific or technical discipline directly related to the labor category and five (5) years relevant experience,

OR

  *   Seven (7) years relevant experience.



Certifications:

  *   Industry standard certifications such as GWAPT and OSWE are a plus but experience carries more weight.
