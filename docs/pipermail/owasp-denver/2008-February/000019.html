Hey folks,

We have had several new subscribers in the past week so I figured I would
send out a reminder about our meeting one week from today.

-----

On behalf of the OWASP Denver Chapter I would like to invite you to attend
our first Denver OWASP meeting since June of 2007.

The event will be held Wednesday, the 20th of February at the offices of
Raytheon Polar Services in Centennial, CO. from 6pm to 8pm.

We are very excited to welcome Michael Sutton from SPI Dynamics (HP), who
will deliver our keynote presentation.  I will also be delivering a short
presentation of highlights from the OWASP/WASC Nov 2007 Conference in San
Jose, CA.

Full details on the event are on the website, at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Denver#Next_Meeting.

Please RSVP to dcampbell at owasp.org so we can get an idea of how many
attendees to expect.  (Note if you've already RSVP'd no need to do so
again).  Food will be provided courtesy of HP/SPI.

Note that for CISSP folks, CPE's will be awarded for this event.

Also please save the date 10 June 2008 for the Front Range OWASP
Conference (in partnership with BOULDER OWASP).

Cheers!
