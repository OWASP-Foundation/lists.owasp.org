Outsourcing Conference - "Risk Management and Data Security in an Outsourced World"

Tuesday, January 11, 2011; 7:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.
Colorado Convention Center
700 14th Street, Denver, CO 80202

"Mark your calendars and take advantage of early bird registration (ends by November 19, 2010)"

Register now<http://www.isaca-denver.org/Conferences/Outsourcing-Conference/registration.php>

ISACA Denver Chapter and IAOP (International Association of Outsourcing Professional) are partnering deliver a comprehensive one day outsourcing conference addressing information security, IT auditing, compliance and governance challenges associated with outsourcing and off-shoring. The conference promises a day of education through discussions with industry-leading Outsourcing IT, Telecom and Network service providers, Cloud Computing service providers, Managed Security Service providers and local information security, audit, compliance and IT professionals.

Our goal is to provide you with a one-day, low-cost, "no travel required" hiatus from those pressures so you can learn about the latest security and compliance  trends in outsourcing; interact with industry thought leaders and leading security and auditing professionals from leading outsourcing organizations; and network with peers to share project successes and war stories.

Key Benefits

Competition is pushing organizations to explore more outsourcing opportunities for every area of the business including security outsourcing. This conference and exhibition will help you to:

*       Make informed decisions about security, compliance strategy and governance with outsourcing.
*       Understand the security and compliance of leading outsourcing service providers.
*       Understand legal requirements and best practices for managing outsourcing organization and service providers.
*       Understand the latest security and compliance services offered by Managed Security Service Providers.
*       Determine which new alternative security delivery models best meet your requirements.
