Dish Network is recruiting for the IT Security Team. The team has broad 
responsibilities for Security Assessments, Security Operations, and 
Security Governance. On our team, everybody has a chance to do a bit of 
everything. You will have a chance to contribute to the team effort by 
leveraging your strengths and experiences, while learning and growing as 
a security professional through challenges of supporting a Fortune 250 
company with a dynamic and complex IT environment.

For immediate consideration, please send your resume to me at 
chad.brownhill at dishnetwork.com <mailto:chad.brownhill at dishnetwork.com>. 
We hope to move quickly on this position, so please don't delay.

*Job Duties & Responsibilities*

    * Perform security assessments against applications and
      infrastructure components;
    * Manage security-centric infrastructure components such as NIPS,
      NIDS, and WIDS;
    * Work with multiple teams throughout IT on remediation activities;
    * Advise on system security requirements, architectural designs, and
      secure software development;
    * Participate on Incident Response Teams.


*Education & Experience*

    * Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related
      discipline, or equivalent work experience and certifications.
    * CISSP or specialized GIAC certifications highly preferred.
    * 5+ Years in IT with at least 2 years in IT Security.


*Skills & Qualifications*
 

    * Ability to implement, maintain and monitor high availability cyber
      threat technical countermeasures.
    * Hands-on experience with network intrusion prevention and
      detection systems.
    * Hands-on experience in performing application layer penetration
      tests and infrastructure vulnerability scans.
    * Knowledge of network protocols, data flows, and vulnerabilities
      within a TCP/IP environment.
    * Have a solid understanding of common vulnerabilities associated
      with operating systems and applications.
    * Ability to perform network protocol analysis and raw data capture.
    * An understanding of dynamic routing protocols and static routing.
    * Some proficiency in programming and scripting languages common to
      security such as Perl, PHP, or Python.
    * Have excellent verbal and written communication skills with
      experience presenting information to groups.
