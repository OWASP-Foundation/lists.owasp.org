Dear Reboot 2012 Project Leaders,

As some of you may know, Eoin, Kate and I have been working hard to
get funding for OWASP's Reboot 2012 projects. We have come to a point
in our management efforts where we are in need of a status update from
each of the project leaders participating in Reboot 2012.

I would like to ask if all of you could provide us with a status
update for your project. The information we need is outlined below:

• Progress to Date.
• Funds you have spent: Please see the Reboot 2012 Budget document and
update your expenses there.
• Roadblocks you have encountered.
• Project Leader Availability to complete the Project: A yes or a no
will suffice.

The Reboot 2012 Budget spreadsheet will help us make sure we don't
overspend our awarded funds so please do update your relevant sections
accordingly.

As Eoin has suggested before, this is in no way a micro-management
effort. We just need to know if you are still able to complete the
project proposed to Reboot 2012, and if we can assist you in
eliminating any obstacles you may have encountered. We are here to
help, after all.

Thank you all for your assistance with this endeavour, and we look
forward to your updates.
