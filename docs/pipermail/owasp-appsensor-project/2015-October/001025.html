Folks,

Just saw this ... Dominik Schadow (https://twitter.com/dschadow) presented
AppSensor at JavaOne today! The presentation and sample application using
AppSensor are both top notch. Please have a look if you're interested.

https://blog.dominikschadow.de/events/ (Dominik's event page with other
presentations)

https://github.com/dschadow/ApplicationIntrusionDetection (the sample
application)

http://files.dominikschadow.de/event_javaone2015-con2022.pdf (the
presentation pdf)

I asked him what, if any, feedback he received. A couple of things he
mentioned were that a) there was a lot of interest and that b) AppSensor
needs more advertisement!

Thanks to Dominik for his hard, excellent work on this.

If anybody has ideas about "advertising" or wants to help get the word out,
certainly jump in.

A special note of thanks to all of our contributors. This was a good
validation of the effort from everyone - thank you!
