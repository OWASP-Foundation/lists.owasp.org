AppSensor Team,

I'm thrilled we have two of our top notch AppSensor leaders interested in
taking over project leadership!  Outlined below is the process I'll follow
to select our next project leader.

The process:
1. John & Dennis send me responses to 3 questions (see forwarded email)
[done]
2. I'll publish the answers (verbatim) to the appsensor mailing list [done]
and ask for feedback [in progress]
3. I'll make a call based on all the info and feedback
4. We'll all continue making AppSensor awesome

We're on step 2. The responses are located here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSensor_Project/ProjectLeaderStatement-JohnMelton
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSensor_Project/ProjectLeaderStatement-DennisGroves

If anyone on the list has feedback or opinions then please send them
directly to me for consideration.
