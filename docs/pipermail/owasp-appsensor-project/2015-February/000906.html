Johanna

I see a Flagship banner has been added above our project wiki page.

When I laid out this style of project page, I was keen to get rid of
all the clutter that used to be above the tabs (e.g. book link,
advert, donation beg)

- to remove distracting content
- to move more of the project content "above the fold"

Subsequently what I did was copied onto most of the projects by others.

We are certainly pleased to be a flagship project, and than the task
force for its work, but I feel these banners are a somewhat retrograde
step, and in my opinion degrades the page design. We already have the
status in the categorisations, and while it help visitors identify
which projects are flagship, that should be done in other places on
the site (e.g. the project main page, and maybe a little on the home
page). This design element could be used within the existing banner
leaders put on the first tab of their project.

The banner should also link... somewhere.

I would like to remove the banner from the top of the AppSensor page,
or move it into a sub-tab.

Colin
