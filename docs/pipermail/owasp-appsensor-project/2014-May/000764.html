(cross posted to the AppSensor project and AppSensor development mailing lists)

The v2.0 release of the AppSensor Guide has been published. OWASP
AppSensor is free to use and it is licensed under the Creative Commons
Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 license. The guide is available free of
charge at:

   https://www.owasp.org/index.php/File:Owasp-appensor-guide-v2.doc

   https://www.owasp.org/index.php/File:Owasp-appsensor-guide-v2.pdf

The black & white book is also available to order at cost in hard copy
from Lulu:

   http://www.lulu.com/shop/owasp-foundation/appsensor-guide/paperback/product-21608107.html

The new guide was conceived at AppSensor Summit held during AppSec USA
2011 in Minneapolis. Although it has taken some time to produce
something comprehensive, we have also managed to include very recent
ideas and developments.

This has all been entirely achieved through unpaid voluntary efforts.
Unfortunately the previously announced HOST grant for professional
design for the book and a flyer never materialised. But we do have a
little OWASP Reboot project funding to help with promotion.

Please join me in thanking co-authors Dennis Groves and John Melton,
and the other contributors, editors and reviewers, Ryan Barnett,
Michael Coates, Craig Munson and Jay Reynolds. The growth and
increased maturity of the project as a whole would not have been
possible without the other people, many on this mailing list, who
contributed project feedback, suggestions and ideas. I have attempted
to name everyone I am aware of in the book's acknowledgements.

I hope you and others will be able to gain further inspiration from
this new guide, and perhaps contribute to an even better next version.

If you do find the guide useful, please consider rating and reviewing
it on Lulu.

If you are able to translate the book into another language, that
would be even more helpful.

Thank you,

Colin Watson
Lead Author, AppSensor Guide v2.0
