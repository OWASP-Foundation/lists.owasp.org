Sounds good, Colin. To add more explanation, I have $3,000 allocated to use
for Project Leaders requiring travel assistance to AppSec USA only. The
event organisers have expressed interest in inviting 5 projects to the
summit so I am reaching out to those projects to see if they are interested
in participating. The $3,000 can only be used for this purpose as outlined
by our 2013 budget, and we have to share it between members of all 5
projects if needed. The idea is to ask PLs to use their project funds for
travel and accommodation, and to only use the $3,000 as a last resort for
expenses since it has to be shared.

The AppSec USA team would like to invite members of AppSensor, but we
cannot afford to pay for everyone. That is why I am reaching out to you to
see if you are interested in participating. That goes for the rest of the
AppSensor team, keeping in mind that our travel budget is limited and must
be shared with other projects.

Do let me know if you are interested in participating.
