The OWASP project summit in Amsterdam is going ahead:

   http://2015.appsec.eu/project-summit/

We have a session on Tuesday 19th to undertake review and update of the Guide:

==============

13:00 – 15:00 hrs Tuesday 19th May 2015

AppSensor (Documentation) – Guide Review

The AppSensor Guide v2 was published in May last year, and has had two
minor updates, the last one mainly due to the important release of the
v2 code implementation. This session is to edit and improve the guide,
since many of the chapters have not been fully reviewed. Participants
should read a chapter or two in advance of the summit (chapter 5
onwards, but choose randomly/what is of interest) and bring their
edits/comments to the session, where the guide will be updated. All
participants will be acknowledged in the guide and on the project wiki
page.

• Briefing
• Live editing
• Publication updated PDF.

==============

We also have a code session on the following day (see separate email
about that). Further details:

   https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Project_Summit_2015/Home

Free registration:

   http://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-project-summit-eu-2015-amsterdam-tickets-16029097462?aff=es2&rank=0

I look forward to meeting some of you there.
