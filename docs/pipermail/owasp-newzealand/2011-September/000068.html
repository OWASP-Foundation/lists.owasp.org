Hi everyone,

Thanks to everyone who attended the chapter meeting on Tuesday - we
had a great turnout in both Auckland and Wellington.

The presentations from the evening are now available online at the
following URL: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_Zealand

Thanks to Security-Assessment.com for sponsoring the meeting. Also, we
would like to welcome Touchpoint (http://www.touchpoint.co.nz) as a
new chapter sponsor. Thanks guys!

If you are interested in giving a presentation at an upcoming chapter
meeting, please let us know. Additionally, if you would like a
presentation about OWASP and its various projects at your local
development user group / meet up, contact us to arrange this - we are
always looking to increase the awareness of web application security
in New Zealand.

http://www.kiwicon.org!
