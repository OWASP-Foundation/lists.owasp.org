Hi everyone,

Aura Information Security and Security-Assessment.com have graciously
offered to forfeit their share of the training fee in order to lower
the cost of the training sessions at OWASP NZ Day 2011. Both
organisations believe in the importance of training developers in New
Zealand, and have sacrificed almost all cost recovery of the training
sessions to make them more accessible to you, the OWASP NZ Day 2011
attendees.

This halves the cost of training at the conference, bringing it down
to just NZD$125 per person for an excellent and informative 3 hour
training session. For more information about the available training
sessions, please see the OWASP NZ Day 2011 website at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2011#tab=Training_.28NEW.21.29

All trainees who have already registered will be refunded NZD$125 to
match the new cost of training.

Seats are limited to 20 per training session - so register soon! You
can sign up at the following URL:
https://www.regonline.com/owaspnzday2011

The registration cut-off for training has been extended to COB Jun 30
- that's next Thursday. Standard attendee registration will close at
COB today.

Please direct any queries to nick.freeman at owasp.org and scott.bell at owasp.org.


Kind Regards


Nick Freeman & Scott Bell
OWASP New Zealand Chapter Leaders
