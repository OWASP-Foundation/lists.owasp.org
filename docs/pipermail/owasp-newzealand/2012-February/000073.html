Just a reminder that the deadline for submission (for the first round of
selections) of the OWASP AppSec AsiaPac 2012 Call for Papers (CFP) and Call

submissions is Friday, March 3, 2012.

Submit your talk here: http://sl.owasp.org/apac2012talks
Submit your training proposal here: http://sl.owasp.org/apac2012training

forward to all interested practitioners and colleagues.
