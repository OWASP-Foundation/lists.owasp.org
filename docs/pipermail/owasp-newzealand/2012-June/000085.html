Hi everyone,

Apologies to anyone who receives this mail twice.

Planning for OWASP New Zealand Day 2012 is well underway, and we have
filled just over 1/3rd of the venue capacity in just two weeks! If you
have colleagues, friends or a development team that hasn't registered,
please point them towards the event page at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2012 . Remember
that we will have a stream focused on management and policy in the
afternoon - so if you or your manager could benefit from this, let
them know too.

We are proud to announce two Silver Sponsors for the event. Our
great thanks go out to these organisations for continuing to support
the conference and helping us keep it free and open. Our sponsors are:

Insomnia Security - http://www.insomniasec.com
Lateral Security - http://www.lateralsecurity.com


The Call for Papers and Call for Trainers is open for another few
weeks. We welcome new speakers, so if you have something you'd like to
share please get your submissions in to us. We have more speaking
slots to fill this year than previous years, so don't hold back!


If you have any questions, please email myself
(nick.freeman at owasp.org) and Adrian Hayes (adrian.hayes at owasp.org).
You can also follow @owaspnz on Twitter for updates regarding the
conference and chapter meetings.


Kind Regards

Nick Freeman and Adrian Hayes
OWASP New Zealand Chapter Leaders
