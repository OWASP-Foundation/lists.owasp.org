Hi everyone,

The next OWASP NZ meeting will be held the 21st February at 6 pm. The
meeting is sponsored by Veda
Advantage. Venue location is:

Level 1, 12 Queen Street, Auckland.

Please access the building from Queen Street side.

Please note that this is the same location of the previous meeting. An
indication will be placed in front of the main door of the Britomart
building as well (Queen Street side).

Please follow this
link<http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=12+queen+street+auckland&sll=37.0625,-95.677068&sspn=31.977057,59.765625&layer=&ie=UTF8&z=15&ll=-36.842709,174.765573&spn=0.015764,0.02918>for
the map.

Participation is free and open to all but please confirm your attendance
as we need to order some food and drinks.

If you would like to speak at the this meeting please send us a title
and abstract of your proposed topic. Currently there is only one
presentation for this meeting so it would be
ideal if someone would be able to bring another presentation as well.

For more information about the current NZ OWASP activities please visit the
following page:

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_Zealand

We are also looking for two or more additional board members to evangelise
the OWASP mission in NZ. If you want to join us, please let us know.

The proposed presentation for this meeting is about Xpath Injection.

Thanks,

Roberto Suggi Liverani

NOTE TO CISSP people: OWASP Meetings count towards CPE Credits.

Sponsorships

Companies who would like to sponsor the event are welcomed to contact

us. This is a not for profit event and sponsorship is inexpensive and
used directly to cover costs.
