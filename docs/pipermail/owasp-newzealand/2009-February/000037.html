Hi everyone,

I have some good news to share about OWASP NZ Day. Myself and Rob had
a talk with the University of Auckland last Friday and they are happy
to provide a venue for the event!

The venue has capability for 300 ppl and it is located in the
University City Campus. Event date is set for the 13th July 2009. I am
setting up a web site on OWASP wiki page which I will soon share with
you. There will be an online registration link. The access to the
event is free for everyone.

Depending on the budget, number of sponsors and number of attendees, I
might also get the coffee break, lunch and snack for free. I will soon
release CFP (Call for Paper) for the speakers and CFS (Call For
Sponsors) for the sponsors in this and other security related
mailing-lists. I will keep you updated on the progress.

In the meantime, feel free to spread the "voice" ;-).

Thanks,
