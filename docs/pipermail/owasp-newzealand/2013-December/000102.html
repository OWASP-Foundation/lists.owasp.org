As you can see, we have our first chapter meeting in Christchurch this
month! This is in addition to us adding webcasting for presentations, so
that we can now reach more of New Zealand than ever before. Details
regarding the webcast will be released closer to the meeting date.

Our previous chapter meeting saw us using Meetup for RSVPs. This is much
easier to manage than multiple mailboxes. All future meeting announcements
will be via Meetup and this mailing list. Please find our Meetup event
here: http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-New-Zealand-Chapter/events/154075992/

As with previous meetings, food and drink will be provided courtesy of our
sponsors, Security-Assessment.com and TouchPoint.

At this stage we have one presentation planned but are looking for more -
so if you have something you'd like to talk about, please let us know. Your
talk doesn't need to be any specific length, as long as it is related to
web & application security, we're keen to hear your idea!


Please contact us with any questions.
