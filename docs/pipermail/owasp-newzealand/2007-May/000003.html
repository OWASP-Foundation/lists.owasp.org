OWASP New Zealand Meeting - 22nd May - Britomart, Auckland
=======================================

Hi all,

We are pleased to announce the first OWASP New Zealand meeting. It will be
held 22nd May at 6.30 pm. The meeting is sponsored by Veda Advantage and will
be held at Level 1, 12 Queen Street, Auckland.

Please follow this link for the map:
http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=12+queen+street+auckland&sll=37.0625,-
95.677068&sspn=31.977057,59.765625&layer=&ie=UTF8&z=15&ll=-
36.842709,174.765573&spn=0.015764,0.02918

Participation is free and open to all. It will help us with planning if you
can confirm your attendance.
Please feel free to share information regarding this meeting with others
either in your organization or outside who you think may be interested in the
meeting. You can also register on the OWASP New Zealand mailing list (
http://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-newzealand ) and/or follow our
web section at http://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_Zealand .

Meeting agenda:
==========

6.30 pm - Socializing - Food and drink
6.45 pm - Sponsor Introduction and OWASP introduction 7:00 pm - NZ OWASP
strategy exposed: why OWASP in New Zealand and what we want to achieve with
it - Roberto Suggi Liverani / Antonio Spera
7:15 pm - NZ Web Security Dossier - Introducing OWASP Top ten project -
Roberto Suggi Liverani / Antonio Spera 8:00 pm - Conclusion/ questions /
feedback

NOTE TO CISSP people: OWASP Meetings count towards CPE Credits.

Call for speakers
=============
If you would like to speak at the next meeting please send us a title and
abstract of your proposed topic as well as few words about yourself.

Sponsorships
=========
Companies who would like to sponsor the event are welcomed to contact us. This
is a not for profit event and sponsorship is  inexpensive and used directly to
cover costs.
