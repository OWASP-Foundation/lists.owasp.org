Hi everyone,

Yesterday's OWASP meeting was great (thanks to Brett Moore and Beau
Butler) and many new people attended. Also big thanks to Vodafone and to
Andrew Davey for the venue, food and drinks in Auckland. Thanks to
Security-Assessment.com for sponsoring in Wellington.

I hope all of you enjoyed yesterday night session. If you have any feedback,
please feel free to drop me an email.

We are always looking to make things better. If you know people who might be
interested to attend the next meeting, please invite them to join the
OWASP maling-list. Subscription can be done here:
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-newzealand

I also cross-post OWASP announcements to the NZ ISIG mailing-list where you
are more than welcome to subscribe as well (it's low traffic and there are
also announcements of the NZ ISIG meetings). Just send an email to:
discussion-subscribe at lists.isig.org.nz

OWASP NZ Web site: http://www.owasp.org/index.php/New_Zealand

Thanks again for attending,
