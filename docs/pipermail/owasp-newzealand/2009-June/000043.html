Hi,



I am glad to announce the first round of speakers that have been selected
for the OWASP NZ Day conference.


* Dean Carter - Security-Assessment.com - "Where Worlds Collide" - PCI-DSS
for OWASP practioners”
* Paul Craig – Security-Assessment.com - “Insecurity and the Internet”
* Brett Moore – Insomnia Security - “Vulnerabilities In Action”

* Roberto Suggi Liverani / Nick Freeman – Security-Assessment.com -
“Exploiting Firefox Extensions”

* Nick von Dadelszen – Lateral Security - “Testing Web Services”



The conference web site has been updated with a speakers section:



http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2009#tab=Speakers<http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2009>



Please note that CFP (Call for Paper) is still open and it will close on the
15th June. There are still available slots for talks.



For more information about the CFP and submission, please refer to my
previous post:



https://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/owasp-newzealand/2009-March/000039.html



I am delighted to announce that 111 people registered so far to attend the
event.



This is an excellent result for OWASP in New Zealand and thanks for
spreading the voice.



If you are reading this post and you haven't registered yet, please do it by
visiting:



http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2009



Please feel free to invite other people who might be interested to join us.
The event registration will end on the 20th June 2009.



For those of you using LinkedIn, please feel free to join the group "OWASP
New Zealand Chapter" at:



http://www.linkedin.com/groups?gid=1966105



Again thanks to everyone for helping the OWASP NZ chapter. Special thanks to
the University of Auckland for providing the venue, and to
Security-Assessment.com and Lateral Security for sponsoring the event.



The final list of speakers and the conference agenda will be published on
the 20th June.



Cheers,



Roberto Suggi Liverani

OWASP NZ Leader
