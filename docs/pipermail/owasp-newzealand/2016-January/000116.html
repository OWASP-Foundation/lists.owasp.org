Hi All.

General registration for OWASP NZ Day 2016 is now open.
The conference will be held at the University of Auckland on the 4th of
February.
The main conferences is, as always, free and spaces are limited but we
would love to see you there. More details can be found here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2016

Call for presentations and trainings is now closed and selections have
been made. Thanks for all who submitted talks and trainings. We've got
an excellent looking line-up that will start showing up on the
conference site very soon.


We would also like to announce this years sponsors which allow us to
keep the conference free and accessible to as many as possible.
Our main sponsors are:

* Insomnia Security
* Aura (RedShield)
* Security-Assessment.com

Followed by Quantum and Wynyard as silver sponsors, Binary Mist as a
support sponsor.


Keep an eye out for an announcement of the paid training sessions to be
held on Wednesday 3rd of February, and the speaker line-up for the 4th.
Please feel free to pass this email on to anyone you think might be
interested!


On behalf of OWASP New Zealand
Thanks.
