Hi everyone,

This is a reminder for those who would like to join us this evening at
the OWASP meeting.

We are also really glad to announce that we have another speaker at the
meeting this evening. Mark Piper, senior security consultant from
Wellington will be presenting "On the job browser exploitation".

Level 1, 12 Queen Street, Auckland.

indication will be placed in front of the main door of the Britomart
building as well. Please enter the building from Queen Street side.
