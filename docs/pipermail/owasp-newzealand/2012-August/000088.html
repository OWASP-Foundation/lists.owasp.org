Hi Everyone,

It is with great pleasure that we can announce the speakers and trainers
for OWASP New Zealand Day 2012 on the 31st of August.

Please find the list of speakers on the conference website:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2012#Speakers_List

We have two training courses available on the 30th of August provided by
Aura Information Security and Pentesterlab. Both training courses will
be run on Thursday August 30th, 2012. For more information about the
course material, length, pricing and presenters, please view them here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_New_Zealand_Day_2012#Training -
training places are very limited so get in soon!

We would also like to extend many thanks to all of our sponsors, without
whom we could not run this conference at no cost to you, the attendee.
Thanks very much everyone!

Our Gold Sponsors are:
* Security-Assessment.com - http://www.security-assessment.com
* Aura Information Security in partnership with F5 -
http://www.aurainfosec.com

Our Silver Sponsors are:
* Insomnia Security - http://www.insomniasec.com
* Lateral Security - http://www.lateralsecurity.com
* Web Drive - http://www.webdrive.co.nz

And of course a big thank you to the University of Auckland Business
School who have kindly provided us with a fantastic venue for the fourth
year.


We have less than 1/3 of our seats left - so if you haven't registered,
please do so now! The URL for registration is:
http://www.regonline.com/owaspnzday2012 - please note that this is the
same URL used for training registration.

For any further information, please contact nick.freeman at owasp.org and
adrian.hayes at owasp.org.


Kind Regards,
Nick Freeman & Adrian Hayes
OWASP New Zealand Chapter Leaders
