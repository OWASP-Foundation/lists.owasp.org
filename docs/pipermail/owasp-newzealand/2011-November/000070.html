Speakers
--
We have two speakers organised for this chapter meeting:

Speaker 1: Adrian Hayes (Security-Assessment.com)
Title: Introduction to the OWASP Top Ten - Part 2
Abstract: This is the second in a series of 3 presentations that will
introduce the OWASP Top Ten risks to developers. The target audience
for this presentation is developers new to OWASP or unfamiliar with
secure coding practices and the risks that web applications face on a
daily basis.
Part 2 will cover items 4 through 6 of the OWASP Top Ten: Insecure
Direct Object Reference, Cross Site Request Forgery (CSRF) and
Security Misconfiguration.

Speaker 2: Quintin Russ (SiteHost)
Title: Hardened Hosting
Abstract: Every day web applications are measured and found
vulnerable. Developers and end users get the blame, while the server
is completely ignored and the role it can play in reducing the attack
surface. We will look at what real world bugs can be mitigated today
while maintaining the functionality of today's web applications.
