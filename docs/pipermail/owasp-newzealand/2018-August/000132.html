The Auckland-area OWASP New Zealand Meetup will next meet tomorrow evening,
14 August, at 6:30 p.m.

We'll meet in the main conference room at Orion Health, 181 Grafton Road,
Grafton (
https://www.google.co.nz/maps/place/181+Grafton+Rd,+Grafton,+Auckland+1010).
Orion has kindly agreed to let us use their facility for our meetings, at
no charge.

This month's meeting will include two presentation/discussion sessions:
- OWASP Top Ten A3: Sensitive Data Exposure
- Live Web Application Penetration Testing Demo, by Shofe Miraz

After our meeting, all are welcome to come along to the Uptown Freehouse (
https://www.uptownfreehouse.com/) for continued conversation and
refreshment.

To register your interest in attending, please visit our Meetup page:
https://www.meetup.com/OWASP-New-Zealand-Chapter-Auckland/events/249448651/
