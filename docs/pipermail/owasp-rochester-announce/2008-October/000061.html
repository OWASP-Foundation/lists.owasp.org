We are pleased to announce our first seminar of the 2008-2009 season.  This
one day seminar will begin with a general introduction to Server
Virtualization.  The remainder of the course will focus specifically on
Microsoft's Hyper-V virtualization technology including lecture, discussion
and hands on labs.

Topics include:

1.	Introduction to Server Virtualization 
2.	Uses of virtualization 
3.	Configuring Hyper-V: Virtual machines, virtual networks, virtual
hard drives. 

Our Instructor: Jim Spignardo

Jim is a full-time employee of New Horizons of Syracuse where he has been
the lead instructor for technical training since the franchise opened its
doors.   In the past three years he has managed the Mentored Learning
program, an innovative learning program pioneered by New Horizons.
