Just a reminder:

The Rochester OWASP Chapter is participating in Digital Rochester’s*/ 
Get Connected!/* networking event.*/ Get Connected!/* is an event for 
not-for-profit networking & service organizations around the region. 
There will be over twenty Rochester networking organizations at the 
event and in past years, this event has drawn 300-400 attendees.

_Please note the following event details for Digital Rochester’s__ 
 _*/_Get Connected!_/*_:_

Date:                Tuesday, October 6th, 2009

Time:                5:30 pm – 7:30 pm  (*Open for set-up at 4:45 pm*)

Location:           Village Gate  Square– 2nd Floor Atrium
                         274 North Goodman Street
                         Rochester, NY 14607-1154

No Charge to Attend

_Refreshments:_    Light refreshments will be available on a first come, 
first served basis.  Free beer will be provided courtesy of High Falls 
Brewery and a cash bar will be available.  Restaurants in the complex 
offer some great places for groups to stop for dinner as they leave the 
event.
