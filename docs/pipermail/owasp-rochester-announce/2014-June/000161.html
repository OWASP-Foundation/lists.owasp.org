discuss plans and ideas for meeting and activities the rest of the
year.  We'll meet at a local eatery, to be announced. Our chapter will

I'd like to discuss:

* Presentation ideas and offers for a future OWASP meeting.
* Suggestions and offers for future chapter meeting locations
* Presentation or training ideas for Rochester Security Summit

* Application security topics and issues.
