We prepared a quick survey that you can fill out online on SurveyMonkey: http://www.surveymonkey.com/s/MTBH3S2

If you prefer to reply by email, please answer the questions below.

It will only take 5 minutes of your time but it will help us a lot!

Looking forward to seeing you at the next OWASP meeting.
