The Rochester ISSA Chapter is holding another free Social event on
Tuesday July 22nd at Mulconry's Irish Pub & Restaurant on Lift Bridge
Lane in Fairport, and they are inviting OWASP members to attend.   
Please come out and enjoy an evening of conversation with your fellow
professionals.

The event will run from 6-9 PM and Appetizers will be provided courtesy
of Invincea.  There will be a cash bar.

Thanks to our sponsor Invincea, this event is free for all ISSA Chapter
members and our friends of OWASP, ISACA, and Infragard.  
*
*
*Please RSVP by July 18th to membership at rocissa.org*
