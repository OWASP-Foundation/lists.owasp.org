I think most of the time it is done by recovery agencies. They use the
technique of pretending as courier service to grab the address of the
defaulter and then sends court's summon or may be recovery agents. One need
not to default something to become defaulter, there have been many incidents
in the past where a mobile number/credit card/etc. is allocated to a fake
person using identification of some real person. These recovery teams are
well equipped with all internet techniques like google/social
networks/naukri.com etc. so finding the history of a well connected person
isn't a great deal. I don't see it a big problem. Let them try their fate
and if they find you, make their life miserable. Next time they ring you up,
pass on my address ;-)
