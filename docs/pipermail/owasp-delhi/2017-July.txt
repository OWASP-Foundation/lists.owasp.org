Hi OWASPians,

We are glad to inform you that we are hosting the first edition of Security
BSides conference in Delhi (India) on October 27, 2017. The 2017 edition of
BSides Delhi includes talks, workshops, sub-events, contests, exhibition,
CTF etc. Security BSides is a global framework of community conferences.

There are currently more than 60+ individually organised BSides events
worldwide which are organised every year. This is the first year of
Security BSides Delhi and we are hoping to see a lot of interesting content
and participation from the security community.

The call for papers is open: https://bsidesdelhi.in/cfp2017/

We are looking for some awesome submissions from OWASP Delhi members in the
following categories:

Technical Talks (30-35 minutes)
Workshops (3-4 Hours)
Sub-events / Villages (2-4 Hours)
Rookie Track (15-20 minutes)

For any questions related to CFP write to cfp at bsidesdelhi.in

We are also looking for sponsors. For sponsorship inquiries please write to
sponsors at bsidesdelhi.in (sponsorship document attached)

For any general questions/suggestions feel free to write to us at
info at bsidesdelhi.in

Ticket Sales to open soon (There will be discounts for OWASP Delhi members.)

Feel free to spread the word.

Thanks
Saumya Vishnoi
BSides Delhi Organizing team
