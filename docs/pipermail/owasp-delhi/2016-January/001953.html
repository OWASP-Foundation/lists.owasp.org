Hi all,

Announcing the OWASP Delhi chapter January meet on 30th January, 2016
(Saturday) - 11:00 AM IST

Note – All OWASP chapter meets are free and open to everyone. Prior
registrations are mandatory in order to be allowed to attend the meet.

Please RSVP/register here
http://bit.ly/1P0I172

Details:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Delhi_NCR

*Session Details/Agenda:*
-----------------------------------------
11:00 AM - 11:15 AM: News Bytes by Arpit Maheshwari
11:15 AM - 11:30 AM: Introduction
11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Threat Intelligence & Incident Response Revisited by
Sandeep Singh

Abstract: This talk focuses on concepts of Threat Intelligence and Incident
Response. The talk will introduce the key concepts and practices of threat
intelligence and incident response to attendees. The broad list of topics
include (but not limited to):

- What is Threat Intelligence?
- Type of Threat Intelligence?
- Intelligence Lifecycle
- Threat Intelligence - Classification & Vendor Landscape
- Threat Intelligence Standards (STIX, TAXII, etc.)
- Open Source Threat Intel Tools/Processes
- Incident Response
- Role of Threat Intel in Incident Response
- Bonus Agenda

P.S. - This talk has been cancelled couple of times in the last few months.
This is an enhanced version of the previous talks

12:30 PM - 12:45 PM: Tea/Coffee/Networking Break
12:45 PM – 01:30 PM: SIEM - Activating Defense through Response by Ankur
Vats
01:30 PM - 02:00 PM: Work in Singapore - A Special Session for Security
Pros by Contact Singapore
02:00 PM - 2:15 PM: Feedback and Topic Discussion for Next Month
