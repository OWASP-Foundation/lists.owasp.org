Hi all,



Announcing the combined OWASP/null Delhi chapter August meet on *31st
August, 2014 (Sunday) - 04:00 PM IST*



*Note -* All OWASP and null Delhi meets are free and open to everyone.
Prior registrations are mandatory in order to be allowed to attend the
meet.



Please RSVP/register here - http://bit.ly/1tc1Lcg



Further details about venue and agenda can be obtained at the below link

*Session Details/Agenda:*

*-----------------------------------------*

4:00 PM - 4:15 PM: Introductions by *null and OWASP Delhi Team*

4:15 PM - 4:30 PM: News Bytes and What's New by *null and OWASP Delhi Team*

4:30 PM - 5:30 PM: All Your Slot-Tables are Belong to us by *Shubham Mittal*

5:30 PM - 5:45 PM: Tea/Coffee/Networking Break

5:45 PM - 6:45 PM: Android Security & Pentesting 101 by *Sunil Anand*

6:45 PM - 7:00 PM: Feedback and Topic Discussion for Next Month *by null
and OWASP Delhi Team*



*When:* 31st August, 2014 (Sunday) - 04:00 PM IST



*Where:* Adobe Systems (Near Spice Mall)

I-1A, City Center, Sector-25A,

Noida – 201301



*Google Maps: *http://goo.gl/13WyMa



*Landmark:* 200 meters ahead of Spice Mall
