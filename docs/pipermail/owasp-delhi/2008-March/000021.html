Dear OWASP Members,

 

It is indeed my pleasure to share exciting news about OWASP - Delhi Chapter.
Mr. Puneet Mehta is OWASP - Delhi Chapter Co-Leader and will help us in
promoting our local activities in NCR area. I met him last day and we talked
on various strategies for taking this group to next level. If all goes well
then sooner you will notice lots of happenings in OWASP - Delhi Chapter.
Below is Puneet's short profile for your consideration.

 

 

 

Puneet Mehta, CISA CISSP CEH CPTS BS7799-2002 LA

 

Puneet Mehta is the Manager and Principal Consultant of information security
& risk management services, at SDG India. He has over 12 years of experience
in architecting, building and integrating enterprise security solutions and
compliance frameworks for Fortune 500 clients ranging from banking, finance,
logistics, aviation, IT, manufacturing and other industry verticals. He has
published over 10 articles in leading technology publications and online
Internet media on various technology areas ranging from network security to
compliance frameworks.

 

He currently sits on the board of ISACA New Delhi chapter as a Director,
leading various chapter initiatives.

 

 

 

Join me in wishing him great luck to accomplish OWASP plans.

 

Many Thanks,

Dhruv Soi

OWASP - Delhi Chapter Leader
