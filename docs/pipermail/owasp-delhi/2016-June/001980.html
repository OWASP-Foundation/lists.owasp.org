Hi all,

Announcing the OWASP Delhi chapter June meet on 18th June, 2016 (Saturday)
- 10:30 AM IST

Note – All OWASP chapter meets are free and open to everyone. Prior
registrations are mandatory in order to be allowed to attend the meet.

*Please RSVP/register here:* https://goo.gl/4dJCKd

*Details:* https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Delhi_NCR

*Session Details/Agenda:*
-----------------------------------------

*10:30 AM - 11:00 AM:* Registration & Networking

*11:00 AM - 11:15 AM:* Introductions & Chapter Updates

*11:15 AM - 11:30 AM:* InfoSec News Bytes by Mohammed Zuber

*11:30 AM - 12:10 PM*: Mapping your digital footprint by Aakash Goel

Abstract: This session would specifically deal with privacy issues
pertaining to an individual's digital presence and how to map it well, in
order to keep a track of it.

*12:10 PM - 12:40 PM:* Quick & Dirty Forensic Techniques by Kamal Ranjan

*12:40 PM - 01:00 PM:* Tea/Coffee/Networking Break

*01:00 PM – 01:40 PM:* Meet the DIVA - Part 1 by Ankit Prateek

​Abstract: DIVA (Damn insecure and vulnerable App) is an App intentionally
designed to be insecure. DIVA is a great way to learn android pentesting by
finding the vulnerabilities deliberately created in the App.

This is the first part of the walk through of challenges covered in DIVA
(Damn insecure and vulnerable App). We will cover the following challenges
in the first part of the talk:

1. Insecure Logging
2. Hardcoding Issues – Part 1
3. Insecure Data Storage – Part 1
4. Insecure Data Storage – Part 2
5. Insecure Data Storage – Part 3
6. Insecure Data Storage – Part 4

*01:40 PM - 2:00 PM:* Feedback and Topic Discussion for Next Month

*When:* 18th June, 2016 (Saturday) - 10:30 AM IST (Please be on time)

*Where:* NEA Auditorium,
B-110, Next to Centre for Cyber Crime Investigation,
Sector 6, Noida 201301 (UP)

*Landmark:* Near Paytm Office

- Auto available from Noida Sector 15 metro station to Noida Authority
