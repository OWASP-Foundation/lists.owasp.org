Greetings OWASP Members!
After the successful launch of OWASP in India with OWASP AppSec India 
Conference 2008, we would like to continue with the spirit of getting 
together for common goal of fighting with the insecure code. With your 
active support and participation, we intend to plan out monthly OWASP 
meetings in the NCR region. Participation into these meetings will be free 
of cost and is open to everyone whosoever is concerned about application 
security. If you have any burning AppSec topic that you would like to 
share with others or you want to demonstrate the power of some AppSec 
assessment/educational tool that helped you in keeping your application 
secured, then you may submit your presentation in the attached template to 
Delhi Chapter Board. 
These meetings will be of short duration (3hrs) occurring on weekends 
(most likely Saturday evening). Incase, your company would like to sponsor 
these meetings in terms of venue and snacks accommodating 70-100 
participants then kindly let us know. Your company logo will be carried 
over the chapter meeting page on OWASP website as a prime sponsor. 
CFP (Call For Papers) is open for upcoming OWASP Delhi Chapter Meeting, so 
submit your papers now!! Venue and timing for this particular meeting is 
yet to be finalized and we hope to fix it up for mid of October 2008. 
Please feel free to share your thoughts over the same to help the 
community taking maximum benefits out of such meetings.
Many Thanks,
Dhruv
OWASP Delhi Board
