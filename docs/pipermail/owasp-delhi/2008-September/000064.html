Hi Surinder,
 
I think we got confused here. I have the link to the presentations in
pdf format.
I was referring to the participation certificates so that we can keep
the same as a record for claiming CPE (continuing professional
education) points with ISC2 and ISACA. Also in most ISC2 sponsored
events the CPEs are calculated and added automatically to your ISC2
member id.
On day 2, I saw a lot of participation certificates lying on the owasp
representative tables and I was told the same will be duly signed by the
presenters and mailed to all the attendees. If physical copies cannot be
mailed, I would expect pdfs scans of the certificates (for day 1 and day
2) for our record.
For claiming CPEs, both ISC2 and ISACA mandate retaining a record which
indicates the timelines of the event as well as the fact that one
satisfactorily attended the same.

Greetings OWASP Members!

After the successful launch of OWASP in India with OWASP AppSec India
Conference 2008, we would like to continue with the spirit of getting
together for common goal of fighting with the insecure code. With your
active support and participation, we intend to plan out monthly OWASP
meetings in the NCR region. Participation into these meetings will be
free of cost and is open to everyone whosoever is concerned about
application security. If you have any burning AppSec topic that you
would like to share with others or you want to demonstrate the power of
some AppSec assessment/educational tool that helped you in keeping your
application secured, then you may submit your presentation in the
attached template to Delhi Chapter Board. 

These meetings will be of short duration (3hrs) occurring on weekends
(most likely Saturday evening). Incase, your company would like to
sponsor these meetings in terms of venue and snacks accommodating 70-100
participants then kindly let us know. Your company logo will be carried
over the chapter meeting page on OWASP website as a prime sponsor. 

CFP (Call For Papers) is open for upcoming OWASP Delhi Chapter Meeting,
so submit your papers now!! Venue and timing for this particular meeting
is yet to be finalized and we hope to fix it up for mid of October 2008.


Please feel free to share your thoughts over the same to help the
community taking maximum benefits out of such meetings.

Many Thanks,

Dhruv

OWASP Delhi Board

Share the below link with your contacts to help them becoming part of
this fastest growing community in your region.

OWASP Delhi Mailing List:
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-delhi
