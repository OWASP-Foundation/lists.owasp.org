Dear Fellow OWASPers,

Its time to bring the Delhi chapter out of long period of dormancy!
Here is what has been done/planned till date:

1) The mailing list will be moderated actively. Priyanka Tomar posted a
query earlier today and we felt that her timing was perfect! Why so?
    >> This mail not only serves as introductory mail from our end but also
as a detailed response to her query.

2) A new Wiki page has been setup and will have regular updates -
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Delhi_NCR

3) Chapter meets will become a norm instead of an exception. The revival
meet is being planned for July 2014.

But, all of this effort will not sustain unless there is active
contribution from the community. Its for the community after all! Help
could be in the form of assisting with organizing the chapter meet,
stepping up to conduct a session on some topic, finding venues, good
technical discussions on the mailing list etc. Matter-of-fact, if there's
an existing/new OWASP project a bunch of us could contribute to, bring it
up.

Whether you're simply interested in AppSec/seasoned pro. interested in
giving back to the community/interested in networking, join the bandwagon!
Fun and learning along the way is assured. :-)

Feel free to reach out to us in case you wish to talk or discuss something
