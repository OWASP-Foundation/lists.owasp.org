> Some thoughts:
>
>
> 0. Are u going to encrypt the contents of the cookie? Because if u r having
> the number of attempts etc in the cookie, they could be modified by anyone.
> 1. Are you planning to have the captcha text within the cookie? If so,
> that?s a bad idea.
> 2. The normal rule in general is that the user account needs to be locked
> after some predefined number of invalid attempts. If the account is not
> locked, you are only increasing the scope for trying out still different
> passwords. This will give the attacker to try breaking in even after several
> attempts. Instead of that, you can flag the account for locking and
> disabling. That will at least try to prevent an unauthorized entry into the
> system
> 3. I would not still suggest CAPTCHA & re-login after X attempts as you are
> giving the attacker more chance to meddle around with the system.
