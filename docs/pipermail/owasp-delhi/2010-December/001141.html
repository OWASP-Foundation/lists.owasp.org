SCIPP International ( www.scippinternational.org ) offers two courses that are accredited by ANSI.

 

The first course is directed at application developers and related technical staff and is a 4-hour, on-line course that satisfies 6.5 of the PCI-DSS and 5.2 of the PA-DSS that introduces the participant to the current OWASP Top-10.

 

The second course is geared to general end-users and is a combination of a general awareness course on best business practices (traditional security awareness) combined with a Payment Card specific module, that meets the training requirements found in section 12 of the PCI-DSS.

 

You can contact me directly if you need additional information.
