It has been found that some Chinese websites of domain .gov.cn are hosting
pages that exploit the the recent IE 0-day vulnerability.The same
vulnerability was exploited to compromise Google, Adobe, and other vendors
and also known as 'Aurora'.
 .wscz.gov.cn, .zhepb.gov.cn, .jssalt.gov.cn are few more websites where the
malware is being hosted.
It is said that due to intention of spying its own citizens, Chinese Govt is
sponsoring it. Or might be the case that these are also exploited by the
hackers.
