AKJ Associates focus exclusively on global risk and security matters and have 8 years experience <http://www.e-crimecongress.org/india/website.asp?page=About_the_organisers> in developing and running international conferences and forums specifically in this niche .  Over the last 7 years the e-Crime Congress has established itself as one of Europe's most influential cyber crime forums, welcoming over 550 senior level delegates from over 40 countries <http://www.e-crimecongress.org/india/website.asp?page=History> .













An agenda-rich forum:

[cid:image026.gif at 01CA9B54.F18517B0]








Spread across 2 full days, the event is divided into plenary sessions and a dedicated series of education seminars that form part of the main agenda.  Plenary sessions are designed to provide delegates with  practical, strategic and tactical knowledge, complimented by education seminars which offer hands-on technical know-how. This format ensures that information security stakeholders are provided with all the relevant information they need on how to manage threats and overcome challenges and allow attendees to tailor their conference programme and take home the best solutions for their business.













Key Themes:

[cid:image026.gif at 01CA9B54.F18517B0]




[cid:image028.gif at 01CA9B54.F18517B0]<http://www.vcindia.com/e-mailer-of_ecrime/conf_e-crime_jan11-OWASP_emailer.asp#top>

 *   Increasing productivity by deploying proactive, strategic, and cost-effective security processes
 *   Reducing risk of data loss posed by business processes, network, and employee focused threats
 *   Evolving and harmonising security technologies to tackle new challenges
 *   Protecting against key vectors of attack such as web and mobile application vulnerabilities
 *   Monitoring business processes to identify where critical risks exist
 *   Introducing greater information security efficiency at enterprise level
 *   Meeting regulatory compliance and corporate governance requirements effectively
