Dear All,

 

There have been few requests stating that mailing list is gradually becoming
high volume list with more than required discussions on a single topic. For
those with same opinion, just wanted to highlight the option available under
your subscription configuration, you may follow below steps to change your
subscription options and customize the mail flow as per your comfort.

 

1.       Open https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-delhi

2.       Login your email and password. If you don't remember your password,
use forgot password option

3.       Under different available options, turn on "Set Digest Mode" to
reduce the mail flow to your INBOX. 

4.       Save the options and logout. You may read through different
available options for further customization.

 

For any comments/suggestions in this regard, please connect with me offline
to reduce the traffic J

 

Many Thanks,

Dhruv
