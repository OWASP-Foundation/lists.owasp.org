Sameer, I dont understand what you mean by protection against 'Turing 
Test'.
If you are looking at protection against automated password-guessing 
attacks then its pretty simple.
The counter to display a captha or lock the account is not based on the IP 
or the cookie.
It depends on the user account.
If you get lets say five failed login attempts on a particular username 
then act on it, dont even bother with the IP and cookie.

If you were looking for something else then it might help to give more 
details.
