The Executive Seminar Series on Application Security is held on 22nd & 29th
March 2009 (Sunday) in Delhi at Hotel Oberoi and the timings are  from 10:00

mission-critical applications -- both the technologies you'll need, as well
as the policies and procedures you would be well advised to wrap around

technology and the manner in which it is deployed - it also places an equal
emphasis on managing how that technology is used.
