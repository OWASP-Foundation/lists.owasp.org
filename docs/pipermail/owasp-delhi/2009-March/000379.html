> Dear All,
>
> After reading comments from different Experts i created a summary for this
> important attack:
>
> *Condition-1*
>
> *Both vulnerable website and e-mail account are opened within the mini
> tabs of the same parent browser:*
>
> In this case as a user clicks upon the crafted link in e-mail either by
>
> > right-clicking and opening it in in New Tab or in New Window
> > just click it simply
>
> In both the cases the XSS attack will occur. Again no matter which browser
> you are using.
>
> *Condition-2*
>
> Both vulnerable website and e-mail account are opened within the Fresh
> Browsers Windows (and no new tab).
> In this case as a user clicks upon the crafted link in e-mail either by
>
> > right-clicking and opening it in in New Tab or in New Window
> > just click it simply
>
> *The attack will occur only if:*
>
> -> A Browser is used which shares cookies for the same domain opened across
> different fresh browsers windows. ie: Chrome and Firefox 3.0.x handles
> cookies in same way even in tabs/Windows.
>
> *However many important websites especially financial do not allow users
> to open two instances of the same site for the same user which is an
> important security feature.*
> **
> *Please let me know if i am wrong at any point.
> *
