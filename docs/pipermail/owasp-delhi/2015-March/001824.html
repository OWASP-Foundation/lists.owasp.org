Hi all,

Announcing the combined OWASP/null Delhi chapter March 2015 meet on 28th
March, 2015 (Saturday) - 03:00 PM IST

Note – All OWASP and null meets are free and open to everyone. Prior
registrations are mandatory in order to be allowed to attend themeet.

Please RSVP/register here
http://bit.ly/1ElPILK

*Session Details/Agenda:*
-----------------------------------------
3:00 PM - 3:15 PM: Introductions
3:15 PM - 3:30 PM: News Bytes
3:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Need of Positive approach in the field of Information
Security by Paramveer Singh
4:00 PM - 4:30 PM: Beyond the Virtual World - Physical Security and its
importance by Saumya Vishnoi
4:30 PM - 4:45 PM: Tea/Coffee/Networking Break
4:45 PM - 5:45 PM: Cryptographic Implementations in Software Security by
Arju
5:45 PM - 6:00 PM: Feedback and Topic Discussion for Next Month

*When:* 28th March, 2015 (Saturday) - 03:00 PM IST (Please be on time)

Sector 21,

Dundahera, Gurgaon 122016
Haryana, India

*Nearest Landmark:* Trident Hotel.(Straight from Trident Hotel towards

*How to Reach Venue:* Take Rapid Metro from Sikanderpur Metro station to
