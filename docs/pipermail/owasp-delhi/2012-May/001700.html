No Source Code analyzer (or any other tool for that matter) is 100%
effective as it misses out lots of issues and provides lots of false
positives also. Even Fortify will report silly false positives. Manual
inspection and validation is essentially required. Tools must be used as
complimentary option. But I have found Fortify the best in its calls, dues
to its coverage and flexibility of use. They are clearly a leader in the
market.
