Folks,

 

OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project - www.owasp.org
<http://www.owasp.org/> ) Delhi chapter is proud to announce first ever
grand OWASP event in India with capacity of 500 delegates, one day
conference & exclusive CXOs Power Submit and one day multi-track Advanced
AppSec trainings/workshops encompassing live demonstrations with 6 different
sessions on advanced AppSec topics to be conducted by most respectable
Application Security Gurus of international repute.

 

Please visit OWASP Delhi home page at: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Delhi
to collect further information about this event. 

 

OWASP members (subscribed before 31st June 2008) will have special discounts
to attend conference/training sessions in OWASP AppSec India Conference
2008. So, encourage your friends/colleagues/contacts to get subscribed at
our mailing list available at
http://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-delhi before they loose this
opportunity. Online registrations will open from 15th June 2008.

 

Would you like:

*      to understand why the applications developed by your/partner company
are insecure?

*      to watch and understand, how attackers are gaining access to your
confidential information by exploiting the application security bugs without
your knowledge?

*      to understand how other companies manager their enterprise
application security program?

*      to understand how do experts protect the private and proprietary
information of their companies? 

*      to establish Application Security Processes and/or Team in your
company?

*      your Developers/Architects/IT/Information Security/QA teams to
recognize and fix application security related issues?

*      to learn application security right from the scratch?

*      to gain grip over advanced application security topics?

*      to know industry trends and challenges in application security?

*      to adopt and promote OWASP standards inside your organization?

*      to launch a new security/software product?

*      to network with CXOs (exclusive CXO power summit - special invite
only) and build relationships with them?

*      to gain visibility in the industry by promoting your company's brand
in-front of the right audience through right channel by sponsoring this
event?

*      to proudly showcase your commitment to information security by
delivering secure code to clients by sponsoring this event?

*      to get uniquely identified in the industry as a Security savvy
organization by sponsoring this event?

*      to get recognized globally as an OWASP supporter by sponsoring  this
event?

 

OWASP AppSec India Conference 2008 is the right platform for you to have
answers for all the above questions.
