Hi All
Its my pleasure to inform you that we have announced a new e-magazine on 
7th Feb during the nullcon hackers conference in Goa.
http://chmag.clubhack.com

Remember its not a Information Security Magazine, we are trying to keep 
it technical and more geeky in nature.

We have started this magazine with following sections to start with.
0x00 Tech Gyan of the month
0x01 Legal Gyan of the month
0x02 Command Line Gyan of the month
0x03 Tool Gyan of the month
0x04 Mom's Guide of the month
0x05 Awareness Poster of the month

In future we'll add more sections as per the demand of the community.
Hope to see a lot of contribution from you all for the magazine.
