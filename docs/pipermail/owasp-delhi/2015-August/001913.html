*Session Details/Agenda:*
-----------------------------------------
*11:00 AM - 11:15 AM: Introductions*
*11:15 AM - 11:30 PM: News Bytes by Shantanu Goel*

*11:30 PM - 12:15 PM: Setting up a cost effective Application Security
program from scratch by Tusnin Das and Ram K Awasthi*

*Abstract:* Bugged by the astronomical price of the enterprise SAST and
DAST tools how difficult it is for a mid-size/ small size organisation to
set up their own secure SDLC program, the speakers came up with their own
solution which would help any organisation to set up a cost-effective
application security program

*About the Speakers:* *Tusnin Das *is a security professional with 14 years
of extensive experience in the area of application security. He is
currently working as a security researcher in Times Internet Limited. He
has worked in a leading global financial institution and was instrumental
in setting up its application security program. He has also filed several
patients in the field of information security. His interest area also
includes application scaling and artiﬁcial intelligence

*Ram K Awasthi* is a security professional with 14 years of extensive
experience in the area of application development and application security.
He currently heads the security program in Times Internet Limited. He has
worked for several internet product companies and has been instrumental in
building many of India’s prominent internet portals. His interest area also
includes application performance, scalability and machine learning.

*12:15 PM - 01:00 PM: Undersurf: Dark Reading by Akash Goel*

*Abstract: *

   - DarkWeb, DeepWeb and DarkNet?

Brief Coverage of the concept, need and components for elementary knowledge.

   - Constituents: Information, Services and Access Tools

Covers the various components of the information and services contained
within DeepWeb and the basics on how to get to access to it.

   - Crypto-anarchism and Transhumanism; Ashley Madison and Hacking Team
   Leaks
      - Description about the terror and anarchy, these dark services have
      led to. Their linkage to the recent hacks and growing reach/impact.
      - Bitcoins
   - Effect on the Globe: Economy and Harmony
      - Cyber Espionage and Political Disruptions
         - Hidden Wiki
         - Wiki Leaks
         - Exploits and Weaponry
         - .onion e-commerce and terrorism
         - Silk Road
         - IS and other militant groups
      - Not all Dark is depraved
      - The Dark Web: An Untapped Source For Threat Intelligence
      - Dark web doesn't have to be evil
   - More info references and suggested readings
      - Online Reading and Practical Suggestions
         - Tor
         - Reference News and Studies
      - Relatable Fictions/Stories/Books
         - God is a Gamer – Ravi Subramanium
