Dear all
ClubHack is pleased to announce the opening of CFP for ClubHack2009. 
ClubHack is India's own international hackers' convention started in 2007.

We are expecting good deep knowledge technical 
presentations/demonstrations on topics from the world of Information 
Security.

These presentations are expected to be of 40 minutes each. The schedule 
time for each presenter would be 50 minutes out of which 40 minutes are 
for the presentation & 10 for the question-answer sessions. We'd request 
you to submit the papers keeping the time constraint in mind.

For indicative list of topics and more information, please visit
http://clubhack.com/2009/CFP



Cheers,
team ClubHack
