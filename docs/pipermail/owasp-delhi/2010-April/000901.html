This is all about mitigating the risk..  (If there is no control in
place, there is risk. If all the relevant controls are in place, still
there would be some risk).
 
That's depends upon the business, how they would like to proceed with RA
(Risk Assessment). If they find the application is critical to business
they will put extra control in place like having SSL, if the application
has least severity in terms of impacting the business.. they may not go
with the SSL.
 
I hope this will clarify your doubts.
