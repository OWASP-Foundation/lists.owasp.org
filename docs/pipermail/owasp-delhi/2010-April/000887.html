This is a fairly old and ongoing effort on the part of Chinese. I am not very sure that it is supported by some official agency, but seeing the amount of effort involved and the resources available, it is hard not to believe so. During my association with the Govt the impression that I got was that while we were very much aware of these activities and the steps required to protect ourselves, but the inherent inertia in govt organisations prevented any proactive approach. 

Of the agencies chartered to protect our critical information infrastructure, the less said the better. One of these issued an advisory against Lenovo laptops bringing out a malicious executable of which the examiner knew nothing about. Google research proved the "offending" file to be a sound card driver !!!
