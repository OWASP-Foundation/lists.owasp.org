It is a very good question and i have seen organisations do not have a clear
cut idea weather they should implement SSL inside organisation or not.
            The answer for this question comes straight from Risk Assessment
& Risk Management. If You have an important application into your
organisation which is authenticated by passwords then you have the risk of
sniffing passwords. Now, how do you manage this risk?  By applying some kind
of control. And implementing SSL is one of the control .
            So weather to implement SSL or create VLAN for segregation of
traffic depends on Risk Assessment and Risk Management.
