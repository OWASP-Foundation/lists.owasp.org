> Below are couple of updates from OWASP India that I would like to share
> with the members:
>
> 1. Following the OWASP GIC's (Global Industry Committee) initiative, *OWASP
> India Advisory Board* has been formed and we are pleased to find interest
> from industry known information security leaders to join OWASP's mission.
> Please feel free to nominate InfoSec leaders from your contact list to join
> us as an advisor. We need to get maximum participation from the industry and
> Govt. of India to align our mission of "finding and fighting with the
> insecure code" with the industry and regulatory needs. We seek their regular
> feedback and guidance by closely working with them.
>
> 2. *Organizing Committee for OWASP AppSec Asia Conference 2009 *has also
> been formed and we are glad to see the volunteer participation from the
> InfoSec community to put the efforts in making it a successful event of
> 2009. Your contribution is important to us and if you feel that you could
> help us in driving the mission better, please contact us to be the part of
> the committee.
>
> 3. *Event Agenda *has been updated on the OWASP wiki and more will be
> uploaded as soon as we keep confirming with the speakers/trainers.
