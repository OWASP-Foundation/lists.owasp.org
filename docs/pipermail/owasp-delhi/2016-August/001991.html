Fellow OWASPians,

Contact Singapore is an alliance of the Singapore Economic Development
Board and Ministry of Manpower. They engage overseas Singaporeans and
global talent to work, invest and live in Singapore.

They are holding an informational session titled 'Working in Singapore:
Cyber Security, Data Analytics & Electronics (IC Design)'. This series of
information sessions provides you with insights into Singapore’s key
industries, the work pass framework and career opportunities. Join their
Area Director, Mr Gabriel Lim, as he provides a snapshot of the Infocomm
and Electronics scene in Singapore and addresses questions that you may
have on working and living in Singapore. Gabriel is based in Singapore and
will be presenting this session via video conference.

Date: 30 August 2016, Tuesday
Time: 6.30 pm to 9.00 pm
Venue: Chennai
Unit 402, Omega Wing Raheja Towers,
177 Anna Salai Chennai 600 002
Mumbai

Singapore Centre, 216, The Capital Building,
Plot no. C-70, Block G, Bandra Kurla Complex,
Bandra East, Mumbai 400051

Those based in other cities will be able to dial in via video conference.

Visit the link to register and join their event.
http://bit.ly/CSWISV-India-30Aug2016

Admission to the event is by registration only. Those who are successful in
their registration will receive a confirmation
email from Contact Singapore with the dial-in details for the WebEx video
conference, closer to the date of the event.
