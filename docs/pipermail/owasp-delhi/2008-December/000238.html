Welcome Siddhartha on the list. OWASP membership, material and tools are
free and open for everyone. You may download the material from the OWASP
website (www.owasp.org <http://www.owasp.org/> ) and start sharpening your
AppSec skills. In case you find any query or want to discuss something then
please feel free to post your queries on the list. We recently had chapter
meetings in October and November. You may find the presentations at
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Delhi_Meeting_October_18th_2008 &
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Delhi_Meeting_November_29th_2008 

 

We dropped the idea of organizing the meeting for this month and most of us
