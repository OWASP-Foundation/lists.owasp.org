Not only OWASP India but OWASP Global chapters are eager to help government
bodies. I and Puneet proposed and were chairing the working session on
"OWASP Intra Government Affairs" at OWASP EU Summit last month. We also
prepared a detailed presentation on it but unfortunately, couldn't
participate in this session which was well taken care by David Campbell.
Here is the link for the objective of that session:
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Working_Session_-_OWASP_Intra_Governmen
tal_Affairs

 

We met a top decision making government official (I don't want to name
publicly) in this space some time back to propose such initiative, we also
keep sending personal invitations to govt. officials to participate in free
and open OWASP monthly meetings so that atleast we get a chance of
explaining and exploring such avenues, but then who cares! You know how it
goes here.

 

Post me offline if any one has got a wake-up bell for our govt. officials,
probably it might work with your suggestions.

 

Many Thanks,

Dhruv
