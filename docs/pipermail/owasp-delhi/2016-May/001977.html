Announcing the OWASP Delhi chapter May meet on 21st May, 2016 (Saturday) -

*Session Details/Agenda:*
-----------------------------------------

*10:30 AM - 11:00 AM:* Registration & Networking

*11:00 AM - 11:15 AM:* Introductions & Chapter Updates

*11:15 AM - 11:30 AM: *InfoSec News Bytes by Arpit Maheshwari
