Paco,

When Fred Piper supported me in starting the OWASP-RHUL chapter, it was
supposed to have been a join chapter of the (ISC)2 and OWASP. I hope that
you will help me make that a reality.

I have added you to the OWASP-RHUL list. You may post here at this chapter
as you see fit.  I do not speak for Royal Holloway, nor all of OWASP, but
Royal Holloway certainly embraces the (ISC)2; and as an alumni of the ISG,
so do I. Similarly, at my chapter of OWASP-RHUL you and the (ISC)2 are
always welcome.

When it comes to security education I do believe we all have something to
contribute to the problem. I personally would welcome the opportunity to
have you  and members of the (ISC)2 speak to the students at the OWASP-RHUL
chapter, and we would most definitely welcome the participation of (ISC)2
and its associates here.
