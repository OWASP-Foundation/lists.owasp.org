All - I'm sending this out to the list as very relevant and as such we've negotiated a special rate for OWASP Members. If you're not an OWASP member, sign up now and you'll still end up saving around £70!
--

The inaugural ISSD Conference is taking place at The Westminster Conference 
Centre, London from 20th to 21st May 2010, and will feature a range of UK 
and international speakers giving presentations on developing new 
applications securely, and adding security to legacy applications - both 
standard and web-based.


The full programme for the event is available online at 
www.issdconference.com – key speakers include a number of OWASP contributors 
as well as Lord Toby Harris, Professor Jim Norton, Ari Takanen, Peter Wood, 
Microsoft’s Geoffrey Hill, Oracle’s Duncan Harris as well as conference 
chair Sarb Sembhi.


The conference is designed to benefit complete Policy-level, Project and 
Technical implementation teams responsible for developing secure systems 
within an organisation. This would include Lead Developers, Systems 
Architects, CISO's, CTO's, Compliance, Risk & Audit Professionals, 
Government Policymakers and others involved in systems development, 
designing new systems in house or managing existing/legacy systems.


Delegate bookings are now open for the event via www.issdconference.com, 
with OWASP members qualifying for a £100 discount on the “Early Bird” and 
“standard” rates – simply enter the code OWASP01 at the paypal payment page 
when you book.
