Hi all,

Just a polite notice to say that the 44CON CFP has opened and we’re looking for talks on all manner of subjects. If anyone’s interested in submitting a talk, they can find out more about it here: https://44con.com/2018/02/27/44con-2018-cfp-is-open/

I’d really love to see more submissions on workshops targeting getting the basics right. The fancy pants stuff is all well and good, but workshops have real utility to attendees, and things that might not seem sexy tend to be the most valuable.

The CFP closes on the 30th of April, and I’ll (hopefully) be around at the next OWASP London if anyone wants to chat.
