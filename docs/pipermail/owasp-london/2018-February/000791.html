All,

A quick reminder to those of you who missed OWASP London Chapter's last 
two events.

OWASP Application Security Europe 2018 Conference (AppSecEU 2018) will 
take place in London, July 3-6 at the QEII Conference Centre in Westminster.

1000 attendees from Europe and all around the world are expected to 
attend the conference.

The deadline to submit a presentation and/or a training session proposal 
to AppSecEU Conference in London is *Wednesday 28th February* - if you 
want to be considered as a speaker or a trainer - hurry up and submit 
your proposals now!

Visit:  https://2018.appsec.eu website for the links to submit the 
presentation and training proposals and to find out more about the 
conference and already confirmed keynote speakers.


Regards,

Sam, Sherif and Greg

OWASP London Chapter Leaders

P.S. AppSecEU 2018 Conference is also looking for sponsors - 
cybersecurity vendors - please check the https://2018.appsec.eu website 
for sponsorship opportunities
