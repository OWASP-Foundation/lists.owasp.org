All,

First of all a big thank you to everyone who attended OWASP London 
Chapter's last event on the 25th January 2018 at Goodman Masson. The 
slides and video recordings are now online, the links can be found on 
our webpage here: 
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_25th_January_2018_.28Central_London.29

The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 22nd 
February 2018

Location: Capital One, White Collar Factory, 1 Old Street Yard, London, 
EC1Y 8AF

Nearest Tube: Old Street (1-minute walk)

Time: Doors Open at 6pm for registration, pizza, drinks and networking, 
the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab a drink 
and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

  https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_22nd_February_2018_.28Central_London.29

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-22nd-february-2018-630pm-tickets-43005148557?aff=ml

Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

We will be live-streaming this event via our public Facebook page as 
usual. If you are unable to attend in person, but would like to watch 
the event remotely please remember to follow us on Twitter and Facebook 
where we will announce the link to watch the live stream at 18:30 on the 
event day. As usual the video recordings of talks in high definition 
will be available on OWASP London YouTube channel 
(https://www.youtube.com/OWASPLondon) at a later date.

Looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan, Sherif Mansour and Dr. Greg Fragkos
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
