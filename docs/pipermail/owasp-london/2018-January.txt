All,

The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 25th 
January 2018

Location: Goodman Masson, 120 Aldersgate Street, London, EC1A 4JQ

Nearest Tube: Barbican (1-minute walk)

Time: Doors Open at 6pm for registration, pizza, drinks and networking, 
the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab a drink 
and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_25th_January_2018_.28Central_London.29

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-28th-september-2017-630pm-tickets-33237487219?aff=ml

Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

We will be live-streaming this event. If you are unable to attend in 
person, but would like to watch the event remotely please remember to 
follow us on Twitter and Facebook where we will announce the link to 
watch the live stream at 18:30 on the event day. As usual the video 
recordings of talks in high definition will be available on OWASP London 
YouTube channel (https://www.youtube.com/OWASPLondon) at a later date.

Looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Regards,
