OWASP Members: This reminder is for you - the voting will close on 
Thursday 9th November 2017!

All eligible OWASP members should have received an e-ballot via e-mail 
by now with subject "OWASP Board Election Open With Clean Slate - Please 
Vote" asking you to vote even if you have already voted before 19th 
October 2017. Due to an admin error the previous vote tallies have been 
zeroed out and the voting process was restarted on the 19th October 2017.

The Board Election email that you should have received includes a 
specific link for you to cast your vote.

It is critically important that our community participate in this 
important election in which we will be choosing four new OWASP Board 
members.

Meet The Board Candidates here (includes links to audio interviews in 
mp3 format):

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2017_Global_Board_of_Directors_Election#Meet_the_Candidates_and_listen_to_their_interviews

Please note that one of OWASP London Chapter Leaders - Sherif Mansour is 
a Global Board Candidate this year. You can read Sherif's Bio and 'Why 
Me' section here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Sherif_Mansour_2017_Bio_%26_Why_Me%3F

Sherif's audio interview can be downloaded here: 
https://www.owasp.org/download/2017-board-election/Sherif_Mansour.mp3

Please vote and help us shape the direction of OWASP Global Foundation 
community!

You can read the message from OWASP Board Chairman Matt Konda here:

https://owasp.blogspot.com/2017/10/board-election-open-with-clean-slate.html

Thanks and regards,

Sam
