Hi dear London OWASP chapter fellows,

the OWASP election period began today (until Oct-25). The registered
members upon you should have received an email from
owasp at simplyvoting.com <mailto:owasp at simplyvoting.com> with subject
"OWASP 2013 Board Election"

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2013_Board_Elections#How_Do_I_Vote

*Candidates for the election* are listed here along with links to the
written and audio responses.
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2013_Board_Elections#2013_Board_Candidates

There are also questions on the *WASPY awards* and also the *project
sponsorship/branding discussion.*

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/WASPY_Awards_2013
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Governance/ProjectSponsorship


Your vote is important. Please take a few minutes this week and cast
your votes.

Thanks and all the best, Tobias
