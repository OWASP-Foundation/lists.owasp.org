Hi guys,

nice to meet many of you tonight at our London chapter meeting.
Just in case you haven't voted yet. The voting period for our global
OWASP elections ends today, on Friday at 23:59 CST. So this is your last
chance to voice your opinion on the candidates for the board, for the
WASPY Awards and also on how we want to handle project sponsoring in the
future.
Every voting member should have received personal invitation and
reminder emails over the last couple of days, so follow the link there
and cast your vote and voice your opinion.

Cheers and see you soon at our next chapter meeting coming very soon.

Tobias
