Hi dear OWASP London fellows,

things are starting to build up for some great OWASP events this year!
We just opened the Call for Papers and Presentations for the upcoming
AppSecEU in Cambridge in June.
Please feel free to submit your proposals until March 21st.

Cheers and see you, Tobias


    The AppSec Europe 2014 Selection Committee is accepting papers,
    presentations and training submissions for the OWASP event in
    Cambridge, June 23rd-26th.


    We invite all practitioners of application security and those who
    work or interact with all facets of application security to submit
    speaker and trainer proposals.


    For more information, read the appropriate document:


          * Call for Presentations
            <http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Call-for-Presentations-v1-1.doc>
          * Call for Training
            <http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Call-for-Training-v1.1.doc>
          * Call for Papers
            <http://2014.appsec.eu/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Call-for-Papers.doc>


    All submissions should be sent via EasyChair
    <https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=appseceu2014> /(Select
    the appropriate track once you have registered.)/

    _Important Dates:_
         CFP/CFT Open: Feb 1st
         CFP/CFT Closes: March 21st
         Acceptance Notification: April 25th
         Conference Schedule Publication: May 16th


    Please help us spread the word by sending this e-mail to all your
    chapter members!

Thank you very much,
