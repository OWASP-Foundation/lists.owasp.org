All,

A quick reminder that OWASP London Chapter's next event is this Thursday.

Location: Telegraph Media Group, 111 Buckingham Palace Road, London, SW1W
0DT
Nearest Tube: Victoria (3 minute walk)

Doors will open at 18:00, the talks will start at 18:30 sharp (please aim
to arrive by 6:15pm to allow yourselves enough time to register and grab a
drink & a slice of pizza).

Talk details and speaker bios are available on the OWASP London Wiki Page
here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

This event is fully sold out. If you were unable to grab a ticket you can
watch the talks on OWASP London YouTube Channel:

https://www.youtube.com/OWASPLondon

Looking forward to seeing you all on Thursday.

*Please note:* If you have booked your place already, but unable to attend,
please either use EventBrite app/website to cancel your RSVP
<https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-30th-march-2017-630pm-tickets-32845017331>
