All,

The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 30th 
March 2017 at 6:30pm (doors open and refreshments provided at 6:00pm).

This event is kindly sponsored and hosted by The Telegraph Media Group.

Location: The Telegraph, 111 Buckingham Palace Road, London, SW1W 0DT

Nearest Tube: Victoria (3 minute walk)

Doors Open at 6pm, the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time!).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm-6:15pm to get a chance to grab a drink, a 
slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The details of the talks and speakers are now available on the OWASP 
London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

Please note that two speakers will be travelling from abroad to present 
their talks, so the agenda is subject to change.

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-30th-march-2017-630pm-tickets-32845017331?aff=ml

This event is free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP.

Please note that space is limited, so please only book tickets if you 
are able to attend.

If you have booked your place already, but unable to attend for some 
reason please either use EventBrite app/website to cancel your RSVP or 
email us: owasplondon at owasp.org


Looking forward to seeing you there!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
