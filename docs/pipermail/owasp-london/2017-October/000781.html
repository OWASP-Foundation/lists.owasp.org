All,

BlackHat Europe has offered OWASP London the opportunity to have a booth 
at the upcoming BlackHat Europe 2017 December event. The BlackHat Europe 
conference takes place at Excel London (Prince Regent DLR) on 4th-7th 
December 2017. We need volunteers to staff the OWASP booth only on two 
days:  Wednesday the 6th and Thursday the 7th December 2017.

Conference Details:

http://www.blackhat.com/eu-17

Before we can accept this offer we need to secure volunteers to staff 
the booth.

Responsibilities include:

* spend at least one full business day at the OWASP booth in BlackHat EU 
2017 Business Hall (EXCEL London) arriving at the venue at 9am (lunch 
and coffee breaks will be provided to all).

* meet & greet booth visitors, ask them what they know about OWASP - 
give them OWASP flyers & swag, talk to them about OWASP, our mission and 
projects, answer their questions

* ask visitors if they are London-based and if they would like to 
subscribe the owasp-london mailing list and attend our meet-ups, 
potentially scan their badge using a scanner provided by BlackHat 
organisers (availability of the scanner yet to be confirmed). Collect 
names & e-mails of anyone wishing to become OWASP members using the 
OWASP membership form

*  be able to answer questions about OWASP Foundation, OWASP London and 
OWASP Projects, in particular the OWASP Flagship Projects: 
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Project_Inventory#tab=Flagship_Projects

* you will need to bring your own laptop which will be used to help you 
showcase OWASP website/documents/projects/tools and send e-mails.

There are 1,600 attendees expected at the event, so it is going to be a 
busy one.

If you would like to volunteer please get in touch with OWASP London 
Chapter Leaders Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour via e-mail: 
owasplondon at owasp.org  (do not reply to this message as you may 
accidentally spam the entire mailing list) indicating on which date you 
are prepared to volunteer (6th or 7th December).

Thanks,

Sam & Sherif
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
