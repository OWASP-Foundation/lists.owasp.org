All,

The presentation slide decks from the OWASP London Chapter meeting on 
28th September 2017 are now available online on OWASP London site: 
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

Video recordings of talks presented at the event will be published on 
our YouTube channel later this week - please make sure you subscribe to 
OWASP London YouTube channel if you haven't done it yet: 
https://www.youtube.com/OWASPLondon

The Facebook Live Stream video recording of the whole event is available 
on our Facebook page (please note that we had a 10 minute break between 
minute 54 an 1:04 so you will want to fast-forward/skip that bit):

https://www.facebook.com/OWASPLondon/videos/1009373345872622/

Many thanks to our speakers - Benjy Portnoy and Suleman Malik. We also 
would like to thank our sponsors and attendees, and of course huge 
thanks to John Lewis Partnership who kindly hosted our event in their 
head offices,  baked tasty pizzas in their canteen kitchen for our 
attendees and provided cold beer and soft drinks.

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
