-------------

On March 9th 2006, Thursday, there will be another Owasp-London Chapter gathering in the Hammersmith 's Auriol Kensington Rowing Club, 4 Lower Mall, Hammersmith, London, W6 9DJ.

Here are pictures of the club's venue http://www.akrowing.com/rebuild_gall.php?race=venue&pic=1, here are detailed instructions to how to get there http://www.akrowing.com/page.php?page=findus (specially important for the ones that will come by tube (Auriol Kensington is between two pubs the Blue Anchor and The Rutland )) and if you are driving you might find this google map useful http://maps.google.co.uk/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=W6+9DJ&ll=51.489935,-0.231056&spn=0.047885,0.21698

In this session, I am very happy to announce that Owasp founder Mark Curphey will deliver a presentation on the night's topic "Naked Application Security - A striped back look at the industry today". I (Dinis Cruz) will also deliver a presentation on the latest developments at Owasp, the forthcoming European Owasp Conference, and the new Owasp tools SiteGenerator and VulnReport.
