All,

The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 22nd 
November 2018 at Microsoft Reactor London offices.

Location: Microsoft Reactor, 70 Wilson Street, London, EC2A 2DB

Nearest Tubes: Old Street (7-minute walk), Moorgate (7-minute walk), 
Liverpool Street (7-minute walk)

Time: Doors Open at 6:00pm for registration, pizza, drinks and 
networking. The talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time)

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab a drink 
and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The details of the talks and speakers are now available on the OWASP 
London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_22nd_November_2018_.28Central_London.29 


You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meetup-thursday-22nd-november-2018-630pm-tickets-52568134706?aff=ml 



Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

Looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Regards,
