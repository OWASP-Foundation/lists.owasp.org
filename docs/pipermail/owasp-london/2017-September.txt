All,

The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 28th 
September 2017 at 18:30 (we start on time!).

This event is kindly sponsored and hosted by John Lewis Partnership.

Location: John Lewis Head Office, 171 Victoria Street, London, SW1E 5NN

Nearest Tube: Victoria (3 minute walk from the Cardinal Place exit)

Doors Open and registration starts at 6pm, the talks start at 6:30pm (we 
start on time).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab a drink 
and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Next_Meeting.2FEvent.28s.29


You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-28th-september-2017-630pm-tickets-33237487219?aff=ml

Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

We will be live-streaming this event via Facebook Live. If you are 
unable to attend in person, but would like to watch the event remotely 
please remember to follow us on Twitter and Facebook where we will 
announce the link to watch the live stream at 18:30 on the event day. As 
usual the video recordings of talks in high definition will be available 
on OWASP London YouTube channel as well at a later date.

This event is free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP.? 
Please note that OWASP Global Board Elections are approaching, so if you 
want to have your say in the future of OWASP we urge you to become a 
member and would like to remind you that Sherif Mansour - one of OWASP 
London Chapter Leaders is a candidate for the OWASP Global Board. To 
qualify to vote you need to have a paid OWASP membership ($50 US 
Dollars/40 Euros ) by 30th September 2017. The link to the OWASP 
Membership page is here: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership


Looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
