The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 28th 
July 2016 at 18:30 (we start on time!).

Event location is: Expedia.com Ltd, Block 1, Angel Square, London, EC1V 
1NS.

Nearest tube: Angel

Doors open at 18:00, the talks start at 18:30 sharp (please allow 
yourselves enough time to register and grab a drink & a slice of pizza).

I am pleased to announce that Scott Helme, Lucian Corlan and Chris 
Rutter will be our main presenters.

Talk details and speaker bios are now available on the OWASP London Wiki 
Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London


You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-28th-july-2016-630pm-tickets-26474895124 


Please note that space is limited, so please only book tickets if you 
intend to attend.

If you have booked your place already, but unable to attend for some 
reason please either use EventBrite app/website to cancel your RSVP or 
email us: owasplondon at owasp.org
