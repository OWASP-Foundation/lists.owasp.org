All,

Now that we’re back from holidays etc, I thought I’d make sure you’re all aware that the OWASP AppSec Europe conference for 2014 is going to be held nearby, in Cambridge, from the 23rd to the 26th of June 2014 (23-24 Training, 25-26 Conference days).

The conference website(*) can be found here: https://2014.appsec.eu

If your company is interested in Sponsoring AppSec Europe, the the Sponsor pack is available at: https://2014.appsec.eu/sponsorship/

Make sure you put it in your planners, and Call for Papers and Call for Training announcements are due to come out shortly.

Best regards

Justin


(*) before you all point out rendering issues on iPhones and some browsers, we are already working on these
