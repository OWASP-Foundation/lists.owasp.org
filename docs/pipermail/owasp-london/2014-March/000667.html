OWASP London,

Apologies for hijacking your list but I hope this will be of some relevance to you.

I am in need of a SecOps (Security Operations Engineer) to work for an awesome cloud-software house, with a global product. The position is permanent, with a good package but I'm open to having conversations with contractors also.

The skills I'm after are as follows - would anyone be interested in finding out more be able to recommend a friend/colleague?

  *   Understanding of ModSecurity, Snort or similar security solutions
  *   Knowledge of the OWASP top 10 web vulnerabilities, the nature of the threat vectors, protection mechanisms and mitigations
  *   Thorough understanding of the http protocol
  *   Linux system administration skills
  *   Scripting or programming skills in a language such as Python, Ruby or Perl
  *   Threat analysis and threat profiling
  *   Attack detection and response
  *   Qualifications - probably nice to have - CISSP would be a massive benefit
  *   Knowledge of IIS and understanding of the security aspects of the MS platforms that run on IIS (ASP, ASP.NET web forms, ASP.NET MVC)
  *   Familiarity with PCI DSS compliance
  *   Able to use pentest tools such as Zap, Cenzic, Burp suite
Any help greatly appreciated and apologies again for misuse of the mailing list, I won't make a habit of it.

Many thanks,

Matt
