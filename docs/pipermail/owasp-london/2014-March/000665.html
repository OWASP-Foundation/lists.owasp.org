Hi Colin,

thanks a lot for explaining Cornucopia again.
Happy we had a great round last night. This was the first time I played
and it was very easy to learn (just 5 minutes or so) and lots of fun and
security!
This is the most enjoyable way of working out security requirements and
concerns for a project ever!
So highly recommended!
And for those who missed this opportunity, here again the project page,
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Cornucopia

See you at our next chapter events in London and in June at our AppSecEU
in Cambridge (https://2014.appsec.eu/ ) ,
Maybe we can even have a little Cornucopia game evening round there... ;-)
