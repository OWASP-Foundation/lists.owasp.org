Hi folks,

Just to let you know a couple of things:

We're in the process of setting up 2 new UK chapters -
Bristol<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Bristol>and
Newcastle <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Newcastle>.
Still early stages, but please publicize them and join the relevant mailing
lists if you're interested in getting involved (running/speaking/attending)
in them.

And we've just set up a new list: owasp-uk-leaders at lists.owasp.org
If you are an OWASP UK Chapter leader then please join this list - the
intention is to use it for things like helping to arrange speakers, trying
to make sure our meetings dont clash, exchanging tips and trick etc etc.
You can also join it if you just want to know whats going on - after all
OWASP is an open organization :)

Cheers,
