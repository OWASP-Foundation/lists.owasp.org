A lot of people would disagree with you Paco: e.g. [The Crazy 
Ones](http://herestothecrazyones.com/). In fact historically it is not 
just computer science advances (as Dinis rightly points out); but 
***everything*** is advanced by the so called ***hackers.***

Now, no doubt living at the edge produces is a risk of its own; and 
change always disrupts somebodies security.

To ask who is the more harmful - those who fight change or those who 
fight for change? And to marginalise one or the other; that is the wrong 
question (and the one you seem to be posing). The important question is 
how do we keep it balanced? (This is the role of Gandalf in the lord of 
the rings, and it is our role as security people.)
