Dennis Groves, Fabio Cerullo and myself are helping support an
application security event in Cambridge, next Tuesday.

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Pilot_Cambridge_WebAppSec_Mini_Conference_2012

We're hoping to see if it can act as a catalyst to create an OWASP
local chapter there. The free event has been promoted to local
students, alumni and academic staff, local technology groups and local
BCS branches and about 70 people have registered.

Please register (free) if you would like to attend to hear the
presentations about OWASP, the projects OWASP O2, OWASP Open SAMM,
OWASP ESAPI and OWASP AppsSenor, or just if you would like to support
this initiative.

The event runs from 4pm to 8pm in the Lord Ashcroft Building, Room 002
at Anglia Ruskin University. registration and location details are on
the page linked above.
