Dear all,
Just a reminder that there are only few days left to register for the AppSec Europe 2016 Conference which is taking place 27th June - 1st July 2016 in Rome, Italy.
http://2016.appsec.eu
AppSec EU will offer three full days of secutiy training followed by two days of conference sessions, as well as several interactive events.
This is a unique chance to network with more than 800 participants ,which include prominent application security experts, penetration testers, developers, Global and European OWASP leaders, security industry leaders and speakers.
What Happens at an AppSecEU Conference?
- Technical talks by experts in security, devops and cloud - Panels to debate tough topics - Training sessions for hands on learning in top security areas - Keynotes from industry leaders - Vendor booths to promote the latest advances in security technology - Capture The Flag - Security Tool Training
Additionally OWASP is excited to be hosting our 1st Developer Summit and are looking forward to kicking it off at AppSecEU 2016!
The Developer Summit will be held from 8am-5pm on June 28 & 29 at AppSecEU 2016. It will consist of two working sessions per day with a lunch break.
OWASP will be providing a platform for developers who want come and contribute in a working session environment. The purpose of this summit is to ?get things done?. Bring your laptop and be ready to code!
Full conference program can be found here:
http://2016.appsec.eu/?page_id=43
Looking forward to seeing you in Rome,
Regards,
Sam Stepanyan
