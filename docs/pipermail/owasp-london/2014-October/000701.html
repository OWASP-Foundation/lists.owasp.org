Hello dear OWASP London chapter,

*Voting for OWASP 2014 Board Election is now open.

If you are a registered member of OWASP you can elect leadership in this 
years election to take office in 2015 for a two year term. This year's 
elections will be for 3 of the 7 seats on the board for a 2-year term 
(the other 4 seats will be up for election in 2015)*

*Candidates (in alphabetical order): **
*-Abbas Naderi Afooshteh
-Israel Bryski
-Bil Corry
-Tahir Khan
-Timur Khrotko
-Matthew Konda
-Jim Manico
-Mateo Martinez
-Nigel Phair
-Andrew van der Stock

*All of the candidates were interviewed and asked questions about WHY 
THEM. **

All registered voters should have received their voting invitation 
today. If you are not sure if you are a listed as a MEMBER of OWASP 
Foundation check the membership directory here: 
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership

Official information can be found on the OWASP candidate election page 
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2014_Board_Elections

I wish all of the candidates best of luck in service of OWASP 
Foundation's mission to make software security visible, so that 
individuals and organisations worldwide can make informed decisions 
about true software security risks.
