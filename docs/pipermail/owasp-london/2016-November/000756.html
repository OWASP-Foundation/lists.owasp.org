All,

The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 24th 
November 2016 at 18:30 (we start on time!)

This event is kindly sponsored and hosted by Empiric.

Location: Empiric offices, 1 Old Jewry, London EC2R 8DN

Nearest Tube: Bank (2 minute walk)

Doors Open at 6pm, the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time). Please 
arrive on time to get a chance to grab a drink, a slice of pizza and 
network with other participants.

I am excited to announce that Jeremy King and Gareth Heyes will be our 
main presenters.

Talk titles and speaker bios are now available on the OWASP London Wiki 
Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London


You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-24th-november-2016-630pm-tickets-29073490593?aff=ml

This event is free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP.

Please note that space is limited, so please only book tickets if you 
intend to attend.

Ticket booking will close the evening before the event so we get names 
for the building security.

ADDITIONALLY: I am pleased to announce that the OWASP London Hackathon: 
Secure Development Workshop and Capture The Flag (CTF) Competition event 
at the end of November is definitely on! - the exact dates and location 
details will be announced very soon.
