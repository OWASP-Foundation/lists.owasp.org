All,

As previously announced there will be another OWASP London Chapter event 
NEXT WEEK. The reason for such tight scheduling is due to venue 
availability.

September OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 6th 
September 2018 at Facebook London offices.

Location: Facebook London, 1 Rathbone Square, London, W1T 1FB

Nearest Tubes: Tottenham Court Road (3 minute walk), Oxford Circus (8 
minute walk)

Doors Open at 6:00pm for registration, food, drinks and networking. The 
talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time)

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab some 
food & drink and network with other participants.

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_6th_September_2018_.28Central_London.29

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-at-facebook-thursday-6th-september-2018-630pm-tickets-49612881464?aff=ml

IMPORTANT - ID REQUIRED:  Facebook building security requires all 
visitors to show a form of ID matching the name on the ticket, so please 
use your real name for registration and remember to bring a form of ID 
such as: driving licence, passport, debit or credit card.

Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

Looking forward to seeing you at the event!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan, Sherif Mansour and Dr. Greg Fragkos
