All,

The next OWASP London Chapter event is on Thursday 19th April 2018. It 
will be a combined event with the OWASP Bristol Chapter. The talks will 
physically take place in Bristol and will be live-streamed to a big 
screen in London where the audience will have a chance to participate in 
live Q&A using the video link between Bristol and London.

This event is kindly hosted and sponsored by Just Eat.

Talks:

• "Application Hacking Through The Eyes of An Attacker: - Rob Hillier, 
XQ Cyber

• "Exploiting Unknown Browsers and Objects" - Gareth Heyes, PortSwigger


Doors open at: 6:00pm for registration, networking, pizza & beer.

The talks will kick off at: 6:30pm

Register to attend this event here:
