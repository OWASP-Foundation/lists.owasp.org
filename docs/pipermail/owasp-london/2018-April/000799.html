All,

The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Thursday 26th 
April 2018 at EY (Ernst & Young LLP) offices.

Location: EY, 1 More London Riverside, London, SE1 2AF (please note: 
there are two EY offices on the same street - No 1 and No 6, this event 
will take place at Number 1 More Place)

Nearest Tube: London Bridge (5-minute walk)

Time: Doors Open at 6pm for registration, pizza, drinks and networking, 
the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm to get a chance to register, grab a drink 
and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Thursday.2C_26th_April_2018_.28Central_London.29

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-26th-april-2018-630pm-tickets-45216218928?aff=ml

Please note that space at this event is limited, so please only book 
tickets if you are able to attend.

Looking forward to seeing you at the event!
