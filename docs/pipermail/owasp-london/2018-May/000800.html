OWASP Annual AppSecEU Security Conference will take place in London this 
year in July 2018 at the QEII Conference Centre (opposite Westminster 
Abbey).
AppSecEU is the premier application security conference for European 
software developers and security experts. Application Security Europe 
2018 provides thought leadership, amazing talks, informative sessions 
and great social experiences.

The main AppSecEU conference this year spans two days from 5th to 6th of 
July 2018, offering four full tracks of talks: for pentesters and 
ethical hackers, developers and security engineers, DevOps/DevSecOps 
practices and GRC/Risk level talks for managers and CISOs.

July 2nd-July 4th will be dedicated to Training sessions and workshops 
delivered by the industry experts for hands-on learning in top 
applications security areas.
