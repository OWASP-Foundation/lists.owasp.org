Hi All,

 

I have contacts on the Master Courses in Royal Holloway, UCL, South Bank, Greenwich, Portsmouth, Plymouth, etc. through my capacity as President of the London Chapter of ISACA, (and have lectured in some of these places). Also, apart from this funding, there are a few other competitions around at the moment that and OWASP led project may be eligible for. Also, I’d be happy to help with contacts in Europe if need be.

 

Also, if we do involve any students on a Master course, if they use this as part of their thesis, they may be eligible for the ISACA London Chapter Dissertation Competition; although the prize is only £500 (ish), [it may even have gone up to a £1000 – I’ll have to check], it does offer excellent exposure to industry practitioners.

 

Dinis, I would be happy to lead on this if you like, and just to get him into more trouble, Colin (great to see you again yesterday), I would be happy to work on this with you, if you are able to?

 

Cheers

Sarb Sembhi
