Hello,

The Open Group is planning our 2nd Security 
Practitioners Conference for London Monday April 
27th. This one day event will immediately precede 
the InfoSec show. The topics we intend to explore in this conference include:
·       Identity management issues related to cloud computing
·       Web 2.0 security
·       User experiences in implementing an 
Information Security Management System like ISO 27001

We would like to potentially involve your local 
OWASP organisation or chapter in our event in a 
couple of ways. First, we are open to 
presentation proposals from your membership. I 
have attached a Call for Presentations for the 
event, and if you could share this with your 
membership, that would be ideal.  Second, we are 
happy to extend our affiliate discounted 
registration rate to your membership. We will 
send a message to you in a few weeks, with more 
information about the conference, and with the 
affiliate code for your members use.

We believe this conference will deliver a lot of 
value to security professionals, and we look 
forward to your participation. Please advise if 
you have any questions that we can answer.

Regards,
