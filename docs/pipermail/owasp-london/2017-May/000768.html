All,

The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 18th 
May 2017 at 6:30pm (doors open and refreshments provided at 6:00pm).

This event is kindly sponsored and hosted by Worldpay.

Location: Worldpay, The Walbrook Building, 25 Walbrook , London EC4N 8AF

Nearest Tubes: Bank (take exit 9 towards Walbrook) and Cannon Street 
(2-minute walk).

Doors Open at 6pm, the talks will start at 6:30pm (we start on time!).

Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm-6:15pm to get a chance to grab a drink, a 
slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The details of the talks and speakers are now available on the OWASP 
London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-18th-may-2017-630pm-tickets-33237461141?aff=ml


This event is free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP.

Please note that space is limited, so please only book tickets if you 
are able to attend.

If you have booked your place already, but unable to attend for some 
reason please either use the EventBrite app/website to cancel your RSVP 
or email us: owasplondon at owasp.org


Looking forward to seeing you there!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
