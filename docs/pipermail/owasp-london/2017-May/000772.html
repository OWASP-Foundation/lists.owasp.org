All,

A quick reminder that OWASP London Chapter's next event is this Thursday 
(tomorrow!)

Location: Worldpay, The Walbrook Building, 25 Walbrook , London EC4N 8AF

Nearest Tubes: Bank (take exit 8 towards Walbrook) and Cannon Street 
(2-minute walk).

Doors will open at 18:00, the talks will start at 18:30 sharp (please 
aim to arrive by 6:15pm to allow yourselves enough time to register and 
grab a drink & a slice of pizza).

Talk details and speaker bios are available on the OWASP London Wiki 
Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

This event is fully sold out. In fact all 100 tickets were gone in under 
36 hours when they were released on 2nd May.

If you were unable to grab a ticket please check our Twitter and 
Facebook pages as we are going to try to LIVE STREAM this event (subject 
to guest WiFi quality).

The talks will be video recorded and published on OWASP London YouTube 
Channel:

https://www.youtube.com/OWASPLondon


*Please note:* If you have booked your ticket already, but unable to 
attend, please either use EventBrite app/website to cancel your RSVP or 
email us: owasplondon at owasp.org <mailto:owasplondon at owasp.org>

Looking forward to seeing you on Thursday.

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
