Reminder :-

May 13th­14th 2009, OWASP will hold its annual European Application Security
conference in wonderful Kraków, Poland. Not only do we have 2 fantastic key
notes (Ross Anderson and Bruce Schneier) but we have 3 tracks stuffed with
high quality topics and great speakers and 8 one and two day tutorials. This
year we organise the conference together with OWASP Poland and
Confidence2009, a conference in Kraków on May 15th-16th.

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSec_Europe_2009_-_Poland

For those of you who haven't been to an OWASP AppSec conference, they are
well worth the trip, and generally a lot cheaper to attend than other, more
commercial, conferences. Also, Kraków is fairly cheap to get to and stay in,
so those of you on a limited training budget (especially in this economy!)
this might be an ideal choice.

Best regards

Justin
