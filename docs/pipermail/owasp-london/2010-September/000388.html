It's past time we expanded the London chapter from a one person coordination effort (i.e. me) to a larger group of folks, and therefore have the time to commit to building this to the next step.  As part of that, one of the things we need to sort out is an OWASP chapter board to coordinate our activity. Needless to say, this is an absolute prerequisite for us looking seriously at pitching for AppSec Europe in 2012 for example.

So, first steps - is anyone aware of a platform for holding organisational elections?  For example, something that would allow nominations to go up, and possibly ultimately to allow people to vote on candidates?  This would make running the process a lot smoother.... providing of course that some of you out there don't end up gaming the system ;-)
