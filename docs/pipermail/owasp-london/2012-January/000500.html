Hi Max
This is another first time caller, and I want to put in my tuppence worth
as I feel that TDD is misunderstood in this context. Its goal isn’t to
ensure correctness but to conquer complexity, driving out simple design and
supple, maintainable code. This task is orthogonal to gathering and
managing technical requirements, which is where I see the security
engineering under focus.
I believe TDD is a tool used in implementation process, whilst tools like
threat modelling and attack surface analysis are used in the design
process, and fuzz testing in the QA process. This is not to say that these
processes should be executed at different points in time or by different
principals, that is another story best left for another day :)

Regards,
