The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 29th 
September 2016 at 18:30 (we start on time!)

Location: Skype (Microsoft) offices - 2 Waterhouse Square, 140 Holborn, 
London EC1N 2ST

Nearest Tube: Chancery Lane


I am excited to announce that Ken Munro, Andrew Tierney and Dinis Cruz 
will be our main presenters.

Talk titles and speaker bios are now available on the OWASP London Wiki 
Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-29th-september-2016-630pm-tickets-27611813678

intend to attend.

Ticket booking will close the evening before the event so we get names 
for the building security.
