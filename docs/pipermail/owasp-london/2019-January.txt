The next OWASP London Chapter Meeting will take place on Wednesday 13th 
February 2019 at Amazon London offices in Shoreditch.

Location: Amazon, 1 Principal Place, 115 Worship Street, EC2A 2FA, London

Nearest Tubes: Liverpool Street (6 minute walk), Old Street (11 minute 
walk), Shoreditch High Street Overground (8 minute walk)

Time: Doors Open at 6:00pm for registration, pizza, drinks and 
networking. The talks will kick off at 6:30pm.

TALKS:

* "Introducing the OWASP ZAP Heads Up Display (HUD)" - Simon Bennetts 
(@psiinon)

* "Incident Response in Your Pyjamas" - Paco Hope (@pacohope)

* "Developers - The Lucrative Target for Social Engineers" - Stuart Peck 
(@cybersecstu)

The full details of the talks and speakers are now available on the 
OWASP London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London#Wednesday.2C_13th_February_2019_.28Central_London.29

TICKETS and PHOTO ID REQUIREMENT:

IMPORTANT: Amazon building security rules require that all event 
attendees need to bring a form of Photo ID such as driving license or

You can register to attend this event here (Photo ID required to gain 
access to the venue):

https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/owasp-london-chapter-meetup-at-amazon-wednesday-13th-february-2019-630pm-tickets-55533038814?aff=ml
