I've just been looking at Imperva today funnily enough- the last of four
vendors - and i must say it is quite a strong player in this field.
Similar in functionality to NetC however there are some key
differentiators such as the layer 2 bridge, plugin modules and database
support for extra $$$.  Alls i know is the network guys dont like
another proxy/termination device.  NetC will have layer2 bridge soon....

Pro's and Con's for both vendors thats for sure.

Another good XML firewall is Vordel out of interest.  Additionally has
agents which i believe Datapower doesnt.  Agents (isapi, mod_so) are
good and bad I guess.

Anyone played around with Cisco's AON? (yeah yeah i hear you, what are
plumbers doing playing with apps? ;)
