Hi all,

I attended a ISSA UK (London Chapter) event last Thursday, where amongst the
speakers were Howard Schmidt, Whitt Diffie (of Diffie-Hellman algorithm
fame), and Ed Gibson (the newly appointed Microsoft Security Spokesperson
and ex-FBI agent), and would definitely recommend hearing Howard Schmidt,
and I aim to go on the 5th October as well. One can assume that Ed Gibson
will be there as well, and if you have never met an ex-FBI agent (lets face
it most of us haven't, let alone have the opportunity to meet a White House
advisor) it may be your only chance.

Sounds like it will be a great session.
