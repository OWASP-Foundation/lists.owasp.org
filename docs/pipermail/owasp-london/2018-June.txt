All,

A brief reminder that OWASP Application Security Europe 2018 Conference 
is coming to London next week!

The first 3 days (Monday-Wednesday 2nd-4th July) are dedicated to 
hands-on training sessions for developers and testers delivered by the 
industry experts.

Here are the training courses delivered next week:

* *Exploiting Websites by using Offensive HTML, SVG, CSS and other 
Browser-Evil *by Mario Heiderich
* *Practical DevSecOps: Continuous Security in the Age of Cloud* by A. 
Imran Mohammed and Raghunath Gopinath
* *Advanced Web Hacking - Black Belt Edition* by Sumit Siddharth, Sunil 
Yadav and Sudhanshu Chauhan
* *Automated Defense Using Serverless for AWS, Azure and GCP* by Madhu 
Akula & Subash Sn
* *Pentesting the Modern Application Stack* by Francis Alexander and 
Bharadwaj Machiraju
* *Access Control for Rest API's* by Johan Peeters and Michael Boeynaer
* *Web Application Security Essentials* by Fabio Cerullo

Thursday & Friday 5th and 6th June are the main AppSecEU 2018 Conference 
days with talks from 65 speakers + 4 keynotes from Prof. Ross Anderson, 
Mario Heiderich, Georgia Weidman and Allison Miller.

On the evening of the 5th July we have the dinner & networking event at 
the Imperial War Museum.

For OWASP Members there will be a Members Lounge operating. If you are 
not yet an OWASP Member, the conference is a great time for you to join 
OWASP as a paid member as you will get a welcome gift (Bluetooth speaker).

For full training conference agenda and tickets please visit 
https://2018.appsec.eu

Please note that online registrations close on Friday 29th June 2018 at 
noon.

Looking forward to seeing you next week!

Best regards,
