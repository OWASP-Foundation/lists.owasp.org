All,

The next OWASP London Chapter meeting will take place on Thursday, 27th 
July 2017 at 6:30pm (doors open and refreshments provided at 6:00pm).

This event is kindly sponsored and hosted byJust Eat.

Location: Just Eat, Fleet Place House, 2 Fleet Place, London, EC4M 7RF

Nearest Tube:St Paul's (5-minute walk).

Doors Openat 6pm, the talks start at 6:30pm (we start on time).


Please aim to arrive at 6:00pm-6:15pm to get a chance to register, grab 
a drink and a slice of pizza and network with other participants.

The details of the talks and speakers are now available on the OWASP 
London Wiki Page here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/London

You can register to attend this event here:

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/owasp-london-chapter-meeting-thursday-27th-july-2017-630pm-tickets-33237474180?aff=ml

This event is free to attend for both members and non-members of OWASP.

Please note that space is limited, so please only book tickets if you 
are able to attend.

Following our successful pilot of live streaming of the 18th May 2017 
meeting (3000 viewers!) we will be live streaming this event as well via 
Facebook Live. If you are unable to attend in person, but would like to 
watch the event remotely please remember to follow us on Twitter and 
Facebook where we will announce the link to watch the live stream at 
18:30 on the event day. As usual the video recordings of talks in high 
definition will be available on OWASP London YouTube channel as well at 
a later date.


Looking forward to seeing you there!

Regards,

Sam Stepanyan and Sherif Mansour
OWASP London Chapter Leaders
