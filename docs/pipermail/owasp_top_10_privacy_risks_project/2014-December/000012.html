Dear project members,



Yesterday our project was presented at the German OWASP Day
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/German_OWASP_Day_2014/Programm> in Hamburg
and we received very positive feedback. People were impressed how fast we
made progress.

Another presentation is planned for 5 March 2015 at the IAPP Global Privacy
Summit in Washington, DC and we will participate at the IPEN panel at CDPD
on 22 January in Brussels. Feel free to present the project in your
organization as well. We can provide slides.



Currently we are working on the evaluation of our project to get from the
status of an OWASP “Incubator Project” to a “Lab Project” (see also: OWASP
Project Categories <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Category:OWASP_Project>).




Furthermore we start collecting countermeasures to address the risks
identified
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Top_10_Privacy_Risks_Project#tab=Top_10_Privacy_Risks>
and we need your help with that. Please insert your references and ideas
for countermeasures in this google document
<https://docs.google.com/a/owasp.org/document/d/1g4Q_XDVGEAbVR_7DLNIbDN2men57BQ0pNn8CyRc2od8/edit>.
Solutions could be code snippets, other projects, concepts, examples of web
pages, sequence plans etc. Every idea and example is welcome. If you know
which risk your solution is addressing you can post it in the corresponding
section. Otherwise you can post it in the chapter “Miscellaneous”.



And once again, thank you for the great progress we made this year and we
are looking forward to your contribution in 2015 as well.



Until then, happy holidays!



Stefan & Florian
