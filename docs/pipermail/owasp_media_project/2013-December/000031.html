Jonathan,

I've actually never made changes to OWASP's wiki front page.  I've been
spending my time with the underlying software running the wiki rather then
the wiki itself if that makes sense.  I just updated the EmbedVideo
extension to use https for Youtube so we don't have mixed content on the
wiki.

There's only a hand full of people who are wiki admins and therefore can
update the front page.  If I recall correctly, Kate and Sarah are both
admins and can (and have) updated the front page.  There may be more admins
- I haven't looked at who has access to what on the wiki since the AppSec
LATAM conference in Montevideo.

BTW, the idea of a playlist is a brilliant way to integrate the wiki + the
media project.  I also agree that its sad that so much of our content isn't
presented directly or even just linked in the wiki.  Thanks for creating
the video page.  That represents some significant effort and a great win
for OWASP and our wiki.
