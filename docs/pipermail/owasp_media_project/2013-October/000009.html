Sure,

we uploaded a video from a screen capture of a TV announcement about OWASP.

This make us do a copyright strike automatically since the content was
automatically recognized by the system:

"""
Your video may include clips that are owned by a third party.

To watch the matched clips please play the video on the right. The video
will play from the point where the matched content was identified.

Your video is available and playable.

Here are the details:

"Joy News @ 8 6 9 13)", audiovisual content administered by: 0:01
Multimedia Group
"""

This is one strike. Three strikes and our account is banned. And
actually I think we automatically lost some features of our partnership.

More infos:
https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2814000?hl=en


In order to get youtube.com/owasp I wanted to show professionalism from
us, but now this has been jeopardized quite much... so I'm kind of
disappointed on myself that I didn't think that people would upload
other things than what we discussed (Google Hangouts). But we are in a
learning process and I learned that in my documentation and presentation
at OWASP projects summit I'll have to talk about copyright issues.
