Hi Michael,

this is great news for Media Project as we continue to spread awareness
with the project. I'm sure the community call will also get more
exposure from this.

I added you as a related project on Media Project wiki page, but really
the promotion of the Community Updates will be with the YouTube channel
once you are integrated with it.

I've made a playlist where you'll be able to put future recording easily
in OWASP Global channel :
http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLpr-xdpM8wG-Ju2GQva18XVK85n-E-ZPA

To broadcast onto our official channel, it's the same steps except that
you did with previous Hangouts with the exception of having to switch
account to OWASP Media Project. See "YouTube + Hangout streaming
procedure" on our wiki page:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Media_Project#tab=Help
I just added you as a manager so you are good to go and you'll want to
join our mailing list since with that access you have access to
everything (that's a limitation with have at the moment).


I think every "global reaching" multimedia contributions such as yours
with "OWASP Community Call" should be published directly from Media
Project in order to join our efforts and get more views. But for less
globally related content, let's say the local chapters meetings, they
can use our methods to do their own channel and then we will link to
them like we do on that page:
https://www.youtube.com/user/OWASPGLOBAL/channels
They can also chose to be on any platform they want as we are looking to
support Vimeo as well with Josh's help: http://vimeo.com/owasp

Note that Media Project is still a big work in progress and right now we
are focusing on getting the conferences content right and integrating
compatible concepts such as the OWASP Community Call and the Webinars.


Thanks,
