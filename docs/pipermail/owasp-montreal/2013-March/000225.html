Hi OWASP Montreal list,

if you are using or making open source software or are interested into
Drupal you may not want to miss our next talk. It will be done by Ben
Jeavons, a software engineer at Acquia (the company behind Drupal), and
will be talking about the Drupal Security Team and how to deal with
security issues

Register here :
http://www.eventbrite.ca/event/5707605602

Also, if you know any developers that does work with Drupal, that talk
may interest them as it will cover Drupal security best practices.

If you still hesitating to join us, take note that we will provide
quality free food for the crowd. Also, it will be live on YouTube on our
channel : http://www.youtube.com/user/owaspmontreal

Full information on our official wiki page :
