A way to see if the patches are backported is to check the rpm
history, as Redhat lists the CVE it has patched for. It's no silver
bullet but it will give you a better idea.

http://www.cyberciti.biz/faq/linux-find-out-patch-can-cve-applied/

Also, to enhance the security of PHP, consider installing the suhosin
patch by Stefan Esser. This is one of the guys behind "The Month of
PHP Bugs" initiative.

http://www.hardened-php.net/suhosin/index.html
http://www.php-security.org/
