OWASP chapter meeting attendance count towards CISSP CPE points

1 hour = 1 CPE point

 

Procedure:

1) You provide an attendance sheet, such as
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Image:OWASP_Chapter_Attendance_Sheet.doc

To be filled in by CISSP's during the meeting.

 

2) Afterwards the chapter leader signs the sheet and sends a scan back.

 

3) Each CISSP does claim their own points and has this sheet as proof if
challenged.

 

PS: maybe we could ask ISC2 to automate this based on cissp # ?

Anybody has contact to get this ball rolling?
