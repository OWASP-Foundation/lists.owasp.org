Hi all,

I mentioned this to a couple of people at the meeting, but thought I'd drop
out a link.

http://shodan.surtri.com/

Basically a search engine for banners from scans of HTTP/Telnet/FTP servers.
Shows up some interesting things

http://shodan.surtri.com/?q=jetdirect - printers online
http://shodan.surtri.com/?q=camera+%22200+OK%22 - Webcams with open access
http://shodan.surtri.com/?q=port%3A23+%22list+of+built-in+commands%22
- Even logged in telnet shells!

They've disabled the net: filter at the moment which is a shame as it was an
easy no-touch way of assessing a netblock for some potential issues...
