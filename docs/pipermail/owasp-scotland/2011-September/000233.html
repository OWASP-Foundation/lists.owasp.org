I was going to raise this at the latest OWASP meeting, but we ran a little
late (due to two great talks!).

 

In terms of developing the Scottish security community and doing more in
Scotland, I have become a SANS Mentor with the aim of helping out with
providing more opportunity locally for knowledge transfer and personal
development for security professionals. 

 

As part of this, could I ask what the level of interest would be in people
taking the SANS GCIA certification
(http://www.giac.org/certification/certified-intrusion-analyst-gcia) or in
attending the SANS SEC 503 course
(http://www.sans.org/security-training/intrusion-detection-in-depth-43-mid).


 

 

Or if you know any individuals that would be interested?

 

If there is enough interest then we would be able to look at running a
Scottish based SANS SEC 503 course in either Glasgow or Edinburgh or both!
