Dear friends,

 

At my talk, I mentioned a research workshop we’re holding in the Informatics
Forum this week.

 

The schedule is now up on http://groups.inf.ed.ac.uk/security and appended
below.

 

You’re all most welcome to attend individual technical sessions at no charge
(but please drop me a note so we know you’re coming).  The short talks are
quite technical and probably only accessible to specialists but I hope the
two tutorials would be generally accessible.

 

Best regards,

 

Andy
