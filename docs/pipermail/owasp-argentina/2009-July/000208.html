This is a fantastic idea. I will gladly add Spanish podcasts to the current
series on it's own private feed, and send it through the mastering process
that I use for the current series.

We just need to find a Spanish host and someone to help edit the file before
it's sent to me to review and master.

I'll be glad to train that person about audio and skype recording.

OWASP pays 100$ USD to get each show mastered. Recording and editing the
show is by volunteers.

If you find a volunteer to host and edit the Spanish show, the mastering
will be free - or at least paid for by OWASP.

Our mastering guy is Mexican and speaks Spanish, btw. :)
