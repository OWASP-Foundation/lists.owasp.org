*Excellent Students for the Security Division, NDS*

NDS (now part of Cisco) is looking to fill several positions in our
security division. We are looking for* *talented developers (student,
recent graduate or seasoned developer) for a new software development team
in an unorthodox R&D security group. The team develops tools, cryptography
and security related prototypes, libraries, simulators and server side apps.

Team members usually work with C/C++ or Python, and reusing open source
packages.

The team develops new products from the requirements stage to a deliverable
product in a variety of leading technologies.

We're looking for quick-learning talents with an open mind, mathematical
ability, team spirit and a hands-on attitude to tackling technical
challenges.

*Requirements*

   - 2ND year student in Computer Science- Average 85+
   - Excellent graduates in CS, AM, Math or EE - Average 85+
   - Knowledge in C/C++, especially for low-level performance-critical tasks
   - Security/hacker mindset
   - Good, broad understanding of computing and technology (compilation,
   tool chains, networking, OS, web, DB, ...)
   - Ability to study, assess and integrate 3rd party libraries
   - Mathematical and scientific skills, abstract thinking
   - Fast, independent learner
   - Familiarity with Linux/Unix environment - an advantage
   - Knowledge in Python – an advantage

Please send CVs to gbarnhart at nds.com
