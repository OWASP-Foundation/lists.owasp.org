*Hi all,*



*AppSec Labs* (founded by Erez Metula) are looking for a senior application
security expert.



*Job Description:*

Join our great team as a senior application security expert providing our
services: penetration testing, consulting and training.

   - Providing technical support for the team members
   - Performing application level penetration testing in advanced projects
   (web, desktop application, mobile, IoT – black/gray/white box)
   - Providing SDLC consulting and secure coding training.



*Job Requirements:*

-          At least two years’ experience in conducting pen-tests and
consulting on the application level

-          Knowledge of applicative attacks such as parameter manipulation,
CSRF, XSS, SQL injection, etc.

-          Knowledge in server side programming (Java, .Net, C++, etc.) &
protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, SSL, DNS) – the more the better!

-          Experience in code review – must!

-          Knowledge in system architecture

-          B.Sc. graduated in Computer Science – advantage

-          Knowledge / Experience in SDLC and secure coding processes -
advantage

-          Training experience - advantage

-          Other relevant skills (Reverse engineering, Threat-Modeling,
Cryptography, etc.)



*Required Skills:*

-          Independent work and self-learning ability

-          English written and verbal skills

-          Training skills – advantage

-          Willingness to travel overseas – advantage





*About AppSec Labs*

Our services focus on secure coding consultation, advanced training
programs and advanced application penetration testing.

Our projects are very unique & challenging, as we are known as one of the
top quality firms for application security, mainly leading the field of
mobile hacking (including training in BlackHat 2013 to 2017!) and secure
coding training in all computing languages.



Does this position interest you? If so, please contact me here or in our
mail: *jobs at appsec-labs.com* <jobs at appsec-labs.com>
