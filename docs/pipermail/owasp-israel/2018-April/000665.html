Hi OWASP Israel members,

 

We’re excited to announce that DevSecCon is coming to Tel Aviv for the first time, to bring together DevOps and Security in a unique conference! Join us for a day filled with inspiring talks and interactive workshops about DevSecOps – the practice of building security into development processes. This is your chance to learn from key industry experts, meet with your peers and learn how DevOps and Security can work together to make continuously secure development a reality. As part of our ongoing partnership with OWASP all OWASP Israel members receive a 20% discount on conference passes.

 

Where and when: 07 May 2018 | Tel Aviv Convention Center

Registration: Standard pass – ₪685.00 and 20% off for OWASP members (use promo code OWASP-DSC-TLV-20)

https://www.devseccon.com/tel-aviv-2018/

 

Scholarship scheme

As part of our new scholarship scheme we have set aside a number of free tickets for women and other underrepresented groups at our conference. Find out more here:https://www.devseccon.com/blog/2018/04/11/scholarship-scheme/

 

To be able to fund the scholarship scheme, we’ve introduced a new type of sponsorship level, the Diversity & Inclusion sponsorship. If you’re interested in supporting the conference as a sponsor, please contact us.

 

We hope to see you at the conference!

 

Francois Raynaud    

DevSecCon Founder
