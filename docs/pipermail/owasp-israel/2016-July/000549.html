The Needs Assessment Report for the  <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/9156c55f90/c44c82c64b/13bfade249> Website Project has been completed.  Before the board votes on how to go forward, it is imperative that we receive input from you.  The report is available  <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/9156c55f90/c44c82c64b/0e96bf83a4> here please look through it and leave your comments on the <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/9156c55f90/c44c82c64b/4677e9bdaa>  Discussion Page.  We will also be hosting a Twitter and FB chat to discuss the project.  

The thread for the Facebook chat will be posted from 09.00 UTC to 15.00 UTC at our Facebook page <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/9156c55f90/c44c82c64b/53b6927425> .  The Twitter chat will be from 15.00 UTC to 16.00 UTC. Use  #OWASPchat to be part of the discussion. 

 

This is a major investment for OWASP in terms of funds, time, and importance.  The decisions made here will have lasting impacts on the OWASP website and experience.  Please talk about this with your chapter and project teams on mailing lists, chapter/team social media, and in your meetings should you have them.  I STRONGLY ENCOURAGE you to comment on the  <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/9156c55f90/c44c82c64b/7fcb46f8e2> discussion page and join the Twitter/FB chat.

Please also reach out to your project or chapter leader to ensure they know what is happening as well as other OWASP members in your network. 

 

Feel free and encouraged to post from your personal social media accounts.
