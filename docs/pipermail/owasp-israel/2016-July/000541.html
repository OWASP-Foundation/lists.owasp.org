Hi All,

As in every year, OWASP AppSec Israel is the best, biggest, and most
interesting conference in Israel for Application Security. This year we are
expecting well over 600 attendees, including security professionals,
developers, managers, and more….


Whether you have a product to showcase, or you are recruiting, sponsoring
the OWASP AppSec Israel event gets you the right exposure. It also helps
the OWASP community, and ensures that we can keep up doing better and
better conferences.
This year, our conference will be hosted in “Michlala Leminhal” in Rishon
Letzion, Monday, September 19, 2016.

For more details on the available sponsorship options please see:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2016_Sponsorships


For any questions and to confirm your sponsorship you can reach out to us:
Or K. katz3112 at gmail.com and Avi D. douglen at hotmail.com
