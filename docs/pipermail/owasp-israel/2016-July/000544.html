GE Digital is growing and looking for new talents to join along!

GE's Cybersecurity Research Lab (Located in Herzliya) is looking for a Talented Cyber Security Researcher to join our great team!

Job description -Cyber Security Researcher
GE Cybersecurity Research Lab develops and applies innovating technologies to protect critical GE products from a broad spectrum of Cyber-enabled risks and threats. The ideal candidate will possess deep knowledge and experience in Cyber Security attack techniques and proper mitigation solutions in both the application level and infrastructure level. Essential duties include (but are not limited to) the following:

*         Pen-Testing skills and proven experience
*         Discover and mitigate vulnerabilities in sensitive Critical Infrastructure
*         Develop and design innovative cyber security solutions for unique and complex technologies
*         Work in partnership with government agencies, leading industry experts, and academia
*         Leverage traditional and non-traditional research methodologies to advance GE's overall Cybersecurity practice

Job description - Software Engineer (Herzliya)

Job description
As part of the cyber security lab in Israel, we are responsible for delivering security components for GE cloud based solution named Predix. The group develops solutions for the company's dedicated PAAS layer and cyber security products. The challenge is huge - not every day a development group creates such innovative solutions for the IIOT world and therefore, the sense of creativity and satisfaction is enormous.

If you'd like to be part of an amazing team, perform challenging and meaningful work and make the difference - we are looking for you!



Essential Responsibilities

In this role you will:
*         Be a part of a software development team, which should be able to deliver very complex software platforms and products consistently
*         Work with an agile development cycle
*         Support process improvements to drive higher quality and productivity
*         Work cross functionally with other business departments to align activities and deliverables
*         Drive world-class quality in the development and support of products



Qualifications/Requirements
*         Bachelor's degree, or higher, in Computer Science/Computer Engineering/ equivalent experience
*         2+ years software development experience with proven skills of architecture and design
*         A clear understanding of cloud service and deployment models
*         Loves writing code in languages like: Java, Groovy, Python, Node.JS, Go, Ruby
*         Experience with cyber security, distributed systems server side development and web architectures
*         Experience with Platform as a Service (PaaS) such as Cloud Foundry, Heroku or similar
*         Experience with virtualized infrastructure and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) such as: vSphere, OpenStack, Amazon Web Services, or Google Compute Engine
*         Strong team player - collaborates well with others to solve problems and actively incorporates input from various sources
