Amdocs IT is looking for information security line manager:



*         Determines organization wide vision for iinformation security issues, policies and standards

*         Identifies and communicates security protection goals and objectives with suitable measurement KPI's to support the business security requirements

*         Audit and reporting on all aspects of Information security

Definition and monitoring of the Information Security incident processes

*         Serve as an internal Information security consultant to the various business stakeholders to assist / advise / educate on all aspects of Information Security and compliance

*         Ensure proper access controls and identity verification are in place
Create and manage a Qualitative risk register conforming to all appropriate standards

*         Ensure effective levels of data asset protection are in place and monitored

*         Initiate, facilitates and promote activities to create information security awareness within the organization.

*         Monitor compliance with the organizations security policies and procedures among employees, contractors and other third parties and takes corrective action where necessary

*         Monitor advancements in Information security Technologies
Monitor changes in legislation and accreditation standards to ensure compliance

*         Be the IS interface into all Business accreditation requirements



Required Knowledge / Experience:



*         +10 years of experience in information security

*         +5 years of experience in managing global distributed teams

*         Experience in implementation ISO27001 and/or similar standards

*         Proven track record of creating and driving a security aware culture within an organization

*         Detailed knowledge of international differences in data protection laws and compliance

*         PCI/DSS background

*         Proven record of implementing valid KPI measures

*         Pragmatic approach to risk and mitigation

*         Ability to converse with Technicians, developers and business unit sponsors about all things security in a manner they will be able to associate
software testing for Security vulnerabilities
