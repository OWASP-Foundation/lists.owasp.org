Security Architect, position #24230

Amdocs Cyber Security team is looking for security architect which will play a key role in implementing security controls and tracking security development activities for supporting projects / initiatives.

 Role Definition:
Lead the security solution architecture throughout project lifecycle (Business development, Presale, Scoping, Design, Development, Ongoing operations). Ensure security solution meets customer business and technical needs by leveraging Amdocs assets and best practices while integrating with customer IT environment.
* Lead the security design throughout the project lifecycle and be accountable for security solution integrity.
* Gain insight into customer's security environment, challenges, and opportunities in order to propose areas where Amdocs products and solutions can add value. Ensure proposed security solution reflects Amdocs value proposition and competitive advantage
* Maintain deep knowledge of industry and technology security trends, security standards in order to identify business opportunities.
* Share and communicate end-to-end security solutions (orally and in writing) to executives, business sponsors, and technical resources in a clear and concise manner that is in the vernacular of each group.
* Guide various product experts with regard to the security solution.
* Create and support POC/demos and present security solutions to client.
* Analyze and evaluate security trends, vulnerabilities and provide ongoing feedback to products management about for delivery of best practices.
* Develop, implement, and administer security policies and plans for Amdocs products.
* Maintain, update and administrator the implementation the Amdocs Secure Development Life Cycle (SDLC) within Amdocs development teams.

3 reasons why you should apply for this role!
1. working with multi channels and divers area of expertise
2. making a change and contribute to the success of the company
3. Managing complex program in a global company

Critical Experiences and skills:
* At least 3+ years of proven experience in defining security requirements, security solutions and security project implementation.
* At least 2+ years of customer-facing experience.
* In-depth knowledge of information security concepts, design/architecture and methodologies
* Knowledge with security aspects of operating systems (Windows, Linux, and Unix), Database and network

* Knowledge in (PCI DSS, SoX, DPA, and so on).
* In-depth knowledge on information security standards (ISO 27001, OWASP, and so on)
* Knowledge of Secure SDLC methodology
* Must be willing to travel on short notice
o Bachelors degree in Computer Science or related field - Advantage
o Security related certifications (CISSP, CISM, CISA, etc...) - Advantage
