לאחר חופשת הקיץ, כמו בכל שנה, ניפגש השנה לכנס השנתי, ביום ב', ה-6/9/2010.

Hello All,

 

Like very year, after the summer vacation we will meet this year for the
Annual OWASP Israel Conference on Monday, 6-Sep-2010.

The conference will be held, as every year, in the IDC in Herzliya, Thanks
to the kind help of Dr. Anat Bremler-Barr.

An agenda and additional details will be sent closer to the conference date.

 

Lecturers wishing to present in the conference and companies wishing to
sponsor the conference should contact me by mail.
