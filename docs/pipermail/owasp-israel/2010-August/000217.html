CALL FOR PAPERS - OWASP ISRAEL 2010

 

If you wish to give a lecture in the upcoming OWASP, please submit to me by
email by Saturday, 28/8/2010.

 

Each submission must include:

.         Presenter name (& title)

.         Presenter bio (3-5 lines)

.         Lecture Title

.         Lecture Digest (couple of paragraph)

 

 

Topics:           

Being OWASP, anything that has to do with Web Application Security. 

We are also open to submissions in related topics, including (non-web)
application security, cloud security, etc.
