RSA FirstWatch, a world-class threat research and intelligence team is
looking for a talented senior security researcher to join our Cybercrime
Research Lab in Israel (Herzliya).



*Job Description:*

-          Professionally leading the malware research team in the
Cybercrime Research Lab

-          Reverse Engineering malicious code designed for Windows and
Linux operating systems

-          Attending and presenting the malware team’s findings at leading
security conferences

-          Writing whitepapers and publishing the malware team’s findings
in the RSA blog



*Job Requirements:*



-          Must:

o   At least 2 years of hands-on experience in RE of x86 assembly (static
disassembly and dynamic debugging)

o   Hands-on experience with executable protectors and packers

o   Hands-on experience with common RE tools: IDA, Ollydbg/Windbg/gbd

o   Hands-on experience with any scripting language (Python, Ruby, PHP,
Perl)



-          Advantage:

o   Hands-on experience with malware research and analysis

o   Solid knowledge in Cryptography

o   Hands-on experience with vulnerability research

o   Good familiarity with web application security concepts
