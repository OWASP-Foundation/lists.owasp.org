All volunteers are entitled to a free conference ticket!

(They will also receive an official conference t-shirt…) 

If you are able to spend some of the time to support the running of the conference, please be in touch with Hemed Gur Ary (hemed at gurary.co.il <mailto:hemed at gurary.co.il> ) and send him your phone number.

Only ONE week left!! 

AppSec Israel, the biggest and bestest application security conference in Israel – celebrating our TENTH year running, and bigger and better than ever! 

Many hundreds of your peers have already registered – as of now, we are expecting close to 650 (!!) attendees. 

Registration is only open until the end of the week – do it now! https://appsecil2016.eventbrite.com/ 

 

The conference is scheduled for Monday, September 19, 2016 (all day, from 8:30 to 18:00), and will have multiple tracks of fantastic talks by an amazing group of speakers.

More details here: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2016 

 

In addition, this year AppSec Israel will also be hosting our very first Capture the Flag competition! 

(With prizes, of course!) 

More details, including explanation on how it works, are here: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2016_CTF 

 

All this for the incredible price of FREE!
