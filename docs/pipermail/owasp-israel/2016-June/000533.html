Cyber Together will have a special event with a very special guest:  Mr. Ray
Rothrock    <https://www.linkedin.com/in/ray-rothrock-75b9403>
https://www.linkedin.com/in/ray-rothrock-75b9403  

 

Mr. Rothrock Chairman and CEO of RedSeal. since February 2014.

He is a general partner at Venrock, one of RedSeal's founding investors.

At Venrock Mr. Rothrock invested in 53 companies including over a dozen in
cybersecurity including Vontu, PGP, P-Cube, Imperva, Cloudflare, CTERA, and
Shape Security.

He is on the board of Check Point Software Technology, Ltd. an original
Venrock investment, and Team8, both Tel Aviv-based companies.
