Subject: Akamai's Threat Research Team is Hiring

 

Hello,

 

Our team is rapidly growing, and we're looking for superstar researchers.

 

Akamai's Threat Research team is at the core of Akamai's Web Security
business unit, which provides cloud based application layer protections for
the largest organizations in the world.

The team is responsible for researching and designing security controls for
products such as - Akamai's Bot Manager, Akamai Kona Site Defender (WAF),
Client Reputation, DDoS protection, etc. Our research is based on Akamai's
unparalleled visibility into as much as 30% of all Internet traffic - more
than 2 trillion web hits per day! (more than 2 Petabytes of data!), which
are accessible only to us, through Akamai's Cloud Security Intelligence
(CSI) big data platform.

 

If you are interested in web technologies, application layer attackers,
detecting, profiling and fingerprinting bots, devices and malicious users,
and dream about having access to intelligence from all over the Internet -
you should join us!

 

You can find the job description in our web site at:

one for a team lead.
