In one sentence
We are looking for a senior product manager to lead our security strategy and roadmap, to serves as a security liaison between our customers, various stakeholders and senior/ executive management.  Operate as a technical subject matter expert and advice project team regarding data and application security. Create and Maintain risk management model to be utilized as a decision making tool.

What will your job look like?

  *   You will define and drive Amdocs cross portfolio Security strategy and roadmap
  *   You will define the Security Development Life Cycle (SDLC) model across Amdocs product portfolio
  *   You will lead the security solution, tools and methodologies
  *   You will threat management and security Analysis models  management
  *   You will oversee the creation of architectural security blueprints and best practices
  *   You will lead customer validation processes
  *   You will collaborate with Amdocs main vendors and 'brings' the latest trends, best practices and tools to Amdocs
All you need is...

  *   Deep understanding and knowledge in security regulation requirement  and standards - PCI , PII , OWASP , SOX
  *   5+ years of experience in product management, preferred in Application security  -  Authentication, Authorization, Audits, PKI, Encryption methods   and protocols
  *   Experience in security products such as  FW,WAF,DAF and LB
  *   Deep understanding of infrastructure technologies including Cloud, Virtualization, Deployment and Operational tools and best practices .
  *   background in Linux and Java security required (hand-son, design, architecture)
  *   Ability to simplify technologies discussions and arguments to executive levels while maintaining low level knowledge when discussing with engineers.
  *   Proven ability to interact and collaborate across the organization, including working closely with R&D, delivery (professional services) and customer support
  *    Self-learner - being able to understand the Telco business , the main components and the high and low level related architecture
Why you will love this job?

  *   You will be able to use your expert knowledge of market needs and product capabilities to drive game-changing insights
  *   You will be a key member of a global, dynamic and highly collaborative team with various possibilities for personal and professional development
  *   You will have the opportunity to work in multinational environment for the global market leader in its field
