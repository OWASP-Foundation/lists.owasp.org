All the positions are in GO IT Unit at Amdocs:
Cloud Architect
In one sentence
Lead the Cloud related security solution architecture throughout project lifecycle (Business development, Presale, Scoping, Design, Development, Ongoing operations). Ensure security solution meets customer business and technical needs by leveraging Amdocs assets and best practices while integrating with customer (Internal and external) IT environment.

Who are we?
If you're a smartphone user then you are part of an ever more connected and digital world. At Amdocs, we are leading the digital revolution into the future. From virtualized telecommunications networks, Big Data and Internet of Things to mobile financial services, billing and operational support systems, we are continually evolving our business to help you become more connected. We make sure that when you watch a video on YouTube, message friends on SnapChat or send your images on Instagram, you get a great service anytime, anywhere, and on any device. We are at the heart of the telecommunications industry working with giants such as AT&T, Vodafone, Telstra and Telefonica, helping them create an amazing new world for you where technology is being used in amazing new ways every single day.
What will your job look like?
*         You will work with multiple hybrid cloud environment and vendors.
*         You will be a member of the cloud security Architects responsible for the security aspects of the design throughout a project lifecycle and accountable for security solution integrity with the relevant to cloud environment and integration with it.
*         You will maintain deep knowledge of cloud related solutions on every level IaaS, PaaS and SaaS, containers and other virtualization technologies and its related security trends.
*         You will have deep knowledge of security standards and practices that will assist in identifying business opportunities with emphasis on cutting edge security technologies.
*         You will guide various product experts regarding the security solution in general and cloud related.
*         You will create and support POC/demos and present security solutions to client (Internal and external).
*         You will analyze and evaluate and study cloud security trends, vulnerabilities and provide ongoing feedback to products management about for delivery of best practices.

All you need is...
*         3 years of security architect experience in large company - Must
*         Proven strong network and infrastructure background - Must
*         Good writing, reading and speaking skills in English - Must
*         Cloud security in the enterprise - Must!!! (I wrote advantage to include more applicants)
*         Cloud security certification (CCSK, CCSP) - Advantage
*         General security certificate (CISSP, CEH, C|CISO) - Advantage
