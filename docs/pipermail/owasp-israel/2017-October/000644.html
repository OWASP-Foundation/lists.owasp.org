On 2 November at 12:00 we are opening the German-Israeli Cybersecurity Accelerator (HIPA) at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. Among others, the goal is to generate an ecosystem to foster innovation in security between the two countries. Who should take part: entrepreneurs, startups, and experts in cybersecurity. The ceremony is opened by the German Hessian minister of science Boris Rhein. Details are in the enclosed invitation.

RSVP to hipa at sit.fraunhofer.de<mailto:hipa at sit.fraunhofer.de>
