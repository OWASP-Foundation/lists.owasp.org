Appsec Israel is less than two weeks away and we are working hard getting
everything done.
As in past years we are happy for all the help we can get and we are
building a team of volunteers for the event days.

The core team of volunteers will be doing some or all of the following
roles:

   - Hospitality
   - Lecture room hosts
   - Crowd control
   - Logistics
   - Go-fer'ing

Additionally we are looking for volunteers to do one of these specialized
roles:

   - Survey management - writing conference surveys and analyzing the
   responses
   - Resume assessment booth - we will designate a booth where anyone can
   come and have his/her resume assessed by professionals

So if you can donate an hour or more to any of the roles above the
community will really appreciate it. Please contact me by e-mail.

Cheers,
Hemed
