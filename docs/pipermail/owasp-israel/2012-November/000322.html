Hi All,

 

You may be bothered by the number of job offering e-mails sent lately. I
think this is an important service to the community. While the number has
been large in recent days, my experience is that this is a tide that comes
and goes: when people see others posting they post themselves. While I tried
in the past to create a "digest" of messages I found out that a week or two
down the line it became unused.

 

I am committed to make sure that this is not abused. Specifically that the
same people don't post over and over again.
