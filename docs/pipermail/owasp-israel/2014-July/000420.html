Cyber Together

 

We are planning to have a small and intimate round table meeting with guests from Australia :

1)     Mr. Mark Sayer, Head of Security Strategy & Architecture- National Australia Bank  :  <https://www.linkedin.com/in/marksayer> https://www.linkedin.com/in/marksayer          

2)     Mr. Kobi Ben-Shabath, Managing Director- Open Platform Systems  :  <http://www.opsystems.com.au/> http://www.opsystems.com.au/  

3)     Mr. Rob Siganto, Managing Director- Bridge Point Australia :     <http://www.bridgepoint.com.au/content/managed-security-services-0> http://www.bridgepoint.com.au/content/managed-security-services-0

The speakers will make a presentation or lecture about the cyber security trends, needs, regulations and business practice in the Australian market.   

We will host only 20 cyber security vendors from Israel in this event (one per vendor).

The vendors representative should be level of : CEO, CTO, VP Sales, VP Marketing, VP Business Development , VP Product 

The event will be in Tel Aviv Tuesday 15th July 2014 16:00 – 19:00.

A notice about the exact location will be sent with the confirmation.

Registration is by a reply e-mail with your name and company details.

Registration and attending this round table event is free of charge, however we need your commitment to attend the event.

Thank you.
