*Denial of Service Researcher*



Position Summary:



Being a member of the security research team which is responsible for
vulnerability and denial of service research, evolving threats analysis,

attack signatures development and product security assurance.



Responsibilities:



·         Performing  DoS Layer3-7 volumetric and more complex attacks to
evaluate product's protection mechanisms.

·         Analysis of traffic from customers and assisting emergency
response team.

·         Analysis of evolving DDoS attack techniques and bot samples.

·         Providing input into the behavioral analysis research.

·         Mining DDoS related intelligence from blogs, forums, conferences
and academy papers

·         Collaborate with developers to deliver new defense mechanisms.



Qualifications:

·         At least 2 years in conducting denial of service penetration
testing in Layer3-7.

·         At least 3 years expertise in DDoS detection and prevention.

·         Wide experience with real DDoS attacks.

·         Familiarity with DoS attacking tools.

·         Extensive background in TCP/IP networking and Unix based
operating systems.

·         Proven background in application level DoS.

·         Experience with writing tools for penetration testing.

·         Strong understanding of security principles, theories and attacks.

·         Advantage: Experience in reverse engineering

*C programmer*

An innovation team at the F5 Networks Tel-Aviv R&D center is looking for a
proficient C programmer to help us implement a cutting edge distributed,
self-learning and self-adjusting scalable web application security product.



If you like:

·         building high quality software from scratch with cutting edge
technologies

·         have proven experience in crafting efficient and maintainable
software systems in C

·         able to divide and conquer complex problems into simple, easy,
maintainable, modular and testable solutions

·         A master of your domain

– we’re looking for you.



Who we’re looking for:

·         Someone with high proficiency in C programming (a C expert)

·         With a solid understanding of data structures and algorithms

·         A software craftsman

·         experienced in developing under GNU/Linux

·         has very good design skills

·         initiative, self-motivated, gets-things-done attitude

·         collaborative who doesn’t let ego into the workplace

·         strong problem-solving ability

·         excellent reliability, dependability and trustworthiness

·         high attention to detail and accuracy



You might also have:

·         familiarity and understanding of Internet Protocols, especially
TCP/IP and HTTP/S

·         experience in scripting languages such as Perl or Python

·         knowledge of web applications and web servers and how they work

·         understanding of functional programming and of design oriented
programming

·         AWS stuff: EC2, S3, Autoscaling, etc., etc., etc.



Responsibilities:

·         Develop our infrastructure in C for stability, scalability,
performance, testability and for change

·         Be responsible for coding, unit testing, debugging, performance
testing and deployment of major components of the system

·         Participate in design of components and of APIs

·         Participate in design reviews, peer reviews and work according to
the team’s development methodologies

·         Design and build robust software in C to handle high-performance
computing and networking as well as solutions for big data problems

·         Be the owner of major components of the software modules

·         Handle production issues and ad hoc performance issues
