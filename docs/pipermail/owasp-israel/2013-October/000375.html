Hi Everybody! 

 

The OWASP AppSec Israel conference last Tuesday was a huge success - well
over 450 people attended! 

Thanks to everybody who participated and supported us! 

 

Most of the presentations are already online -
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Israel_2013_Presentations , the rest
will be uploaded as soon as possible. 

 

If you have any pictures, videos, etc of the conference - please send a link
to them, to Ofer Shezaf (ofer at shezaf.com <mailto:ofer at shezaf.com> ) to share
on the web page. 

 

Some numbers about the Conference: 

-          Well over 450 attended (out of 615 registered). For context, this
was bigger than AppSecEU! 

-          11 Sponsors

-          9 Booths

-          12 Track sessions (in 2 auditoriums)

-          2 Plenary sessions

-          1 Keynote

-          17 Speakers 

-          3 coffee breaks and one lunch :) 

 

Our thanks go out to: 

-          A special thanks to Dr. Anat Bremler and the IDC, who graciously
host us every year. 

-          Thank you to all the sponsors that enabled us to make the most
out of the conference, without worrying about hunger, thirst, or caffeine
deficiency. 

-          Thanks to all the speakers, who made it all worthwhile.  

-          Thanks also to all those who submitted a proposal for a talk, but
unfortunately we were not able to fit in this year. I hope you continue to
contribute, and that we'll be able to hear you next time! 

-          And thanks to all the staff that helped organize the event -
Ilil, Ofer, Or, Ory, and Itzik! 

 

See you next year! 

 

Avi D 

Chairman, OWASP Israel
