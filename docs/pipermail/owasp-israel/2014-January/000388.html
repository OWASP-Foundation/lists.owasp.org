We have a new exiting research team leader position, below are the details

Application security research team leader

Imperva, the leader in Data Security, is looking to hire an Application security research team leader as part of our CTO group, based at our technically advanced R&D Center located in Tel Aviv.

A senior application security research team leader, reporting directly to the company's CTO. Managing a team of researchers and analysts focused on web application security. Responsibilities include:
Defining and leading research projects and activities related to vulnerabilities and mitigations as well as new web technologies.
 Publishing research documents and giving conference presentations in leading trade shows.

Qualifications:


  *   8-10 years of industry experience.
  *   Strong information security background and knowledge, application security knowledge - an advantage.
  *   Strong background in web technologies and computer networking.
  *   Technical and hands on. Capable of understanding the technical details of issues and providing technical guidance to team members.
  *   Proven experience in managing teams and managing research.
  *   M.Sc. in computer science or similar domain (electric engineering, physics).
  *   Excellent writing and speaking capabilities in English.
  *   Experience in public speaking. Experience in giving conference presentation - an advantage.
