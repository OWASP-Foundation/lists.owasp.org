Hi Everyone,

The conference is still in need of additional sponsors in order to cover the expenses of this year’s event. You can find more details about the sponsorship plans here:  https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_IL_Sponsorship

** Attention Non-profits, Government offices, and small Start-ups: **
For the first time, we have an entry-level sponsorship plan called “Community Sponsorship” ( https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_IL_Sponsorship#Community_Supporters ) - the price is only $500 USD.

This entry level sponsorship enables you to:

 *   Show your appreciation to OWASP Israel and this event in particular (especially if you send numerous employees to the conference)
 *   You will be able to leave a stack of flyers or swag at a central table
 *   Have your logo appear on the conference page

This level does not entitle you to a booth/table of your own - but it’s still a great way to be a part of this event!

Thank you for your support,

Subject: OWASP AppSec IL 2014 Conference - Call For Sponsors

Hello Everyone,

The annual OWASP AppSec Israel Conference will take place this year on Tuesday, September 2, 2014. The conference will take place at the IDC in Herzliya, as in previous years.

OWASP Israel encourages companies to sponsor the conferences and help pay for expenses such as refreshments, photography, video etc. The conferences are not commercial and the cost goes directly to cover expenses. Since our conferences draw an increasingly large number of attendees, conference expenses also rise accordingly.

If you or your company benefit from OWASP materials and/or conferences, we encourage you to support us! Moreover, the associated publicity and community goodwill can go a long way. We have several levels of sponsorships available, appropriate for the different organizations that wish to support OWASP Israel activities. Of course, if you are an OWASP Corporate Member (at any level) and allocate a percentage (20-40%) of your membership fees to the Israeli chapter, we can consider those funds as conference sponsorship. It is also possible to be creative and find non-financial ways to support us and contribute to the successful running of the conference.

You can find more information about conference sponsorship in the following page:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_IL_Sponsorship

If you have any questions, or would like your organization to sponsor the event, please contact Ory Segal: orysegal at gmail.com<mailto:orysegal at gmail.com>

We truly appreciate your help!
