AppSec Labs are looking for quality, open-minded people!

-          Programming languages - the more the better! (PHP, Java, .NET

The following are not mandatory requirements, but would be considered a

-          Programmers

-          Knowledge / experience in the field of information security

-          High level English

-          Willingness to travel overseas
