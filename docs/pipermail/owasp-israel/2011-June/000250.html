Hi all,

 

We are looking for a Security Researcher - see below.

 

Regards,

Ziv Gadot

SOC Team Leader


Security Researcher, Tel-Aviv


 

Position Description 

 

    * The Security Researcher will join the research group in Radware's
security business unit, which researches new network attacks. Research
is focused on Denial-of-Service attacks and includes analysis of malware
and botnet, attacking tools and technique, development of mitigation
techniques and more. The researcher will witness real attacks
experienced by our customers, participate in their mitigation and
analyze them. 

 

 

Requirements

 

    * Relevant experience in Vulnerability Research, Reverse Engineering
and Malware Analysis

    * Relevant experience with OllyDbg, WinDbg or IDA Pro

    * Excellent understanding of networking design principles and
protocols (e.g. TCP/IP, IPv4)

    * In-depth knowledge of the inner-workings of operating systems

    * In-depth knowledge of Python and C/C++ programming languages

    * Excellent English written and verbal communication skills

    * BSc/BA -an advantage
