Hi all,

 

OWASP is supporting the Black Hat Briefings & Training USA event, July
23-28,  2005 at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, described by organizers as
the world's premier technical ICT security conference. Black Hat
profiles next generation threats, delivers practical security
techniques, and an understanding of legal and policy issues. The
Briefings are designed to foster peer-to-peer communication and
networking opportunities with over 2,000 security professionals from 30+
nations. Featuring 29 hands-on training courses July 23-26, and 10
conference tracks at the Briefings July 27-28, with presentations by
security experts and "underground" security specialists - and no vendor
sales pitches.  OWASP members can receive a $100 discount off the
Briefings fees applicable on the date of registration by inserting the
code OWASP2005US in the box marked Coupon Code on the electronic
registration form.  The Early bird rate of $1395 (discounted to

$1295) expires July 1.  Register at www.blackhat.com
