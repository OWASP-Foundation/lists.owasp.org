MyHeritage is recruiting a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) to
lead its security efforts. The CISO is responsible for developing and
executing an Information Security Program to maintain the protection of our
consumer-facing products and internal IT services, and to continue ensuring
that MyHeritage’s physical and digital information assets are compliant
with external regulations and policies.
