About the speaker
Tal Be’ery is an EIR at Singtel Innov8 VC. Previously, Tal was a Senior Security Research Manager at Microsoft and the former VP Research at Aorato (acquired by Microsoft). Tal was the lead author of the TIME attack against the HTTPS/SSL protocol, returning speaker at security industry prestigious events including RSA, Blackhat and AusCERT. Tal holds a B.Sc and a M.Sc degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), and is a columnist for the Securityweek.com magazine.
