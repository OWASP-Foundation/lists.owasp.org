Dear all,
You are cordially invited to join us in this month's Security Theater
seminars.  In contrast to the previous seminars, which were videotaped,
there are four very real and very interesting speakers this month (see
below).

Hope to see you there!
Yossi.

Wednesday, May 6, 15:00 – 16:30
Speaker: *Itsik Mantin*, Leader of Security/Cryptography Group at NDS
Subject: *Beyond Cryptography: Securing Digital Content*

Wednesday, May 13, 15:00 – 16:30
Speaker: Superintendent *Meir Hayoun*, LLC., Lahav 433 computer crime squad,
Israel Police
Subject: *Computer Crime in Israel
*
Wednesday, May 20, 15:00 – 16:30
Speaker: *Adi Sharabani*, manager of the IBM Rational Security Group
Subject: *Active Man-in-the-Middle attacks against Web Applications*


Wednesday, May 27, 15:00 – 16:30
Speaker: *Alon Rosen*, IDC Hertzliya
Subject: *Cryptographic Elections - How to Simultaneously Achieve
Verifiability and Privacy*
