Hi all

RSA, the security division of EMC is looking for a Phishing researcher !!
The Phishing researcher will assume full ownership and responsibility for
researching Phishing attacks and analyzing sophisticated attacks to learn
their method of operation while trying to identify vulnerabilities that may
help in fighting such threats.

* *

*PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES*

   - Analyze Phishing attacks and Phishing kits
   - Identify vulnerabilities
   - Develop techniques to combat online threats and implement them

*Qualifications:*

   - 2-4 years of experience in the IT Security Industry
   - Strong knowledge of web application security and network protocols
   - Working knowledge of UNIX, LINUX and Microsoft technologies
   - Proven experience in network vulnerability scanning
   - Scripting capabilities (Perl, Python – advantage)
   - PHP knowledge - Advantage
   - Previous working experience at web application security's company

*SKILLS*

   - Knowledge with appropriate networks, products and protocols
   - Ability to work independently
   - Self learner
   - Self managed
