GeekCon is an annual invite-only geek weekend held for the past 7 years, at
which creative technological people build projects, hack and drink.
This year we're adding a separate hacking track for on-location activities

We're having a Pre-Con participants meeting this Thursday the 24/5 at 20:30
on Wix.com roof (Tel Aviv port) to introduce new people and the new track
and activities, and to start brainstorming ideas and building teams.
