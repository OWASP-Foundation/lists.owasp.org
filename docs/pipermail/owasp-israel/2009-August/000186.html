About Checkmarx

Checkmarx was founded in 2006 with the vision of providing comprehensive
solutions for automated security code review. The company pioneered the
concept of a query language-based solution for tracking technical and
logical code vulnerabilities.

Software developers are expected to rise to the challenge and deliver
applications which are both safe and secure.

The company is located in Tel Aviv. Today Checkmarx's R&D and sales
activities are expanding and they are looking for "stars" to join the team
in the following positions:

 

 

 

(cx-10) Application Engineers 
We are looking for excellent application engineers to support our R&D
activities.Job requirements:

.         At least 5 years of software applications  development


.         At least 23- years of software development experience in C#
(Winform clients and / or Server backend) 

.         Experience in development .NET version 3/3.5 - advantage

.         Development UI Winform. MVC/MVP- advantage

.         Excellent development skills 

.         Great understanding of OO concepts 

.         Experience developing/designing large software systems 

.         Enjoy learning new technologies

.         Self-learner 

.         BS in Computer science 

.         Preferred - a background in the software security industry
(penetration testing,  

                code review.) 

.         Excellent team / autonomous work capabilities 

 

 

(Pre-11) Pre/Post Sales Engineers
We are looking for talented pre/post-sales engineers to support our Sales
and Business Development activities worldwide. Job requirements:

.         Excellent presentation skills in front of customers, business
partners and large audiences (English and Hebrew)

.         Experience working with customers abroad

.         At least 23- years of software development

.         Edit / perform technical presentations and product demonstrations 
Answer technical aspects of Requests for Proposals (RFP) and project
proposals 

.         Design implementation solutions according to customers technical
requirements  

.          Manage customer implementations of Checkmarx solutions, from

.         Experience with developing applications and the software

Technical Lead, or similar role, and be experienced with object-oriented
programming languages (C#, .NET, JAVA...) 

.         A background in the software security industry is preferred
(penetration testing, code review...)  

.         Academic degree preferred; Masters Degree and/or CISSP
certification a plus 

.         Fluent English (writing / spoken)
