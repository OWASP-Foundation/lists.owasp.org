Hello OWASP Israel,

Security Art is looking for Hackers to join its ranks, to design and execute
the new generation of security products and services.
Security Art offers a dynamic and ambitious working environment that
includes working for Fortune 500 customers (willingness for international
travel required).
If you eat, sleep and breathe security, want to speak at Security
Conferences (as oppose to attend them) and publish articles we’re looking
for YOU!

Job Requirements:

•    Good written and oral communication skills in English and Hebrew
•    Knowledge and proven experience in at least one programming language
(Sorry HTML/XML/CSS/JS does not count!)
•    Deep knowledge and proven experience in application security and/or
network security
•    Autodidact and self-motivation are a must!

Please send resume to jobs at security-art.com
