Hi,



The next meeting of our chapter will take place on Thursday, May 26th 16:00-18:30, at IBM's offices in Petach Tikva.



Following the success of the previous meeting, and feedback we got from you, this meeting will focus on two of the most interesting aspects of cloud security today: 

Sneak Peak into the future - Innovative Cloud Security technology

War Stories - Actual experience in cloud security implementation.

The agenda for the upcoming meeting is as follows:




* CSA general updates

* Driving an Electric Car down the Amazon :  Aharon Avitzur, Chief Architect - Better Place (with Guy Alfassi)

* Can we achieve both the privacy of a private cloud and the functionality of a public cloud? : 
Dr. Eli Talmor, CEO, SentryCom

* Cloud and Cyber Warfare. : Joseph Tal, IBM Security Solutions 

* Security Infrastructure and security at the infrastructure : Yaron Sheffer, CTO, 
Porticor Cloud Security


Attendance is free, but requires prior registration.

Please respond through LinkedIn or drop me an email and say you're coming (guy at alfaconsulting.co.il), so we can prepare the logistics accordingly.



Looking forward to seeing you there,
