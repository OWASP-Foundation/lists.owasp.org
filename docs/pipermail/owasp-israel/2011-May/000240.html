Hey All,

 

This is an Important Note for Those Registered to OWASP-ISRAEL in Digest
Mode

 

The Digest Mode of our list server does not work well with Hebrew. Due to
the fact we send our invitations in Hebrew, this can pose a problem, and
since there is very little traffic on the list, this is a redundant feature
anyway. 

 

So,  In order to prevent new users from registering with the digest option,
the digest option will be disabled this upcoming Thursday, 5/5/2011. If you
are registered with the digest-only option - this will result in your
registration being disabled. 

 

So, if you want to change your registration to normal registration, please
unsubscribe and subscribe again without the digest option at
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-israel. 

 

Thanks, and sorry for the inconvenience.
