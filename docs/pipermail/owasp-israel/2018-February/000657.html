Hello,

 

The US Department of Defense and Israel Ministry of Defense have opened the
2018 Combating Terrorism Technology Startup challenge.  

 

Two winners will receive $100,000 each and two runners-up will receive
$10,000 each.  (all prizes are no-strings-attached - ie, no impact on equity
or IP).

 

The contest is open to any technology that can be useful in combating
terrorism (cyber or physical).

 

Information and to enter: www.cttsc-x.com <http://www.cttsc-x.com>  . Also
feel free to contact me with questions - gideon at cttsc-x.com

 

Deadline for entries is March 9!

 

Good luck!

Gideon Miller

Chairman, Combating Terrorism Technology Startup Challenge

 

 <http://www.cttsc-x.com/>
