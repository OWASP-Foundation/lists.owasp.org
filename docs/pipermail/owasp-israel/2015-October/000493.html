Hi Everybody! 

AppSec Israel 2015 conference is TOMORROW! Very exciting – it looks like it
is going to be a fantastic event! 

 

We are needing some volunteers to help with on-site organization and
coordination (e.g. room proctors, doormen/women, etc.)

No climbing or heavy lifting involved, I promise. 

If you are able to help out – please get in touch ASAP with Or Katz
(katz3112 at gmail.com <mailto:katz3112 at gmail.com> ) with your cellphone
number.

Volunteers will receive a free lunch, and free tickets to next year’s
conference :).

אנחנו צריכים מספר מתנדבים לעזור בארגון ותיאום במקום (כגון סדרנים וסדרניות,

המתנדבים יקבלו ארוחת צהריים חינם, וכרטיסים לכנס בשנה הבאה בחינם :)

 

The conference will take place TOMORROW, Tuesday, October 13th, 2015, at the
College of Management (Michlala LeMinhal) in Rishon LeZiyon, starting from
9:00.

Opening words will start at 9:30, and the Keynote immediately after. 

The address is Eli Weisel St. 2, Rishon LeZiyon. There is plenty of parking
available, entrance is via gate 4. 

(Please note that parking should be paid during the day, to avoid a lengthy
backup if everyone pays on exit. The cost of parking is ~10 shekel for the
entire day). 

 

We had to close the registration, as we are expecting over 500 people to
attend!

We ask everyone to please try to get there as early as possible, to avoid a
backlog at the registration desks, and to ensure you have a spot in the main
auditorium. 

(Safety codes prevent us from allowing more than the maximum number of
people to get in to the rooms). 

If you’ve already registered, we’ll see you tomorrow – make sure to come say
hi! 

If you haven’t registered – what were you thinking?? 

 

We are looking forward to two tracks of fantastic talks by an amazing group
of speakers, see below for details. (Please note, there have been some
changes to the schedule). 

And one more thing… The Keynote address will be given by Jeremiah Grossman,
Founder of WhiteHat Security, and one of the original innovators of the
appsec field! 

 

More details are at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2015, and
the final schedule is posted at https://appsecil2015.sched.org/ . 

 

I look forward to seeing you there!

Avi D
