 I am honored – and very excited! – to announce that the Keynote address at
the AppSec Israel Conference will be given by none other than *Jeremiah
Grossman*!

Jeremiah Grossman, CTO and Founder of WhiteHat Security, possesses a unique
combination of technology savvy, customer advocacy and personal passion for
application security. A world-renowned web security expert, sought-after
speaker and influential blogger, Jeremiah brings a literal lifetime of
information security experience, both homegrown and from his days as
Yahoo!’s information security engineer. The ultimate “WhiteHat,” Jeremiah
is also founder of the Web Application Security Consortium. In his spare
time, Jeremiah practices Brazilian Jiu jitsu and has earned a black belt.

He will likely speak about the history of AppSec, how much of it began
right here in Israel, and share a bit of stories around that and the past
couple of decades since.

The Keynote will be in English, and more details will follow.

If you haven’t registered yet – now is your chance!
https://appsecil2015.eventbrite.com/
