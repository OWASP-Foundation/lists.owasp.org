Dear OWASP Israel Forum Members,

I'm a bit tardy on this, but there's a new kid in town and his name is
Hackito Ergo Sum (or, for short, HES)

Hackito Ergo Sum conference will be held from April 8th to 10th 2010 in
Paris, France and it is part of a series of conferences known as “Hacker
Space Fest” taking place since 2008 in France and all over Europe.

HES2010 will focus on hardcore computer security, insecurity, vulnerability
analysis, reverse engineering, research and hacking.

Hackito Ergo Sum is currently looking for:

   - Sponsorships
   - Business Track Presentations
   - Research Track Presentations

The call for papers is open until March 1st.

The programming committee would like to see some new names among the list of
presenters, and encourages students and graduates with initiative approaches
to submit proposals

You can learn more about the conference and get its latest news at:
http://hackitoergosum.org/

Sincerely,
Itzik Kotler
