Hi All,

 

Seeker Security is looking for a top notch Pre/Post Sales engineer for
the European market. The candidate should be able to travel extensively
and willing to relocate to Europe after a year.

 

Qualifications: 

 

*         Experience working with customers overseas

*         Pre-Sale experience - strong advantage

*         Knowledge and experience with application security - strong
advantage 

*         Willingness to travel 50-70% of the time, option for a
relocation after a year

*         Deep knowledge of Windows and Linux/Unix servers on a system
administrator level 

*         Networking knowledge and experience, strong knowledge of
TCP/IP 

*         B.Sc. in Computer Science 

*         Strong interpersonal skills

*         Additional European languages - advantage 

 

This is an opportunity to join a young & rapidly growing company with
cutting edge technology, enjoy a dynamic working environment, heaps (and
stacks) of technological challenges and interest,  and a versatile
position with strong ability to influence.  

 

Send your CVs, notify your friends.
