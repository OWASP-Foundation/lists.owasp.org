Cyber Together is planning an event for companies who are planning to attend
and exhibit at RSA conference  <https://www.rsaconference.com/>
https://www.rsaconference.com/  in San Francisco.

 

When : Sunday 5th February 17:00 - 18:30 (please put a note in your
calendar) 

Where : Kiryat Atidim Tel Aviv (A notice about the exact location will be
sent with the confirmation)

 

The content and distinguish speakers are :

 

1)   Uri Levy VP sales at Skybox Security : A journey to Success, Building a
Sustainable Effective Sales Organization for Cyber Security Vendors.

Theme: Penetrating to the global cyber security markets, has become more
challenging than ever, on this session Uri will share with us his vast
experience and insights on building a sustainable sales growth, starting
from the first few sales, and up to having a multimillion run rate business
in global markets.

Uri is also  advisory board member in several cyber security startups and
previously was the CEO of Netcom Systems, VP Marketing at CA and VP Sales
and Business Development at Xpert Systems. 

 

2)   Nathan Shuchami - Head of threat prevention  sales at Check Point
Software Technologies : Pragmatic lead sales tactics in the US market, how
to promote a cyber security solution in the most effective ways.

Nathan used to be the CEO of Hyperwise Security, which acquired by
Checkpoint, and before the CEO of Sentrigo that been acquired by McAfee.

 

People and companies can network and share together resources and
opportunities - this is the right time and right place to meet before going
to RSA the week after.

 

The main goal and outcome of this event, is to send a message to 1,000 CSO
from the USA,  who are planning to visit RSA, with the list of the Israeli
companies and their booth number, in order to schedule a demo.

 

Who should attend : CEO, CTO, VP Product/Sales/ Marketing/ Biz Dev and
senior management of Israeli cyber security vendors.   

 

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to  <mailto:info at CyberTogether.org>
info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending to this event is free of charge, however we need
your commitment to attend the event.
