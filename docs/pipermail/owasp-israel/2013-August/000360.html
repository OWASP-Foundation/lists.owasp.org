Job Description

Are you an expert in application security? Are you charismatic and good at
explaining to others? Are you passionate about what you do? If so, we are
looking for you!! 

 

AppSec Labs provides training services in secure coding and hacking, which
are taught world-wide.We are looking for quality application security
experts to teach these courses as freelance contractors. If you are
interested, please email us your CV and tell us why you think you'll do well
in this job: jobs at appsec-labs.com

Candidates should be knowledgeable with secure coding best practices
(Java/.NET) , should have training skills, and must speak English fluently.

Trainers will take part of AppSec labs global training initiative with its
customers and is intended to train the developers of organizations such as
HP, Intel, Motorola, etc. mostly in the USA.

 

Relocation is an option.

Interested? Email us your resume to Jobs at AppSec-Labs.com !

 

 

Desired Skills & Experience

We invite you to join a group of professional pen-testers and trainers,
providing tests for a large client base made of large and small customers
from the fields of finance and technology as well as military and government
organizations. The tests include penetration testing in a variety of
methods, using innovative techniques. The position will enhance your
experience in:

.         Conducting pen-tests for applications and websites.

.         Use of some of the best manual and automated tools.

.         Creating PoC for your findings and writing reports.

.         Consulting companies regarding secure programming.

.         Performing risk assessments.

.         Working with clients.

 

Fields of responsibility:

.         Performing penetration testing.

.         Code review.

.         Customer training in secure coding.

.         Consultation and performing risk assessments.

 

Required skills:

.         Knowledge of applicative attacks such as parameter manipulation,
CSRF, XSS, SQL injection, etc.

.         Knowledge of web application secure developement principles in
Java and/or .NET and/or PHP environments.

.         Experience in performing penetration testing for websites.

.         Knowledge of databases and web services.

.         Knowledge of PT tools (scanners, proxies, etc.)

 

Company Description

AppSec Labs is a dedicated application security organization, focusing on
sharing our hands-on experience by providing cutting-edge training and
consulting for the software development industry. Our courses, advanced
mobile and web application penetration testing and secure coding practices
are provided by experienced world renowned professionals whose mission is
helping you write secure code.

We are offering a variety of courses and seminars in information security
while focusing on secure software development in the real world.
Our dynamic training programs enable our clients to enjoy a tailor made
course, including customized course content pertaining to your real time
needs.
Courses dates and location (our class rooms or your facilities) will be
determined to suit your needs. Among our clients are worldwide organizations
from different sectors such as software firms, banks, military industries
and more.
