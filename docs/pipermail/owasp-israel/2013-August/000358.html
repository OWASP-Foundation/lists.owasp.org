Hi all,

ThetaRay is looking for a Junior Security Researcher.
Please send resume to: ayelet.heyman at thetaray.com

Job Description

   - Malware analysis: APT, targeted attacks research
   - Researching new trends in the information security filed
   - Investigating suspicious security event  for leading organization
   - Incorporate analysis results into the products line
   - Definition of security requirements to the different groups (R&D,
   Algorithms team, product management etc.)
   - Write analysis reports for internal and external consumption

Desired Skills & Experience


   - Active interest in Security, incident detection, network and systems
   security
   - Professional experience in the Information Security field, typically
   gained in 1-3 years
   - Familiarity with various types of malware, attack methodologies and
   hacking techniques (both consumer and enterprise)
   - Knowledge with network protocols and standards or SCADA
   - Programming/scripting experience in one or more languages
   - A motivated, self-managed, individual who can demonstrate above
   average analytical and technical skills
   - Work professionally with peers even under pressure
   - IT problem-solving skills
   - Strong verbal and written communication skills (all written company
   communications are in English)

Advantages


   - BSc. in Math or Computer Science  (Last Year Students  are also
   optional)
   - Hacking skills
   - Experience with SCADA technologies
   - Reverse engineering
   - Working at strartups environments

 Company description

ThetaRay was founded by Professor Amir Averbuch of Tel Aviv University and
Professor Ronald Coifman of Yale University.
The Company's cutting-edge technology provides detection and prevention of
APT and Zero-Day attacks on critical infrastructure, strategic
installations, communications systems and major financial systems,
leveraging patented algorithm.
