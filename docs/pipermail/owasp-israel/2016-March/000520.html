Hi,

Please see below the open position we have in Gett:

Security Architect



*Responsibilities*

·        Build and maintain the Security Perception of Gett's systems
according to the existing  threats and business needs.

o   By reviewing the design of new features / key changes to the system -
and contributing to the Security Aspects of it.

o   By educating the R&D about the Security Perception and best practices.

o   By being parts of the Architecture team, and co-working with Product
Management to better understand the business needs.

·        Manage Security Incidents (with the team) and implement deep
learning from it.

·        Peer the Anti-Fraud team and Gett's Global (non-R&D) Security
Expert to share knowledge and efforts.



*Requirements*

·        Development Background at Hands-On level: Read code, help others
with code, be able to write some code.

·        Minimum of two years of experience in a similar role.

·        Passion for Security and the everlasting fighting against security
threats and attacks.

·        Ability to aim for "good enough security" and well balance between
security and business needs.

·        A Good technological understanding: AWS, Databases, Web, and
Networks.

·        A Good communicator, being able to work with people at different
levels of expertise / seniority and communicate technical knowledge
clearly.





Apply now:  doronp at gett.com
