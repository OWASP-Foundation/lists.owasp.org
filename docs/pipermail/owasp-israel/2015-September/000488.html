We are pleased to announce the list of presentations that have been selected
for the upcoming AppSec Israel conference!  

 

Following is the list of talks, for more details please go to
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2015_Presentations 

 

Note that this list is not sorted according to order or track, and the final
schedule will be published shortly. Also this list may still be subject to
change as circumstances require. 

 

If you haven’t signed up yet – this is a great time to do it!
https://appsecil2015.eventbrite.com/ 

Note that the sooner we know how many will be attending, the better we will
be able to be organized.

 

 

Agenda: 

 

*         Yanovski Shai, Security Product Manager, Check Point
   Certifi-gate - Front Door Access to Pwning hundreds of Millions of
Androids Devices - the aftermath.

*         Jeff Luszcz, Palamida, Founder and CTO
Greg Kelton, MD, Palamida EMEA
   Creating the Right Process to Manage Open Source in the Post-Heartbleed
Era

יצירת התהליך הנכון לניהול קוד פתוח בעידן פוסט-Heartbleed

and a lecturer at the College of Management
   Cross-Site Search Attacks

*         Moshe Ferber, Chairman, Cloud Security Alliance Israel
   From zero to secure in 1 minute

*         Amit Ashbel, AppSec Strategist at Checkmarx
   Game of Hacks: Play, Hack & Track

*         Erez Metula, Application Security Expert, Chairman of AppSec Labs
   Internet of Things (IOT) Insecurity

*         Roee Hay, X-Force Application Security Research Team Lead
   One Class to Rule Them All: Deserialization Vulnerabilities in Android

*         Ofer Maor, Director of Security Strategy, Synopsys
   Security Automation in the Agile SDLC - Real World Cases

*         Irene Abezgauz, VP Product, Dyadic Security
   Do one thing every day that scares you. Encryption.

*         Helen Bravo, Product Management Director at Checkmarx
   The Node.js Highway: Attacks are at Full Throttle 

*         Yossi Oren, Senior Lecturer at the Department of Information

   The Spy in the Sandbox: Practical Cache Attacks in Javascript and their
Implications

המרגל בארגז החול: התקפות מטמון ב-Javascript, וההשלכות שלהן

   Theories of Agile, Fails of Security

   Why Are Hackers Winning the Mobile Malware Battle
