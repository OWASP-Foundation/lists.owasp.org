We are planning to have a small and intimate round table meeting with guests
from Crowell & Moring LLP  <http://www.crowell.com> www.crowell.com  

 

The main topic is : Cyber Security Law - Addressing the US market trends and
needs

 

In this meeting the speakers will address the following :

1)   Understanding the U.S. Corporate Cybersecurity Environment: Large U.S.
have increasingly imposed requirements on vendors and other business
partners to implement security controls on their information systems and
comply with reporting requirements in the event of an incident. In order to
do business in the U.S., it is increasingly important, in advance, to
understand and meet these requirements.

2)   Best Practices for Developing US Cybersecurity Protocols and Data
Breach Responses: Developing a comprehensive cybersecurity risk compliance
program; preparing for cyber incidents; insurance and other risk management
tools; managing supply chain contracting; Top 10 cyber security issues for
in-house counsel.

3)   Trends in the US Market for Cybersecurity Technology and Products:
What US companies are looking to acquire.

 

When : Wednesday April 13th 18:00 - 20:00

Where : Herzeliya (A notice about the exact location will be sent with the
confirmation).

 

We will host only 40 cyber security vendors from Israel to this event.

The vendors representative should be level of : CEO, CTO and senior
management. 

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to  <mailto:info at CyberTogether.org>
info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending in this meetup event is free of charge, however
we need your commitment to attend the event.

Thank you.

The Cyber Together Team

The Israeli Cyber Security Association
