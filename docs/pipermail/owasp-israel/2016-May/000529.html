Prevision Data Security, from SuperCom group, is looking for Application and system security experts to our Consultancy services department, for unique & challenging projects.
Job Requirements:

•      At least two years’ experience in conducting pen-tests/consulting on the application level (Desktop/Web/Mobile Apps).

•      Experience with application security tools such as Fortify, Nessus, Qualys, Burp etc.

•      Integration of testing mechanisms with industry best practices such as the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP).

•      Knowledge of applicative attacks such as parameter manipulation, CSRF, XSS, SQL injection, etc.

•      Knowledge of threat modeling and other risk identification techniques, Knowledge of system security vulnerabilities and remediation techniques.

•      Programming knowledge (Java, .Net, C++, etc.) & protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, SSL, DNS, etc.).

•      Good knowledge of cryptography.

•      Experience in code review – advantage.

•      Knowledge / Experience in SDLC and secure coding processes – advantage.

•      Additional relevant skills (Reverse engineering, Threat-Modeling, Cryptography, etc.) – advantage.

•      B.Sc. in Computer Science – advantage.
 Please send CV’s to jobs at prevision.co.il<mailto:jobs at prevision.co.il>
 Prevision Data Security - Company Profile
Prevision is an international provider of Cutting Edge Cyber Security consultancy services. The Company services are based on leading methodologies and best practices with wealth of years of consulting experience in the Information Security & Cyber world.
Prevision specializes in protecting strategic level assets, ranging from large enterprise to global organizations. The company also consults States, senior government officials & board members of global groups.
