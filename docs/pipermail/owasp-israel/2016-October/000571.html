 מצ"ב תיאורי המשרה:

The Job: Cyber Analytics Research Team Leader (full-time)
Job Description:
Imperva, the leader in protecting data and applications, is looking for a team leader for the Web Attack Analytics Team.
The team is part of Imperva Defense Center, a research group specializing in finding cutting-edge cyber-attacks, understanding cyber threats and developing new defenses.
As a leader of the Web Attack Analytics Team you will:

·         Lead a team of brilliant and capable researchers

·         Take responsibility for extracting knowledge from data in the web attack arena

•         Lead the specification of data-based protection mechanisms for Imperva Web Application Firewall

•         Lead the analysis of web attack trends

•         Collaborate with cyber research teams in other domains

•         Author Hacker Initiative Intelligence reports and blogs on web attacks
Job Qualifications:

•         M.Sc. in computer science or similar domain (electrical engineering, mathematics or statistics)

•         At least 6 years of experience (at least 3 of them in management or technical leadership position) in data analysis, machine learning or other algorithmic research

•         Excellent English – speaking and technical writing

•         Hands-on capabilities, and passion for data crunching, scripting and prototype development

•         Team player

•         Excellent communication skills. Ability to convey complicated ideas to heterogeneous audience

•         Information security background - an advantage

•         Cloud technology background – advantage
 The Job: Senior Cyber Threat Researcher (full-time)

•         Job Description:
Imperva, the leader in protecting data and applications, is looking for senior researcher in the domain of cyber threats on data systems.
The researcher will join Imperva Defense Center, a research group specializing in finding cutting-edge cyber-attacks, understanding cyber threats and developing new defenses.
As a cyber threat researcher you will:

o   Gain technical leadership in the domain of cyber-attacks on data systems

o   Build and maintain the knowledge base of attacks on data systems

o   Research data systems to discover vulnerabilities and invent new attacks

o   “Represent” the attacker in the design of new defenses

o   Analyze malware and explore malicious insider practices

o   Author Hacker Initiative Intelligence reports and blogs on web attacks

o   Develop research software (research tools, prototypes, security infrastructure)

o   Keep up with latest attack technologies, trends and publicized breaches

•         Job Qualifications:

o   Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science / similar degree or equivalent experience

o   At least 6 years of experience in software / IT (at least 3 of them in cyber security)

o   Familiarity with Windows Internal OS

o   Good grasp of internal network protocols and components in a domain environment

o   Hands-on capabilities

o   Passionate for technology and security

o   Creativity and excellent problem solving skills

o   Excellent English – speaking and technical writing

o   Team player

o   Excellent communication skills. Ability to convey complicated ideas to heterogeneous audience

o      Experience in reverse-engineering – advantage
