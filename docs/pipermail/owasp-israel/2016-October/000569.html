GE’s Cyber Security Research Lab (Located in Herzliya) is looking for a Talented Cyber Security Researcher to join our great team!

Please send your CV (or any questions) to: ge.il at ge.com<mailto:ge.il at ge.com>  or
Tal.menashe at ge.com<mailto:Tal.menashe at ge.com>

Job descriptions –


1.       Cyber Security Researcher
GE Cybersecurity Research Lab develops and applies innovating technologies to protect critical GE products from a broad spectrum of Cyber-enabled risks and threats. The ideal candidate will possess deep knowledge and experience in Cyber Security attack techniques and proper mitigation solutions in both the application level and infrastructure level. Essential duties include (but are not limited to) the following:

·         Pen-Testing skills and proven experience
·         Discover and mitigate vulnerabilities in sensitive Critical Infrastructure
·         Develop and design innovative cyber security solutions for unique and complex technologies
·         Work in partnership with government agencies, leading industry experts, and academia
·         Leverage traditional and non-traditional research methodologies to advance GE’s overall Cybersecurity practice

Qualifications/Requirements:

·         Cyber security professional with a minimum of 5 years of experience
·         Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field with at least 2 years of relevant experience in software/hardware research and development
·         Must possess strong verbal & written communication skills in English, with demonstrated experience
·         Must be willing to work out of an office located in Herzliya, Israel
·         Must submit to a background investigation, including verification of past employment and educational background
·         Experienced talented individuals who have expertise in these types of technologies:

o   Developing POCs and pen-testing tools
o   Threat Modeling
o   Researching network protocols
o   Deep understanding of cryptography
o   Bypassing intrusion Detection and Prevention systems / applying evasion techniques
o   Reverse Engineering
o   Experience in industrial networks and SCADA protocols

Desired Characteristics:
·         Reliable and responsible
·         Passionate about cyber security
·         Self-motivated
·         Versatile, fast learner and highly resourceful
·         Ability to work independently and as part of the team


2.       Cyber Security Researcher to join a special training program

Job description
General Electric's Security Lab is looking for Researchers!

General Electric’s Security Lab is looking for bright people with "out-of-the box" thinking who wish to become cyber security researchers. We are eager to train curious individuals and introduce them to the world of cybersecurity. If you can imagine yourself hacking the most sophisticated industrial and cloud systems then we can provide you with the tools, the knowledge and the right skills.
Your role will involve investigating various application, network and SCADA technologies for security vulnerabilities. You will engage a large number of different industrial platforms and technologies.  The job will include devising attack scenarios and performing penetration tests on various products and technologies.

To emphasize, we offer a special training program that will enable developers, infrastructure specialists and network engineers to transition into the world of security research and develop a long career path in one of the most cutting-edge technology companies in the world.

 Requirements:
·         Basic knowledge of networks OSI layers; network tools
·         Familiarity with various programming languages
·         Background with operating systems or computer architecture
·         Must possess strong verbal & written communication skills in English
Advantages:
·         Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
·         Professional experience in the IT industry
·         Programming experience
·         Proven experience with penetration testing
·         Strong reporting and written documentation experience
·         Experience in any of the following:
o    Reverse Engineering
o    Threat Modeling
o    Developing POCs and pen-testing tools
o    Networking communication research
o    Deep understanding of cryptography
o    Bypassing intrusion Detection and Prevention systems
