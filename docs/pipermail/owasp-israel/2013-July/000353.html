3 open positions in the Information Security group of General Electric  Herzliya:

Lead Cyber Security Researcher (Job# 1689792) for a role involving penetration, research and characterizing solutions for the critical infrastructure world such as aircraft infrastructure, power stations, nuclear plants etc...
Qualifications:

*         B.Sc. in Computer Science/MAMRM/Army Intelligence - a must!

*         2 years experience of software research and development or Firmware - a must!

*         Information security expert with at least 3 years of experience in analyzing threats and penetration tests at applicative level - a must!

*         Proven experience with handling a wide range of technologies and large, complex systems - a must!

*         A high level of English - a must!
Description:

*         Identifying solutions for information security weaknesses in critical infrastructures in real time.

*         Designing and developing new information security solutions for both unique and complex technologies.

*         To leverage traditional and non-traditional research methods to promote the level of information security of the company.

*         Working with Government Offices and leading experts in the industry and academia.

*         Overseas working trips.

Red Team Analyst (Job# 1778084)
The Red Team Analyst is responsible for delivering controlled threat simulation services against company assets. Red Teaming includes threat research, structured attack development, vulnerability research and exploit development/testing, scripting, controlled exploitation of configuration weaknesses and software vulnerabilities, evasion of detection mechanisms and more.
Qualifications:

*         B.Sc. in Computer Science or a related technical degree /MAMRM/Army Intelligence/equivalent work experience (minimum of 4 years of professional experience).

*         Minimum 3 years of recent experience attacking or defending enterprise class systems/software, (e.g. penetration tester, system administrator responsible for securing systems, network administrator, reverse engineer, source code auditor, malware analyst).

*         Minimum 2 years of experience in scripting.

*         Minimum 1 year of deep, hands-on, technical security experience in at least one of: Wired Network technologies (CISCO routers/switches, Checkpoint), Wireless (WLAN, WIMAX, RFID), Enterprise Storage Systems, UNIX/Linux, Windows/AD, iSeries/zSeries, ORACLE, Web applications and Services, Cryptography, Social Engineering and Open Source Intelligence Gathering (OSINT), Mobile platforms, Software Security (Source Code Auditing and Binary Reversing).

*         Ability to work remotely with a distributed team.

*         Strong team player - collaborates well with others to solve problems and actively incorporates input from various sources

*         A high level of English - a must!

Senior Red Team Analyst! (Job# 1778077)
The Senior Red Team Analyst is responsible for delivering controlled threat simulation services against company assets. Red Teaming includes threat research, structured attack development, vulnerability research and exploit development/testing, scripting, controlled exploitation of configuration weaknesses and software vulnerabilities, evasion of detection mechanisms and more
Qualifications:
*             B.Sc. in Computer Science or a related technical degree /MAMRM/Army Intelligence/equivalent work experience (minimum of 4 years of professional experience).
*             Minimum 3 years of experience in executing penetration tests.
*             Minimum 2 years of experience in scripting.
*             Minimum 2 years of deep, hands-on, technical security experience with at least one of: Wired Network technologies (CISCO routers/switches, Checkpoint), Wireless (WLAN, WIMAX, RFID), Enterprise Storage Systems, UNIX/Linux, Windows/AD, iSeries/zSeries, ORACLE, Web applications and Services, Cryptography, Social Engineering and Open Source Intelligence Gathering (OSINT), Mobile platforms, Software Security (Source Code Auditing and Binary Reversing).
*             Minimum 1 year of experience developing exploit code or novel attacks.
*             Previous experience leading penetration testing engagements.
*             Previous experience with the command line in Windows and/or Linux.
*             Ability to work remotely with a distributed team.
*             Strong team player - collaborates well with others to solve problems and actively incorporates input from various sources
*             A high level of English - a must!
