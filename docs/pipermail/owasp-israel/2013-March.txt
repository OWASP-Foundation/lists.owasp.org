Hi all, 

 

Secure Islands is looking for: 

 

Security/Sales Engineers (SE)

 

The Security Engineer (SE) will work alongside Sales Team and oversee the
sales support of Secure Islands' products to existing and prospective
customers with the assigned geographic region. The SE is an integral part of
the sales organization and assists the sales team with pre-sales technical
knowledge and presentations. They work closely with sales managers,
prospects and partners as a subject-matter expert to demonstrate how a
proposed solution meets and exceeds customer requirements.

Additional Responsibilities:

1.     Participate in sales conference calls and prospect visits, providing
product demonstrations in person and via WebEx, and working with prospects
at both a technical and business level to show how Secure Islands' solutions
can provide value to them.

2.     Possess demonstrable skills in customer-centric selling and a proven
track record using it to help drive revenue.

3.     Participate as a technical resource at trade shows and conferences,
when needed.

4.     Work independently and as a vital member of a sales team.

5.     Work with the sales team to strategize on sales approaches to develop
business.

6.     Ensure successful on-site product evaluations and post-sale
installations when necessary.

7.     Plan, manage and execute customer product pilots.

Qualifications:

1.     3+ years of experience in IT security, and pre-sales engineering with
large enterprises - must

2.     Familiarity with the IT security market in EMEA - an advantage

3.     Experience in development, or real world use of security related
solutions; preferably experience working in a start-up environment - an
advantage

4.     Experience with Windows networks, especially with Active Directory
and authentication mechanisms

5.     Knowledge of TCP/IP, HTTP, Load balancers, proxies and firewalls

6.     Compliance standards knowledge (PCI, SOX, HIPAA, ISO277001) - an
advantage

7.     Able to effectively present technical material/train large groups
including executive level audience.

8.     Excellent time management, multi-tasking, and prioritization skills.

9.     Excellent written, oral communication and strong interpersonal,
organizational and presentation skills

10.  BS degree in Computer Science or related field - An advantage

11.  Ability to travel intensively overseas

12.  Fluent in English -- both written and verbal-required; Additional
languages highly desired

 

Professional Services Engineers

 

This Professional Services Engineer will be responsible for on-site,
post-sales consulting and product implementation, primarily focused on
customers within the EMEA Region. Beside deployment, the Professional
Services Engineer will engage in basic training services, in both classroom
and on-site environments, for Secure Islands' customers. The successful
candidate must be a self-starter, capable of being highly productive while
on the road, delivering quality service engagements and customer
satisfaction.

Additional Responsibilities:

1.     Ensure that Secure Islands' products are deployed and operating to
meet customers' expectations

2.     Provide customers with progress reports and other project
documentation

3.     Troubleshoot technical issues with the help of the Support and RnD
Teams

Skills and Experience:

1.     This position requires strong technical skills and solid experience
working with Security products, Windows Networks, authentication, and
Networking Technologies.

2.     Experience delivering training to post-sales customers and partners -
An advantage

3.     Ability to scope, design, document and implement integration
requirements

4.     Self-starter and independent, but also able to work within a team
environment, helping out when needed on various deployments

5.     Able to accept constructive criticism and implement relevant changes

6.     An aptitude for mastering new software applications

7.     Ability to understand and communicate concepts quickly, succinctly
and accurately

8.     Highly motivated, driven and passionate about technology, training
and customer success

9.     Excellent written and verbal communications skills; excellent
interpersonal skills; at ease and comfortable working in a client
environment;

10.  Able to plan for and deliver a project on a specific deadline

Qualifications:

1.     3 years of experience of working with customers in a
Consulting/System integration/system administration or similar position.

2.     Deep knowledge of Windows, Active Directory, IT security

3.     Knowledge of TCP/IP, HTTP, Load balancers, proxies and firewalls

4.     Knowledge of Databases and Web servers- preferably MS-SQL/IIS

5.     Required travel (approximately 80%) within EMEA and internationally
when needed.

6.     Optionally, in this role one year relocation (Switzerland) is
possible.

7.     Fluent in English -- both written and verbal-required; Additional
languages highly desired

 

QA & Support Team Leader

As a QA & Support TL, you will be in charge of the QA & Support Team working
alongside our exceptional team of professionals in RnD, Product, and Sales.
In this role, the TL will lead the Quality Assurance activities within
Secure Islands reporting to the VP RnD. In addition, the TL will be in
charge on Customer Support activities such as dealing with customers''
tickets(frontline support), onsite support, product inquiries, packages
deliveries and technical documentation.  

Qualifications:

1.     Experience in QA & support of both Applications and Infrastructure
software systems - must

2.     Excellent communication and interpersonal skills - Must.

3.     Ability to understand complex products involving many technologies.

4.     Deep knowledge of Windows, Active Directory, IT security.

5.     Knowledge of TCP/IP, HTTP, Load balancers, proxies and firewalls.

6.     Knowledge of Databases and Web Servers- preferably MS-SQL/IIS

7.     Experience in virtualization technologies.

8.     Good knowledge of QA methodology and tools.

9.     Experience with QA Automation Tools - Advantage.

10.  Experience with QA of Security Software - Advantage.

11.  At least 3 years of QA & support experience and 1 year as TL
experience.
