We are looking for an application security research person for the research
team at Breach. As part of the job the researcher will analyze public
research work, real world applications and information from our
installations in order the product security content, including security
rules, settings & signatures. The researcher will report to the CTO (that is
me :-)). The job is a full time job. In special cases we may consider a part
time employment.

 

A candidate should have the following qualifications:

 

Either:

+ Security consulting experience, preferably in areas such as intrusion
detection or application security 

+ At least basic understanding of programming.

 

Or: 

+ A programmer with some system administration/security experience wishing
to explore the security space.

 

And in any case very good English.
