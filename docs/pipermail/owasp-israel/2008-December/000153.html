Hi All,

 

In addition to the January meeting on the 28th at Checkpoint, F5 has
generously agreed to host us for a meeting on March 17th. So mark your
calendars.

 

The 1st presentation already scheduled for the Checkpoint meeting would be
by Gregory Fresnais from BreakingPoint systems who will speak about
“Improving Web Application Firewall testing for better deployment in
production network”.

 

To sum up, the upcoming OWASP Israel meetings are:

 

    * January 28th 2009, Checkpoint (5 Ha'Solelim Street, close to Hashalom
Train Station, Tel-Aviv)

    * March 17th, 16:00 at F5 (24B Habarzel Street, Ramat Hachayal,
Tel-Aviv) 

 

All monthly meetings are between 16 and 19. Program and detailed arrival
instructions for each meeting would be published and sent to the list when
ready. If you plan to come, please send an e-mail to ofer at shezaf.com.

 

If you would like to speak in OWASP Israel monthly meeting, let us know at
ofer at shezaf.com
