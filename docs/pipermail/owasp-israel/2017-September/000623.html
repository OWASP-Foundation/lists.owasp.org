Role & Mission:
Protected Media is looking for self-learner and hands-on professiona*l web
security researcher* to join the research team. If you are an accomplished
web security researcher or a web vulnerability researcher, have a* deep
understanding in browsers and Javascript*, and curious about us- it’s our
lucky day -come join us!

In this role, you’ll be responsible for identifying new threats impacting
our customers.

You’ll research web-oriented attacks on online advertisers and how to
detect and mitigate them. You will read browsers sources codes and perform
deep Javascript analysis so we could profile bots. This includes anything
from malware-infected bots to individual hackers playing dirty tricks.

Why Join?
Your actions will have an* immediate impact on millions of users*. You will
work with* several billion requests per day* and several TB per day. You
will have the chance to perform deep browsers research while using the
newest technologies.


Your profile:

   - You enjoy solving complex problems.
   - Strong understanding of the web networking stack.
   - Real-world security experience, including tracking and investigating
   cyber threats.
   - Experience with deep JavaScript analysis and browser internals.
   - SQL- you know how to answer “group by” questions and handle complexed
   joins.
