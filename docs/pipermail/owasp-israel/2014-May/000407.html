Security Engineer (SE) Israel, Greece, Turkey & Cyprus - Tel Aviv, Israel

Imperva, the leader in Data Security, is looking to hire a Security Engineer (SE) for the Mediterranean Region, based at our center located in Tel Aviv. Imperva is a pioneer and leader of a new category of business security solutions for critical applications and high-value data in the data center. Thousands of the world's leading businesses, government organizations, and service providers rely on Imperva solutions to protect against data breaches, meet compliance mandates, and manage data risk.

The SE will work alongside the Mediterranean Regional Sales Director and oversee the sales support of Imperva's SecureSphere Data Security products. The SE is an integral part of the sales organization and assists the sales team with pre-sales technical knowledge and Presentations. As the subject-matter expert SEs work closely with sales managers, partners and prospects in developing and shaping solutions to exceed customer expectations.

Qualifications:



  *   Bachelors degree or equivalent in Computer Science or related field -Required
  *   5+ years of experience in network security, development, databases, web applications - preferably with 3+ years as a sales engineer working in a vendor start - up environment
  *   Possess a solid understanding of web applications, database, security, and auditing environments
  *   Deep knowledge of TCP/IP, HTTP, Load balancers, proxies and firewalls
  *   Knowledge in databases (Oracle, MS SQL, Sybase, DB2)
  *   Knowledge in application development (ASP, .NET, PHP, JSP)
  *   Hands-on experience with UNIX/Linux
  *   Experience with Network or Application Firewalls and/or IDS/IPS - an advantage
  *   Compliance standards knowledge (PCI, SOX, HIPAA) - Advantage
  *   Able to effectively present technical material to small and large groups
  *   Excellent time management, multi-tasking, and prioritisation skills.
  *   Excellent written, oral communication and strong interpersonal, organizational and presentation skills
  *   Ability to travel domestically and overseas - up to 50
  *   Ability to do proof of value (at customer site or helping channel partners)
  *   Ability to train, teach and assist our channel partners
  *   Ability to follow up, help and support our customer base
