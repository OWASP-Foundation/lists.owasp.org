Hi,
US based A10 networks (NYSE:ATEN), an emerging leader in application delivery and security, had recently opened a new R&D office in Ramat Hachayal.
The Israeli R&D office is responsible for A10's broad security portfolio, which includes among others: WAF, DDoS prevention system, SSL VPN and many other technologies.
The office is growing very rapidly, and we are looking for numerous positions

If you (or anyone else you may think of) are:

*         Really passionate about security, in all forms and flavors

*         Live, dream and breathe SW/HW performance

*         Experienced in various security products / fields (WAF, IPS, NGFW, DDoS)

*         Looking for a challenging opportunity with a company which has just gone public, and is very hungry to show why
You may belong here :)

For additional details, please contact me.

Responsibilities:
*         Design, implement and maintain product features and modules related to network security and L7 DPI.
*         Implement control and data plane embedded code designed for high performance networking applications
*         Participate in architecture discussions and planning with software development
*         Research market trends and be familiar with the ever-evolving security arena
*         Provide technical leadership and mentoring to junior team members
*         Work in a cross cultural, multi-national R&D team Professional

*         4+ years of experience in software development with a distinguished track record for technically demanding projects

*         Strong C/C++ programming skills and hands-on development experience using Linux
*         Experience in multi-threading, multi-core, and real-time high availability embedded systems is a plus
*         Good understanding of control plane and data plane architecture in networking devices
