Topspin security is looking for a one of a kind data scientist student to
join our efforts to find the needle we call malicious activity in the
haystack we call the internet.


*Job description*



·         Apply Data Mining techniques for directed pattern discovery

·         Implement advanced Machine Learning and statistical approaches to
analytic problems

·         Generate flexible decision rules and build advanced risk models

·         Retrieve and manipulate data from both internal and external
sources

·         Identify and interface 3rd parties APIs for data resources and
services

·         Write and interpret complex queries for SQL/NoSQL databases

·         Prototyping, development, maintenance, and benchmarking of
predictive analytic models

·         Communicate findings and make suggestions to Product, R&D, and
Management teams

*100+ hours a month*





*Required qualification*



·         student in mathmatics, Computer science or other relevant fields

·         experience working with real data, applying statistical modeling
and data mining

·         Statistical analysis and mathematical skills

·         Machine Learning/ Data Mining experience

·         Creative and innovative

·         Strong analytic thinking & Problem solving capabilities

·         A highly motivated team player

·         ADVANTAGE: Experience with associated tools and platforms:

o       Big Data technologies and NoSQL databases (e.g.  BigQuery, MongoDB,
etc)

o       Data mining and statistical analytic platforms (e.g. R, SaS,
RapidMiner etc)  Visualization and reporting tools (e.g. Tableau, Pentahoo,
etc)


*About the company*

TopSpin Security develops DECOYnet, a security product that detects attacks
in progress and defeats them. It reveals the presence of malware and
attackers that have infiltrated your network and are trying to steal your
information, using a new and unique approach of decoys and traps in the
network, while identifying CnC-asset communication. Once DECOYnet has
discovered an attacker at work, it proceeds to deceive, confuse and slow
down the attack.
