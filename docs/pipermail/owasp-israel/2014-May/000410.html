Hi all,

AppSec Labs are looking for quality, open-minded people!

 

AppSec Labs (founded by Erez Metula) are looking for application security
experts for unique & challenging projects (our consulting and training
services), enabling the employees to choose their favorite areas from a
variety of fields, working with maximum flexibility. 

 

Job Requirements:

-          At least one year experience in conducting pen-tests/consulting
on the application level

-          B.Sc. graduated in Computer Science 

-          Knowledge of applicative attacks such as parameter manipulation,
CSRF, XSS, SQL injection, etc

-          Knowledge in programming (SQL, Java, Python, C++, .Net) &
protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, SSL, DNS) - the more the better!

-          Experience in code review - advantage

-          Other relevant skills (Reverse engineering, Threat-Modeling,
Cryptography) - advantage

-          Knowledge / Experience in SDLC and secure coding processes -
advantage

-          Training experience - advantage

 

Required Skills:

-          Independent work and self-learning ability

-          English written and verbal skills

-          Willingness to travel overseas

 

About Us

Our services focus on secure coding consultation, advanced training programs
and advanced application penetration testing.

Our projects are very unique & challenging, as we are known as one of the
top quality firms for application security, mainly leading the field of
mobile hacking (including training in BlackHat2013 and 2014!) and secure
coding training in all computing languages. 

 

Does this position interest you? If so, please contact me here or in our
mail:  <mailto:jobs at appsec-labs.com> jobs at appsec-labs.com
