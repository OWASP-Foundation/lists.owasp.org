Hi all,



The OWASP AppSec Israel 2015 conference has extended its Call for Papers
deadline to September 16, 2015. The OWASP IL conference is seeking
presenters from the application security community to discuss interesting
topics related to the security of applications, from the fields of web,
mobile, cloud, etc.



CFP details and submission form:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2015_CFP





We're looking forward for interesting talks.

Please forward this email to relevant people who might be candidates for
speaking at this great event.



Thanks,

Erez Metula
