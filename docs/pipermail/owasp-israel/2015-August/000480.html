Early registration for AppSec Israel 2015 is now open! 

 

The annual OWASP AppSec Israel Conference will take place this year on
Tuesday, October 13th, 2015 – exactly one week after Sukkot.

This year, the conference will be held in The College of Management
(Michlala l’Minhal), in Rishon LeZion, 2 Eli Weisel St. 

 

Attending the conference is free of charge, however advance registration is
required. 

If you are planning on attending the conference - please register now! 

Registration page is at https://appsecil2015.eventbrite.com/  

 

OWASP’s AppSec Israel is the largest conference in Israel for application
security, and regularly draws over hundreds of participants. Last year more
than 450 attended – don’t miss it this year! 

The Conference will include multiple lecture tracks and a sponsors display
area for vendors of products and services of interest to Application
Security professionals and developers. 

More details will be available at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2015.  

 

FOR A SHORT TIME ONLY! 

Early registration at half price! So that’s like TWICE as free! 

So sign up now! https://appsecil2015.eventbrite.com/   

(Yes, it is still free.)

 

FYI, the Call for Presentations (CFP) is still open, so if you haven’t
submitted your talk proposal yet, you don’t have much time left! See details
here: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/AppSec_Israel_2015_CFP 

Likewise, we are still looking for more sponsors – details are available
here https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_IL_Sponsorship.
