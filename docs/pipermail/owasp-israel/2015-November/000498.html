Cyber Together

 

We are planning to have a small and intimate round table meeting with :

Mr. Nico Siebelink - Technical director strategic verticals & Head of the field security consultancy EMEA

Mr. Aviram Zrahia - Cyber security consulting engineer

Mr. Arik Kasha Regional Sales Director EMEA at ObserveIT 

The event’s 2 main topic is supporting Israeli cyber security startups in Europe: overview & best practices.

Also we will present to the Israeli cyber security companies the Juniper Networks Open Policy Enforcement and the path into Juniper’s Cyber Security Ecosystem : Routing, Switching and Security in the domains of Datacenter, Campus and Branch, WAN and Cloud. If you are looking to establish a partnership with Juniper – this is the right opportunity.

Approximately 900 customers (enterprises, SME, hosting centers , cloud companies and service providers) across EMEA.

The value of the global cyber security market is expected to grow by 11.3% each year, reaching $120 billion by 2017. The Western Europe region alone is estimated to contribute $28.1 billion to this industry, driven by changing threats and technologies.

This event we will meet 2 EMEA vast experience sales and technical managers, so we can also share ideas and insights such :

-      How to promote innovation in cybersecurity throughout Europe as an Israeli cluster ?

-      What are the best practices and the main challenges penetrating the EMEA markets ?

-      What supportive actions should be implemented to support Israeli cyber security start-up to gain success in EMEA ?

We will host only 40 cyber security vendors from Israel in this event (one person per vendor).

The vendors representative should be level of : CEO, CTO , EMEA Sales, Marketing and Business Development managers.  

The event will be in Azrieli Center Tel Aviv on  Wednesday 25th  November  17:30 – 19:30.

A notice about the exact location will be sent with the confirmation.

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to  <mailto:info at CyberTogether.org> info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending in this round table event is free of charge, however we need your commitment to attend the event.

Enclosed, please find Cyber Together one pager.  

Thank you.

The Cyber Together Team
