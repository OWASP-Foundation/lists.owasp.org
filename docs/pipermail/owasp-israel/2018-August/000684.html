I would like to let you know about the Cyber Security startups accelerator
that The National Cyber Security Center (NCSC) in the UK and O2/Telefonica
is setting up. We are currently looking for startups that could benefit
from the technical support of the NCSC, and explore if the product could be
used by Telefonica/o2, or can be re-sold to our 300M customers worldwide.
