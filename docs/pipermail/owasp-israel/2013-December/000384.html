A Mobile Security Researcher position (Junior) opened recently in our team.


*Responsibilities:*

•             Identification and research ongoing mobile threats,
predictive analysis.

•             Reverse engineering applications and network protocols.

•             Add/maintain signatures.

•             Development of detection and analysis systems, POCs, tools

•             Identify major sources of distribution, applications and more.



*Requirements:*

•             Information security background.

•             “Hands-on” experience in reverse engineering and malware
analysis.

•             Systems-level knowledge of Linux and derivative mobile

•             Development and scripting experience (Java, C, Python, Shell).

•             Solid understanding of Internet technologies, Networking,

•             Dynamic, fast learner and versatile individual, willing to
learn and explore new territories.

•             Good communication and personal skills.

•             Strong analytical and solving skills.

•             Highly organized with attention to detail.

•             Fluent in Hebrew and English (verbal and written).



*Nice to have (any of the following):*

•             Experience building Automation Engines – a huge plus.

•             Mobile/Android reverse engineering and malware analysis – a
huge plus.

•             Familiarity with Android internals (Kernel, Libraries,
Framework, Android API’s, Android development process, debugging Android
applications) – a huge plus.

•             Professed "Android Geek", owning an Android device and
replacing ROMs for fun - a plus.

•             Working experience in information security company (Creating
malware signatures, writing virus entries) and/or relevant IDF/GOV
background - a plus.

•             Experience with large-scale distributed analysis – a plus.

•             B.Sc in Computer Science – a plus.

•             Experience with IDA Pro - a plus.
