Fraunhofer Society, the largest European organization for applied research, is organizing a workshop led by the cybersecurity institute Fraunhofer SIT to promote women in science. The workshop is interdisciplinary and is not focused only on cyber but is open for female students and researchers from any disciplines. The workshop will take place in March 2018 in Darmstadt, Germany and is fully funded. Apply to learn how to build a successful research career, how to excel in science, how to apply for positions in Europe. Use the opportunity to establish international relationships and to network. More info in the enclosed flyer, and on the webpage below:

https://www.sit.fraunhofer.de/science-campus/
