New Product Introduction Engineer
The New Product Introduction engineer is a senior position concentrating on the next generation of F5 products. This position requires strong product and customer deployment knowledge. The NPIE exhibits technical sales ability, high level network engineering knowledge, and knowledge of enterprise architectures and business processes. Because of their close working relationship with product development NPIEs have extensive knowledge of software development processes and development lifecycles. NPIEs spend about 75% of their time interacting with product development issues and 25% of their time on customer facing issues and departmental projects
Requirements:

*         Minimum 8 + years relevant work experience in enterprise level environments.

*         Ability to function as an individual contributor to long term software development projects.

*         Experience with product development lifecycle planning, user stories, requirements processes and software development management

*         Expert technical knowledge of applicable applications, protocols and network devices (routers, switches, filers etc.)

*         Demonstrate experience and expert-level knowledge with complex enterprise architectures.

*         Advanced knowledge of F5 products and multi-tiered architectures including testing and reproduction.

*         Scripting and programming experience including at minimum Perl and C experience.

*         Strong leadership and presentation skills, both oral and written, with the ability to articulate and educate other engineers and customers about complex technology in a simple way.

*         Ability to lead projects exhibiting complicated technical and political situations for enterprise class customers.
