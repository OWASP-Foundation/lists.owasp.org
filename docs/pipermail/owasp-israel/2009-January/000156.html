Hello All,

We're pleased to announce a new security-related video seminar in Israel -
the Security Theater. This will be a weekly event where we
will view a video lecture about security, cryptography and hacking and then
hold a discussion. We may have live speakers too occasionally.

We have a very impressive lineup of speakers (especially since we don't have
to pay them to come over!): Kaminsky's DNS talk, Nohl's
Mifare talk, Xbox hacking, you name it. We're also open to suggestions.

It will be held on Wednesdays at 14:30, in room 146, Wolfson Maabadot
building.
First meeting will be next week, Jan 21.

Refreshments are generously provided by the Check Point Institute for
Information Security <http://www.cs.tau.ac.il/cpiis>.

For more information, please see the seminar web page:
http://www.eng.tau.ac.il/~consel/SecurityTheater/

Directions how to get to the room appear on the site.

IMPORTANT: there's a "me too" link on the page - please click it if you're
thinking of coming, since we need to estimate the size of the room (and the
amount of cookies...)

See you there,
Yossi Oren & Avishai Wool.
