In this specific case (Discount bank), the username is in the form of an
Israeli I.D. number.
I.D. number can be easily deduced from details such as the name and address
of the victim using "population registration" databases (such as
"Reshumon", etc.).
Therefore, as Erez suggested, a targeted attack against a victim should be
rather simple.
