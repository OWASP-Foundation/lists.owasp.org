Hi everybody! 

 

I am very pleased to announce that OWASP Israel is joining forces with the
Israel chapter of CSA! 

Our upcoming chapter meeting will be a joint meeting, with both chapters
hosting. This will give us an opportunity to expand our horizons, hear
different relevant topics, and network with a slightly different group of
people.

 

The meeting will take place on Tuesday, June 16th, at Microsoft's Herzeliya
office, in the Herzeliya Pituach industrial area. The address is 13 Shenkar
St., Building Gev-Yam 5.

Thanks very much to Microsoft for hosting us! 

 

As always, attendance is free, however advanced registration is required, so
that we can plan accordingly. 

Please register at https://owasp-israel-june-2015.eventbrite.com 

 

Agenda:  

 

*         17:00 - 17:30 

Gathering, food, and drinks (KOSHER) 

 

*         17:30 - 17:45

Opening notes

 

*         17:45 - 18:25 

One Key to Rule Them All: Detecting the Skeleton Key Malware

Itai Grady & Tal Be'ery, Microsoft

 

*         18:30 - 19:10

Software Defined Networks are emerging  - How will it affect security?

Almog Ohayon, Javelin Networks

 

*         19:10 - 19:30

Coffee Break

 

*         19:30 - 20:10

Outsmarting researchers: Fraudsters and their security practices

Julia Karpin, F5 Networks 

 

 

I look forward to seeing you all there!
