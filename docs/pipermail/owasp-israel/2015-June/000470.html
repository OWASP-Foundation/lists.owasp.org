Another few announcements, as I mentioned at the meeting: 

 

1.      AppSec USA (https://2015.appsecusa.org/) - anyone planning on
attending the upcoming AppSecUSA conference in September, is invited to use
our chapter's discount code - US2015_IS. This will grant you a $100 discount
per ticket - and, if enough of our members use this, possibly a little
something extra - like a free ticket(s) to be raffled off to the chapter
members! 
So please register using the discount code - US2015_IS. 

 

2.      OWASP Israel chatroom - the OWASP Foundation has recently opened a
Slack for the entire OWASP community. (For those unfamiliar with Slack, it
is like IRC on steroids - or, a hipster version of a chatroom - but very
sleek and useful). 
You can register for OWASP's Slack here: http://owasp.herokuapp.com/ After
you sign up, you will have access to various chatrooms with other OWASP
members around the world, including some dedicated to specific areas of
learning. 
Specifically, I opened a chatroom for our local chapter -
https://owasp.slack.com/messages/chapter-israel/. Feel free to discuss any
local issue, argue about security in Hebrew (it works, I checked), or just
idle community chitchat. I hope this will be a useful resource for the
community (and, of course, if it is not, then don't feel any pressure to
make it active - it is there if you want it!)
