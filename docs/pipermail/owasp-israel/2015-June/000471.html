The preparations for the 13th August Penguin Conference are now underway.
This is the annual community conference of the free software community and
the open source in the country, organized by Hamakor
<http://www.hamakor.org.il/?p=752> NPO.

The conference will be held on Friday, August 7, 2015 at Shenkar College.
Anyone who wishes to give lectures on topics of data security and free
software are invited to send an email to adir at hamakor.org.il. Duration
of the lectures are between 30-50 minutes.
Due to the need to organize, topics should be sent to 2.7.15 (strict).
