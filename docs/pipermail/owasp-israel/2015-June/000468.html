Just a reminder, the chapter meeting will take place next Tuesday, June
16th, at Microsoft's Herzeliya office, in the Herzeliya Pituach industrial
area. The address is 13 Shenkar St., Building Gev-Yam 5.

The meeting will be a joint meeting together with CSA Israel, so make sure
to come to hear different relevant topics, and network with a slightly
different group of people.

 

Thanks very much to Microsoft for hosting us! 

 

As always, attendance is free, however advanced registration is required, so
that we can plan accordingly. 

Please register at https://owasp-israel-june-2015.eventbrite.com

Gathering, food, and drinks (KOSHER)

Introductions and Opening notes
