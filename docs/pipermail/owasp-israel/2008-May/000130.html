Conference updates:

 

I am glad to announce that the upcoming OWASP Israel conference on September
14th will have two tracks which enables us to split presentations into a
beginners and experts tracks making the conference suitable for everyone.

 

I am also glad that Ofer Maor (Hacktics) and Adi Sharabani (IBM) has joined
me in the content committee ensuring we will improve on our already high
quality of presentations. 

 

As a reminder: details and call for participation are at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Israel_2008_Conference

 

~ Ofer

buy you drinks (details:
