Hi OWASP Israel members,

OWASP Israel is glad to participate in IDC Security Road Show in Israel on
June 3rd. We will have a booth in the event and OWASP members are invited to
join at a reduced cost of 150is instead of 260is. We created this relation
as we felt the IDC event is among the select few information security events
worth attending for more than just networking. See you there!
