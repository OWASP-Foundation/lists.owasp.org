Hi All,

 

Thanks for whoever attended the OWASP IL 2 mini conference at IDC last
week and contributed to its great success. With 7 wonderful talks, over
150 people, 8 distinguished organizations sponsoring and tasty
refreshments, I had a great time. I hope you also had a good time. And
for whomever did not come:  you missed a wonderful event.

 

Many of the presentations given last week in OWASP IL 2 mini conference
at IDC are already online on the conference page at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2nd_OWASP_IL_mini_conference. I hope to
get the rest in a few days and I will e-mail the mailing list once I get
them.

 

If you feel like it, I would appreciate comments, feedback and ideas on
how to improve next time as well as feedback to our presenters. Can you
assign a grade between 1 to 5 to each of the presentations and add any
written comment you have. I will provide the speakers with your feedback
without disclosing the source.
