Hi all,

Just a reminder that registration for this event at the end of May is now
open here: https://telaviv.appsecglobal.org/registration/registration-locals
!
(Note that prices on that page include VAT)

This is the first time a Global OWASP conference has come to Israel and it
is important that we make it a success! For more information about how this
conference is different to the regular AppSec Israel conference, you can
see my slides from the last chapter meeting here:
https://www.slideshare.net/JoshGrossmanCISSP/intro-to-global-appsec-tel-aviv-137272294


I particularly want to highlight the excellent set of training courses we
have on the programme. Many of the trainers who are coming here will be
going on to deliver their courses at Black Hat, Hack In Paris and other
major conferences (at a higher cost!).

Places on the training courses will be limited so I would strongly
recommend registering early to avoid missing out!

Any questions, feel free to speak to me or any of the local board members!

Thanks,

Josh
