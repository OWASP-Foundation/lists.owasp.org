Hi Everyone, 

Following the big success of the 1st one, I'm glad to announce the 2nd
OWASP IL mini conference at Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya (IDC) on
May 21st 2007. The mini conference would be a half day event from around
14:00.

I'd like to officially make a call for speakers and sponsors. As at the
last event we had around 100! attendees, this can be a nice exposure for
both presented and sponsors.

Call for speakers
=============
If you would like to speak at the conference please send me a title and
abstract of your proposed topic as well as few words about yourself. 

Sponsorships
=========
Companies who would like to sponsor the event are welcomed to contact
me. This is a not for profit event and sponsorship is pretty inexpensive
and used directly to cover costs, mostly refreshments. In return to
sponsorship the sponsoring company would be able to have a small table
and a poster in the event lounge and handle out brochures to event
attendees. Please contact me for details at ofers at breach.com or at
054-4431119.

~ Ofer
