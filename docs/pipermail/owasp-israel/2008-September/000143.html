Hi Everyone,

 

Time to register for the upcoming OWASP Israel 2008 conference. The
conference will be held at the Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya on
September 14th from 8:30 to 5pm. The event is free for all, but you need to
registered by e-mailing me. More information can be found at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Israel_2008_Conference

 

See you at the conference!
