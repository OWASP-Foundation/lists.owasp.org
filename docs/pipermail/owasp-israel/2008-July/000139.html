Hi Everyone,

We had a nice response to the CFP for the OWASP IL 2008 conference and we
have now set a deadline for presentation submission, so that we can finalize
the program. The deadline is Monday, August 4th 2008. We will be informing
submitters on acceptance starting on the 4th and no later than August
15th.The conference will be held on September 14th at the IDC in Herzliya,
Israel. More details at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Israel_2008_Conference
