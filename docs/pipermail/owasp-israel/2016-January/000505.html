Cyber Together

 

We are planning to have a small and intimate round table meeting with guests from SOPHOS :

The main topic is :  Security for the mid-market - the opportunity and challenge 

The items we would cover would be:

•        The opportunity

•        Go to market challenges 

•        Making complex security solutions accessible to mid-market

The 2 executives who will share with us the insights are :

•         Joe Levy – CTO he was previously CTO of SonicWall, Solera and Bluecoat https://www.sophos.com/en-us/company/management/joe-levy.aspx 

•         Ari Buchler – General counsel and SVP BizDev  <https://www.sophos.com/en-us/company/management/ari-buchler.aspx> https://www.sophos.com/en-us/company/management/ari-buchler.aspx

A little bit about Sophos : 

In the past year Sophos went public (after 27 years) and is now valued at 1.6B USD with almost 500M USD of sales growing at 26% YoY, in addition SOPHOS added two new companies to their portfolio Relexion for Cloud email security and SurfRight to strengthen SOPHOS’s nextgen Endpoint security. Sophos is recognized as a leader in 4 Gartner MQ Endpoint, UTM, Encryption and Mobile.

https://www.sophos.com/en-us/press-office/press-releases/2015/12/sophos-acquires-surfright.aspx

https://www.sophos.com/en-us/press-office/press-releases/2015/06/acquisition-of-reflexion-networks.aspx

We will host only 40 cyber security vendors from Israel to this event (one per vendor).

The vendors representative should be level of : CEO, CTO and senior management.  The event will be in Herzeliya on Thursday 28th January 17:30 – 19:30.

A notice about the exact location will be sent with the confirmation.

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending in this round table event is free of charge, however we need your commitment to attend the event.

Thank you.

The Cyber Together Team
