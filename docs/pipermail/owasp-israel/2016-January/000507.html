We're looking for a new security researcher for our Application Defense Center group. Come work with us on innovative solutions to challenging cyber security problems.
Learn more or apply to job here http://www.imperva.com/company/careers?jvi=ohqo2fwR%2cJob
Security researcher
As an application security research team member you will be collaborating with a team of researchers and analysts focused on web application security. Responsibilities include:

  *   Analysis of cyber-attacks and mitigations.
  *   Developing new concept projects.
  *   Researching new web technologies and protocols.
  *   Publishing research documents in known security forums.
Qualifications:

  *   B.Sc. in computer science or similar domain (electric engineering, mathematics) or graduate of a technology unit.
  *   2-3 years of programming experience.
  *   Strong background in web technologies and computer networking (HTTP, SSL, Javascript).
  *   Passionate for security and technology.
  *   Excellent writing and speaking capabilities in English - an advantage.
  *   Information security background and knowledge, application security knowledge - an advantage.
  *   Malware analysis background - an advantage
