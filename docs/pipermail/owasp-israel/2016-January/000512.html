Hi - below are flyers and links to two $100K security technology contests
sponsored by the US Department of Defense.

 

Contests are open to any developer/startup with security-related technology
or apps.   Finals will be held in Tel Aviv.

 

    - Contest 1 is for Mobile Apps

    - Contest 2 is for all other security (physical or cyber)

 

Deadlines are Feb 19 for the Mobile Apps, March 4 for the general.

 

The two contests have $200K in prize money contributed by the US DoD.

 

Details and register here:
http://www.mit-forum.org.il/#!the-2016-100k-combating-terrorism-start/c1ldr 

 

Go for it !
