Here’s the “formal” job description:

Security Researcher / Developer

About the security research team

The Security Research team is in the forefront of Cyber Crimes defence. The team is comprised of security experts, conducting researches to find cutting-edge methods in order to attack (And defend). The team deals mainly with network & web security.

What makes this position unique?

Being part of an amazing team that handles a versatile and broad range of challenges, from different disciplines (Ranging from top notch DDoS attacks to sophisticated web based attacks done by bots or hackers). In additions, the team proactively conducts researches in our labs, to always make sure we’re #1.

What we are looking for

A clever & good-hearted Hacker. We are looking for a motivated, self-learner and hands-on professional with thorough understanding of Web Technologies, Network Protocols, and Security to join our security team.



Requirements:

• Passionate about security

• Self-motivated, self-learner

• Thorough knowledge of web technologies: HTTP, SSL, SQL, and Browser Internals

• Programming/Scripting experience

• Good communication skills

• Good understanding of network protocols
