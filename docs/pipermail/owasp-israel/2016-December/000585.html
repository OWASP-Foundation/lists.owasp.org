Amdocs is looking for security architect with 5+ years of experience to join our security team.
This is an opportunity to be involve with all the aspects of security: infrastructure, applications and R&D.

*You will maintain the organization's effectiveness and efficiency by defining, delivering, and supporting strategic plans for implementing information security,
understand, evaluate, and respond to security incidents
*You will recommend information technology strategies, policies, and procedures by evaluating the organization's outcomes, identifying problems, evaluating trends, and anticipating requirements.
*You will conduct the information security risk assessment program. Review compliance with the information security policy and associated procedures
*You will update and maintain risk register for the organization. Provide periodic reporting on information security issues to management
*You will keep updated with emerging security threats and alerts. Conduct research on emerging products, services, protocols, and standards in support of security enhancement and development efforts
*You will perform leads and commissions activities related to contingency planning, business continuity management, and IT disaster recovery in conjunction with relevant functions and third parties
*You will be respond and analyze business unit requests, ensuring CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability) of customer and Amdocs data and providing solutions without business interruptions
*You will perform routine line management and leadership of staff within the Information Security Management function
*You will provide leadership and strategic direction for the security management function, including planning and budgeting
*You will lead the design, implementation, operation, and maintenance of the Information Security Management System based on the ISO/IEC 27000 series standards, including, where applicable, certification against ISO/IEC 27001
*You will work with different groups to provide, on time, the right answers that address customer security needs.

All you need is...

* Full-time work experience in information security management
* In-depth knowledge of security architectural consideration from End To End security perspective
*In-depth knowledge of information security concepts and methodologies
*In-depth knowledge of compliances (PCI DSS, Sox, DPA, and so on) and IS standards (ISO 27001, BS25999, ISO 20000, OWASP, and so on)
*In-depth knowledge of risk management methodologies and approach
*Knowledge of Secure SDLC methodology
*Team leadership and management experience
