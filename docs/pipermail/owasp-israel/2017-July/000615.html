Comsec is hiring!! We are looking for:

1. GRC - Consultant
- Experience in project management / information security processes in      organizations
- experience in GRC and security-oriented )in particular risk surveys of  Information Systems / Security
- Information, business processes, procedures, ISO27001, methodologies)
- English – Fluent – Must!
- Excellent communication skills
- Multi-tasking capabilities

2. CISO
Performing IT security risk assessments and reporting on ways to minimize threats
Monitoring security vulnerabilities and hacking threats in network and host systems
Ensuring business continuity
Communicating with key stakeholders about IT security threats
Implementing an effective process for the reporting of security incidents
Complying with the latest regulations and compliance requirements
Championing and educating the organization about the latest security strategies and technologies
Key responsibilities
Managing the daily operation and implementation of the IT security strategy
Conducting a continuous assessment of current IT security practices and systems and identifying areas for improvement
Running security audits and risk assessments
Driving change projects and building new IT capabilities
Devising strategies and implementing IT solutions to minimize the risk of cyber-attacks
Reviewing, analyzing and delivering data information
Key skills
Ability to adapt to a fast-moving IT landscape and keep pace with latest thinking and new security technologies
Strong customer focus – able to meet the demands of internal and external customers
Excellent communication skills – providing verbal and written communication that is outstanding to both direct reports and senior management as well as other stakeholders
Flexible and adaptable – capable of changing direction where required and showing flexibility to meet new demands
Multi-tasking – can manage several concurrent projects and priorities demands
Experience in writing of LLD, HLD.
In-depth knowledge of tools and methods in Cyber Security field.
Knowledge of the different regulation and standards.
English – Fluent (Speaking and writing).

Qualifications
A Bachelor's degree is a minimum with many organizations selecting candidates with a computer science degree or a qualification in a related subject.
Relevant experience
This level of seniority usually demands around a decade of proven IT security experience but some companies will take candidates who are newer to the industry if they can demonstrate they are a serious IT professional
Change management and business process experience is ideal together with a proven track record of driving large-scale change programmers
A proven record of dealing with complex projects and meeting conflicting demands

3. Application Security consultant

- 2 years of proven experience in the field of application Security

- 2 year of proven experience in application penetration testing
- familier with Mobile PT  ( IOS, Android ) – advantage
- Excellent verbal and written English
- Excellent writing, communication and leading skills.

4. Senior Cyber Security Consultant to our Amsterdam/ London Subsidiary
Responsibilities:
Lead and perform Cyber & Information Security activities, such as, Security Design & Code Reviews, Penetration Testing, Red-Team, Incident Response, Social Engineering, Security SDLC support, Trainings.
Communicate with & consult to, on daily basis, a variety of clients, to answer their constantly-growing needs.
Work closely with extremely experienced developers & IT personnel, to assist with further developing their skills, products, and processes.
Enhance the current and invent new methodologies, tools, and services
Write & review professional reports to our clients
Lead presentations in-front of our clients’ technical and management teams
Requirements:
3-5 years of proven experience in the field of Cyber Security (e.g. Security Design & Code review, Information Security Architect, Penetration Testing)
1-2 year of proven experience in Application Penetration Testing
Infrastructure security experience (Networking/OS/Database) - an advantage
Relevant certifications in the domain (CISM, CISSP, etc.) – an advantage
Independent learning & management capabilities
Excellent verbal and written English
Excellent communication skills
Multi-tasking capabilities
Experience with Mobile Security Testing – an advantage
Familiarity with information security standards and regulations – an advantage
Formal programming experience - an advantage
BSC. or BA in Computer Science - an advantage
Ability to simplify (verbally and written) complex problems
A team player who can thrive in unstructured environments and independently
Service orientated, ability to work with a client, interpersonal skills
Lives in or wishes to relocate to the Netherlands
Foreign citizenship – an advantage
