We have several new openings for our Research Group, specializing in research of web & network attacks, and developing new products for Imperva. Positions are for our campuses in Rehovot (Science Park) & Tel Aviv (Sarona area). Both has great train connectivity.

CV’s / Questions: ben.herzberg at imperva.com<mailto:ben.herzberg at imperva.com>

TL;DR Positions:

·         Senior/Experienced security researcher (Experience of 2+years in security – PT/dev/research, writes code)

·         Security researcher (Experience of 1-2 years in security – PT/dev/research, writes code)

·         Python+Javascript developer (2+ years experience)

Looking mainly for full-time workers, one student position also available.

All positions require: Passion for security, ability to (constantly) work with new technologies, ability to lead projects.

Positions full details:
The full-time position:
Senior Security Researcher / Developer (1512)
About the security research team
The Security team is comprised of elite researchers & developers. We deal with research of today’s (And tomorrow’s) attacks on web application and networks, fight bots & DDoS attacks, plus we develop innovative stuff. The team also collaborates with many elements from within the company and externally.

What makes this position unique?
Being part of an amazing team that handles a versatile and broad range of challenges, from different disciplines (Ranging from top notch DDoS attacks to sophisticated web based attacks done by bots or hackers). In additions, the team proactively conducts researches in our labs, and codes innovative stuff to always make sure we’re #1.
What we are looking for
A clever & good-hearted Hacker. We are looking for a motivated, self-learner and hands-on professional with thorough understanding of Web Technologies, Network Protocols, and Security to join our security team.
Requirements:

  *   B.Sc./M.Sc. in in Computer Science or related technical/quantitative field an advantage.
  *   Passionate about security + TechGeekness
  *   Self-learner
  *   Extreme Ownership
  *   Great interpersonal skills
  *   Thorough knowledge of web technologies
  *   Programming/Scripting experience
  *   Good understanding of network protocols

The student position:
Imperva, the leader in Application Security, is looking to hire an Application Security Researcher, based at our R&D Center located in Tel Aviv.

As an application security research team member you will be collaborating with a team of brilliant researchers and developers focused on web application security.
Our mission is to fight hackers off by:

  *   Analyzing security vulnerabilities and application layer anomalies
  *   Developing new security solutions
  *   Presenting security research in security forums and conferences
Qualifications:

  *   Passionate about security and technology.
  *   B.Sc./ M.A student in computer science or similar domain (electric engineering, physics).
  *   Background in web technologies, computer networking and computer infrastructure.
  *   Programming skills (c/ c++/ java/ php/ python /ruby/ shell)
  *   Experience in penetration testing – an advantage.
  *   Excellent writing and speaking capabilities in English – an advantage.
  *   Information security background and knowledge, application security knowledge - an advantage.
