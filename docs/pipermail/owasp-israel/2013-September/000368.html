Hi all,

We're emailing on behalf one of the presenters at the upcoming OWASP AppSec
IL Conference, since their talk is actually based on YOUR input. 

 

We won't give away details just yet, but we will tell you that during their
talk they'll test the security posture of your favorite WordPress plugins. 

So please email back to douglen at hotmail.com <mailto:douglen at hotmail.com>

then.

 

As a further incentive, the first 5 folks to send in their list will win a
cool gift!
