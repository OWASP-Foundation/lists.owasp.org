The annual OWASP AppSec Israel Conference is taking place this year on
Tuesday, 1/10/2013, between 9:00 and 17:00, at the IDC in Herzliya, at the
Efi Arazi School of Computer Science.  

 

OWASP Israel is the largest conference in Israel for application security,
and regularly draws over 300 participants - don't miss it! 

 

Following is the schedule for the conference, which will consist of plenary
sessions and two tracks. The up to date schedule is available at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Israel_2013 . The full abstract for
all the talks, and all the speakers' bios are available there too. 

 

Attending the conference is free of charge, however advance registration is
required. 

Many have already registered, but if you haven't yet, you can register
online at: https://owaspil2013.eventbrite.com
<https://owaspil2013.eventbrite.com/> 

YOU MUST REGISTER TO GUARANTEE YOUR ADMISSION.
