Cyber Together

 

We will have a small and intimate round table meeting with :

Mr. Jean Philippe Lion, Vice President Sales EMEA at Qosmos :   <http://www.qosmos.com> http://www.qosmos.com

The French company will share with Cyber Together their strategy how they positioned their company differentiation and how to become a market leader in the niche of Deep Packet Inspection (DPI).

>From a European vendor perspective, we can learn ideas, tips and case studies how to succeed in OEM deals (vendor to vendor), and best practice business models and partnership agreements.

Together we will find how Qosmos can benefit from Israeli technologies and how Israeli vendors can use Qosmos technologies, by bringing added values for their network security products (FW, WAF, APT’s, SIEM, Anti-DDoS etc. as well as SDN and NVF products - to become smarter than the standards are currently supporting :   <http://www.accumuli.com/pages/files/datasheets/DPI-and-Metadata-for-Cybersecurity-Applications_Qosmos.pdf> http://www.accumuli.com/pages/files/datasheets/DPI-and-Metadata-for-Cybersecurity-Applications_Qosmos.pdf

Typical audience of this event are network element vendors, big data and security infrastructure vendors – if you seek a technology partnership with a leading state of the art DPI engine – this can be a good opportunity.

The Israeli vendors representative should be level of : VP Product , Product Managers, CEO, CTO, VP Sales, VP Marketing, VP Business Development.

The event will be in Tel Aviv or Herzeliya Tuesday 2nd December 16:30 – 18:30.

A notice about the exact location will be sent with your confirmation of attending.

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to  <mailto:info at CyberTogether.org> info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending for this round table event is free of charge, however we need your commitment to attend the event.

Thank you.
