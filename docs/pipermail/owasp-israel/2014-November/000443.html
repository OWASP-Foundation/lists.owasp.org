Cyber Together

 

We are planning to have a small and intimate round table meeting with :

Mr. Steven Schoenfeld , Senior Vice President Products at Blue Coat Systems  :   <https://www.bluecoat.com/company/blue-coat-leaders#steve-schoenfeld> https://www.bluecoat.com/company/blue-coat-leaders#steve-schoenfeld 

As an ex-VP Products and Strategy at PGP Symantec and Director of Product Management at CheckPoint and Cisco, Steven will share with us how Blue Coat Systems define the products strategy, products vision and road map with insights about the latest acquisitions of Solera Networks, Norman Shark, Crossbeam Systems , NetCache, Packeteer, Permeo Technologies, Ositis Software and Entera.

If you seek a technology partnership with a leading network security solutions provider – this can be a good opportunity.

We will host only 40 cyber security vendors from Israel in this event (one person per vendor).

The vendors representative should be level of : VP Product , Product Managers, CEO, CTO, VP Sales, VP Marketing, VP Business Development.

The event will be in Tel Aviv Wednesday 12th November after noon time.

A notice about the exact location will be sent with the confirmation.

Registration is by a reply e-mail (to info at CyberTogether.org)  with your name and company details.

Registration and attending in this round table event is free of charge, however we need your commitment to attend the event.

Thank you.

The Cyber Together Team
