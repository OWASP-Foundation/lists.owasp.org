Greetings,

F5 Global Service Israel is looking for a candidate to expert level ASM WAF position.

Please send relevant CV's

New Product Introduction Engineer

  *   Expert technical knowledge in applications, protocols, network devices and technologies, particularly relating to WAF (Web Application Firewall), D/DoS, Web 2.0, HTTP/S, HTML, AJAX, JS, WebSockets, REST, JSON. Also to be very conversant in L4, L7 and SSL attack vectors and protections.
  *   Advanced knowledge of F5 products, particularly BIG-IP ASM and AFM together with multi-tiered architectures including testing and reproduction.
  *   Demonstrated experience and expert-level knowledge of complex enterprise architectures.
  *   Scripting and programming experience, such as PERL, python or C.
  *   Demonstrated success in troubleshooting and diagnostics.
  *   Ability to function as an individual contributor to long term software development projects.
  *   Experience with product development lifecycle planning, user stories, requirements processes and software development management
  *   Strong leadership and communication skills, both oral and written, with the ability to articulate and educate other engineers and customers about complex technology in a simple way.




Qualifications:

     *   Candidates should have a minimum of 12 years relevant work experience in enterprise level environments.
     *   Fluent in spoken and written English  - must
     *   HTTP and application deep understanding  - must
     *   Web app attacks deep understating and knowledge  - must
     *   Ability to present to technical audience - advantage
     *   F5 product experience and/or relevant experience - advantage
     *   Undergraduate Computer Science degree or equivalent work experience.
