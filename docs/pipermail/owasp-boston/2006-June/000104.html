Building Secure Software from the Ground Up
Start with an effective applications security strategy-save time and money 
How secure are your applications? According to statistics, more than 90% of
your vulnerabilities lurk in your applications, not in your network. Don't
let a security breach cripple your business!
Foundstone invites you to attend our FREE 1/2-day seminar in your local
area, and learn to build an application security strategy that minimizes
your risk. 
If you're a security manager or executive, software development manager,
auditor, or system architect, you won't want to pass up this opportunity to
learn why secure software is such a vital aspect of your overall security
posture.
Some of the topics we'll address include:
*         Why is software security so important? 
*         Why fixing security flaws after the fact is not the optimal
approach 
*         How building security into your applications in the early stages
saves time and money 
*         Best practices for your software development life cycle 
*         Why educating your staff is one of the most powerful investments
you'll ever make 
*         The truth about automated tools 
Foundstone invites you attend our NO COST invitation-only executive
