Hello security enthusiasts,

The IEEE Secure Development Conference 2018 is coming up! The conference
will be Sunday, Sept. 30 through Tuesday, Oct. 2 at the Hyatt Regency in
Cambridge.

The SecDev Organizing Committee is pleased to announce a $150 discount off
conference admission for OWASP members! When you register, choose "OWASP
Member" from the pull-down menu and have your OWASP membership number
ready. Please note, this offer cannot be combined with the IEEE member rate
- the rate for IEEE member is better.

IEEE SecDev is an exciting conference with papers from both industry and
academe. Some of the tutorial sessions are filling up, so register ASAP to
get your first choice!

Conference agenda and registration: https://secdev.ieee.org/2017/agenda/
To join OWASP (only $50 and it qualifies you for $150 off conference
admission): https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership

Thanks and I hope to see you there!

-Andrew Gronosky
IEEE SecDev 2018 Registration Chair
