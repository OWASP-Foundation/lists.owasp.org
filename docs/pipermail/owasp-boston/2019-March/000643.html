Hi Folks,
OWASP is moving to Google Groups for it's email service. The project
rationale is at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Staff-Projects/Mailman-EOL.
You should all be getting emails with requests to join the Google group.
I'll approve groups of requests every few days.

>From  Matt Tesauro, *OWASP Foundation*
Director of Community and Operations

   1. I've create a Google doc that explains how to join a Google Group for
   people with existing Google/@owasp.org accounts and *those you don't
   want to create Google accounts*. *You don't have to have a Google
   account to join, send and receive email from a Google Group*.
   How to join with or without a Google account is documented here
   <https://drive.google.com/open?id=1TBzgvB8Tb0aAZnEy2qYzLnrzJuRzK_T91Em09DVf5YA>
   .
   2. I've updated the project's wiki page
   <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Staff-Projects/Mailman-EOL> with the
   above document and also started creating a FAQ at the bottom of that page
   based on questions I've gotten so far.


The form to join the group is at  -
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1TBzgvB8Tb0aAZnEy2qYzLnrzJuRzK_T91Em09DVf5YA/edit

Thanks, Jim
