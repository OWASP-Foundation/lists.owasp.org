Hi Folks,

OWASP has formed a partnership with SC Congress. Included in our agreement
is their upcoming SC Congress event happening November 12, 2015 at the
Metro Meeting Centers in Boston.  http://www.sccongress.com/boston/.  They
are expecting 200+ in attendance.



They have offered us expo space to help promote OWASP and the local Boston
Chapter.  In addition to the expo space they also offered complimentary VIP
passes for people who volunteer to staff the OWASP booth. We need people
there from 10:30 am to set up until 6:30 pm. If you would like to volunteer
for the OWASP booth for any time span, please email boston at owasp.org with
the hours you can be there and phone contact information. OWASP will supply
collateral and booth materials, we just need people to hand it out and talk
about the local chapter. If we get 2 or more people for the same time they
can share the VIP passes and attend the conference. You can also have one
of our blue OWASP Boston Top 10 tshirts, altho we only have XL.



Thanks, Jim
