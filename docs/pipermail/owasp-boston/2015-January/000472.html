Hi all and happy new year! OWASP RI is back with our meetings again after a
restful December. I just wanted to let you know about the upcoming meetings
and a switch that we made. We flipped the January and February speakers, so
on January 21, we will have David Sherry, CISO at Brown University talking
about the Maturing Security Model and on February 18, we will have Prof.
Vic Fay-Wolfe talking about Forensics. Our full upcoming schedule is below,
along with the links to meetup.com where you can RSVP your attendance.

I also wanted to let people know that the OWASP Boston meeting is this
week, Wednesday night at 6:30 pm at Akamai in Cambridge (150 Broadway,
formerly 8 Cambridge Center). Jim Weiler will be talking risk analysis for
POODLE and I will be presenting on how a hacker views your web site. And
like us in RI, the Boston chapter also has pizza and drinks available, so
come on by! (https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Boston)

As always, OWASP Rhode Island meets at 6:30 pm at 10 Dorrance Street, home
of Swipely!

Jan 21, Maturing Security Model with David Sherry -
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/events/215040192/


Feb 18, Digital Forensics with Prof. Vic Fay-Wolfe -
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/events/209084842/

Mar 18, Mining Ruby Gems for Fun and No Profit with Larry Cashdollar -
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/events/215041872/

April (Date TBD), Threat Intelligence and OSINT with Stuart Gorton -
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/events/218761874/

That's what we have and we're always looking for new speakers and new
attendees. OWASP is always free and open to everyone. Hope to see you soon!

Patrick Laverty
OWASP RI
