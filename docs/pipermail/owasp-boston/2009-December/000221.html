Hi Folks,

Does anybody have any comments, experience, suggestions etc. for
estimating how long it takes to do a web app pen test, or how long you
have found it takes? I know it can vary widely depending on the quality
asked for or the fact that you may only be allowed a fixed number of
hours anyway, but I wanted to ask.  

 

At the OWASP conference in DC last month I asked premier pen tester
Kevin Johnson about that and he said if he didn't really know anything
about the app - 2 weeks.  Talking to other pen testers, they get an idea
from the number of parameters, dynamic pages and main execution paths;
and the time varies from a week to 3 weeks.
