June 11, 2008: Boot Camp-Style Training in the Hottest Security Topics

Six hours of intensive training on some of the hottest security topics, led by a major provider of CISSP boot-camp training. Select two 3-hour sessions from the following topics:
- Security Architecture and Application Security. Case study on virtualization.
- Security Laws, Regulation and Compliance. Case study on Payment Card Industry (PCI) safeguards.
- VOIP and Wireless Security. Case study on securing rich multi-media.

If you have a question about your membership status, please email membership at nymissa.org. You may join the New York Metro chapter by applying at http://www.issa.org/Join/Join-Online.html.
