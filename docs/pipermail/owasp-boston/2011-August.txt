Hi Folks,

 

The OWASP Boston Application Security Conference is on Saturday, October
8, Microsoft New England Research and Development center, MS NERD, in
Cambridge.

 

You can find out more about the conference and how to register at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2011_BASC_Homepage.

 

Also, if you would like to present a topic at the conference,
information about the call for papers is at the same URL. The email
address to send presentation abstracts to is
BASC2011Presentations at gmail.org

 

If you know someone that you think would make a good presentation at the
conference, please forward this email to them or let them know about the
conference URL and email for abstracts.

 

Also, you can help the chapter and the conference by becoming an OWASP
member and listing Boston as your chapter affiliation. OWASP is a 501C3
charitable organization so the cost to you or your employer is tax
deductable.
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership#How_Can_I_Become_An_OWASP_Mem
ber.3F
