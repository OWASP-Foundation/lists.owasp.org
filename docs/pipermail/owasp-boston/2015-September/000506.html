Hi Folks,

You can register for OWASP BASC 2015 now at
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/boston-application-security-conference-basc-2015-tickets-1783483447

We'll have the agenda with presentation descriptions and speaker bios on
the web site in a few days; just click the 'Agenda' on the conference home
page at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2015_BASC_Homepage

In addition to the keynote, twelve presentations and a closing event, we
have a new feature where you'll be able to meet with most speakers in a
separate room, directly after their talk for more detailed Q and A.

We'll also have a 'resume room' where other information security
professionals and placement consultants will help you with your resume,
career choices, skill set and training decisions.

And of course it's always been a great opportunity to meet and talk with
other application security professionals.

We'll also have a raffle at the closing for sponsor and OWASP prizes.

See you there!
