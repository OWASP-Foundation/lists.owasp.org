Hi Folks,
BASC 2015 is Saturday Oct. 3. Our call for papers is open. If you or
someone you know (a coworker, teacher, friend) would like to do a
presentation at BASC, please submit your idea at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/BASC_2015_Call_For_Papers

Form -
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1YW8AcSWR1vUx2vdFufpBl0f8_8SsQdTX5Ts7va4_YcA/viewform

We encourage first time presenters; this is a great way to get noticed,
practice speaking and presentation skills.  We encourage students and
faculty and application security professionals as presenters. New research,
current practices, things that have worked and not worked are all useful to
our audience.

OWASP BASC 2015 Program Committee
