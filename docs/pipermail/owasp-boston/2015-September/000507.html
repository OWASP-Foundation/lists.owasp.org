Announcing our next OWASP RI meeting for October 6, 6:30 pm at Swipely's
headquarters. 10 Dorrance Street in Providence.

We are excited to have Larry Pesce from Inguardians, SANS and Paul's
Security Weekly to talk to us about his experiences while performing
penetration testing. Larry has additional expertise in ethical hacking of
wireless devices, as he teaches the SANS SEC617 Wireless Ethical Hacking
course. Larry will give an overview of penetration testing as well as give
examples from his own experiences. You can also hear Larry weekly on the
Paul's Security Weekly podcast, for which Larry is a co-creator and
co-host.

We will also have Bernadette McHugh giving us a brief overview of the OWASP
Top 10.

We also just announced our next OWASP RI meeting on November 3rd, with
Kellen Kleinfelter talking about account checkers and rewards program
fraud. Additionally, Amber Lee will talk briefly about the types of XSS.
You can find out more information on the group's meetup page.

As always, Swipely will be providing food and drinks for the group, which
is very appreciated. Thank you Swipely!

Please RSVP for the meetings by either replying to this email or using the
group's Meetup page here:
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/

See you on the 6th!
