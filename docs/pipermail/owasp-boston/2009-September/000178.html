Downtown Boston location for this meeting, 6 - 9 pm 

Core Security

The street address is 41 Farnsworth St. Boston, MA 02127 - 6th floor.
The lots in the back have entrances on Congress St. and Seaport Blvd.

For directions by car and T go to
http://www.coresecurity.com/content/directions

 

The building will be locked, but Core employees will be there to let
everyone in. Walk straight into the building and up the escalators, the
entire 6th floor is Core Security, so walk off the elevator and directly
into the offices. There will be someone there to greet you.

 

Presentations - 

Paul Asadoorian

Bob's Great Adventure: Attacking & Defending Web Applications

 

Summary: This presentation will look at some common, and many newer,
techniques for web application recon and attacks.  Wrapped in a story
about Bob's quest for world domination, we'll look at some of the more
interesting web application attack methods in practice and how to defend
yourself against them.

 

Paul Asadoorian is at http://www.pauldotcom.com/.  

 

The second presentation will be by Alex Horan of Core Security. I've
heard Alex do great presentations, the most recent was about the
technical details of the Heartland breakin.

 

Pizza will be provided by Core Technologies.
