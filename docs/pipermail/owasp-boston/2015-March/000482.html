7 pm - Patrick Laverty - “Are You An Imposter? Me too!”

Many people in the information security field feel like a fraud, or an
imposter. In reality, we often know far more, and are capable of far more
than we believe we do. There is so much to know and we constantly hear
about the latest groundbreaking research done by others, which makes us
feel less important or worthy. Let’s talk about what Imposter Syndrome is,
its prevalence, and then we can start to realize just how capable we are
and go forward with the confidence in the field.
