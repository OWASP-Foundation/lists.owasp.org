The OWASP Boston chapter (http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Boston) would
like to announce a call for papers for the Boston Application Security
Conference 2010; Saturday November 20, Microsoft New England Research
Center, Cambridge MA.

 

This will be a free, one day informal conference, to try to increase
awareness and knowledge of application security in the greater Boston
area.

 

There will be two tracks: Track 1 - Basic/Current Application Security,

 

Track 2 - Future / Advanced / New Research in Application security. 

 

Each presentation will be 50 minutes.

 

We hope to attract both people who are new to web application security
as well as people who are experienced in web application security.

 

Please email a copy of your presentation abstract to each member of the
program committee:

 

jim.weiler at starwoodhotels.com

Mark.Arnold at computershare.com

zach at n0where.org

 

This call for papers will close Oct. 10. We will decide on the final
presentations by October, 17 and notify all submitters ASAP after that
date.

 

Please refer to the OWASP Speaker Agreement at
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Speaker_Agreement

The time frames mentioned in that document do not apply to this
conference.
