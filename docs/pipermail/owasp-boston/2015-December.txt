A Multi-Stakeholder Summit on Medical CyberSafety

On Friday, December 4th 2015, the inaugural CyberMedRx event will be held
at the Microsoft Technology Center <http://cybermedrx.org/event/location/> in
Cambridge, Massachusetts from 8am-4pm. This event will bring together
different stakeholders from the medical ecosystem to collaborate on the
intersection of patient safety and cyber security. Organizers, speakers
<http://cybermedrx.org/event/speakers/>, and attendees include
representation from a variety of stakeholder groups. *The event is free,
and limited to 150 participants so register
<http://cybermedrx.org/event/register/> early.*

   - Medical Device Manufacturers (Large and Small)
   - Healthcare Delivery Organizations (Large and Small)
   - Physicians
   - Patients
   - Payers
   - Government Agencies
   - Academic Institutions and Researchers
   - Security Researchers
   - Policy and Legal Organizations

The goal is to outline collaborative efforts to ensure we are *safer,
sooner, together*. For more information, follow @CyberMedRx
<https://twitter.com/cybermedrx> on Twitter, keep an eye out for updates
<http://cybermedrx.org/news/>, and look for a final agenda
<http://cybermedrx.org/event/agenda/> soon.
Organizing Committee

The organizing committee for the event includes individuals from:

   - I Am The Cavalry <https://iamthecavalry.org>
   - MITRE <http://www.mitre.org>
   - ISC2 Eastern Massachusetts <http://isc2easternma.org>
   - NH-ISAC
   - Academic Institutions
   - Healthcare Providers

When Friday, December 4, 2015 from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM (EST) -  Where
Microsoft Technology Center - One Cambridge Center Cambridge, MA 02142
