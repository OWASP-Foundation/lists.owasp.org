Hi all,

Our Boston MetroWest client’s 50-person Marketing organization has recently
earned a place of prominence with the company.  This software firm is now
considered a marketing-oriented tech firm.  If you are looking to have
visible impact and to take ownership, this is an opportunity to influence an
early stage business backed by an industry leading brand.  They are seeking
a Senior Solutions Marketing Manager for their ID and Security practice.
  
Our client is targeting someone who:
• is passionate about enterprise software marketing
• has B2B Identity Management and Security (IAM, Risk & Compliance, etc.)
domain experience
• earned his/her MBA degree from a top-15 program
• is mid-career stage (4 to 8 years post-MBA, currently at the Marketing
Manager or Sr. Product Marketing Manager/almost Director level)
• has demonstrated career success (steep career trajectory)
• has impressive communications skills and an assertive personality

If this target list describes you and you’re interested in a new venture,
we’d love to hear from you.  Please send a Word version of your resume and a
cover letter that addresses the stated search criteria to Courtney Homer at
chomer at careerencore.com.  If you’re not interested at this time, please feel
welcome to pass these details along.
