*Main presentation description - *


*Topic:  Containers have moved from Hype to Deployment in the Enterprise.
We will discuss Security’s role in defining policy without inhibiting Dev.*



What is the impact of Containers on the process and practice of Security,
from SDLC controls through administration, network, cloud and Incident
Management? What do you need to demand from DevOps in order to maintain
your security standards and acceptance testing in fast moving deployments?
We will review real world examples, such as Spectre/Meltdown, Equifax,
among others, and determine what the impact/security risk could be if the
apps were containerized.



*About Our Speaker:*


Tsvi Korren, CISSP, has been an enterprise IT professional for 20 years,
with background in business process consulting in large organizations. Tsvi
is currently the director of technical services at Aqua, concentrating on
building bridges between DevOps and Security.
