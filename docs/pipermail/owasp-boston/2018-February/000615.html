Jim Weiler will repeat his presentation on OWASP Top 10 2017 - What's In,
What's Out and Why.

While the 2017 Top Ten continued the trend toward risks and not
vulnerabilities, there was significant differences of opinion among the
main contributors leading to some changes in the document leadership and
the list itself. We'll cover the various arguments around the list
contents, the data interpretation, list selection methodology, and how you
could realistically use the list.
