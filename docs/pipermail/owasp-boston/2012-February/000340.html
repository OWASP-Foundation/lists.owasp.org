Hello and thank you for taking the time to read my note.  We are involved with a significant information security project in the Metro Boston area and are seeking a Network Information Security Engineer on a multi-year project who has a combination of the following background:


 *   Enterprise Network, Information Security, Forensic, Intrusion Analysis, and/or Incident Handling and Administration;
 *   Operating systems that include: Linux, OS X, Windows desktop/server security architectures;
 *   A combination of:  Security threat models, attack vectors, exploit scenarios including shell code, web-based exploits, botnets and Trojans;
 *   Depth and breadth of knowledge in network operations, systems engineering/administration and how security concepts may be adopted to best fit in any given situation;
 *   Well-formed understanding of impact on business procedures/continuity that given security measures and practices may have;
 *   Understanding of risk management concepts;
 *   Ability to communicate security, computing and network concepts to the general community, peers, and other security professionals;
 *   Comfort, familiarity and enjoyment of teaching concepts and procedures to both the less technically equipped as well as peers.
