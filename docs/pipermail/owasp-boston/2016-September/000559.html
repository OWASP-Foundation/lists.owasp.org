If you planning to attend OWASP BASC this Saturday from 8:30 am to 6:30 pm
at the Microsoft Technology Center, 5 Wayside Road Burlington, MA and you
have not yet registered, please do so right away so that our catering count
will be accurate.  The registration link is available on the BASC 2016
Homepage.

 

www.owasp.org/index.php/2016_BASC_Homepage

 

If you have registered but will no longer be able to attend, please
unregister.  To do this, log into Eventbrite, click on your name in the
upper right, select "Tickets" from the drop down, click on the ticket(s) you
wish to release, and then push the "CANCEL ORDER" button.

 

Most of the workshop tickets have sold out, but there one session is still
open.  If you are holding a workshop ticket but can no longer attend, please
unregister to free that ticket for someone else.  There are no waitlists for
the workshops.

 

You must be registered as a General Attendee and have a government-issued ID
with you in order to be admitted to the building.

 

See you Saturday!
