This is NOT for OWASP BASC 2016,
We have free passes to SC Congress that can be shared by OWASP booth
volunteers.
Setup is just taking the OWASP collateral out of the boxes at the booth;
breakdown is just taking any leftover collateral with you and bringing it
to one of the OWASP Boston board members sometime.

http://www.sccongress.com/boston/

Working the booth just means telling people about OWASP in general and the
local chapter activities and handing out the stuff below.
There will be an expo table for OWASP - Booth #14.  The set up time is from
7:30am - 9:30am, Expo Floor opens at 10:00am.  Breakdown is 4:30 pm. There
will be two packages sent for the conference.

If you want to help reply to this email and I'll send you the volunteer
registration link.

The following are being shipped:
100 pens
50 stress balls
50 because the world is cruel sticker
50 community cloud sticker
50 yellow cinch up drawstring backpacks
Flyers
AppSec USA postcards

Thanks, Jim
