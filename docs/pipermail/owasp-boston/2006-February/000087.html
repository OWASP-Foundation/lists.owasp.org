The presenter from Cenzic will be their Senior Vulnerability Research
Analyst, so you will be able to ask more technical questions about web
application vulnerabilities than you might have otherwise.
 
Tom Stracener is Cenzic's Senior Vulnerability Research Analyst. Tom has
been a leading technologist in the security space for over 6 years. Tom was
the head of vulnerability research for nCircle and various consulting and
open source projects. He invented the industry's first numeric vulnerability
scoring system and is the co-inventor of 6 network security technologies
with patents pending. He is the author of many security related whitepapers
and has spoken at various conferences on network and application security
topics. He is also responsible for the critical application alerts that
Cenzic's research lab, Cenzic Intelligent Analysis (CIA), issues on a
monthly basis
