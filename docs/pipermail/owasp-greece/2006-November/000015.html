Websense® Security Labs™ has received reports of a phishing attack that
targets customers of Emporiki Bank. Users receive a spoofed email
message which claims that an error was detected in their online
information. Users are prompted to log on to verify their details. The
email provides a link to a phishing site that attempts to collect
personal and account information.

This phishing site is hosted in the United States and was up at the
time of this alert.

http://www.websense.com/securitylabs/alerts/alert.php?AlertID=697
