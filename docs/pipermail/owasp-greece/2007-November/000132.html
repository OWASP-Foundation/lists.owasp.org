Pera apo to thema twn kamerwn, to opoio einai kai gnwsto kai
polu-suzitimeno, to simantiko nomizw edw einai oti i Arxi agnoithike
plirws. Oi thesmothetimenes apo to Syntagma, Aneksartites Arxes mas
einai apo ta simantikotera organa tis Ellinikis koinwnias, oi opoies
omws dexontai sunexws xtupimata, eite apo pithana sumferonta eite apo
ton aplo kosmo o opoios mesa stin agnoia tou parasuretai apo pompwdeis
dilwseis laikismou diaforwn dimosiografwn kai politikwn.

Elpizw to gegonos auto pou prokalese polles suzitiseis na apotelesei tin
aformi gia ton epanaprosdiorismo tou rolou kai tis dikaiodosias twn
Aneksartitwn Arxwn, kati pou exoume suzitisei kai palaiotera apo edw,
para na apotelesei kako proigoumeno gia paromoia peristatika sto mellon.
