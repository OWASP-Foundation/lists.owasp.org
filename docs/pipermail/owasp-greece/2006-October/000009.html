The Security Officers Management and Analysis Project (SOMAP.org) is all
about Open Source Information Security Risk Management. It is our belief
that risk management processes and best practices need to be offered in
an open kind. Only freely available risk management informations can
potentially lead to a better security management and further development
of the whole risk management field.
