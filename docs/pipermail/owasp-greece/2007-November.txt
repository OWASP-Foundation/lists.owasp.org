mporei enas astunomikos na theorei 'epikindunes'. Mia diadilosi mporei gia 
paradeigma na einai 'epikinduni' alla den einai paranomi oste na 
dikaiologeitai i katagrafi tis apo ton eisaggelea. Apo tin alli ean se mia 
diadilosi exei suntelestei ena autoforo egklima i ean o eisaggeleas krinei 
oti uparxoun endeikseis pou na stekoun se dikastirio gia tin prothesi 
diapraksis egklimatos tote den nomizo oti i APDPX mporei logika na 
diamarturithei ean xrisimopoiithoun oi kameres gia tin dioksi tou egklimatos.

Poly sosta ta osa mas eipe o Kostas kai symfono apolyta. Sthn sygkekrimeni 
periptosi pistevo den eprepe na paraviastei h apofash ths Arxhs poy exei 
san geniko kanona (kai poly sosta) na mhn epitrepei thn parakolouthisi 
diadiloseon mesa apo tis kameres. Parola ta ektropa pou exoume dei stis 
poreies, paroles tis katastrofes, h xrhsh kameron einai akros ypervolikh 
kai tonizei thn adynamia tou kratous na diasfalisei oti mia poreia mporei 
na pragmatopoiithei omala. H diasfalish ths poreias mporei na ginei me 
pollous tropous. Mporei na enimerothei h Astynomia gia ton dromo pou tha 
akoloythisei h poreia meso ths syntonistikhs epitrophs (poy afto panta 
ginetai), sthn synexeia na sxediasei ena plano kai na entopisei tis 
"evaisthites" perioxes kai na tis prostatefsei topothetontas astynomikh 
dynamh. An xreiastei na topothethsei kai astynomikous me politika, mesa 
sthn poreia kai na sylamvanoun olous tous koukouloforous. Polles alles 
idees gia PROLIPSI mporoun na anaferthoun edo.

Genikotera h adynamia tou kratous na diasfalisei me anthropino dynamiko mia 
epityximenh poreia, odhgei sthn xrhsh kameron, h an thelete, sto prosxhma 
gia xrhsh kameron. Den mporo na pistepso oti h astynomia den gnorizei kathe 
fora poioi prokaloun to provlima, kai poioi einai aftoi oi 
gnostoi-agnostoi. Apla eite tous volevei na einai ekei, eite exoun 
topothetithei epitides gia na dhmioyrgoun to provlima kai na meta na 
mporoun na dikaiologhsoun alla metra pou mporei na lavoun.

Telos, pistevo oti h xrhsh ton kameron akomh kai gia tis troxaies 
paravaseis, tha prepei na katargithei. Allo na milame gia PROLIPSI, kai 
allo na tous theoroume olous san PITHANOUS EGKLIMATIES. Afto synevei me tis 
kameres sthn diadilosh stis 17/11. Oloi osoi diadilosan antimetopistikan 
san pithanoi egklimaties apo to kratos, kai afto einai lathos.

Giafto sygxaritiria ston Proedro kai sta melh ths Arxhs..!! Htan apo tis 
kalyteres kinhseis pou mporousan na kanoun kathos etsi edosan kai parapano 
dhmosiotita sto thema, pou toy xreiazotan, eidallos mporei tora na mhn to 
syzitagame kai na eixe thaftei me synoptikes diadikasies.
