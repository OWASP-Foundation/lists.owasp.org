“It is time to turn the repeated political concern about spam into
concrete actions to fight spam,” said Viviane Reding Commissioner for
Information Society and Media. “In line with EU legislation outlawing
spam, the Dutch authorities have managed to cut domestic  spam by 85%  -
I’d like to see other countries achieving similar  results through  more
efficient enforcement. I will revisit this  issue again next year  to
see whether additional legislative measures  against spam are required.”
