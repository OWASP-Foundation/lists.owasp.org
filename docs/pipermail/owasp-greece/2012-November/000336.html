The ISACA Athens Chapter is happy to announce the 2nd ISACA Athens Chapter
Conference on 6 & 7 December 2012, which is organized in cooperation with
Hellenic American Union. You may find additional information about the
conference and the workshop here:
http://www.isaca.gr/images/stories/isaca/2ndISACAConference/2ndISACAAthens.pdf

The theme of the conference is: “Information Assurance at a Crossroad:
Choosing a New Strategic path through Security, Governance and IT Audit
Best Practices”.

The OWASP Greek Chapter actively supports one of the most significant
conferences in Greece.

OWASP Members can benefit of a discount on registration price by completing
the attached form.
