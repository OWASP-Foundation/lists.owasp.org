Greetings Security Professionals,

OWASP Silicon Valley will host its next meeting at the Mozilla Foundation
Offices in Mountain View on Aug 25th, 2011. As usual attendance is free and
food and beverages will be provided. This should be yet another excellent
event with great speakers and exciting new topics. This is also a great
opportunity to network with industry peers. The event is open to the public;
please forward this invite to your colleagues and friends who are interested
in computer and application security.
