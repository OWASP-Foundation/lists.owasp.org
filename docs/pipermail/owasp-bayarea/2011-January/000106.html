Hi Everyone,

Every year during RSA we organize a WASC luncheon. A place for those interested in webappsec to get together to enjoy some free food & drinks, play pool, and talk Web security with a 100 or so like minded people. Everyone on the "owasp-bayarea" is of course invited. The time and location details are below. Remember Jillian's only hold so many. So if you think you want to come, please make sure to register to get on the guest list.

Take a Break @ RSA and Meet-up with Your Peers at the WASC Meet UP

Join your Web application security peers for lunch at  Jillian's at Metreon. Take a break from the RSA show floor, relax and shoot some pool courtesy of WhiteHat Security and F5 Networks. 

To register please copy and paste this URL into your browser, then complete the form and click submit: http://mktg.whitehatsec.com/forms/WASCMeetupRSA2011 

WASC RSA Meet-up
Wednesday, February 16, 2011
Buffet Lunch served: 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM
Jillian's at Metreon <http://sanfrancisco.jilliansbilliards.com/> 
101 Fourth Street | 415.369.6101 
RSVP by February 11, 2011* 

On the day of the event go directly to  Jillian's to check-in with WhiteHat/F5 staff and then you will be  granted access to the party. We look forward to seeing you there.
