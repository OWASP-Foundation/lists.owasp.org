Hey all, moved to the area recently from Austin.

I spoke at OWASP Austin once, and Austin Hacker's Association (AHA)
several times.  Both of them were presentation-oriented, with OWASP
Austin tending towards the "one long talk on web security" format and
AHA tending towards "many small talks on any topic, but mostly
security" format.

I looked around for a presentation-oriented mostly-security org here
and couldn't find one (OWASP is closest but a bit more specialized),
so decided to start a mlist to discuss the idea:

http://lists.bitrot.info/mailman/listinfo/baha

Please consider joining in the discussions.
