Just wanted to quickly followup on this previous email. We had our first

All,

Thanks so much for attending last night's chapter leadership brainstorming.
Here are the notes from our discussion.

*Our Approach*
*1. Monthly chapter meetings at a set time and place *- this will be the
repeatable great speaker talks. Great ideas about having themes that carry
across multiple months. We want talks to be happening in all three core
locations (SF, Peninsula, East Bay) every month (or every other month at
the latest) for each location. This is thinking big and means we're aiming
for upwards of 36 talks in the bay area per year.
*2. Quarterly big events *- this will vary and could be a workshop,
training, ctf, mentor event, etc. It will be a bigger event though for sure
*3. Videos -* we'd love to record and potentially live stream our events
*4. Ideas - *we have lots of ideas we can experiment within our events such
as time boxed problem solving discussions, lightning talks, arsenal
showcase etc.
*5. Bay Area -* this first leadership discussion was focused on SF. The Bay
Area chapter includes SF, Peninsula and East Bay. We'll repeat these
leadership discussions in Peninsula and East Bay to build project teams for
those areas too.

*Leadership*
To accomplish the above we will have project teams that can operate
autonomously. We'll also have project teams for each of our 3 areas so a
focus on 1 area doesn't starve progress in other areas.

I've committed to provide overall guidance to each of these teams in
regards to previous processes, standard documentation, requirements etc.
Each team should feel empowered to figured out approaches and plans and
make decisions. I'll ask those teams to keep me looped in so we have a
central knowledge point (which would be written down too).

*The project teams*


*San Francisco*
*Speaker Team:* Ben, Luqi, Sam, Will
- Goal is to find great speakers at get them lined up for our chapter
events.

*Quarterly Big Events: *Tom, Rajat, Sam, Ivor, Ben
- Goal is to plan an amazing larger event for each quarter. There's lots of
flexibility here.

*Location Team:* Michael, Anshur
- Goal is to identify a repeatable large space where we can host our
chapter events. Key elements are capacity, repeatable time/date, ease of
access

*Video Team:* Christian
- Goal is to figure out how we could do reasonable quality recording of our
talks and potentially live streaming

*Peninsula (these teams will grow after our peninsula leadership meeting)*
*Speaker Team: *Travis, Bassam
- Goal is to find great speakers at get them lined up for our chapter
events. Note, the speaker teams for S, Peninsula and East Bay should also
communicate to each other so we can have speakers talk at multiple locations

*Location* *Team*: Brendan
- Goal is to identify a repeatable large space where we can host our
chapter events. Key elements are capacity, repeatable time/date, ease of
access

*What's Next*
1. I'll send individual emails to the project teams so the teams have
background info and all email addresses for the participants.
2. Project Teams - start working. You own your area and it will succeed or
fail based on what you do.
3. I will setup similar leadership chats at a Peninsula location and a East
Bay Location
4. We don't have another meeting scheduled yet. We'll get one based on the
above project teams delivering on their respective areas!
5. Ben Hagen will setup a slack channel for leadership to communicate in
real time. Please send emails though for more important progress notes.

Thanks Everyone!
