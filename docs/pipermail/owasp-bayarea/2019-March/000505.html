Hi All,
Happy to announce a hacker days on "Blockchain security". please fill the
form to confirm attendance.

Date: Wednesday, March 27th | 6:00PM - 8:30PM
Venue: Solana , 3903, 645 Howard St, San Francisco, CA 94105
Form:- https://goo.gl/forms/TEdo4vbuyPb8VKQk2


Session Description:
In this session we’ll be taking a dive into the challenges and
considerations around blockchain security. For this workshop we’ll using
Solana as the underlying blockchain. We’ll be starting with a high level
breakdown of blockchain concepts, then proceeding to analyse past security
failures in the blockchain space. Understanding these attack vectors will
allow us to understand what design considerations need to be made when
building or interacting with blockchains. The session will be concluded
with a hands-on workshop where participants will be able to stress test and
hack the Solana blockchain. At this workshop we intend to give an appetizer
for those interested in decentralized technologies.

Requirements:
    Laptop with internet access
    Link to Solana Github


Trainer:
Anatoly is the creator of Solana. He led development of operating systems
at Qualcomm, distributed systems at Mesosphere, and compression at Dropbox.
He holds 2 patents for high performance Operating Systems protocols, was a
core kernel developer for BREW which powered every CDMA flip phone (100m+
devices), and led development of tech that made Project Tango (VR/AR)
possible on Qualcomm phones.
