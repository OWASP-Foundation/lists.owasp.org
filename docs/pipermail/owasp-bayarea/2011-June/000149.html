Friends,

On July 22nd we are running a workshop on Internet Tracking and Privacy 
at Stanford University.   The workshop will discuss Internet privacy 
given the need to track users for targeted advertising or fraud 
prevention.  Confirmed speakers include Ed Felten (FTC chief 
technologist), Dean Hachamovitch (vice president of Internet Explorer), 
and Omar Tawakol (Bluekai).

More information is available at:
	http://seclab.stanford.edu/witap2011/

The workshop is open to the public, but we ask that everyone register to 
help with lunch planning.


Looking forward to seeing everyone there!
