We're starting a mentorship program for students and those early in their
security careers. The goal is to match these people with more experienced
security professionals for mentoring and coaching.

We're kicking off this event on Tuesday, December 1st in San Francisco at
6pm.

If you are interested in being a mentor or mentee then fill out our
mentorship form and RSVP for the meetup. Note, if you skip the mentorship
form you won't be accepted on the meetup.

Step 1. Mentorship Form
https://docs.google.com/a/owasp.org/forms/d/1AYOgpqJ6hRYL_kURX5x5DrG6kdDweiQupJvOQZHV2gs/viewform

Step 2. Meetup RSVP
http://www.meetup.com/Bay-Area-OWASP/events/226890416/



Space will be limited, please complete registration early if you're
interested.
