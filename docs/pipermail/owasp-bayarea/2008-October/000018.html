SAVE THE DATE:  Friday, October 24, 2008

ISACA-SV and IIA-SJ are hosting their Annual Joint Fall Conference on
Friday, October 24, 2008

This year's topic is, "Future Risks: Are You Ready?"  7 CPE's are available
at a very cost effective training rate.  Topics include the following:

- Real World Perspectives on Corporate Fraud
- The Automated Internal Audit Function of the Future
- Protecting Your Company from Cyber Crime
- Disaster Recovery Planning / Business Continuity
- The Risks and Opportunities of Cloud Computing
- Audit Skill Sets for the Future

To register please go to: http://www.acteva.com/booking.cfm?bevaID=164877.

conference-director at isaca-sv.org

treasurer at isaca-sv.org
