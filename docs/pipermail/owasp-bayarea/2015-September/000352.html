Bay Area OWASP AppSec!


I want to make sure you’re aware of OWASP’s biggest event AppSec USA
<https://2015.appsecusa.org/> that’s taking place this week!. The
conference is here in San Francisco at the Hyatt Regency
<http://sanfranciscoregency.hyatt.com/en/hotel/home.html> on September
24-25.


You can still register (until we run out of tickets that is).

We have a great roster of influential leaders joining us including Alex
Stamos, CSO of Facebook, Troy Hunt, Microsoft MVP, Josh Corman, founder of
Sonatype, Dr. Phyllis Schneck, Department of Homeland Security along with
security enthusiasts from Salesforce, Wells Fargo, Netflix, Docker and
LinkedIn.

We've added some very interesting tracks to this year's agenda that we
think security engineers and developers will really enjoy. See here
<https://2015.appsecusa.org/agenda/talks/> for full list of sessions.


Leading an AppSec program? We've also got a variety of talks on building
security programs and how top tech companies are approaching these
challenges.


You may also be interested in joining an exclusive group of us for a dinner
cruise around the Bay on Thursday, Sept 24. See details here
<https://2015.appsecusa.org/agenda/dinner-cruise/>.

Would love to offer you a discount on the registration fee - enter US2015_BA
and register here
<https://myowasp.force.com/MN4__PublicEventRegistration?id=a2oU0000000LK2SIAW>
.

Hope to see you there!
