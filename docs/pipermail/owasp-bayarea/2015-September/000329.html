Having trouble figuring out how to get to OWASP AppSecUSA this year?

Here's a way to attend for free. We need volunteers!
If you volunteer an 8 hour shift on one of the conference days, you can
attend the other conference day for free!

If interested please fill out this form as soon as possible.
https://2015.appsecusa.org/get-involved/volunteer/

*Conference Details*
AppSecUSA.org
Sept 24 & 25 (Trainings on Sept 22 & 23)
San Francisco - Held at the Hyatt by the Ferry Building
Check out the talks here: https://2015.appsecusa.org/agenda/speakers/


Thanks!
