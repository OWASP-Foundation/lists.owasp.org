Tadhg Pearson wrote:
> I'm a relatively new WebGoat user, but I've been quite impressed by both 
> the concept of WebGoat and by what I've seen, and I've coded a couple of 
> lessons of my own for my own test purposes, related to my area of work 
> which is anti-patterns.
> 
> Now that I've written the lessons, I can't seem to find the build.xml to 
> compile them back into webgoat. At a pinch, javac would probably do the 
> job, but I'd rather use the suggested method. Can someone either direct 
> me to a copy of the build file, email it to me, or stick it on SVN?
> 
> Cheers, Tadhg.

Easiest way is just to put the lessons into the same hierarchy as the 
rest of the lessons. i.e. org.owasp.webgoat.lessons

The build.xml file is under main/build.xml, but you don't need to make 
any changes to it if you do as I suggested.

Hope this helped.
