All

Hopefully everyone is aware of the recent activity on WebGoat 6 including
the release of WebGoat 6.0.1 (webgoat.github.io).

The team has decided to use the WebGoat mailing list as a means to
communicate, and have a record of, the activities that are currently in
progress.  With the stabilization of the new WebGoat container we are now
focusing on:

Internationalization
Lesson Plugin Architecture (
https://github.com/WebGoat/WebGoat/wiki/Plugin-Architecture)
Separation of view/controller in existing lessons
Updating the Github site.

We want to allow everyone to see the emails and decisions the team is
making.  Please feel free to comment or jump in and help.
