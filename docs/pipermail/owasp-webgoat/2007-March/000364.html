cgi phantom wrote:
> I wanted to expand on what I was trying to specifically achieve.
> 
> I'm attempting to snatch the AuthCookie ID's with WebScarab SessionID 
> for analysis to attempt to construct a AuthCookie for Alice (the How to 
> Spoof an Authentication Cookie exercise). But first I want to collect 
> the cookies for analysis. I know I can do it manually but that defeats 
> the purpose of learning how to use this particular feature of WebScarab.
> 
> Here is the RegEx's I have tried but I keep getting "No Session ID found"
> 
> AuthCookie=*;
> .*AuthCookie="(.............)"*.

Do not use the regular expression field, and WebScarab will pick up all 
cookies.

If you do want to use the regex, use something like (.*). In particular, 
the regex does not include the name of the cookie, it is only matched 
against the VALUE.

FWIW, there is/was a better lesson in WebGoat for learning to use the WS 
SessionID plugin. I don't recall what I called it, but it was something 
along the lines of "predictable cookie".
