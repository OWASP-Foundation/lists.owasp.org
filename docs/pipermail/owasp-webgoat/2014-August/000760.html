*WebGoat* is undergoing a major *modernization effort*.  Already, we’ve
replaced the old UI and infrastructure - really great stuff. As exciting
and difficult as that has been, we're at a point of where we need your
feedback, because you know, you’re pretty awesome.

The first release will wrap all the old lessons with a *new service layer.*
Naturally, our next major push will be to port the good lessons to the *new
plugin architecture* - easier said than done. So, *we need your help* (and
input).

*Which lessons should we import?*

Fill out our *survey

takes about 5 minutes), and *help us prioritize our next effort*.
