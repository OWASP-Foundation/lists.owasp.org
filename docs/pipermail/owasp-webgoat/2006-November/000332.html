You're on the right track...   
 
Remember that the & is the Windows command separator AND also the URL parameter separator.  You may have to encode the & in order for the web application to see it as part of the HelpFile parameter.
 
Also, pay special attention to the feedback the WebGoat gives you.  You may have to get the quoting correct.
