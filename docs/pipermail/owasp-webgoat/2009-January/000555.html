Updates: I found that the WAR's name isn't supposed to contain the version
numbers, and that will be included on the wiki shortly.  I fixed that and
restarted, and saw no improvement.

Then I found that the current catalina log was changing when I ran
shutdown.sh to restart, saying that Tomcat wasn't stopping (and thus not
actually restarting).  This too turned out to be a false positive:
http://www.myeclipseide.com/PNphpBB2-printview-t-10622-start-0.html

Now for your suggestion, li bo.  It didn't solve the problem, but I still
truly appreciate it.  Each suggestion that's not a solution gets me closer
to the one that is.

I first set the role of guest/guest to "guest," but that was apparently
invalid and gave me the below error.  Then I changed the role (and its
declaration) to "role1," which was suggested by Google as one originally in
Tomcat, and WebGoat no longer asked for my password; it just went straight
to a 403 "Access to the requested resource has been denied."  When I deleted
the role and user, and restarted Tomcat, I got the old 401 "This request
requires HTTP authentication" when refusing to give a password.  The same
thing happened for my admin account when I tried it, so I think the solution
lies outside of tomcat-users.xml.
