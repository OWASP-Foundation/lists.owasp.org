Make sure you're not setup to bypass the proxy for local addresses.  
That gave me trouble on my Mac at first, too.

For Safari, that's at the bottom of the control panel where it says  
"Bypass proxy settings for these Hosts & Domains" - I think it  
defaults to having "localhost" and "127.0.0.1" there - remove them.

For Firefox, it's also at the bottom of the dialog: "No proxy for:"  
and it has "localhost, 127.0.0.1" in there by default. Blank those out.

Good luck!
