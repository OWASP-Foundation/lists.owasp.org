Hi everyone,
as we unfortunately still don't have a speaker for the next OWASP
Switzerland Meeting, I'd like to try something new: Lightning Talks.

The concept is simple: If you have anything AppSec related to share,
independent if it's about a tool you like, a metric, a paper, research,
or whatever, you'll have around *10min* to present it to the crowd. This
way, also people who don't have a topic which fits into a normal
presentation slot will have a chance to talk.

If you have something to talk about, please either enter your name and
presentation title at http://techup.ch/111/owasp-switzerland-meeting
(only for people with a Twitter account) or send me a short mail with
the mentioned information.

Best regards,
Sven
