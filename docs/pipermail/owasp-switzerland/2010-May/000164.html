Hi everyone,
there are some good news on OWASP Switzerland which I want to share with
you:

- - Antonio Fontes who's leading the OWASP Geneva chapter is now also a
member of the board with the function "Representative of OWASP Geneva".
This will allow us to better operate in the whole country and not only
in the German speaking part of it.

- - We have our own domain. You can now reach our OWASP wiki page over
http://owasp.ch which makes it a lot easier to remember and to tell
people about it. Thanks to Antonio for that.

- - I updated our wiki page with some additional information and I'm
looking forward to also publish presentations etc. after future meetings
there. Remember: http://owasp.ch :)

- - Tobias and me are actually planning an OWASP Meeting for the end of
June. We're quiet late for that but we're looking forward to make it
happen and to be able to inform you early enough about the presentations
and everything else.

Best regards,
Sven
