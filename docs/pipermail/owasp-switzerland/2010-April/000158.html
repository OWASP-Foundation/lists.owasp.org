Hi everyone,
one week ago we had our last OWASP meeting in Zurich and it was a great
success with more people than we had ever before at a meeting. Thanks to
all of you who made this possible.

To make the future meetings even better and to get more attendees, there
are two updates I'd like to inform you about:

- - From now on, Tobias Christen will assist me in leading the OWASP
Switzerland Local Chapter. Our long time goal here is to build a small
board of about 2-3 people who are responsible for our chapter.

- - We're actually preparing a Local Chapter survey with a few questions
to hear from you what we need to improve and also what you like at the
meetings. I'll send out another mail as soon as it's online. (don't
worry, it will take only about 5-10 minutes to participate)

Best regards and thanks for your support,
Sven
