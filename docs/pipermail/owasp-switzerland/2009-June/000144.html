Hi everyone,
I know it's quiet a bit late for the following invitation but we should
really try to make it happen.

On the 25th of June (yes, thats already in about one week), there will
be an OWASP Meeting in Zurich with special guests.

Jerry Hoff and Jason Li from Aspect Security [1] will come to
Switzerland and give a talk about the benefits of a security API such as
ESAPI on this meeting. They will also give a class during the weekend at
the Jazoon conference.

at the AppSec Conference in Krakow about "Advanced SQL injection
exploitation to operating system full control".

So even if we're late, it's really worth to attend this meeting.

25th of June from about 17:00 to 18:30.
