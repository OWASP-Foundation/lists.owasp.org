Hello everyone,

Sorry for the crossposting.

I wanted to let you know that the registrations for BSides Zurich, the
first BSides security conference in Switzerland, which will take place in
Zurich on the 17th September, are open:
https://bsideszh.ch/registration/

As you may also see in more details on the website, we have decided to
deviate a bit from the standard conference format in favour of a more
interactive one. In fact there will be short talks of 20min each (which we
believe are enough to state a point, whether is a problem we all face on a
daily basis or a new attack/defence technique), followed by open
discussions between the speakers and attendees. More details can be found
here https://bsideszh.ch/call-for-papers/

Due to this particular format attendees seats are limited, so get your
ticket while still available.

Hope to see you all there,
Cheers

Pasquale
