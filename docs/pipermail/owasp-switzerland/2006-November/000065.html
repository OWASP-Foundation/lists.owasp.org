Dear Swiss OWASP members,

Following the good example of Alessandro Moretti I would like to take the 
opportunity to introduce myself to the OWASP members.

My name is Tobias Christen. After having worked in SBV&UBS in the late 
nineties in the area of architecture and security I moved for 6 years to 
Finland where I served as R&D Manager and CTO of a security software 
company (Stonesoft). Now I'm back in Switzerland, working for Zurich 
Financial Services in the area of Security Architecture and IT Risk 
Strategy. We are currently developing new standards for secure application 
development (as a chapter to the security architecture handbook).

I will be glad to support the Swiss OWASP where ever possible and look 
forward to interesting discussions and sessions.

Best regards
