Hello all

To revitalise this chapter I am looking for a Chapter Lead who is
interested (and willing) to take over the lead for the OWASP Switzerland
Chapter.

What does this mean to be chapter lead? You would be required to organise
some meetings where other chapter members could have a beer and talk with
each other. You could organise speeches and demos (or coordinate others
doing so) and do whatever is good to keep the chapter running and healthy.

Please also see this link for further details:

<http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Chapter_Rules>

I would very much welcome if somebody would step up and take over the
chapter lead for I think the whole topic of web application security in
Switzerland could need some fresh air and a few good events for the
specialists.

If you are interested or know somebody who could be, please drop me a
note.

Regards,
Adrian
