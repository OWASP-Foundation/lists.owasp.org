Traveling east

Take the L.I.E. to Exit 34 South or the Northern State Parkway to Exit
26 South (New Hyde Park Road). Turn right onto New Hyde Park Road.

31 (Glen Cove Road). Go south. (Note: the road will change from Guinea

Avenue. Go one mile and at T-junction turn left onto Hilton Avenue.
Immediately after crossing the railroad tracks, turn right onto Sixth
Street. Continue onto South Avenue. The entrance to campus will be on
your left..
