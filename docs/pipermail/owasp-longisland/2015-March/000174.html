Specialists,

Application Security: complex, unique, difficult, but necessary! Join
us tomorrow evening, March 4th, 2015 @ 6:30 PM as Gabriel Gumbs
(@GabrielGumbs) will be presenting the "The OWASP Application Security
Program Quick Start Guide". Gabriel will provide an overview of the
OWASP project and focus his dicussion around unknown vulnerabilities
in custom Web applications. Take a break from shoveling snow and join
us.

When: Wednesday, March 4, 2015 @ 6:30 PM
Where: Adelphi University
1 South Avenue
Garden City, NY

Campus is just large enough to take several walking minutes to
