OWASP LI Members,

The location details for the March Meeting have been posted to the Meetup.

Please RSVP if you plan on attending so that we can get an accurate headcount.

When: Wednesday, March 4, 2015 6:30 PM
Where: Adelphi University, Garden City Campus
1 South Avenue
Garden City, New York 11530

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Additional Location Information:

All OWASP Meetups take place in the "technology room" within the
basement of Resident Hall "A" / New Hall "A".

Enter the basement to New Hall "A" on the southwestern side door of
the building. Open the door and enter the basement stairwell to the
right. Do not try to enter the badge-security door. Follow the OWASP
Meeting signs.

Note: do not try to enter the main lobby (marked in the map) of the
residence hall. This is a secured area for students.


The Cambridge Avenue Gates "H" & "J" are recommended for entering the campus.


**Note** Please only park in "non-reserved" areas. Parking Fields #7,
#5, and #2 are suggested.


Reference the Adelphi Garden City Campus Page for additional details
and direction information.
( http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Long-Island-Meetup/pages/Location_Details_%7C_Adelphi_Campus_Garden_City%2C_NY
)
