Hi all,

Great news. Secure Decisions just became our first Silver Chapter supporter!

Secure Decisions has donated $500 to the chapter. Director Dr. Anita
D'Amico said this generous gesture "was motivated by the tremendous value
we have been getting in our interactions with OWASP." President Frank J.
Zinghini, Jr. promised to become more active and offered to host our next
meeting in their Northport office.

Secure Decisions <http://securedecisions.com/> is a division of Applied
Visions Inc <http://www.avi.com/>.
