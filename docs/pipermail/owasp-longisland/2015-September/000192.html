OWASP LI Specialists,

Labor Day!?!? Summer isn't over just yet! Join us Wednesday, September
2, 2015 to listen to our very own Chapter Leader Helen Gao.

Get an insider sneak peak at her presentation that she is showing at
this year's New York Metro Joint Cyber Security Conference 2015
(NYMJCSC 2015).

------------------------------------------------------------

__Presentation Title__
Secure Software Development Life Cycle, S-SDLC

__Presentation Abstract__
Have your customers ever asked if you include security in your
software development life cycle (SDLC)? Why is Secure SDLC important?
How to do it right? Come to our meeting to debunk the common myths and
learn the best practice, and leave with practical ideas on how to
integrate security into your existing software development process.

1) Practical advice from a speaker who had mitigated legacy web
applications as well as created secure applications from the start.
2) Common myths of S-SDLC debunked
3) Best practice of secure software development
