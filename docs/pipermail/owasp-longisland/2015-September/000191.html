Dear Specialist:

The OWASP Long Island Chapter is a coalition member for the 2015 NY
Metro Joint Cyber Security Conference & Workshop, we cordially invite
you to pre-register for these events for a limited time only!

The New York Metro Joint Cyber Security Conference is a collaborative
event cooperatively developed, organized and sponsored by the leading
information security industry organizations and chapters. Driven by
the collaboration between members of this coalition, the strength of
organizational membership, the provision of desirable CPE credits and
the concurrence of National Cyber Security Awareness Month, the
NYMJCSC promises -- once again -- to be a well-attended by members of
the information technology, information security, audit, academic, and
business communities.

______________________________________________________________

Tuesday, October 13th – Pre-Conference Workshop

Sponsors: Microsoft Corp. (Platinum), GSA Train, Exelis Inc., One
World Labs and OWASP

For 2015, NYMJCSC is offering a pre-conference workshop on Tuesday,
October 13th featuring four in-depth full-day hands-on classroom-style
educational courses to expand your knowledge and foster security
discussions.

* Seating is limited to 120 (30 per class).

* Certain classes are hands-on and require BYOD.

Registration opens September 1st 6:00 am EDT:
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/2015-ny-metro-joint-cyber-security-workshop-registration-17976836201

______________________________________________________________

Wednesday, October 14th – Conference

Sponsors: Microsoft Corp. (Platinum), Identifty Finder (Gold), Nexum
(Silver), Nuix (Silver) and Dell Secureworks(Emerging News & Trends)

The conference is hosted at the Microsoft Technology Center, 11 Times
Square in NYC and will be opened with a keynote address by Tim Rains,
Microsoft’s Chief Security Advisor for WW Cybersecurity & Data
Protection, followed with a keynote by Ron Ross, Fellow at the
National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). In addition to
the 2 keynotes, the agenda offers 16 sessions over 4 topic tracks, as
well as a CISO panel.  Find out more about our speakers here.

* Seating is limited to 300 attendees (all sessions are general admission).

Registration opens September 1st 6:00 am EDT:
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/2015-ny-metro-joint-cyber-security-conference-registration-17637587499

Please note: As part of our mission to provide educational services as
a non-profit organization, registration fees are only to cover the
costs of the facility, food and refreshments.

NYMJCSC details can be found at the nymjcsc.org website.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact
info at nymjcsc.org or your Long Island Leaders at
longislandleaders at owasp.org.
