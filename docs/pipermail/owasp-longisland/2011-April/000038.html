Hello,



OWASP is a not-for-profit worldwide charitable organization.  Everyone is
free to participate in OWASP and all its materials are available under a
free and open software license.  There are over 150 active projects.  The
OWASP website has been visited for more than a quarter million times in just
the month of March.  Currently there are 1,289 individual members.  The
membership funds various OWASP projects as well as supports local chapters.
If you join or renew your membership today, you will be able to vote
in the upcoming
OWASP board election<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership/2011Election#2011_OWASP_Global_Board_of_Directors_Election_Timeline>
.



If you want to become a Long Island chapter member then please click
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Long_Island or contact *helen.gao at owasp.org*



If you want to become a New York/New Jersey chapter member then please click
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/NYNJMetro.  If you join before May 10 then
you will be entered to a raffle to win a $500 gift certificate.  Contact
kelly.santalucia at owasp.org 973-670-5784 for details.



40% of your donation will go to the local chapter you choose.



Thank you!
