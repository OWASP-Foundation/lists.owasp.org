Good afternoon All,

I'm happy to see the Long Island Chapter of OWASP is growing!
My client here in Long Island is looking to bring on a security engineer who specializes in vulnerability management.
We are looking for someone that has a knowledge of vulnerability scanning tools like WhiteHat, Veracode, and/or Qualys.
My client has a team off site doing the scans and is looking for someone that can analysis the scans and create solutions.
My client has tripled the amount of applications in their environment and has already brought on 2 engineers.
We are looking for one more engineer that can handle the task at hand.
The person needs to have experience with both application and network security best practices and solid understanding of OWASP Top Ten.

I'm leaning on you to possible ping your networks and see if anyone is even passively entertaining new opportunities.
