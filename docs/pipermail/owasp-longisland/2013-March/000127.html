Hi all,

Below is a message from Tom Brennan, the organizer of AppSecUSA 02013.

Helen Gao

The call for volunteers for OWASP AppSecUSA 2013 (www.appsecusa.org) will
be closing shortly (March 15th) If you have not already done so, now is a
good time to review what is available and if you want to help out and
attend the event at no charge a *$600 value*, now is the time to register
for a primary and a secondary task.

http://sl.owasp.org/appsecusa13_volunteer_jobs


• Once you are signed up, you will be contacted for important information
on your task (team, budget, milestones) this will happen this month and
then your off and running!!

The team has also been requesting online feedback on the upcoming Website
Design, Keynotes, Events, Training, Sessions Topics and with a *vote of the
community* we can see what rises to the top very quickly as we all work on
bringing together builders, breakers and defenders from around the world.
To do this 1x task its very simple, visit the conference website:
http://www.appsecusa.org  scroll down ½ way on the page and jump into the
forums or the big "LETS HEAR IT" button.

Thank you in advance for your volunteerism to the software security
community -- that is what it is really all about!

 Tom Brennan
