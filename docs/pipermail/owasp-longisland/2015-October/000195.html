Specialists,

In the spirit of sharing and working with our partners in various
areas of the industry, we are delighted to pass along the announcement
for the Cloud Security Alliance Summit NYC taking place in the month
of October.

=======================

Subject: CSA Summit NYC Wednesday, 10/28 - Quickly approaching! -
https://csacongress.org/event/summit-nyc-2015/

The Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) New York Chapter is delighted to
host a CSA Summit event at the Executive Conference Center (1601
Broadway) in Manhattan on Wednesday, October 28. This “must attend”
event brings together industry practitioners and security luminaries
for keynotes and panel discussions to discuss cloud security lessons
learned by financial services and other industries. It is a fantastic
opportunity to hear from thought leaders, network with your peers, and
earn some CPE credits. The event theme is “Enterprise Lessons Learned
in Cloud Security” and includes a keynote by Vinay Patel (Director -
Global Head of Information Security - Citi Technology) along with
speakers from ADP, Adobe, Amazon, CBS Corporation, GuidePoint
Security, Rackspace, Omni Media Group, Cloud Security Alliance and
more, as they share their experiences in managing security in cloud
computing during this one-day program.

The CSA Summit New York has a modest cost of $25 and you can register
to attend by clicking this link -
https://csacongress.org/event/summit-nyc-2015/#registration


CCSK Foundation Training (Provided by HP Educational Services)

On Thursday, October 29, 2015, an optional CCSK training course is
available for the Certificate of Cloud Security Knowledge (CCSK)
[https://cloudsecurityalliance.org/education/ccsk/#_overview ]

The CCSK credential helps you validate your competence and demonstrate
expertise in the cloud. Improve your chances of passing the CCSK exam
and gain hands-on access to cloud systems to apply best practices!
