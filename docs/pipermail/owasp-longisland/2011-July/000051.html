Dear Long Island folks,

The OWASP board election is around the corner.  If you have paid your
membership fee before 30-June-2011, or if you are a qualified honorary
member, then you are eligibly to vote.  You will receive a confirmation
email on 19-July-2011

out who is running, how to vote and your membership status.  If you have
