Hi all,

We are proud to organize the 4th *University Challenge* at the
AppSec-EU/Research!

Do you want to compete your hacking skills with teams from other
Universities?
The University Challenge will be hold during the training days, August 20th
and 21st.

*The University Challenge is a free event at the OWASP AppSec conference!*

Teams should consist of students from an university or university of
applied science.Don't have a team at your institution, feel free to team-up
with students from other universities!

For more information and registration, see: https://appsec.eu/uni-challenge/
