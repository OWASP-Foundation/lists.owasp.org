### Einladung zum 49. Münchner OWASP Stammtisch am 15.04.2014 um 19:00 Uhr im "Hackerhaus"

### Thema: "How the NSA activities affect our daily life?" von Dr. Gregor Kuznik

"The past of the internet is the most important reason why weakness right from the design or planning stage keeps privacy and security at a low level and why there's neither governmental nor commercial interest to change state of things. The fundamental question is 'Does it impact anybodies life?' Let us suppose that you never use any electronic devices you don't need to think about. In my opinion nobody can ignore the threat caused by data collection and data mining."

Ich für meinen Teil bin schon sehr (vor-)gespannt :-)
