Betreff: 	Discount for OWASP Members for the Upcoming (ISC)2 Security Congress EMEA Event


Hello Chapter Leaders of Germany!

OWASP has formed a partnership with (ISC)2 Security Congress EMEA 2015.  The information below has been provided to me by Security Congress.  Please share this with your chapter members and community.

If you have any questions, please let me know.
==============================================================

Dear OWASP Chapter Leaders,



I’m delighted to announce the Second Annual (ISC)² Security Congress EMEA 2015, developed by (ISC)^2  in partnership with MIS Training Institute.As a Supporting Association of the Congress, OWASP Members receive a *10% discount* from the standard rates advertised.



The Congress programme will showcase current thinking and offer invaluable education to all levels of information security and professionals, a unique chance to keep up with the latest trends and emerging issues. Attendees will leave the event further equipped with tools to strengthen security whilst enabling the business. Find out more: http://emeacongress.isc2.org/



*Keynote speakers for 2015 include: *

·       *Alexander Nouak*, Head of the Competence Center "Identification and Biometrics", Fraunhofer Institute for Computer Graphics Research IGD and Co-founder and Chair, European Association for Biometrics (Germany)

·       *Andy Jones*, CISO, Maersk Line (UK)

·       *Bruce Hallas*, Founder, The Analogies Project (UK)

·       *David Jacoby*, Senior Security Researcher, Kaspersky Lab (Sweden)

·       *Dr. Ciarán Mc Mahon,** *Research & Development Co-coordinator, RCSI CyberPsychology Research Centre, RCSI Institute of Leadership, Royal College of Surgeons (Ireland)

·       *Dr. Harald Niggemann*, Cyber Security Strategist, Federal Office for Information Security (BSI) (Germany)

·       *Mary P. Kirwan,** *Head of Financial Crime, Allied Irish Bank, Dublin (Ireland)

·       *Dr. Ing. Michael Spreizenbarth*, Team Lead, IT Security Consultant, Siemens CERT (Germany)

·       *Dr. Sally Leivesley,** *Managing Director, Newrisk Limited (UK)

·       *Gianluca Moroni,** *ICT & Field Specialist (Italy)



*Top Reasons to Attend:*

·       10+ Keynote Speakers Confirmed for Congress 2015

·       Front-line insights from EMEA's largest information security membership

·       Educational programme offering board review of current industry concern

·       A unique opportunity to bring together professionals at all levels

·       Build on training and event management experience of two industry leaders; (ISC)² and MISTI

·       Networking Opportunities with information security professionals from the EMEA region representing various industries
