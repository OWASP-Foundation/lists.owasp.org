(sorry for speaking English here but as some folks
respond in English...)

This is a standard mailman list, so everybody can manage
his/her subscription by themself. Therefore you can find
in the bottom of each mail originating from this
list a link.

Do the subscribers a favor to be careful to whom you are
responding to as owasp-germany at lists.owasp.org is a couple of
hundred people. Please no sales stuff. Your mail will also be
archived publicly: http://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/owasp-germany/ .

If you need help: You can reach the admins of this list under
owasp-germany-owner at lists.owasp.org .
