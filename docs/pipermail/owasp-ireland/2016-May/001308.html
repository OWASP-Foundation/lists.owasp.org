Hello All,

I'd like to mark the successful event last night organised primarily by our
new chapter lead Tony Clarke, if I may.
It was probably the best chapter event ever held (in the chapters 10 year
history) with a high proportion of female attendees. - very cool!!
Keynote speaker stories of "how they got here" were an inspiration.

It was also great to see all the new faces/committee members "mucking in"
and helping run the event.

The event was streamed on facebook, twitter was very active, The event was
also recorded which I think should take centre stage on the OWASP.org site.
