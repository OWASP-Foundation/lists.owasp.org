ISACA Ireland have released a 'call for papers' for their annual conference
on 11th November in Croke Park. This year the theme is “cultivating a
culture of security” and they are seeking skilled, knowledgeable and
accomplished speakers!

We'd encourage submissions from the OWASP community as much of the work
OWASP is doing, such as Security Shepherd, assists in "cultivating a
culture of security" within organisations and teams.
