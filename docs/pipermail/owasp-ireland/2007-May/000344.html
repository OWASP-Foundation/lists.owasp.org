Its worthwhile for  your career to get involved in these open source
initiatives. OWASP work such as the OWASP Top 10 is used by SANS and
also
part of section 6 of the PCI DSS standard. Many of the Security tool
vendors
have adopted the OWASP Top 10 as a policy which can be run (even though
they
are not certified to do so, we don't certify).
Another example is the Testing guide is used by some of the Big 4 firms
and
also some government bodies in the USA and Europe. Previous participants
have found it rewarding to contribute to standards which have become
widely
adopted by industry.
