Hi Everyone,

Thanks to everyone who came to the OWASP Dublin session yesterday evening.
We had some excellent sessions from Simon Singh, Professor Mike Scott,
Candid Wueest & Patrick Fitzgerald focusing on various aspects of
Cryptography and we hope you enjoyed it. It was fantastic to see an Enigma
machine and learn a new more efficient way of long multiplication!

Without sponsorship we can't hold events such as this, so we'd like to
especially thank the following organisations:

   - Ward Solutions
   - edgescan
   - Island Networks
   - Integrity360
   - Asystec Data Management
   - Kontex Security
   - Symantec
   - Morgan McKinley
   - Dublin Institute of Technology, School of Computing.


Feel free to let us know what you thought of the event and to suggest
events you would like to see. We will make some video of yesterdays event
available in the coming days.

Enjoy the weekend.

Thanks,

Tony
