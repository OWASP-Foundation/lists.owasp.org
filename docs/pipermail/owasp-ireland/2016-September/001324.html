Did you purchase your AppSecUSA 2016 ticket yet? Ticket prices increase by

To purchase a ticket, visit the AppSecUSA 2016 registration page here:
https://2016.appsecusa.org/buy/
<http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/42b9b8b801/2f2274a77e/fc80c048b6>


Here is a glimpse at our exciting speaker line-up:


   -

   Matthew Green
   <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/42b9b8b801/2f2274a77e/291328f488>
   , respected cryptographer and security technologist with over fifteen
   years of industry experience in computer security. Dr. Green is an
   Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Johns Hopkins Information
   Security Institute.



   -

   Samy Kamkar
   <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/42b9b8b801/2f2274a77e/11efc07136>
   , privacy and security researcher, computer hacker, whistle blower and
   entrepreneur. At the age of 16, Mr. Kamkar co-founded Fonality. He is
   possibly best known for creating and releasing the fastest spreading virus
   of all time, the MySpace worm Samy.



   -

   Joe Jarzombek
   <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/42b9b8b801/2f2274a77e/e05bf71f62>
   , former Director for Software Assurance in the National Cyber Security
   Division of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS). He led
   government inter-agency efforts with industry, academia, and standards
   organizations to shift the security paradigm away from patch management.



   -

   Dan Geer
   <http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/42b9b8b801/2f2274a77e/2dc0ca86e2>
   , CISO for In-Q-Tel. Mr. Geer was a key contributor to the development
   of the X Window System, as well as the Kerberos authentication protocol
   while a member of the Athena Project at MIT in the 1980s. Shortly after, he
   created the first information security consulting firm on Wall Street.
