I have to agree, great conference - well done Eoin and everyone else 
involved in organising it.

I would agree on the time slippage, the best system I've seen (and I 
think you started doing it later in the day, happened on my talk anyway) 
was that at defcon. The goon's signal when you have 15, 10 and 5 minutes 
left and then finally when you have none left. Q+A is then in a 
different room so the turnaround time between talks is reduced. I had 
several people contact me today saying they wanted to ask questions but 
we had to stop people asking any more after about 4 of them because of 
the time issue.

Other than that I couldn't fault it :)

Dave
