 *Microsoft & OWASP Dublin Anti-Malware event* March 22nd 2018 6:30pm –
Save The Date One Microsoft Place
​

When it comes to malware, 1 million new pieces of malware are created each
day. According to the 2017 Verizon Data Breach Investigations Report,
malware accounts for 51 percent of breaches.

OWASP Dublin are delighted to have partnered with Microsoft Ireland to host
the next meeting in Microsoft Irelands EMEA HQ building in Leopardstown on
March 22nd. Sessions include:

·         Malware Reverse Engineering – Eoin Ward

·         Malware on Counterfeit Phones – John O’Brien

The *first 50 registrants* will have an opportunity to get a tour of the
new Microsoft campus. Tours to start at 6 pm, and every 5 minutes until
6.30 as it takes about 1 hour to complete a tour of the campus.

The OWASP Dublin team are very excited about the meeting and speakers and
hope to see you there.

*A registration link will be sent next week.*

*Directions*

The Microsoft building is situated:

https://www.google.ie/maps/place/Microsoft+Ireland/@53.2689258,-6.1999248,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x486708597c396aa5:0x36d91d8b0f05f64e!8m2!3d53.2689258!4d-6.1977361

*By public transport:*

Take the LUAS to the Central Park stop and the Microsoft building is a
short walk (see above map)

https://www.luas.ie/central-park.html

*By car*

Take J14 off the M50 and head towards Leopardstown racecourse, follow signs
for Microsoft near the racecourse.
