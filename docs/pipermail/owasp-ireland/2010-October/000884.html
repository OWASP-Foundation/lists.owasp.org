Acquirer Systems is an Irish software company and provider of Card Payment
Test, Certification and Security solutions are having a Card Payments and
Security Conference on the 11th November 2010 at the Clarion Hotel, IFSC,
Dublin 1.
In the morning, they will focus on Card payment industry topics (not
security related) and on Card Data / PCI Security topics in the afternoon.

The topics are: Data Security, Authentication Solutions, End-End Encryption
and Tokenization for PCI compliance.

The event is free and open to interested parties to attend. If you wish to
attend, please register your interest in advance by replying to the email
address:

conference2010 at acquirer.com
