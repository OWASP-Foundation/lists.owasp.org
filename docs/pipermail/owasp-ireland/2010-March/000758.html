Just to follow on with a more serious reply than the one I gave earlier, 
for us its not a lack of investing in people or a bad economy. It's the 
opposite really, we have so much work on we can't really afford to have 
the developers and security staff not work 5 hours (leave the office at 
roughly 1pm to get the meeting on time) once per month if that is how 
often the meetings are going to be. If all of our developers and 
security staff came along we would lose 60 hours per meeting and that is 
something we cannot afford to do.

I think it does have a knock on effect like Mark says, if people can't 
come to chapter meetings will they get involved with OWASP at all 
(contributing to projects, attending conferences, spreading the OWASP 
message etc)?

Dave
