Folks,

Firstly, thanks for allowing IRISS to present at the Owasp Chapter
meeting last night and also for the positive feedback.

As requested, please find below instructions for how to sign-up to
IRISS to receive the 'Vulnerability Alerts & Warnings' messages from
IRISS.

1. Go to https://www.iriss.ie/FWA5EIRE/Resources/Signup.aspx .
2. Fill in the relevant information in the various fields and click 'Submit'.
3. The IRISS handler on duty will then receive an email requesting a
login. We will then enable a login for you.

More information on IRISS can be found at the homepage - http://www.iriss.ie.

With your new login, you will be able to receive the alerts via email
or RSS. You will also be able to login to the site to take part in the
Discussion Boards, view the Operator Blogs etc.

Last night's presentation will hopefully be on the Owasp Ireland
website tomorrow.

If you have any questions or if there's a problem with the links,
please drop me or info at iriss.ie an email.

Cheers, Mark (IRISS Incident Handler)

P.S. If anyone on the OWASP mailing list would like to offer help,
knowledge (particularly your Layer 7 expertise), suggestions,
assistance to IRISS in our fight as a CERT....please step forward, it
would be greatly appreciated :)
