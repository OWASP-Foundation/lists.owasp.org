The key point there is "certified" which is dangerous imho.
Certification pending a full blown pentest may be a better idea so "real
security" can be somewhat addressed?
OWASP has the ASVS document which is attempting to addrress levels of
security which is worth looking at.

PCI is "certification to trade" and in my opinion should be in addition to
other efforts to secure company and client assets :)
