Yesterday, at the Web 2.0 Summit, Google’s CEO Eric Schmidt presented the 
Nexus S. This wasn’t just about a new phone, he also talked about how 
Gingerbread (Android OS Version 2.3) would have support for NFC (Near 
Field Communications) built in. In light of the mobile-phone hotel-key 
(NFC-based) trial going on at the Clarion hotel in Sweden and questions 
raised during the conversation at the Summit, it’s time to take another 
look at the state of NFC security.

Full article here:
