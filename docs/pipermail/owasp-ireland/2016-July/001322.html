Hi Everyone,

Thanks to everyone who who came and spoke at the OWASP & ISACA session on
Threat Intelligence yesterday evening. We had a great turn out and
hopefully you found the sessions from Darktrace, Symantec & iSIGHT partners
interesting. They all have very different approaches to Threat Intelligence
and some interesting incidents and cyber criminal groups were discussed.

Attached is Kevin's presentation from iSIGHT partners and you can see his
talk on the URL below and we've attached some pictures.

https://www.facebook.com/owaspireland/videos/1271956962836757/

The OWASP Dublin chapter also had a session on cybersecurity with HP
enterprise yesterday morning. The session was very well received. A big
thank you to Brendan Gormley, Mick Ryan & Denise Murtagh for all the
preparation and work they put into this session.

The next OWASP speaking event is on September 22nd in DIT (Kevin Street)
where Simon Singh, Candid Wueest, Mike Scott, Patrick Fitzgerald and
Stephen Moran will be speaking about how cryptography can be leveraged for
good and by criminals. Please feel free to register and invite anyone you
feel who may be interested.

https://www.eventbrite.com/e/cryptography-the-science-of-secrets-tickets-26337059855

Thanks to our sponsors for their continued support as without them we can't
hold these types of events.
