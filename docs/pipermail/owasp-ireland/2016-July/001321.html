Hi Everyone,

Gender diversity is currently a hot topic for cyber security, as the
numbers of women continue to decline each year.  Jane Frankland (
https://about.me/jane_frankland), whom some of you may know, is determined
to do something about it. Jane spoke at the last OWASP Dublin event in May.

Jane is aiming to reverse the trend and is in the process of writing a book
about it. Concentrating on 5-key challenges, the book includes data and
stories from cyber security professionals around the world. Jane is looking
for the cybersecurity community to take part in the initiative by
completing and sharing these surveys. There are only 10 questions so it's
really fast to complete.

   - The WOMEN-only link - https://www.surveymonkey.co.uk/r/3CGKZ6W
   - The MEN-only link - https://www.surveymonkey.co.uk/r/R2BXNC8


If you would like to find out more, please email her at
hello at jane-frankland.com or learn more about the book http://bit.ly/1Wq9R0Y

Many Thanks,

Tony
