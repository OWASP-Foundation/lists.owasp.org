*OWASP Ireland Limerick Day* *2013* will take place at Limerick Institute
of Technology on October 31st from 9:00am to 17:30pm.


*OWASP Ireland Limerick Day 2013*

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Limerick_Day_2013



*
**When:* 31/10/2013 9:00am - 17:30pm

*Where:* Limerick Institute of Technology, Moylish Park, Limerick

This free event is open to all participants with an interest in application
security.

https://owasp-limerick-conference-day-2013.eventbrite.ie/


For ISACA and (ISC)² members: This event qualifies for free CPE
credits/hours.
