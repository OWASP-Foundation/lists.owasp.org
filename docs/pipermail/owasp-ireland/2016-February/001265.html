Hi Everyone,

Thanks to everybody who attended the Dublin chapter meeting yesterday and
we hope you found it intersting. We'd like to thank both Gavin and Alain
for taking the time to present and for their informative presentations.

To summarise for anyone who couldn't attend.

Gavin O'Gorman from Symantec gave an excellent talk on malware and
techniques that are currently being employed in the wild. Alain Abou Tass
from Ward Solutions gave a presentation on web application penetration
techniques and how combining vulnerabilities can result in a greater
aggregated risk to organisations. Both sessions prompted interesting
discussions from the group. Pizza, beer and soft drinks were provided by
our generous hosts Morgan McKinley <http://www.morganmckinley.ie/>.

Alain's presentations is on the wiki at the URL below.
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Ireland-Dublin

We'll be sending out information shortly on the next chapter meeting
shortly and we hope you can join us next time.

Many Thanks,

Tony
