Hi all,

I am happy to announce that edgescan, in conjunction with the IISF (Irish
Information Security Forum), are holding a free Ruby on Rails secure
development workshop on April 6th in Dublin city centre.

I know it's a bit away still but places will be limited so if you are
interested, please reserve your place quickly! The workshop will cover many
of the most common application security vulnerabilities and additional
considerations for developers when building applications in Ruby. The
workshop will be lead by Owen Mooney, head of development at edgescan.

Eventbrite link as below:

https://www.eventbrite.ie/e/secure-ruby-on-rails-development-for-the-cloud-tickets-20842524552

*WHEN*
Wednesday, April 6, 2016 from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM

*WHERE*
Buswells Hotel Dublin IE

Hope to see you there!
