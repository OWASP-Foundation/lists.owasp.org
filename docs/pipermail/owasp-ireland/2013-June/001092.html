We are delighted to announce that the OWASP EU Tour is coming to Dublin.

We have a 1 day training course on offer as well as a free conference day.
Dates are June 25th for the training and June 26th for the talks.

Speakers include:
David Rook - The Realex Payments Application Security Story
Paco Hope - The Building Security In Maturity Model (BSIMM)
Mark Goodwin - Using the browser as a platform for security tools
Robert McArdle - Lesson learned from the trenches of targeted attack
Eoin Keary

Defensive Programming training will be provided by Paco Hope

Full details are available here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/EUTour2013
