Hi all,

Microsoft have released an optimisation for secure development life
cycles. It has 4 levels of maturity and has a self assessment guide
included in the download. You can get the optimisation model here:
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/security/dd221356.aspx

They have also released a new version of the Threat Modeling tool
(http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/security/cc421514.aspx) which rather
annoyingly requires Visio 2007 to be installed on your desktop! The tool
does look a lot better than the old one.
