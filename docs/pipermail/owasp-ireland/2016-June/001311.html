Hi Everyone,

We'd like to let you know some of the things going on with the OWASP Dublin team.

The OWASP Dublin chapter recently had an open call for volunteers and have refreshed the board. We now have 27 people on the Dublin committee with the roles below. Many thanks to Owen Pendlebury, Mark Denihan, Fabio Cerullo, Eoin Keary and everyone else for their work over the years. We're delighted that Owen Pendlebury and Eoin Keary continue to be involved with the chapter.

Tony Clarke - Chapter Lead
Tony is a Director of Information Security in ICON and a lecturer in Ethical hacking in DIT.

Myles Cullen - Treasurer
Myles has a number of years experience in start-up companies in Ireland and is currently working on an M.Sc in Digital investigations.

Denise Murtagh - Secretary
Denise is currently working on the security team in PwC and has a M.Sc. in security & Forensic computing from DCU.

Antonio Stano - PR/Comms
Antonio has a wealth of experience in cybercrime investigation and penetration testing and is working as a Security Consultant for Ward Solutions.

Anyone else who wants to be involved or help out at any level just let us know! We've some very exiting plans for the future.

OWASP Dublin News
We'll have videos of our last meeting available soon. Thanks to everyone who took part and came to our event on "Attracting & retaining women in cybersecurity". If you've never been to an OWASP meeting, you can see what events are like from https://youtu.be/zB8p-sJcsso https://www.facebook.com/owaspireland/

Feel free to post on the Facebook page, talk to us on twitter (@OWASPDublin or @appsecIreland) or email on the distribution list.
You can sign up for the distribution list here:
https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-ireland

Upcoming Events
We have an exciting "Threat Intelligence" session that we are running with ISACA on 28th July and will be releasing the details next week or so. On the night we'll have speakers from Darktrace and iSIGHT partners (FireEye).

Please feel free to share this email with any friends or colleagues who may be interested in OWASP and software security.
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Ireland-Dublin

We hope you're enjoying the sunshine, the long weekend and see you soon!
