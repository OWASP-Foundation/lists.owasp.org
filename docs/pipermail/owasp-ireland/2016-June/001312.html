Hi Senan,

Excellent, the more the merrier! We welcome anyone who wants to get involved with the Dublin chapter at any level, regardless of background. We aim to be an inclusive group and with the last event we had 20+ people who contributed to organising it and many more people attending outside of the security industry. Hopefully that comes across here.
https://youtu.be/bYV5bnN03MY

You're going to see more activity from the Dublin chapter in terms of outreach, training, partnership & collaboration with other groups, themed events, improving engagement with OWASP locally, as well as working to get keynote speakers to Dublin that people may not otherwise see. We believe we can make a difference locally and be the model for other OWASP chapters. 

As a not-for-profit group, sponsorship and people like yourself who are willing to give up their time and expertise are key to the chapter achieving this vision.

It's exciting times for the chapter!

Many Thanks,

Tony
