--Credit Card Security Standard Takes Effect

The Payment Card Industry standard, which requires that merchants who
conduct credit card transactions comply with certain criteria for
handling the data, went into effect in early July.  The standard, which
is backed by Visa U.S.A. Inc. and MasterCard International Inc., has
raised concerns that many of those who will be required to abide by it
do not possess the resources to implement the necessary changes and that
some are unclear about what they need to do to comply with the
standards.
