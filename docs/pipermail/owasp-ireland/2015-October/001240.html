I am delighted to announce that Tony Clarke is joining the OWASP Dublin chapter
board. Tony has been a stalwart in the local community promoting security
awareness over the last number of years. We are delighted to have Tony on

things to the community.

bigger. We now have chapters in Dublin, Limerick, Cork and Belfast.
