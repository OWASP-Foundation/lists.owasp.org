Cyberlytic is recruiting now, looking for someone who is a skilled Java developer to work in Belfast with a strong cyber security understanding especially hacking web applications.

As a full-time member of the founding team, you will have broad responsibilities for developing Cyberlytic software products. This will primarily focus on leading and executing software development of our first product, the Cyber Threat Profiler (CTP). You will also be required to input and deliver into our technology roadmap, which will include turning research projects into software reality to add to our ambitious portfolio of security intelligence products.

We are a startup company, with our technical team based in Northern Ireland and ambitions to grow globally. We will need you to advise and take ownership of enhancing our development environments in order to scale and adapt to our customer demands quickly. We want you to be an important member of our team with a voice in the direction of the company.

To succeed in this role you will need at a minimum:
• To be an expert Java developer.
• To design prototype applications, indicate program unit structure, and coordinate application plans with the development team or client.
• Interest in Cyber Security
A job description is available on our website 

https://www.cyberlytic.com/careers/lead-software-developer/#
 If anyone is interested please contact careers at cyberlytic.com.
