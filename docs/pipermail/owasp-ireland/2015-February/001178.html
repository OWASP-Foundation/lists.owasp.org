Dear all,

It took us some time, but I am proud to announce our list of confirmed
speakers, trainers and round table chairs:

* Pravir Chandra, Bloomberg
* Michael Craigue, HP
* Justin Clarke, Gotham Digital Science
* Yan Kravchenko, NetSpi
* Sebastien Deleersnyder, Toreon
* Bart De Win, PWC
*¨Kuai Hinojosa, McAfee Foundstone
* Jerry Hoff, WhiteHat Security

The SAMM User Day on 27 March is now open for registration!
Registration link:
https://www.regonline.com/owaspsammsummit2015
(cost is 150 EUR + VAT)

The venue is the Gibson Hotel, in Dublin, Ireland
At the end of the user day we will organize a social event (not included in
the registration fee).

More details and - soon - the detailed schedule are available on
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_SAMM_Summit_2015

I look forward to see you all in Dublin for our first OWASP SAMM Summit
where we will publish SAMM v1.1 !

The SAMM User day will be followed by the SAMM Project day on 28-March,
this is free of charge & requires SAMM experience and active participation
in the SAMM project as volunteer/participant.

We have some exciting announcements in the pipeline, keep an eye on your
email inbox and/or follow us on twitter: https://twitter.com/owaspsamm
