FREE Pass to the Cyber Threat Summit worth €495 - Only to members of OWASP Ireland

I am delighted to announce that our good friends at the ICTTF have agreed to offer members of OWASP Ireland FREE entry to the Cyber Threat Summit at the Grand Hotel in Malahide in September 2012. 

For more information and to register, please visit http://www.cyberthreatsummit.com/ and be sure to enter promotional code: "owaspireland" when booking your place to receive your free entry worth €495. 

Additional Information: 

Following last years hugely successful event, ICTTF are proud to announce an enhanced event this year over two days on the 20th and 21st September 2012. 

The event will be a conference and exhibition. The syllabus will be delivered by over 20 of the world’s leading cyber security experts with a specific European perspective. 

A cross industry master class developed for senior executives to understand the developments, strategies and best practice in cyber security. 

Learn to understand the types of threats, potential impacts, motivational factors and trends to observe in cyber security. 

Review best practice in protecting your organisation and develop appropriate cyber defence strategies. 

Network with your industry peers and discuss the latest cyber security technologies in the marketplace. 

Nearly 200 places were booked in the first 48 hours. There is a strong international contingent travelling for the event and it will be a significant opportunity for companies to promote their offerings. 


Background on ICTTF 

The ICTTF International Cyber Threat Task Force is a not for profit group comprised of over 2,000 members representing over 100 countries. The objective of the group is to collaborate, share, educate and assist each other in dealing with all types of cyber threats and make the internet a safer place for all. 

Last year, the ICTTF ran a hugely successful event “Cyber Threat Summit” at the Convention Centre Dublin. This year the event is expanded to a 2 day event with over 20 international speakers covering 5 concurrent tracks on 3 main themes: 

• Cloud Computing 
• Social Media 
• Mobility - Smartphones 

This major event will have a European perspective and sectors covered include: 

• Banking – Financial 
• Transport 
• Critical National Infrastructure 
• Defence 
• Government 
• Energy – Utilities 
• Legal 
• Telecommunications
