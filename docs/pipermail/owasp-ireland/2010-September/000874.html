On Wednesday 20th October from 2pm, we will be hosting a series of talks 
on the many aspects of developing in the Clouds at the NCI Campus in the 
IFSC Dublin. Topics to be covered include Scalability, Architecture, 
Virtualisation/Inter-Operability, Software Builds, Monitoring and 
Analytics in the Cloud. 

These talks will focus on the potential strengths and weaknesses of Cloud 
Development, together with the practical experiences of the speakers. 

Guest speakers include Microsoft, IBM, Facebook, Cloudsplit, 
TradeFacilitate, Calom Technologies, Cloud Consulting and many other 
leading experts in this arena. 

This is a free to attend event, however you must register at 
http://www.eurocloud.ie/developing-in-the-cloud
