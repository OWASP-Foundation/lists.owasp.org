Hi Everyone,

In recognition of the excellent students UCD has had previously from the OWASP community, UCD is offering Dublin chapter members a waiver of their application fee for applications for September 2018.
 
Anyone interested in this M.Sc. can find out more at the UCD Open Evening on Wednesday 4 April, when there will be a special info session on this programme at 530pm. Dr Pavel Gladyshev from UCD will talk to  the group about UCD's M.Sc. programmes in this field which include the M.Sc. in Digital Investigation and Forensic Computing for computer science graduates and IT professionals in security or digital forensics roles (available part-time by distance learning or full-time in UCD) and the M.Sc. in Forensic Computing & Cybercrime Investigation (part-time distance learning programme for law enforcement).
 
Please see:
https://www.myucd.ie/visiting-ucd/events/ucd-science-postgraduate-open-evening/
 
Kind Regards,
