Hi Folks

A reminder that registration for IRISSCERT's Cyber Crime conference on November 2014 is open. There is a great line up of speakers including Raj Samani from MCafee, Joshua Goldfarb from Fireeye, Andrew Hay from OpenDNS, and a speaker from Europol's Cybercrime Centre (EC3).  Our cyber security challenge is also being held this year in parallel to the conference and is hosted by the Irish Honeynet Project.  The IRISSCERT Cyber Crime Conference is Ireland's premier conference and offers a great opportunity to network with your peers. More details on the conference is available at http://iriss.ie/iriss/IRISSCON.html and you can register at www.irisscert2014.eventbrite.com  

Also for those of you who are CISSPs this conference is valid for CPEs
