*Description:*
As Application Security Engineer, you will be part of an outstanding team
of security professionals with deep experience in security engineering.
 You will be responsible for driving security into Workday's platform and
ensuring that our software and services are designed and implemented with
security in mind.  Workday is looking for a junior to mid-level security
engineer who has strong analytical skills and can think like an attacker.
You should be familiar with the OWASP Top 10, and also possess practical
knowledge of network and systems security.

*Responsibilities:*
Review design documents, threat models created by development teams
Perform security audits of Workday platform features and supporting
infrastructure
Provide recommendations for hardening environments
Consult with product teams to provide security best practices, secure
design patterns
Analyze incidents and security review findings to determine security impact
*
Qualifications:*
BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience
1-2 years working in an information security discipline
Ability to work on multiple projects at a time
Capable of understanding vulnerabilities and recommending solutions
Strong knowledge of Web technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, HTTP
Fluent in at least one programming or scripting language
