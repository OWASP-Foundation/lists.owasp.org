Hi All,

OWASP in conjunction with a number of security groups are running a free
conference on the 8th of October. Please see below for more information.
Hope to see you all there.

DAGGERCON is a new and upcoming computer security conference.

WHEN
Thursday, October 8, 2015 from 09:30 to 16:30

WHERE
Building 6, IBM Technology Campus, Mulhuddart, Dublin 15


WHO IS IT FOR?

Daggercon  is  for  people  seeking  insights,  staff  and solutions in the
information security space.
It’s  for  people who want to hear what their peers and other major players
are  doing  to  address  the  challenges  and opportunities in this rapidly
growing area.
And it’s for those who want to get a handle on what Ireland has to offer in
cybersecurity — because they’ll all be at Daggercon.


WHY SHOULD YOU ATTEND?

There's  something  for  everyone  at  Daggercon - from cutting edge talks,
hands-on  workshops,  tech  demos, a challenging Capture-The-Flag event and
plenty of relaxed networking opportunities.

COMPANIES
You need to know how to protect your data and your brand, and keep track on
where the market is going.

C-LEVELS
You  need to know how to manage and mitigate the risks associated with data
breaches.

SECURITY PROFESSIONALS
You   need   to   know  the  latest  techniques,  technologies  and  attack
methodologies.

DEVELOPERS
You  need  to  know  how  the leading edge looks to improve your software's
security posture.

ACADEMICS
You need to know where the research opportunities are.

GRADUATES
You need to know what cybersecurity has to offer and who’s hiring.


SIGN UP

Tickets to Daggercon are FREE, but there is limited availability.

•       Entrance to Talks, Workshops and Demos
•       Food and Refreshments
•       Security Swag
•       Parking
