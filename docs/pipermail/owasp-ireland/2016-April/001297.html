Hi All

Please see the below SecureCloud information. OWASP members get a 10%
discount using "SCIRSPCSA".

SecureCloud is the only European conference focused exclusively on
cloud security and aims to provide an opportunity for government
experts, industry experts, and corporate decision makers to discuss
and exchange ideas about how to shape the future of cloud computing
security.

An initial line up of featured speakers include:
•       “Securing the Cloud of Tomorrow” by Raj Samani, VP and CTO for
McAfee EMEA
•       “Privacy & Security in the Cloud: Customer Rights and
Governments’ Lawful Access to Data” by John Frank, Vice President, EU
Government Affairs at Microsoft
•       "Understanding the Crux - Abuse of Cloud Storage Services for
Targeted Cyber Attacks" by Aditya K. Sood, Director of Security and
Cloud Threat Labs at Elastica of Blue Coat Systems
•        “Flying Through a Cloudy Sky” by Michaela Iorga, Senior
Security Technical Lead for Cloud Computing at NIST Computer Security
division

Additional featured presentations will also be given by Laura Koetzle,
Vice President and General Manager‬, Forrester Research; Vinay Patel,
Global Head of Information Security Risk Management at Citi Technology
Infrastructure; and Jim Reavis, Co-founder and CEO of the Cloud
Security Alliance.

A complete agenda for SecureCloud can be found at
https://csacongress.org/event/securecloud-2016/#agenda

The following discount code SCIRSPCSA will provide a 10% discount for
OWASP Ireland members
