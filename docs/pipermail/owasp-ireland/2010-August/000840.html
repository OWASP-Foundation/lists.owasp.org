Dear all,

Thanks for coming to the event and congratulations to the lucky winners of 
the Ipod Touch raffles: Juan Galiana & Niall O'Farrell.

Also, a big thanks to Cathal Courtney and John Marmelstein for providing 
an interesting agenda, with interactive sessions and high quality 
presentations.

As usual, the slides will be available shortly in our OWASP Ireland wiki: 
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Ireland 

For those who are interested in receiving a certificate of attendance 
please send me an email and I will send it to you. From now on, the 
certificates will be delivered through e-mail in pdf format to embrace 
environmentally friendly practices.

Our next meeting will be the unmissable OWASP Ireland 2010 Conference next 
17th September. A number of key figures from industry will provide some 
unique insight into the latest trends, threats and methodologies in the 
world of application security.

You could find all the details including registration fees, location, etc 
in the url below:

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_IRELAND_2010 

And just to make things more interesting while approaching the event, we 
are launching the "Countdown challenges: Win a Ticket to OWASP Ireland 
2010"!!

A challenge will be posted on the conference wiki page every friday up 
until the event. The winner of each challenge gets a free entrance ticket 
to the conference.

Stay tuned,

Fabio
