Hello all,

AppSec Europe <https://2014.appsec.eu/> is returning to the United Kingdom
in 2014, *from the 23rd to the 26th of June*.

Hosted in Cambridge at Anglia Ruskin University, conveniently located near
the ?Silicon Fen? of Cambridge, the leading Tech Hub in Europe, and near
the historical centre of Cambridge, the conference planning team are hard
at work to bring you:

   - Two days of Training and two day conference.
   - Three tracks, focusing on the core OWASP mission (Builder, Breaker,
   Defender), with an added Research track.
   - Keynote addresses by highly respected Industry experts.
   - Exhibit area offering solutions to your problems.
   - And much more...
