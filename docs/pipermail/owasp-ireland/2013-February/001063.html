Ever wonder what a CTF is, or ever been curious to have a go - but have no
idea where to start? If so, this session is perfect for you. Catering for
all skill levels, the Irish honeynet team have a built a CTF to help
encourage beginners to play and also challenge regular participants in
CTF's.

The Honeynet team will talk about level concepts, tips and tricks, tripping
points if you want to set up your own, right down to the ethical questions
that appear when you acquire the CTF skillset.

The webcast details are as follows:

Topic: Capture the Flag
Date and Time: Monday, 25 February 2013 19:00
Event address for attendees:
