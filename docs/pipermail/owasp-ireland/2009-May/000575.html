Thanks to all who replied off-list. A summary of replies for the list archives:

* Máirtín O' Sullivan of Espion periodically presents a 1-day course
http://www.espion.ie/security-training/course/web-applications-security/

* Ernst & Young also provide AppSec training, written by Eoin Keary

* Deloitte provide hands on Web Application Security training. Contact
Leonard McAuliffe

* SANS also have a course on web app hacking and on secure software development
http://www.sans.org/training/description.php?mid=942
http://www.sans.org/training/description.php?mid=1027

In addition to scheduled public courses, many of the vendors were
prepared to do custom group training at their site or on our premises.

As regards pricing, the pre-conference training day in September
appears to be good value at €450.

Cheers,
Kieran
