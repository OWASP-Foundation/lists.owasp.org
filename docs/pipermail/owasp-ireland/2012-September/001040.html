The 19th International COSAC featuring the SABSA World Congress, will run
from Sunday 30 September to Thursday 4 October at the wonderful Killashee
House Hotel near Naas, Ireland.  To secure your place at one of the world's
leading events and join with one of the most influential groups of seasoned
peers from all over the globe for the uniquely valuable COSAC
experience register now at http://www.cosac.net mentioning that you are an
OWASP Ireland member to obtain your discount.

Acknowledged globally for exceptional active participation and high levels
of innovation, the COSAC 2012 & SABSA World Congress agenda is selected by
the participants according to four competitive criteria: value to
professionals with over 15 years of experience, uniqueness, timeliness, and
approach.  This year's agenda promises to be among the best ever and
features over 30 internationally renowned speakers and
facilitators presenting leading edge sessions including a superb set
of morning and evening plenaries and a series of the highest quality
networking events.

Full day Master Classes are included as standard and half-day workshops are
also available.

Whatever your choice of sessions, COSAC provides a unique
value proposition.  For a similar registration fee to 2-day conferences in
London, COSAC provides:
- a minimum of 3 very full days of world class conference activities;
- 4 nights of luxury accommodations, breakfast, lunch and sumptuous 5
course dinners;
- receptions each evening;
- a full programme of networking events;
- complimentary ground transport between Dublin Airport and the beautiful
Killashee House Hotel.

A number of full-board residential places remain open together with a small
number of non-residential places.  Register today to join your
colleagues and peers from all over Europe, the Americas, Asia, Africa,and
Australia / New Zealand.

Time table: http://www.cosac.net/timetable.html
Prices before discount: http://www.cosac.net/downloads/delegatebook2012.pdf
