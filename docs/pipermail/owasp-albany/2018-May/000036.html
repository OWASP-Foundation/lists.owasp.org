Hello OWASP team,

The OWASP Albany Chapter has appointed new chapter leaders - Jon Briccetti
and Joseph Scaperrotta. Both chapter leaders have years of secure software
development experience under there belt and are ready to share their
experiences with you!

We are preparing to announce our next OWASP Albany Chapter meeting which is
set to take place in September 2018.  As of now, the topic of our
discussion will be Secure Authentication. We hope to bring to you an
exceptional meeting full of skill-building, collaboration, and networking
opportunities! There will be plenty of details to follow so please pay
close attention to our wiki page, national social media accounts and the
upcoming OWASP event calendar found here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Category:OWASP_AppSec_Conference
