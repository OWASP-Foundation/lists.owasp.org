Greetings everyone!
I'm glad to announce the OWASP Baltimore March Meetup. Join us as we take a
look into two popular Dynamic Application Security Testing (DAST) scan
tools (Burp and OWASP ZAP) and their most common use cases. Attendees will
walk away with a clear understanding of the differences between the two
tools and when to use each.

*When:*
Tuesday, March 27, 2018
at 6:30 PM

*Where*
TEK Systems
999 Corporate Blvd Ste 200
Linthicum Hts, MD

For more details please check our Meetup.com page
https://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Baltimore-Chapter/events/. We hope to see you
there!
