Dear Members,

We have been invited to attend 1 day workshop alongside other Info Sec
stakeholders such as (CSEAN, BSides, Technical Heads of Industries, NSAs
office and many more) by NITDA to discuss and create a framework to achieve
'NITDA Penetration Testing Checklists Standard' on Web App, Network, DB,
Mobile App & Wireless Security.

The workshop will have 5 breakout sessions and 2 resource will be required
to lead as such.

Workshop Date and Venue: 24/03/2016 at NITDA Software Testing Centre,
Lekki, Lagos

If you are interested in attending, please send your name and email address
for invitation and event pass to suleimanfarouk at gmail.com

Resource Person Requirements for Web App Session
1) Good knowledge of OWASP Top, WASC and OSSTMM Security Controls
2) Ability to communicate and carry everyone along

Resource Person Requirements for DB Session
1) Good knowledge of the 5 major DB Security Controls
2) Ability to communicate and carry everyone along

Thanks
