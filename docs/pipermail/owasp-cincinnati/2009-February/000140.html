Call For Membership: OWASP has lowered the individual membership fees to 50$ from 100$. Please take the opportunity to become a member if you haven't done yet
You can join via the membership link at owasp website.http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership .When a member joins OWASP will send you a member pack with their membership card and certificate, an OWASP DVD, t-shirt, pen and tote bag.  Individual members also receive 10% off OWASP conferences(if your a member and have not gotten your member pack yet email kate.hartman at owasp.org or call ) So keep in mind there is a cost and that comes out of the $50.
