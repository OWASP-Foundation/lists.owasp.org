Due to a grant, we're able to offer the event without charging for admission
this year.  One of the three tracks will focus solely on software security
talks.

*3rd Annual Security Symposium** at the NKU METS Center
Date: Friday, October 30th
Registration is now open for this event at http://imi.nku.edu/security   *

*
Check In: 8 a.m.
Event Time: 9 a.m. - 4 p.m.
Cost: Free (Made possible by a grant from The Department of Justice and The
College of Informatics at NKU)

Event Abstract:
The IMI Security Symposium is a multi-track conference focused on
information security in several technology sectors.  This year’s conference
will cover key findings from “The State of IT Security” study, completed by
the College of Informatics in 2009.  This nation-wide study provides a
snapshot of IT security practices today.  Attendees will have the
opportunity to participate in a variety of sessions on topics such as
virtualization & security, security implications for networking, security in
cloud computing, open source security, and many more.  Tracks for this
year’s symposium include: Application Software Security, Infrastructure
Protection and Information Security Governance & Compliance.*
