Everyone:

I wanted to pass along this information to anyone interested.  The
Cincinnati Chapter of Infragard has a Digital Forensics Working Group that
meet monthly.  We have decided to put on a Tools and techniques Workshop
on Saturday, Sept 18th for anyone interested in attending.  It will be
composed of a single track of presentations put on by FBI and security
pro's from the area who are focused on digital forensics and incident
response.  Pre-registration is required.

For details, check out http://www.dfwg.us or you may also email me with
any questions.
