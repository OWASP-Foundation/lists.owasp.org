Thanks for the current 22 subscribers of the April 22nd event. Just a remind
Cincinnati OWASP chapter welcomes the sponsor selection of Cincinnati for
the preview of The New Face of Cybercrime movie. The sponsor will fly an
executive at their expense here in Cincy and paying for the catering.

for you. Now to raise the interest in the participation I will provide OWASP

me)

friends. I also welcome any ideas on how we can publish the event.  Please
do not miss this opportunity to have your say on the topic of cyber security
and to show that Cincinnati OWASP security community is worth consideration.
