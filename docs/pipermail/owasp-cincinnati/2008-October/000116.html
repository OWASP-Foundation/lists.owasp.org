OWASP Cincinnati Members

 

Please mark your calendar for this important event celebrating 50 years of
UC. 

 

A flier with the program is included herein kindly provided by Dr. Russ
McMahon Assoc Prof of ITCollege of Applied Science University of Cincinnati.
Dr. McMahon is also an OWASP supporter and will be presenting on the topic
of MS threat modeling for the OWASP chapter meeting currently planned for
January 09.

 

Here are the details of the 50 years of computing UC events:

On Monday evening, November 10 at the Kingsgate Marriott, we will recognize
some of UC's alumni, faculty, and staff for their contributions in the
computing field. Robyn Render the former CIO of the University of North
Carolina will be the keynote speaker. She is a both a UC alumnus and the
former director of UC's information services. 

On Tuesday day, November 11 at UC's College of Applied Science, we have 12
exciting speakers who will talk about the past, present, and future of
computing. The topics cover the gambit of computing from music and art to
medical virtual reality.

On Tuesday evening, November 11 at the Cincinnati Observatory Center, we
have 3 astronomers presenting. They will talk about Cincinnati's greatest
astronomer, Paul Herget and the latest endeavor to reach the furthest depths
of the universe.

This is truly a once in the lifetime event. You can go to
<http://www.uc.edu/conferencing> www.uc.edu/conferencing for all the
information and registration or contact Russ directly at
mcmahor at UCMAIL.UC.EDU

Regards
