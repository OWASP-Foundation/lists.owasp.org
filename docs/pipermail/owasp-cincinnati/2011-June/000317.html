Dear list

 

Ohio Information Security Forum  (OSIF) is celebrating its 5th year in
operation with a full day conference on Saturday July 16. To register for
the upcoming OISF security conference please check
http://www.ohioinfosec.org/agenda.htm there is a $10 registration fee. 

 

The fee can be returned to you, but you must attend the conference to
receive it. Or you can make the $10 a donation to OISF. Also when your
registering for the conference you can also make additional donations to
support OISF. Remember OISF is a 501c3 tax exempt charity so all donations
are tax deductible http://www.ohioinfosec.org/anniversary.htm

 

The conference is being held at the Miami Valley Research Park 1900 Founders
Drive Dayton, Ohio 45420 in the auditorium.

http://www.ohioinfosec.org/directions.htm

 

Regards
