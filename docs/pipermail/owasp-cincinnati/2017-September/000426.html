I wanted to take a few minutes and remind you of a couple of upcoming events that may be of interest to you.

On Friday October 13th NKU is hosting their 10th Annual CyberSecurity Symposium. You can get more information and register here. https://nku.eventsair.com/QuickEventWebsitePortal/cybersecurity/symposium

I’d love to see some of you at these events and if you are make sure to find me and say Hi.
