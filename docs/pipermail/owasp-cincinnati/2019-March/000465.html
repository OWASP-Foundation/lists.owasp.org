Cincinnati OWASP:



You should have received a few emails from the OWASP Foundation about the
sunsetting of the Mailman mailing lists and migration to Google Groups.  *The
sunset occurs on March 22nd (Friday of this week)*.  We appreciate your
membership – the value of our chapter is driven by the network effects of
members like you.



To ensure uninterrupted communications of upcoming events and
opportunities, please take a moment to register with the Cincinnati OWASP
Chapter Google Groups.  If you have any questions with the registration
process or about the Cincinnati OWASP Chapter and our value proposition,
please feel free to contact me.



*Information from Corporate OWASP in regards to Google Groups registration:*



Here is a one-click link to join the new Google Group which is replacing
this list:

https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/#!forum/cincinnati-chapter/join



You can also join by sending an email to
cincinnati-chapter+subscribe at owasp.org



The new Group is also available online at:

https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/#!forum/cincinnati-chapter



Detailed Information on how join the Group is at
https://drive.google.com/open?id=1TBzgvB8Tb0aAZnEy2qYzLnrzJuRzK_T91Em09DVf5YA



NOTE:  You are NOT required to have a Google account, an @owasp.org email
address, or to provide your phone number to join and participate in the new
Group at cincinnati-chapter at owasp.org - just an email address.



Finally, full details on the plan to retire Mailman, links and FAQs are
at: https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Staff-Projects/Mailman-EOL





Thanks,




*Adam Leisring*

*Cincinnati OWASP Chapter Lead*
