Job Description:

Security Test specialist will be responsible for planning, designing and
executing security test efforts for OWASP top 10 issues. The specialist
will be actively involved in manual security testing and automated
testing using Appscan and Fortify PTA. In addition, the test specialist
will be involved in the review of requirements, test cases, and other
project artifacts.
 
Responsibilities

	
*	Develop Security testing strategies and manual test cases for
OWASP top 10 issues. 
*	Review requirements and security risk documents, and define
security scenarios. 
*	Configuring and executing of Appscan and Fortify PTA. 
*	Analyze and compile security testing results. 
*	Effectively communicate issues and provide. 
*	Support test lead by identify risks and to develop mitigation
strategies. 

 
Required Experience

	
*	3 -5 years experience in planning and implementing security test
efforts. 
*	3 -5 years experience with manual security testing. 
*	2 - 3 years of experience with Appscan. 
*	2 -3 years of experience with Fortify PTA. 
*	Basic understanding of Security concepts( CIA Confidentiality,
Integrity, Availability) 
*	Practical knowledge and experience with OWASP top ten issues 
*	Excellent Written and oral communication skills 

 
Preferred Experience

	
*	2 -4 years experience as a lead security Tester 
*	2-4 years experience with Web-based testing methodologies. 
*	Experience working with iterative development methodologies
