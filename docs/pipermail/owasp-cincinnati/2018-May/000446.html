NKU is hosting our 11th annual cybersecurity symposium on October 11-12th this year. We’re looking for speakers and would appreciate it if you would submit a presentation for consideration and share our call for speakers with your contacts.

To submit an abstract of their presentation for consideration, speakers just need to complete the form at nku.eventsair.com/PresentationPortal/cybersecurity/speakers.

If you or potential speakers have any questions about a presentation or anything else about the event, please contact me via email (waldenj at nku.edu).
