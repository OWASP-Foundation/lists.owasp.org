Please joint me to welcome John Fellers as member of the OWASP Cincinnati
chapter board

 

I asked John to share a little bio of him with us (*).

John has specific interest in reverse engineering and incident response and
is 

an enthusiastic supporter of OWASP. 

 

I am very happy to have John onboard and I hope that he will be able to get
young and bright engineers like John

to participate to our OWASP chapter meetings.

 

I am looking forward to a prepare a presentation with John on CSRF for the
May meeting.

 

Again welcome to OWASP John

 

Marco Morana

OWASP Cincinnati Chapter Leader

http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Cincinnati

http://securesoftware.blogspot.com

 

 

(*)

John Fellers is an IT professional with a passion for Information Security.
He currently serves as a Sr. IT Security Engineer with Cincinnati Bell
Technology Solutions and is full time outsourced to GE Infrastructure, where
he provides reverse engineering, incident response, and ethical hacking
services. In his spare time he runs a collaborative group on the internet
known as the Ethical Hackers and Reverse Engineering group, and continues to
study the ever changing landscape of Information Security. He hopes to help
the forward movement of OWASP by through his current knowledge and
expertise.
