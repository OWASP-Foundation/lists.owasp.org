OWASP local Cincinnati chapter members

 

As announced last year, I confirm that the first local chapter meeting is
scheduled for January 29th at Citigroup in Blue Ash, 11.30-1.00 PM.

Please visit our OWASP web page  <http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Cincinnati>
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Cincinnati for further information about the
meeting.

 

The goal of the first meeting is to introduce you to OWASP and to give an
opportunity for local members to socialize.

The presentation material is herein included for your reference.

 

I encourage you to inform friends and colleagues about this meeting.

Invite them to participate to OWASP and to enroll to our mailing list.

Everybody that has keen interest on learning about application security is
welcome.

 

Above all, I am looking forward to your participation to the meeting. 

Eventually, if each of you can send me a note at (marco.m.morana at gmail.com )
with you intent to participate or not 

at the meeting that will be very helpful!

 

Thanks and Happy New Year 2008
