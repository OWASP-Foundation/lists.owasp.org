Hello World!

I just wanted to remind the list of the upcoming OWASP mini conference
this October 21st. If you did not get a chance to attend the NYC OWASP
conference now is your chance to see speakers such as Jeff Williams
Chair of the OWASP foundation and CEO of Aspect Security, Arshan
Dabirsiaghi Director of Research at Aspect Security. We will also host
Brian Chess Chief Scientist and Founder of Fortify Software, Richard
Stallman founder of the Free Software Foundation and Anil Kumar
Information Security Architect for Microsoft to mention a few.   This
will be a one day event.  Admissions are just $25! which includes
lunch and the chance to meet with speakers and sponsors. Each speaker
and sponsor will be moderating a topic of discussion during the
luncheon.  Don't miss this opportunity! time is running out and
registrations are selling!

See the conference wiki page for more details and to register. Lots of
interesting topics!

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Minneapolis_St_Paul_2008_Conference

See you October 21st!! and thanks for your support!!!
