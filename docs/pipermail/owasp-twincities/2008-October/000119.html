*Good evening!
This is your last reminder to register for the MN OWASP Conference on
October 21st to guarantee your seat and lunch.  Lunch will not be guaranteed
for those registrations which come in after 5 pm CST on Friday 10-17-08. We
will be able to take registrations the day of the event through the online
CVent system  for those of you paying by credit card or check.*

*
*

* Those of you who have previousely registered will be getting a separate
e-mail through the CVent system with directions, parking information, etc.
on Monday.
*

*
*

*MN OWASP is very excited out the line up of internationally known speakers
we were able to bring together for this one day of Application Security
talks. Jeff William's co-founder of OWASP will start off the presentations
which include: Arshan Dabirsiaghi, Anil Kumar Revuru from Microsoft, Brian
Chess from Fortify Software, Elliot Glazer from DTCC, Corey Benninger from
Itrepidus Group and Richard Stallman of the GNU project and Free Software
Foundation . The local MN OWASP chapter is hosting this event which we've
brought to you at a nominal fee of $25.00 per person which includes
continental breakfast and gourmet box lunch.*


**

*This event is being held at the University of Minnesota, St. Paul Campus in
the Theater and Ballroom of the Student Center on Buford Avenue.
