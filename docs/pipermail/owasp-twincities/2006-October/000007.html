Reminder:
Here is the agenda for our October meeting.
Please pass this on, especially to folks that would like to "give back" by
writing some new CISSP Exam items on Application Security.

Date: October 17 Location: Metro State University, MPLS
Agenda:
6:00pm - Food, Introduction and optional sign-in for CISSP credits.
6:10pm - Application Centric Identity Management, (Arun, see below)
6:50pm - CISSP Exam Item Writing,(Bob and Ray)
7:50pm - Book Giveaway
7:55pm - Web Auditing (ISACA) and Web Services(ARCTEC) training report
7:50pm - Upcoming events

Location:
http://www.metrostate.edu/bldgservices/location.html#mpls
Room Specifics:
Technical Building, 1501 Hennepin Ave., room 2305
(This building is immediately South West of the MEC building where we have
recently met.)

Street parking is free after 6 pm.
If you get there early it's just a $.25 for a half hour.
There is also a ramp which is $5.00.

Food:
Lorna will bring a sandwich tray, cookies and pop.

Arun Kothanath: Consultant, Integral Solutions
Application Centric Identity management - What does this mean?

We will talk about how applications (especially externally facing web
applications) need to be identity aware. What are the options, how
technology is solving this issue.

CISSP Exam Item Writing:
The CISSP certification is shaping our field. The CISSP exam is painfully in
need of some fresh questions on application security. We'll form groups to
discuss and write exam items for the CISSP exam. We'll have the guidelines
and a stack of reference books available.

Facilitators:
Bob Sullivan: CISSP since 2002, OWASP Chapter Leader, WebGoat contributor
and gainfully employed by UnitedHealth Technologies (Informatio Risk
Management)
Ray Kaplan: (hopes to be on hand) to provide CISSP exam background,
guidelines and insight. Ray is a CISSP and has taught the official ISC2
CISSP Reivew class.

Door Prize:
We will have a drawing for a publisher's copy of the book Web Services
Platform

Put your business card in the OWASP bucket for a chance to win:
Web Services Platform Architecture
SOAP, WSDL, WS-Policy, WS-Addressing, WS-BEL,...
By Sanjiva Weerawarana, et al
Prentice-Hall 2005

Training Review:
Members have recently attended the ISACA Web Audit class and the ARCTEC Web
Services Security class. They will give you a brief report.

Upcoming Events:
CISSP Exam is November 3 in White Bear Lake. Registration information and
details at: http://www.mn-issa.org/html/certifications.html

Check the updated OWASP meeting posting at
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Minneapolis_St_Paul  for more detail about
the presenters.

Thanks to the folks at Metropolitan State U for the room and Integral
Business solutions for the food.
