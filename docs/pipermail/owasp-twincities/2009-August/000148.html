Hello World!

I want to publicly thank Lorna Alamri from Integral and Adam Baso from  
Symantec for their hard work and dedication helping us put this event  
together. As you know the three of us volunteer our time to  
coordinate, arrange these series of meetings and events and with our  
current work load it could be sometimes challenging and frustrating.  
So please join me thanking them for their support and dedication to  
our local chapter. It is because of people like them that we are able  
to make such event possible.  Let's also thank our sponsors, you well  
know this is a non profit organization and we rely on the support of  
our faithful sponsors to be able to offer a venue, provide food and  
drink and in some cases cover some travel expenses to some of our  
speakers.

THANK YOU!!!!  Office of Internal Audit and OIT Security at the  
University of Minnesota, Integral, Symantec, The Upper Midwest  
Security Association (Secure360) and The Center for Strategic IT and  
Security & Imperva for supporting this event.

In another note, please notice that  ONLY ONE registrant at a time can  
register for this event so if you wish to register a colleague,  
forward the event to them and have him/her register on their own.
