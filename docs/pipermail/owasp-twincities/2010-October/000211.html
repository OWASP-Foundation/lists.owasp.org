Just 2 days left to register!



OWASP MSP is hosting our yearly one day event featuring keynote, Bill
Cheswick. Bill is a legendary figure behind the network firewall concept and
co-author of Firewalls and Internet Security, one of the most influential
books on the topic. Bill coined the term "proxy" under its current technical
meaning and is a creator of the Internet Mapping Project. At our event
on October
8th Bill will discuss one of the most fundamental aspects of security: the
password, in his talk titled “Rethinking Passwords”.



Full speaker line-up:

   - Bill Cheswick, AT&T Research Labs – Rethinking Passwords
   - Andrew Becherer, iSec Partners – Attacking Kerberos & the New Hadoop
   Security Design
   - Joe Teff, Wells Fargo – Can you implement a static analysis program
   using the OWASP Code Review Guide?
   - Charles Henderson, Trustwave SpiderLabs – Global Security Report
   - Jason Rouse, Cigital – Mobile Security
   - Andre “Dre” Gironda – Application Assessments Reloaded



Make sure to reserve your seat now for just $25.00.
