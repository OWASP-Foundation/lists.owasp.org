Principal Security Analyst
The Principal Security Analyst provides business and technical advice on a
wide variety of information security issues, concerns, and problems.  This
position ensures that all business applications developed in-house or
developed by outsiders on behalf of Jostens include adequate control
measures.  The Principal Security Analyst also acts as an auditor and
assessor for the compliance of information systems to industry, legal,
regulatory and best practices including SOX, PCI DSS and HIPAA.  Working on
committees and task forces throughout Jostens, the Principal Security
Analyst is an in-house subject matter expert who diligently assists with the
improvement of security on information systems and applications at Jostens.
This position is a visible internal spokesperson of the Information Security
Department and is charged with gaining widespread support of and compliance
with information security requirements.  The position requires a
specialization in multiple areas, such as Internet commerce, desktop
machines, telecommuting and mobile computing, and particularly secure
systems development.

See www.jostens.com for more information
