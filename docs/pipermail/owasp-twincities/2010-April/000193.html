Happy Monday everybody. There's plenty to be excited about in
application security in the Twin Cities area.

David M. N. Bryan (aka, videoman) will be speaking at the OWASP MSP
meeting one week from today on Monday, April 26. Register for his
talk, "Do VLANs allow for good application security?", at
http://owaspmsp20100426chaptermeeting.eventbrite.com/.

The OWASP Top 10 for 2010 was officially released today. Check it out
at http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Top_10. This is a great document for
anyone involved with producing web application software, so do your
part and spread the word! If you have friends in media, link them up
with the Connections Committe contact and our local chapter VP, Lorna
Alamri, at lorna.alamri at owasp.org.

The local Secure360 conference is only three weeks away. Visit
http://www.secure360.org/ for more information.

Not only is David M. N. Bryan speaking at the next meeting, he's
recorded and posted a bunch of videos over the past several months.
Here are a few:

Enjoy, and hope to see you in a week.
