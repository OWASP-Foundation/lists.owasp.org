All chapter members,

As a follow up message to our June chapter meeting notice you may have
recognized that we are no longer using Eventbrite and have switched over to
use Meetup.com. This is to encourage other meetup groups to find our local
OWASP chapter and to join us. There are a lot of developer centric groups
that use Meetup that could benefit from our knowledge and education.

I apologized if this is an uncomfortable switch for anyone. If it is please
provide me feedback I would love to discuss the change.

-Todd
