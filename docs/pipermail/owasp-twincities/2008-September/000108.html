Just a quick reminder about today's luncheon sponsored by WhiteHat and
F5 Networks:

WhiteHat Security, the leader in SaaS website security, and F5
Networks, the global leader in Application Delivery Networking,
cordially invite you to a luncheon on Wednesday, September 10th at the
W Hotel Minneapolis.

At this event, you will learn about advancements in protecting and
securing Web applications from attacks. You'll hear from Ken Salchow,
Manager, Technical Marketing, F5 will offer insights into how F5
customers are driving website security in their organizations. We will
then present a live demonstration of a revolutionary new solution that
closes the loop from Web application vulnerability detection to
remediation – an integrated solution delivering TOTAL website
security.
