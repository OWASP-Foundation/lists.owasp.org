Speaker and Topic Information

*1st Presentation:* *Defcon 17 Highlights*

* *

*David Bryan, NetSpi* (CISSP)

*Abstract:*  This will be a review of a few of the popular topics and
presentations from this year’s Defcon 17 conference.   In this we will
outline new risks and threat vectors, such as how poorly browsers implement
TLS/SSL security, contest results, and other fun tidbits.





*2nd Presentation: **The problem with Pen Testing*



*Marcus Ranum, Chief Security Officer, Tenable Security*
*Abstract:*  Penetration testing certainly is a crowd-pleaser and, now, it's
required by significant industry standards such as PCI. There's a problem,
however, with the entire notion of penetration testing. In this
presentation, I will explain why penetration testing must evolve away from
its initial premise in order to become a really useful component of the
infosec practitioner's tool-set.
