Hi Michael,

It was yesterday and was largely a security awareness event for UCF. I'd say
was roughly 80 folks or so, mostly UCF staff. Content was very high level.
Not much of interest for technical folks but glad to see they are educating
their people and opening it up to the public. I had a meeting with a local
consulting firm that same day who is willing to commit $300.00 to the
chapter and more once we grow our membership a bit. I also made a contact
with UCF I am hoping will be able to provide us a permanent space for
meetings. I think university partnership is an excellent way to grow the
OWASP community and I have high hopes that we can grow something positive
here.

-Tony
