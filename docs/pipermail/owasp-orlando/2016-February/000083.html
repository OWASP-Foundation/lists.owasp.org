Dear OWASPers,

Please save Thursday February 18th on your calendar for our next meeting!
As always, our meetings are absolutely free to attend, and include free
food and beverages.

*Thursday 02/18 Social Meeting*

On 02/18, we’re having a social meeting to network, meet fellow OWASPers,
and reach out to the central Florida developer community. In addition to
free food and beverages, we’ll give away OWASP swag and books as trivia
prizes.

The location for our social meeting will be Downtown PourHouse, at 20 S
Orange Ave, Orlando. We’ll meet from 5:30 PM to 8 PM approximately.

For more details or to join our Meetup community, check out
http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Orlando/events/228782867/
