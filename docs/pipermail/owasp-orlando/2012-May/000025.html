From: distribution at acsac.org [mailto:distribution at acsac.org] 

We realize this is a busy time of year, and several of you have contacted us
requesting an extension to put the finishing touches on your submission to
ACSAC 2012.  The deadline for paper, case study, panel, course/training, and
workshop submissions is therefore being extended by 1 week from June 1 to
*June 8*. Hopefully this will provide an opportunity for you to complete
your submission, or to make one if you had not considered it before.  We
expect the June 8 deadline to be firm in order to allow adequate time for
review, so please be sure to make your submission as soon as possible. 
Details on how to make a submission are available at www.acsac.org/cfp/.

In case you know of a colleague doing interesting work in applied computer
security, please encourage them to make a submission as well.
