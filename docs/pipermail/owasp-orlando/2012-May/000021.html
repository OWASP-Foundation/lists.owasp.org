Dear leaders,

The OWASP 2012 online competition has started. If you login into
Hacking-Lab as a registered user, you should now see all details about the
challenges.

The first users have already solved some *Hackademic *puzzles. See the
scoring here

* https://www.hacking-lab.com/ranking/event.html?eventid=284



*Competition Rules*

* https://www.hacking-lab.com/events/owasp-2012-online-competition.html



*Winner Prize*

The winner can choose one ticket to either

   1. OWASP AppSec US
   2. OWASP AppSec EU
   3. OWASP AppSec Latin America.

There is an ongoing discussion about giving three tickets (winner, second,
third), but not officially communicated until now.


*Winner Announcement*

The winner will be informed/announced June 19, 2012. The competition ends
next June 17th, 2012.

*Level*s

The Hackademics challenges are considered as "easy". Three more challenges
are considered to intermediate/advanced.

*Questions?*
Talk to Martin Knobloch (GEC leader, Academy Portal) or to me
