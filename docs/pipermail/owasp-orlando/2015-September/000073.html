Hello list!

My name is Adrian Pastor. I moved from Oxford, UK to central FL less than a year ago and am really keen to meet InfoSec pros, students, and hobbyists in the area.

I've been passionate about security since witnessing the (authorized) execution of an exploit (WinNuke NetBIOS OOB) for the first time at an Internet café in my early teens.

I work as a consultant specializing in identifying and assessing technical and business security risks and advising organizations on implementing security controls that align with their business goals. I often act as an interface between technical personnel and business stakeholders such as process owners and senior management.

In my free time, I enjoy bug hunting and creating web wargames. Outside security, I love to travel and trying dishes from different regions of the world.

I look forward to meeting and serving OWASP attendees and officers alike. Please let me know if there is anything you need; I'm here to help! :-)
