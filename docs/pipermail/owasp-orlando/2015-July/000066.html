Dear OWASP Board Members, Leaders & Community Members,

We are happy to announce that OWASP has formed a partnership with both
BSides-Las
Vegas <https://www.bsideslv.org> and with BlackHat USA
<https://www.blackhat.com/us-15/>.

Both of these organizations have offered OWASP expo space at their upcoming
events in Las Vegas. BSides will take place August 4 - August 5 & BlackHat
August 5 - August 6. This is a wonderful outreach opportunity for us to
promote our projects, chapters, events, tools, programs, membership, etc.
to thousands of people who share the same interests and concerns as we do
about security.  Also included are a few passes to each event :)  By
donating some of your time to help at either of the booths, you can earn
yourself a pass.

*BSides-LasVegas*
If you volunteer a total of 6 hours at the OWASP BSides booth over the
course of the 2 days, you will receive 1 supporting charity badge. *Please
note that these passes are limited and are on a first come first serve
basis*.

*BlackHat* *USA*
Volunteer 9 hours at the OWASP BlackHat booth over the 2 days and receive 1
full conference badge to BlackHatUSA. *Please note that these passes are
limited and are on a first come first serve basis.* *You must volunteer a
total of 9 hours*.

Volunteer 6 hours at the OWASP BlackHat booth over the 2 days and receive 1
expo only badge to BlackHatUSA. *Please note that these passes are limited
and are on a first come first serve basis.  **You must volunteer a total of
6 hours*.
