Information on the following course taking place at the ACSAC conference is
now available on the web site:

  Course M3 - Keeping Your Web Apps Secure: The OWASP Top 10 & Beyond
  https://www.acsac.org/2012/program/courses/webappsec/

The course was sold out last year, so early registration is encouraged.

Course content covers security risks and vulnerabilities, as well as how to
mitigate them.  Attendees will receive a free web application security book
and printed course notes, along with a certificate of completion which may
be used for documenting continuing education credits.

Note that you can sign up for the course alone if you are unable to attend
the conference.  The fee is $375 before November 12th, or $425 after.
Students get a discounted fee of $200.  Register at
https://www.acsac.org/2012/registration/.
