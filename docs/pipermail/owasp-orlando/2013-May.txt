OWASP Leaders -

Just in time for summer, OWASP is pleased announce 2 new individual
membership options - purchase a two year membership for $95 USD, or a
lifetime membership for $500 USD.  In addition to all the regular benefits
of individual membership <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership>, when
you purchase our new lifetime membership you will also receive a metal
membership card to thank you for your contribution.

Some of the benefits you will receive as an individual member include a
vote in our upcoming board
election<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2013_Board_Elections>
 and WASPY Awards <http://owasp.com/index.php/WASPY_Awards_2013>,
discounted conference admission (including $50 off admission to our
upcoming AppSec USA event <http://appsecusa.org/2013/>), and an
owasp.orgemail address. Learn more about membership benefits here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership

*We are also launching our Summer Membership drive tomorrow (June 1).*
When you sign up to become a new OWASP member or renew your OWASP
membership during the first 10 days of June, you will also be entered into
a drawing for one of the following donated prizes:


   - 2 OWASP Lifetime Memberships


   - 2 Full Conference Passes for OWASP AppSec Research <https://appsec.eu/>,
   August 22-23, 2013 in Hamburg Germany


   - 2 Full Conference Passes for OWASP AppSec USA<http://appsecusa.org/2013/>,
   November 20-21, 2013 in New York City


   - 4 Full Conference (Briefing Passes) to Black Hat USA
2013<https://www.blackhat.com/us-13/>


   - EC Council - Rocket City Takedown
Con<https://takedownconhunstville.eventbrite.com/>,
   July 15-16, 2013, Huntsville Alabama: 1 training class of the attendee
   choice + 1 full conference pass, 2 full conference passes


   - EC Council - Hacker Halted 2013 Angels vs.
Demons<https://www.hackerhalted.com/2013/us/>,
   September 19-21, 2013 in Atlanta Georgia: 1 training class of the
   attendee choice + 1 full conference pass, 2 full conference passes


   - 2 Full Conference Passes for (ISC)2 Secure Asia, Philippines - August
   8-9, 2013


   - 2 Full Conference Passes for (ISC)2 Security
Congress<https://www.isc2.org/Security-Congress.aspx>,
   Sept 24-27, 2013 in Chicago Illinois


   - 2 Full Conference Passes for (ISC)2 Secure Brazil
