*2015 Meeting November 19 *

(Moved 1 week sooner due to Thanksgiving, we apologize for the short notice)

If you are in Orlando on Thursday November 19th, please join us at the next
OWASP Orlando chapter meeting. *Free food and drinks will be provided!* We'll
be conducting a roundtable style discussion of topics of interest such as:

   - Static vs Dynamic Testing (perhaps IAST too)
   - Web App Firewalls vs Runtime Application Security Protection vs fixing
   vulnerable code
   - Other topics - if you have one you'd like to see covered email the
   leaders

The meeting is free to attend and will take place from 5 PM to 7.30 PM. The
location will be kindly provided by HD Supply. Parking is free in the dirt
lot on the north side of Pine St.

Location Details
HD Supply[1] <https://goo.gl/maps/HEsWx3JWuLw>
501 W Church St
Orlando, FL
