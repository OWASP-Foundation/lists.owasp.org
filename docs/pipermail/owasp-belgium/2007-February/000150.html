Hi,

 

Some more details on the upcoming presentation of the 2007 OWASP Top 10
(currently available as OWASP Top 10 2007 RC1
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Top_10_2007> ) on the Infosecurity
event <http://www.infosecurity.be/>  on the 21st and 22nd of March 2007.




We have already received lots of feedback on the new OWASP Top 10. See
e.g. on PCI DSS blog
<http://datasecurity.wordpress.com/2007/02/05/owasp-top-10-for-2007/>
with some interesting comments and of course Sylvan von Stuppe's
comments can be found here
<http://sylvanvonstuppe.blogspot.com/2007/02/owasp-top-10-2007-update-rc
1-a7-a8.html> (A7-A8), here
<http://sylvanvonstuppe.blogspot.com/2007/02/owasp-top-10-2007-update-rc
1-a5-a6.html> (A5-A6), here
<http://sylvanvonstuppe.blogspot.com/2007/02/owasp-top-10-2007-update-rc
1-a3-a4.html> (A3-A4) and here
<http://sylvanvonstuppe.blogspot.com/2007/01/owasp-top-10-2007-update-rc
1-a1-a2.html>  (A1-A2). This is your last change to review the document
prior to February 28th and provide feedback to the 
owasp-topten at lists.owasp.org
<http://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-topten>  mail list.


The presentation will be on the 21st and 22nd of March, each time at 16h
in Room B of the "Vendor" sessions.


Maybe afterwards we can organize a social gathering? 

 

I will be sending personal invitations next week.

 

I hope to see you there!
