Hello

Are there any plans for topics of a more broader approach than the ones
mentioned on this list?  I'm sure that if you look hard enough on the
web or buy the right books you'll find out how to secure an apache
server with PHP.  I think everyone that visits the OWASP meetings knows
a thing or two about websecurity and topics like that aren't going to
attract a lot of people with a large bookcase.  Unless these meetings
are social networking only which isn't really the idea.

Any talks about the future of websecurity?  The tendencies of the
current products on the market and their roadmaps?  The influence of
Single-Sign-On?  The implications and technology behind digital
passports such as the attempt made by Microsoft and Hotmail?  Future
expected attacks and the protection thereof?  SSL VPN strengths,
weaknesses and how to assess?  Testimonies of people responsible for the
security of webfarms of 50-100+ servers and their approach and views on
the matter?

I see a lot of ideas but nothing really new that hasn't been discussed
in an O'Reilly book.
