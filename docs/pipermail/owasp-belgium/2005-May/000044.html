Dear list,

 

On 26th of May 2005 we held the first OWASP Belgium Chapter meeting!

It was a big success: we had nearly 40 people attending, despite the
Belgium-unlike hot weather.

PROGRAM

*	17h30 - 18h00: Welcome & get a drink 
*	18h00 - 18h45: Sebastien Deleersnyder, Ascure 
	    OWASP Introduction 
*	19h00 - 19h45: Erwin Geirnaert, Security Innovation
	    How to Break Web Application Security 
*	20h00 - 20h45: professor Frank Piessens, KU Leuven
	    How to Build Secure Web Applications

We had some interesting discussions with Frank on the position of
security controls: within the code or within the supporting
infrastructure?
Another idea is also to look for a top 10 solutions for Web Applications
and have some guidance system when selecting countermeasures.

 

Check our chapter page http://www.owasp.org/local/belgium.html
<http://www.owasp.org/local/belgium.html>  on meeting notes.

The presentations will also be posted to the chapter mailing list.

 

NEXT MEETING

We'll organize the next chapter meeting in September and look for an
air-conditioned venue (possibly coordinated with ISSA Belgium).

Sign up to the chapter mailing list
<http://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/owasp-belgium/> , introduce
yourself and post topic suggestions for the next chapter meeting.

 

Please forward this mail to anyone you feel would have an interest in
future Chapter meetings.

 

Thank you,
