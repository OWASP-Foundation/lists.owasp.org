OWASP Leaders,

The global conferences committee is pleased to announce a design
contest for the next OWASP conference presentation template.  Many of
you have mentioned that the old template
(https://www.owasp.org/images/5/54/Presentation_template.ppt) is
looking a bit dated, and we agree!  So we've launched a design contest
on freelancer.com at
http://www.freelancer.com/contest/New-presentation-template-for-the-OWASP-Foundation-5302.html.

After 7 days the submissions will be reviewed by the conferences
committee and a winner selected to get $190 in prize money!
