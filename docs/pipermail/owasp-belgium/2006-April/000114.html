Hi,

Maybe I missed this topic by not attending a previous chapter meeting
(sorry for that).

What about a general introduction to "Web Application Security": some
history, definition of the problem space, solutions today (ranging from
programming practices to WAF and beyond) and future trends. I myself
have a solid background and experience in Identity & Access Management
and I have written my share of web applications in the past. However, my
understanding and knowledge about web application security is currently
limited.

I am also convinced that by presenting a broader introduction to the
topic, discussions will spawn that will identify future (expert) topics
for OWASP chapter meetings.

Any followers?
