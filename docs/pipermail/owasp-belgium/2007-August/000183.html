Pre-announcement: International PCI-DSS Conference on 11-10-2007 in
Brussels!


 

 

Dear ISSA-BE Member, 
Dear Security Professional, 

Block your calendar for a high-quality, hot-topic, not-to-be-missed
information-sharing event!

 

ISSA-BE organizes a full day conference on Reaching Payment Card Industry
Data Security Standard Compliance and Certification. Security specialists,
auditors and management responsible for, or involved in PCI-DSS projects
will get valuable information based on practical expertise in the field. 

 

The conference will take place 
on Thursday October 11th, 2007 in the offices of Atos Worldline NV/SA 
(formerly known as Banksys) in Brussels close to NATO Headquarters.

 

Topics and speakers will include:

 

-         PCI DSS Update - Steve Hubbard (MasterCard Worldwide)

-         PCI DSS: the view from a QSA - Brad Harris (AmbironTrustWave)

-         PCI DSS: Case Studies - Claudio Scarabello and Gunther Bloemen
(Verizon Business Security Solutions)

-         PCI DSS: the ASV point of view - Dieter Sarrazyn (Ascure)

-         Be prepared before bringing expensive external auditors in -
Sissel Thomassen (InfoSecure Group)

-         Measuring and Reporting PCI Compliance - Pierre Noël (IBM/Consul)

-         PCI DSS: Case Studies - Phil Cracknell (Deloitte UK)

 

The final program and conference details will be announced in the first half
of September. If you want to be sure you get the e-brochure, send e-mail
with your contact details to pcidss at be.issa.org.
