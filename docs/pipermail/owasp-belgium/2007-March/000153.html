Dear List Members,

This is a follow-up on my blog entry, a couple of weeks ago:
http://blogs.owasp.org/seba/2007/02/19/chapter-democrazy/.
 
To further develop the Belgian OWASP Chapter, I think it is a good idea to
broaden the leadership and create a Belgian OWASP Board. A great example of
how a group of people can boost a chapter is Italy
(http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Italy).

I would like to invite people interested in board participation to let me
know. I hope to organize a first board meeting prior to the next chapter
meeting in May.

If you have any questions and/or remarks, do not hesitate to contact me or -
even better - post it to the mailing list!

Kind regards,

Seba
