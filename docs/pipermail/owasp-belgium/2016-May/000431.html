Dear all,

At the Design & Technology department of Erasmus University College Brussels, we are currently offering a position for research assistant. This is full-time position for 1 year, with possibility for extension after positive evaluation.

Innoviris approved the research project "SeCloud Security-driven Engineering of Cloud-based applications", which focuses on the development and promotion of secure cloud applications. The project fits into the broader context of Secur-IT (http://www.securit-brussels.be/) in which four other security-related projects were launched. SeCloud, with a total budget of more than four million euro, brings together a number of research partners from several educational and innovation companies: Sirris, VUB, UCL, ULB and Erasmus University College Brussels join their forces in this ambitious research project.

We are looking for a full-time research assistant in the field of software security. For the complete official vacancy in Dutch, see https://www.erasmushogeschool.be/over-ehb/vacatures/externe-vacature-wetenschappelijk-medewerker-1

We have an English translation available: https://mobilewearable.ehb.be/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/External_vacancy_scientific_researcher-.pdf

Application deadline is 29 May 2016.

Please forward this message to possible interested candidates. I you have any question, don't hesitate to contact me.

Best regards,
Bert Van Rillaer, researcher and teacher @ EhB
