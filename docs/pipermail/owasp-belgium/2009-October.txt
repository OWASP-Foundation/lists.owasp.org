      A Workshop on Future Privacy and Security Research Challenges in
      Online Social Networks (5 Nov 2009, Heverlee/Leuven)

    * Open workshop to discuss privacy and security concerns in future
      social networks, and how to deal with them.
    * Target audience: industry as well as researchers and practitioners
      from law, computer science, social sciences working

      (ESAT, K.U. Leuven) in collaboration with the groups HMDB,
