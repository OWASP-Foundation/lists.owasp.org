We would like to announce two upcoming events:

    * OWASP BeNeLux Day (2 Dec 2009, Leuven)
    * A Workshop on Future Privacy and Security Research Challenges in
      Online Social Networks (5 Nov 2009, Heverlee/Leuven)


      OWASP BeNeLux Day (2 Dec 2009, 12-22h, Leuven)


        Program:

    * 12:00 AM - 16:00 PM: Workshop OWASP tools
    * 16:00 PM - 22:00 PM: Talks


        Tentative speakers list:

    * Prof. Bart Preneel (Katholieke Universiteit Leuven)
    * Colin Watson (Watson Hall and Global Industry Committee Member at
      OWASP Foundation)
    * Eoin Keary (Ernst & Young and OWASP Code Review Guide Author and Lead)
    * Sebastien Deleersnyder (Telindus, Belgacom ICT and OWASP
      Foundation Board Member)
    * Noa Bar-Yosef (Imperva Application Defense Center)
    * Erwin Geirnaert (ZION SECURITY and OWASP Belgium Board Member)

More info and registration on
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/BeNeLux_OWASP_Day_2009


      A Workshop on Future Privacy and Security Research Challenges in
      Online Social Networks (5 Nov 2009, Heverlee/Leuven)

    * Open workshop to discuss privacy and security concerns in future
      social networks, and how to deal with them.
    * Target audience: industry as well as researchers and practitioners
      from law, computer science, social sciences working
    * Organized by the COSIC Privacy and Identity Management Group
      (ESAT, K.U. Leuven) in collaboration with the groups HMDB,
      DISTRINET and ICRI at K.U. Leuven and Netlog, Belgium.

More info and registration on
https://www.cosic.esat.kuleuven.be/privacyGroup/WorkshopOSN/
