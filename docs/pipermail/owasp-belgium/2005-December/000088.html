Dear chapter members,
 
Up until now I have not received any suggestions and or proposals to do
something on the next chapter meeting, 
except one proposal for a more general non-OWASP related presentation
(appreciated).
 
I want to make clear that the chapter meetings are organized for you to
exchange experience and knowledge on OWASP related subjects.

It benefits us all, when you provide input and feedback on the OWASP
discussions and projects.
This chapter will only become succesful if you participate. 
 
I am personally not looking for "ready-made" presentations, although
they can be helpful.
I am asking you: 
1) your opinion on what should be done during the next chapter
meeting(s)?
Should we elaborate in more detail on current OWASP projects?
Do we create local sub-groups that take on specific topics such as e.g.
Belgian awareness sessions, Legal projects for Belgian Law?
Do we organize a Q&A session on novice topics, or on expert topics?
How do we liaison with Belgian universities or high-schools to put
application security on the curriculum?
etc...
2) to take one of these or your own topic and do something with it
during the next meeting: presentation, Q&A, workshop, sub-group, ...
3) Testimonials on how OWASP material (could have) helped you with your
particular job or project are also highly welcome
 
I am extending the period for proposals/ideas/remarks to Sunday Dec 4th.
If I have not received enough feedback to set up the next OWASP meeting
on Dec 12th we will have to postpone the chapter meeting to next year.
 
Only together can we do this.
 
Thank you
