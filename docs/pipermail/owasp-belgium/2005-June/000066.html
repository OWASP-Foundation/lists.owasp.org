Hi Sebastien, 
 
Theory on practices, techniques etc.. may appear as "deja vu" for
experts, while difficult to catch for newbies (we all know conferences
where you sleep half of the time, or do not get a piece of it he other
time).
But I think that practical case studies where the lecturer can explain
what (s)he lived "with guts", has always an added value for everyone. So
I suggest the following generic agenda:
 
- one/more intro-level topic 
- one/more experienced-level topic (see L-sec, etc..)
- one/more real-live cases
 
depending on speakers availability, etc...
