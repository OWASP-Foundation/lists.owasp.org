Hereby the promised update on the WAF panel:
 
Final PROGRAM

*	18h00 - 18h20: Welcome, get Pizza & Drink 
*	18h20 - 18h40: Sebastien Deleersnyder, Ascure 
	 OWASP,  OWASP Membership and new OWASP Projects 
*	18h40 - 19h30: Philippe Bogaerts, BeeWare
	 WebScarab demonstration 
*	19h30 - 20h45: 
	Web Application Firewalls: Panel Discussion
	I'll start with a Web Application Firewall (WAF) primer.

		Then we set up the panel Discussion with:
		

		*	Philippe Bogaerts, BeeWare
		*	Jaak Cuppens, F5 Networks
		*	Tim Groenwals, Agfa Gevaert
		*	Lieven Desmet, K.U.Leuven

		Important questions will be:
		How mature are WAFs?
		What do WAFs protect you from? What not?
		Where do you position WAFs in your architecture?
		What WAF functionality do you really need?
		 
		The audience is of course invited to add to the
discussion with questions and remarks!
