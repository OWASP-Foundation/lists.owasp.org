hi,

We are reorganising our work as OWASP Belgium for the next year(s).

We invite people with:
1) an active interest in appsec
2) organisation skills
3) some free cycles to invest time
to *become part of the OWASP Belgium board / group of volunteers in the
coming year(s)*.
Main objectives is to help us organize the OWASP chapter events next year.

The typical tasks are:

   - Organisation of BeNeLux Day
   - Chapter meetings:
   - Maintaining speaker wish list
      - Invitation and communication with speakers
      - Speaker gifts
      - Logistics (venue, catering)
      - Communication (website, email invitations)
      - ...
   - Sponsoring
   - Budget and finances
   - External event scouting
   - Contributions to AppSec EU

if you feel like taking up one (or more) of these tasks, please contact me (
seba at owasp.org) by the end of December.

Besides this, we could also use a *couple of helping hands for the next
OWASP BeNeLux days*.
1) during the training registration on Thursday from 8AM to 9:30 AM
2) during the conference registration on Friday from 7:30AM to 9:15 AM
please contact Lieven Desmet (in cc) en myself if you want to help us next
Thursday and/or Friday ?

Thank you!

Kind regards

Seba
