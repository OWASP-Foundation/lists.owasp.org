Dear Seba,

I kindly remind you of our second OWASP BeNeLux Day 2016! The event will
take place on *24 and 25 November 2016*, in Heverlee (Belgium).

On Thursday *24 November*, *three 1-day trainings* are offered:

   - Breakers, defenders and superheroes!  (by Riccardo ten Cate)
   - PWN Android Apps with your Custom Built Toolbox (by Steven Wierckx)
   - Why simply deploying HTTPS will not get you an A+ grade (by Philippe
   De Ryck)

On Friday *25 November*, the *1-day conference* features a line-up of
various regional and international speakers, including:

   - Dario Incalza: Securing Android Applications
   - Yorick Koster: The State of Security of WordPress (plugins)
   - Sebastian Lekies: Securing AngularJS Applications
   - Giancarlo Pellegrino: Compression Bombs Strike Back
   - Zakaria Rachid: Zap it !
   - Tom Van Goethem: Stealing Secrets through Browser-based Side-channel
   Attacks
   - Daniel Kefer: Handling of Security Requirements in Software
   Development Lifecycle
   - Bart Preneel: *Closing Keynote: *The Future of Security

*Attendance to the conference is free of charge*, but registration is
required.
*Attendance to the trainings is reserved for OWASP members* (students and
faculty can attend for free). Find more about becoming an OWASP member for
only 40 EUR at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership
<http://owasp.us4.list-manage.com/track/click?u=979ef73f8345f6ba626526ebf&id=73f44efcf8&e=bfbbb000f5>
.

We have a limited number of seats available, so first come first serve.

More information on the program and venue can be found at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/BeNeLux_OWASP_Day_2016-2
<http://owasp.us4.list-manage.com/track/click?u=979ef73f8345f6ba626526ebf&id=289441ba69&e=bfbbb000f5>
.

Kind regards,
