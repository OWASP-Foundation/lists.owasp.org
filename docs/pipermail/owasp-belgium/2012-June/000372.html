Hello Leaders,

I am happy to say we have chosen 4 projects for the initial project reboot
initiative.
We have appointed an initial seed of $5,000 for each project.

*Projects in question are:*
*
*
OWASP Development Guide,
OWASP CISO Guide,
OWASP ZAP and
OWASP Mobile Project

Some projects may require more time and money, others less so.
The project proposals were reviewed and voted on by the named review team.
The results can be seen on the wiki (Link below).

Anyway's congratulations to the winning proposals.

*There is still time to submit a proposal.* Also, the proposals which were
not chosen in the first round are still eligible for the next selection
round.

*Key dates:*

*First round of proposal selection*: 15 June 2012 - Done
*Submission closing date*: July 30th 2012
***Second round of proposal selection*: 10 Aug 2012

URL to initiative:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Projects_Reboot_2012

Also a big thanks on behalf of the foundation to the chapters who pledged
funding to this effort. I hope the result of this initiative will help
developers and security people for future years to come.

Thanks,
