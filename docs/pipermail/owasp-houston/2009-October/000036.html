OWASP Houston,

I wanted to inform you of a new Information Security group which will
be located here in the Houston area.  The group is called "NAISG" and
it is an international organization that provides and coordinates
security-focused meetings, demonstrations and seminars; as well as an
online communications forum with a global audience.  The next meeting
will be held on November 2nd at 6:30pm at the Microsoft Offices on
Beltway 8 (where the OWASP meetings were originally held).

If you are interested -- the details are below.

David

2. MEETINGS COUNT TOWARD YOUR CISSP RECERTIFICATION
     During the three year recertification period, each CISSP must
earn 120 continuing professional education (CPE) credits.
     Your attendance at NAISG meetings is valid toward these credits.
If you are a CISSP, please print a copy of the form at
     http://houston.naisg.org/Meetings and bring it to the meeting.
Give it to the meeting moderator to sign, after which you
     can submit it to (ISC)2 as needed.  Details on the
recertification requirements are at
https://www.isc2.org/cgi-bin/content.cgi?category=24.
