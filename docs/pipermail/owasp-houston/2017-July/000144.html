August 15th Local Networking Event - Cover Your Apps - Application Security
at JoyStix

The event sponsors have allocated some time for the Houston OWASP Chapter
leaders to promote OWASP and our local chapter, so please come out to
network and support our chapter.

Please RSVP for the event here:
https://www.cvent.com/c/express/0bf8df8f-952e-4130-a879-983f9f43e22d

Here are the full details of the event.

Checkmarx (an OWASP Contributing Corporate Member), Radware, and Critical
Start are hosting an Application Security event at Joystix on August 15.
It will be a fun event with refreshments and retro pinball machines.

>From development to delivery, securing your applications is critical to
implementing a best-practice approach to cybersecurity. Join Critical
Start, Checkmarx and Radware on Tuesday, August 15 at 4:30 p.m. at JoyStix
and learn how you can strengthen your security posture with tools that:
Build secure applications during the development process
Streamline application security testing for developers
Keep web applications secure after deployment
Baseline application use and detect anomalies
Ensure optimal applications service levels across enterprise and cloud data
centers

Game On
The briefing will take place at Joystix, a 9,600 square foot arcade
showroom complete with classic video games, pinball machines, foosball
tables and more. Enjoy a Freebirds burrito bar with all the fixings and
free game play after the briefing.

Win a Nintendo NES!
Attendees who arrive on time will be entered into a drawing for a chance to
win a Nintendo NES Classic Edition with 30 classic Nintendo pre-loaded
games including Legend of Zelda, Super Mario Bros, Donkey Kong and Pac Man.

JoySticks Cybersecurity Event
August 15 | 4:30 – 7:30 p.m.
1820 Franklin St, Houston, TX 77002
