Prosimy o przysyłanie propozycji prezentacji

OWASP Poland team is happy to announce that on *6th October 2015 in Krakow*
we will host local event as part of *OWASP EEE*
<http://owaspeee.appsec.xyz/>.
The objective of the OWASP's EEE (Eastern European Event) is to raise
awareness about application security and to bring high-quality security
content provided by renowned professionals in the European region. Everyone
is free to participate in OWASP and all our materials are available under a
free and open software license.
This initiative will enable participants to get the most out of OWASP
events and meetings in the region. During approximately a week OWASP
chapters will organize events and meetings that will be broadcasted live so
that every day, people could watch presentations from another chapter.

*Call for papers is now open!*. If you like to join us, please send your
proposal using this form
<https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1vvlf8THhK3Pmx2z-L5o9SK2mb75cdqRRnlZpTn_uvNQ/viewform>
.
- If that's a problem, we are able to reimburse your travel expenses.
- Presentation should be delivered in English.
- Presentation will be streamed live and will be available to watch after
the event.
- OWASP Speaker Agreement and presentation templates
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Speaker_Agreement>.
