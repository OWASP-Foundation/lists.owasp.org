around the world. In New York, Boston, Washington DC and Italy, Great
Britain, Belgium, Poland and Turkey OWASP enthusiasts will meet to discuss
meeting's topics with the best specialists. The meeting of Poland OWASP
Chapter will be held in Cracow on September the 6th.

We will meet at the University of Technology and Science, building B1,
room 104.

Interia.pl and Przemyslaw Skowron, system architect in Interia
specializing in security issues. The invitation was also accepted by
Jaroslaw Sajko, who is one of the best polish application security
specialist.

The event's agenda:
6 p.m. - 7 p.m. Robert 'shadow' Pajak OWASP7 p.m.
8 p.m. Przemyslaw 'rezos' Skowron OWASP SpoC8 p.m.
9 p.m. Penetration test - OWASP in practise
