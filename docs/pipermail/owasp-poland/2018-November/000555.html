Witam,

Serdecznie zapraszam na kolejne warszawskie spotkanie OWASP. Tym razem
korzystamy z gościnności organizatorów konferencji Advanced Threat Summit (
atsummit.pl), na którą przy okazji serdecznie zapraszam - z kodem OWASP
przysługuje 10% zniżki. Nasze spotkanie rozpocznie się o godzinie 18:30 w
miejscu konferencji, czyli w hotelu Mariott. Będziemy mieli do dyspozycji
obszerną salę plenarną, więc wszyscy się pomieścimy J

W trakcie spotkania będziemy mogli posłuchać wyjątkowego prelegenta –
globalnego lidera OWASP – Martina Knoblocha. Poniżej pełna agenda spotkania.



Dodatkowo uczestnicy spotkania OWASP będą mogli bezpłatnie wziąć udział w
ostatniej sesji wieczornej konferencji Advanced Threat Summit o godzinie
17:30 - „Hakowanie umysłów - czyli mózg jako komputer i co z tego wynika
dla użytkowników i menedżerów bezpieczeństwa” – wykład Marka Różalskiego.

Link do rejestracji na MeetUp:

https://www.meetup.com/owasp-poland/events/255963198/

Liczę, że się zobaczymy!


Pozdrawiam, Michał





18:30 – 18:35 – Meeting opening, Michał Kurek



18:35 – 19:20 – *Building secure software with OWASP tools and guides*,
Martin Knobloch, OWASP Foundation CEO



All know the OWASP TopTen, some one or more other projects. The problem is,
where the wiki is good to archive the project information, it is hard to
find information if you don't know what you are looking for. Therefore,
only a few have a broader understanding of projects (being tools or guides)
you can use in your software development lifecycle, even before, to create
more secure software. This talk is highlighting various OWASP projects
taking you from CISO policies, security requirements to building and secure
software and verifying the security level.



Martin is  the Chairman of the OWASP Global Foundation Board Of Directors
and OWASP Netherlands Chapter Leader. He is Head of Global Application
Security at Ceva Logistics. His main working area is (software) security in
general, from awareness to implementation. In his daily work, he is
responsible for education in application security matters, advise and
implementation of application security measures. He represents OWASP on
many security, developer and testing conferences.





19:20 – 19:50 – *IoT Application Security*, Michał Kurek, OWASP Poland
Chapter Leader



Internet of Things (IoT) is revolutionizing the world around us. More and
more devices are being interconnected. Since IoT is an interface between
cyber and physical world, IoT vulnerabilities can impact lives and
environment. Security researches alerts that application security is one of
the IoT weakest points. What can be done to improve the situation? Which
OWASP Projects can help?



Michał works in KPMG as Partner responsible for development of cyber
security services. He has more than 15 years of experience in managing team
of security consultants and engineers. He performed hundreds of IT security
projects for Polish and foreign institutions. He has strong technical
background, specializing in network and application security. He is OWASP
Poland Chapter leader and Mentor of SANS Institute in the area of web
applications security. He has obtained the following professional
certifications: CISM, CISSP, GICSP, CISA, LPT, CEH, GWAPT, CRISC, CCSP,
CCSA, GSSP JAVA, ECSA, PMP, CIA, GAWN, CCNA
