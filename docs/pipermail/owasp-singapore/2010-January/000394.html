*MOZILLA FIREFOX BENCHMARK v1.0.0 RELEASED*

The /Security Configuration Benchmark for Mozilla Firefox 3.5/, provides
prescriptive

guidance for establishing a secure configuration posture for Mozilla
Firefox 3.5. This

guide was tested against Firefox 3.5.6 running Microsoft Windows XP and
Windows Vista.

 

Special thanks to Waqas Nazir of Digital Security, LLC for authoring the
benchmark and

to Ron Colvin of NASA, Patrick McCafferty of VMC, Inc, Andy Sampson of
the NSA, and

David Skrdla of the University of Oklahoma for their outstanding
contributions to this benchmark.

 

To download the benchmark go here:
https://community.cisecurity.org/download
