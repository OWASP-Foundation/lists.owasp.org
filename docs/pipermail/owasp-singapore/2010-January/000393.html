Hi Subhasis,

Some companies I know just banned access to social networking sites from
their corporate network.
Of course, this does not stop chatterbox employees to access the sites
when they access from home.
So, blocking access from corporate network is a half-fence solution.

In addition, I am not sure about the legality of any social media policy
that extends into usage of such sites after office hours, as that sounds
like encroaching into the employees' personal time.

Just my $0.02. :-)
