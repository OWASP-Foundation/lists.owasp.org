Hi folks,

A poll was created on http://www.infothatmatter.org/ to seek your
feedback on whether we, as a group, should disclose the identities of
the organisations who are responsible for info leaks.

This is similar to the US regulation on disclosure of data breaches,
albeit we are doing it in a community approach.

Do note the disclosures of identities are still subjected to the code of
ethics, such as no disclosure of leaks' URLs.

Hope to hear from all of you.
