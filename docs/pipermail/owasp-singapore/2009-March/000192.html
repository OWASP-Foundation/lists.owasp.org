Hi,

 

Please be inform that we recently launched the HP SWFScan
<http://www.hp.com/go/swfscan>, a free tool to help Flash developers
protect their websites against unintended application security
vulnerabilities and reduce the risk of hackers accessing sensitive data.

 

HP SWFScan allows Flash developers to deliver more secure code without
becoming security experts. The tool is the first of its kind to
decompile applications developed with the Flash platform and perform
static analysis to understand their behaviors. This helps identify
vulnerabilities that lie under the surface of an application and are not
detectable with traditional dynamic methods.

 

A free download of HP SWFScan is available at www.hp.com/go/swfscan
<http://www.hp.com/go/swfscan>.

 

This is a great yet free tool for flash developers. Can you help to
promote this free tool to your members by listing it in your OWASP local
chapter or newsletter?
