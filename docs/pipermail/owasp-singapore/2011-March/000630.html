Charting New Frontiers, Information Security as an Enabler

 

/*G*//*uest of Honor -- Mr Lui Tuck Yew, Minister for Information,
Communications and the Arts*/

Amidst the global backdrop of emerging infocomm security threats,
governments and organisations around the world are undergoing conceited
developments to enhance their infocomm security strategies. On the local
front, the Singapore Government has been proactive and innovative in our
approach to strengthen our infocomm security capabilities. 

The theme for this year's Seminar is "Charting New Frontiers,
Information Security as an Enabler" and centres on public, private and
people sector collaboration in enhancing our capabilities to prepare
Singapore against new and emerging infocomm security threats.
Singapore's 1st Cyber Security Awareness Day will also be launched, in
conjunction with the Information Security Seminar 2011. While we have
various outreach efforts throughout the year, the Awareness Day serves
as a nation-wide call to action to take personal responsibility to "Go
safe online".
