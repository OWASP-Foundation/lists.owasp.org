Announcing a new August Meetup for OWASP!

What: A walkthrough of the recent (closed) security vulnerability in a
SG govt web site

When: August 13, 2009 7:00 PM

Where:
Cairnhill Community Club
1 Anthony Road Room #03-05
Singapore
98387930

In this meetup, the following items will be covered:

1) Step-by-step walkthrough of how the vulnerability was discovered.
- Learn how to be alert to spot future vulnerabilities. Be an extra pair
of eyes to keep Singapore secure.

2) Demonstration/illustration of potential exploits of the vulnerability
- Learn how the public may be affected by this vulnerability.

3) Analysis of the possible causes and suggested solutions
- Discuss how such vulnerability may arise and how one can prevent
future occurrence

Do note the identity of the SG govt site will continue to be hidden, so
no questions on the identity will be entertained.

The vulnerability had already been reported and closed by the relevant
parties.

Learn more here:
http://security.meetup.com/77/calendar/10873364/

Onn Chee
