Bio:
Eugene Teo works for the Red Hat Security Team. He focuses on Linux
kernel security. He has been an active member of the Linux and open
source community in Singapore for over a decade, having held different
portfolios within the Linux Users' Group of Singapore. Eugene has spoken
at numerous conferences, including the Red Hat Summit, GNOME.Asia, and
Linux Conference Australia.
