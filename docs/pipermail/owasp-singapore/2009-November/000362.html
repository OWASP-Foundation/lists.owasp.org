Hi Ruel,

I gather that quite a number of fellow IS pros have given their  
feedback.

I can only advise that tools cannot replace the expertise a human pro  
can bring.

In one of my previous presentations on the serious vulnerability of an  
e-Gov portal, the portal had been pentested and perhaps scanned by  
vul. scanner(s) too.
But that did not prevent the hole from being present.

So do remember to place strong emphasis on the people beside the tool,  
whichever you may pursue.

Just FYI, if the eGov portal vulnerability was exploited, Singapore   
may become a botnet heaven or most Singaporeans may be forced to  
change their Singpass due to phishing scams.

So will you risk the above by placing 100% confidence on the branded  
tools?

I have said my piece.
Thank you for your time reading my $0.02 above.

Regards,

Onn Chee
