Hi,

I'd like to anounce the "Best Practice Guide: Use of Web Application Firewalls"
now available in English at
   https://www.owasp.org/images/a/a6/Best_Practices_Guide_WAF_v104.en.pdf

This is the translation of the paper published in the German Chapter in March
   http://www.owasp.org/images/1/1b/Best_Practices_Guide_WAF.pdf

This paper is aimed at technical decission-makers, especially those responsible
for operations and security, as well as application owners evaluating the use of
a WAF. Special attention has been paid to the display of work estimates and in
comparsion to possible alternatives such as modifications of the source code.
Further key topics discussed include best practice for processing the 
installation
and operation of a WAF.

The paper was presented at the AppSec Europe 2008 in Gent by Alexander Meisel,
He will present it again at the AppSec 2008 Conference in NYC, see
   https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_NYC_AppSec_2008_Conference

Feedback and discussion is wellcome at
   owasp-germany at lists.owasp.org or directly to the authors listed in the
document.
