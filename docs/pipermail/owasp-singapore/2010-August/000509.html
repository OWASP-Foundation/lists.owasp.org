 Hi folks,

These are the screenshots from the Calomel SSL validation add-on for
Firefox.
Now, as an end user, you can visually see the SSL quality of the
"secure" sites you are visiting.

Screenshots of DBS, UOB, OCBC and SingPass are included for your review.
Accordingly to the Calomel report, in descending order of SSL strength:

1) SingPass - Best of 4 (green - 100%)
2) DBS (blue - 72%)
3) OCBC and UOB (orange - 48%)

Hmmm....did not realise OCBC and UOB are using RC4 symmetric encryption
in their SSL....

Any one who can share these findings with OCBC and UOB?
