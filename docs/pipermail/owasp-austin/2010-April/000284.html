Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour
Austin OWASP and Capitol of Texas ISSA would like to cordially extend an
invitation for you to attend our April Austin Security Professionals Happy
Hour sponsored by Fortify.  The Happy Hour will be held on Thurday, April
22, 2010 from 5 PM to 7 PM at Sherlock's Baker Street Pub.  We will be in
the party room located off to your right as you enter the main doors.
Don't miss out on this excellent opportunity for you to enjoy food and
drinks while networking with fellow security professionals from Austin and
the surround areas.

Sherlock's Baker Street Pub
9012 Research Blvd
Austin, TX 78757
(512) 380-9443
http://www.sherlockspubco.com
The Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour happens tomorrow.  Come see
what all the fun is about!
