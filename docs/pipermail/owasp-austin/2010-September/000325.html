Well over 100 people have registered so far for the Lonestar Application

Security Conference (LASCON) happening in Austin on October 29, 2010,
but

thing out and if you're stalling, you might just miss your opportunity
to 
see 20 of the country's top security minds for as low as 30$!  If you 
haven't registered at this point, what are you waiting for?  After

 You know a good deal when  you see one.  Certainly you want to be "in

membership.  Enter your credit card number.  Done.  Now we'll see you at

the Norris Conference Center in just over a month!
