Twenty years ago the Secret Service raided a lot of hackers and
phreakers.  Locally they busted Steve Jackson's company, seized his
computers, BBS, and office equipment.  These events led directly to
the formation of EFF-Austin, the EFF, and indirectly to many of the
other cyber rights organizations we have today.

Join Steve Jackson, Bruce Sterling and others as we discuss the
events, the changes, and the future of cyber rights.

It's free (but donations to offset event costs are welcomed).

It's got free beer from Independence Breewing.
