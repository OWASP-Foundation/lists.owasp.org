Hello Everyone!

This is a reminder that the Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour for
September is this Thursday, September 8, 2016.  We will be at Sherlocks
Baker St. Pub & Grill at 183 and Burnet.

Our sponsor will be Rapid7, and they will be offering a 90-day trial of
Metasploit Pro for the giveaway!

Here are the details:
