Hello everyone!

The *Lonestar Application Security Conference (LASCON) 2017
<https://lascon.org> *is now 48 days away.  Our many months of planning is
proving worthwhile, and we want to share with you some exciting details.
Please read through the following, and be sure to pay special attention to
the training offerings.

*Keynote Speakers*


We have two great keynote speakers lined up: *Chris Nickerson *and *Georgia
Weidman*

*Conference Schedule*

The conference schedule is posted on our website - see
https://lascon.org/schedule/.  Check out all the great sessions and start
planning which ones you want to attend.


*Paid Training*


   - The training days are Tuesday & Wednesday, October 24-25, 2017.
   - We are offering 1-Day and 2-Day training classes.  Details of those
   training classes can be viewed on the website - see
   https://lascon.org/training/.
   - It is important to note that there are separate Eventbrite tickets for
   the 1-Day and 2-Day training.  Each ticket provides option to select just
   one of the training classes, listing only the title of the training.
   - Each training class has limited seating/space, so be sure to register
   for one you wish to attend.

*OWASP Top-10 Training (free)*

   - We are pleased to provide an OWASP Top-10 Training again this year.
   This is a great intro type of training for those who are new to application
   security.
   - It will be an all-day training from 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM on Wednesday,
   October 25, 2017, at Norris Conference Center.
   - Lunch will not be provided, but places to eat are within walking
   distance.
   - You can register for this free training at the following link -
   https://lascon2017-free-training.eventbrite.com.
   - These tickets will go fast, so be sure to sign up if wish to attend.

*Conference Registration*

   - If you have not already done so, get your Conference ticket today.
   - Discounts are still available through the end of this month (Sept
   30th):

*OWASP Member*


   - If you are an OWASP Member, you can register and receive a discount on
      your ticket. Normally $349, discounted at $279 ... that is a savings of
      *$70*!
      - You will need your OWASP Membership Number.  If you do not know it,
      log in to the OWASP Foundation Community Portal (
      https://myowasp.force.com/login <https://myowasp.force.com/login>),
      go to Profile, and your Membership Number will be listed.
      - To register, use this following link - https://lascon2017.eventbrit
      e.com?discount=OWASPMember
      <https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com/?discount=OWASPMember>.  It will
      require you to enter your Membership Number.

*Non-Member*


   - Not currently an OWASP Member?  For a limited time we are offering a *$50
      discount* to those on this OWASP Austin list.
      - We would, however, like to encourage you to become an OWASP
      Member.  Not only would you be giving back to our local community,
      supporting our local chapter, but you would get the additional discount
      amount.  To become a member, go to https://myowasp.force.com/C
      PBase__store?site=a1e0B000007AckPQAS
      <https://myowasp.force.com/CPBase__store?site=a1e0B000007AckPQAS>.
      - Otherwise, you can use the following link to get the limited time
      discount - https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com?discount=AustinOWASP
      <https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com/?discount=AustinOWASP>.

*Sponsorships*

Sponsors help make LASCON be an affordable conference, and we have some
great volunteers diligently contacting various companies to seek their
interest.  We still have some sponsorship opportunities available, so if
your company, or perhaps you know of a company, is interested in sponsoring
LASCON this year, please send any information to our Sponsor Coordinator,
Matt Snider, at matt.snider at owasp.org. Thanks!


*Other Pertinent Details (for those not already aware)*

*When:* Tuesday & Wednesday, October 24-25, 2017 (Training)
            Thursday & Friday, October 26-27, 2017 (Conference)


*Where:* Norris Conference Center, 2525 W. Anderson Lane, Suite 365,
Austin, Texas 78757

*What: *The Lonestar Application Security Conference (LASCON) is an OWASP
conference held annually in Austin, TX. It is a gathering of 400+ web app
developers, security engineers, mobile developers and information security
professionals. LASCON is held in Texas where more Fortune 500 companies
call home than any other state, and is held in Austin which is a hub for
startups in the state of Texas. At LASCON, leaders at these companies along
with security architects and developers gather to share cutting-edge ideas,
initiatives, and technology advancements.


If you have any questions about the above information, please send them
directly to my email address or to austin at owasp.org.

Thanks!
Tiana Chandler
OWASP Austin Chapter Leader
