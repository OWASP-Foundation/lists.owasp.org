I wanted to send out a reminder to everyone who may not have yet purchased
their LASCON 2017 <https://lascon.org> conference registration ticket.
There are two discounts which are *expiring tonight at midnight.*

   - To receive a *$50 discount*, use the following link:
   https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com?discount=AustinOWASP
   <https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com/?discount=AustinOWASP>.


   - If you are an *OWASP Member*, you can get a *$70 discount*, using the
   following link:  https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com?discount=OWASPMember
   <https://lascon2017.eventbrite.com/?discount=OWASPMember>. Note that for
   this ticket option, you would need to provide your OWASP Member ID, which
   can be obtained from the OWASP Foundation Community Portal (
   https://myowasp.force.com/login <https://myowasp.force.com/login>, go to
   Profile, and your Membership Number will be listed).

We take pride in our ability to provide a quality two-day conference while
keeping the cost to you very low (compared to other similar conferences).
So please take a look at the conference schedule
<https://lascon.org/schedule/> and plan to attend.

If you have any questions, please let us know.

Thanks!
Tiana Chandler
OWASP Austin Chapter Leader / LASCON Planning Committee
