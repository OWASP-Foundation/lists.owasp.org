> Over the last year and a half we've done a lot with Austin OWASP to try and
> do more with and for the security community in addition to the monthly
> meetings.  We now have regular happy hours, weekly study groups, a free
> half-day seminar, and a kickass conference.  A co-worker gave me an idea

> to throw it out to you all to see if it interests people.
>
> I'm going to tentatively call the idea "Secure Tacos" because it combines
> my passion for security with my love for the Don Juan breakfast taco at Juan
> in a Million (http://www.juaninamillion.com).  Basically, it could be
> weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or whatever, but we'd meet on a fairly regular
> basis in the morning for breakfast and to talk about the latest news in
> security.  You know, whatever the hot topic for the week is.  Anonymous,
> 0-day, acquisitions, cool tools....anything security related.  We'd do it in
> the morning at say 8 AM and it'd only last an hour or so.  Good idea?  Bad
> idea?  Let me know if you're interested.
