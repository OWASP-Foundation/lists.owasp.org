Job title: Analyst 1 Security Operations 1 HR:587
Below is the description:

Summary of Responsibilities:

Responsible for ensuring that ERCOT's internal and external security posture
is monitored for the latest threats and risks. Provides recommended actions
and implements them as needed. Responsible for management and administration
of Information Security technologies at ERCOT.

Essential Job Duties:

•  Monitors computing resources for evidence of compromise and report
security incidents.
•  Responds to, and report on, IT security incidents, providing initial
assessment of impact severity and types of incidents being addressed.
•  Analyzes compromised computing resources of routine to moderate
complexity, to assist in improving security design and policy compliance,
and to understand and document new threat profiles.
•  Develops and implements mitigation strategies for threat reduction based
on monitoring of IT systems.
•  Researches, analyzes, designs, tests and implements new or vendor
supplied security software solutions.
•  Provides up to date documentation and procedures on security software
product administration.
•  Assists in the development of technical security standards to support
policies, including creating, coordinating and monitoring standards and
incident investigation procedures
•  Work as a member of the Incident Response Team and perform analysis of
security breaches.
•  Studies and maintains current knowledge of security issues
•  Performs other duties as assigned.

Education:

•  Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or related
field or six years applicable experience is required.
Certifications & Licenses
•  CISSP and/or other security certifications preferred.

 Work Experience

•  Minimum of 1 year (in excess of degree requirements stated above) of
progressively responsible experience in IT.
