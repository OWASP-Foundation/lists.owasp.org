This year's election boasts the largest number of candidates and the largest % voter turnout of any election.<br>
<br>
Thank you to all the candidates and to all the members who took the time to review the information and cast their vote.<br>
<br>

<br>
<a href="http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/7c9d3fa3b2/ec2b956171/0da222332b" target="_blank"><font face="Verdana"><span style="font-family:"Verdana","sans-serif"">THE ELECTION PAGE</span></font></a><br>
<br>
<br>
<font face="Verdana"><span style="font-family:"Verdana","sans-serif"">Special Referendum<br>
<br>
This year, there was an included community refforandum to assist the foundation in defining our approach to handling corporate sponsorhip.<br>
<br>
The three options posted were:<br>
<br>
1 - Project Leaders Decide All<br>
<br>
2 - Standardized across projects & <strong><b><font face="Verdana"><span style="font-family:"Verdana","sans-serif"">allowing</span></font></b></strong> project sponsorship/logos<br>
<br>
3 - Standardized across projects and <strong><b><font face="Verdana"><span style="font-family:"Verdana","sans-serif"">NOT allowing</span></font></b></strong> project sponsorship/logos<br>
<br>
The community has chosen <strong><b><font face="Verdana"><span style="font-family:"Verdana","sans-serif"">#2</span></font></b></strong> as their preferred choice.<br>
<br>
The Foundation Board will work with the Operations team and the Project Leaders to create and document guidelines in this direction.<br>
<br>
Details on all three options can be found on the <a href="http://cts.vresp.com/c/?TheOWASPFoundation/7c9d3fa3b2/ec2b956171/48e11e4590" target="_blank">
Governance/Project Sponsorship page</a></span></font><o:p></o:p></p>
