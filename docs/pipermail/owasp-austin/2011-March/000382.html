1) National Instruments - Programmer Architect
The Programmer Analyst?s primary role is to support all business areas of 
the company by developing and implementing software applications and 
coordinating with business end-users.   Requires the ability to thoroughly 
analyze current and future business processes, make suggestions for 
continuous improvement, articulate requirements to meet business 
objectives, and, finally, define proposed solutions for these 
requirements. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.

These solutions may consist of new or changed processes and/or 
technologies. Working with both the business areas and the development 
teams, the PA will work on the full Project Management Life Cycle (PMLC) 
from project initiation through implementation and post-production 
support.

Core Job Responsibilities
● Serve as part of a highly-skilled cross functional teams providing 
technical and business solutions to extend and enhance our corporate 
business applications
● Assists with project purpose definition, cost/benefit analysis, process 
modeling, and takes part in the planning, design, development, testing, 
implementation, and operations of our corporate business applications, 
including participation in all systems life cycle and project management 
activities 
● Analyze, identify, research, and recommend technology and business 
solutions to meet business requirements
● Authors test plans, conducts testing/QA and delivers end-user training
● Become a technical expert on computer and IT technologies
● Assists with troubleshooting and escalation of issues
● Guides day-to-day work for team members including involvement with PA 
weekly status meetings and reviews.
● Relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish 
goals.
● Provides thorough and appropriate communication to business owners and 
project teams. 
Position Requirements 

● Bachelors degree in Management Information Systems, Computer 
Information Systems, Computer Science, or Engineering
● 3 to 5 years of application or software development experience 
including prior roles in business analysis, requirements gathering, and 
authoring detailed system and process requirements and specifications
● Minimum 1-3 years proven project successes, demonstrating increases in 
productivity/efficiencies, customer satisfaction,  and revenue and 
decreasing costs and deployment time
● Strong data extraction/analysis and requirements gathering /authoring 
abilities.
● Working knowledge of PMLC and SDLC methodologies and practices 
● Self starter able to work independently and in a team environment
● Excellent communication skills ? both written and verbal.  Ability to 
communicate complex ideas with influence outside working group
● Strong organizational skills ?able to handle multiple tasks with 
varying priorities
● Strong interest in working with technology and programming
● Proficiency in multiple technologies, proficiency in SQL a must.  
● Experience in Sales Operations, Engineering and/or Manufacturing 
environments a great advantage 
Let me know if you're interested in this job and I'll get your information 
to the right people.
