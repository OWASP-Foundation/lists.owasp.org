Josh,

We completed our 6th internal CISSP study group session at Dell this
past Spring and were just discussing starting our 7th study group.

I'd like to join forces and have a few of my certified and my
non-certified people participate.

You may want to consider shortening the meeting from 3 hours and only
conducting a 1 hour per week meeting and including a conference bridge.
I'm willing to supply the bridge if people cannot attend in person, plus
most of us are very busy and can only focus/retain the material at most
in a 90 minute period once a week.

Also, we use Shon Harris' All-in-One CISSP Exam Guide 4th Edition
(includes a cdrom) and go through each chapter every week. We start on
chapter 3 (Risk Management) since the first 2 chapters are introductory.
The review book has 10 chapters of content and roughly takes us 12-14
weeks to complete (we've found 2-3 chapters take more than 1 session).

I hope this helps and completely flexible and supportive of this
community activity.

Thanks,

Phil Agcaoili, CISM, CISSP
Dell | Global Information Security Assurance & Consulting
