Austin OWASP Chapter October 2007 National Instruments Meeting

Topic: Social Network Security - Rich Vazquez and Tom Brown

National Instruments, 11500 N Mopac, Building C which is the tallest building on campus (8 levels). Please wait in the lobby and a NI employee will escort you up to the 5th floor.  There will be signs posted in the lobby to direct you where to go and the receptionists will also be able to assist you. If you have any trouble locating us, you can call James Wickett at 512 964 6227 or Scott Foster 512-637-9824.  See directions to National Instruments <http://www.ni.com/contact/map2hq.htm> . 

 

For the most current information on meetings, times and locations for the Austin chapter see Local News http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Austin#Next_Meeting

 

About the presentation: 
Social networking is exploding with ways to create your own social networks. As communities move more and more online and new types of communities start to form, what are some of the security concerns that we have and might face in the future?

About the presenter: 
Rich Vázquez, CISSP, CISA is a board member of the Austin Time Exchange Network and Single Parent Resource Network. 

Tom Brown organized MashpitOpenIDAustin and is to blame for Stuffopolis. 

 

Rich and Tom blog at opensourcecurrency.org.
