Howdy OWASPers,

  We hope that everyone who came out to the Austin Security Professionals
Happy Hour last night had a great time.  It was so good to see so many
people and learn of all the various happenings with everyone.  We couldn't
have had a better sponsor this month in SafeNet.  A great sponsor and great
people made for a great time.  If you missed it yesterday, come join us
next month on Thursday, March 13th.

Also in March is the BSides Austin on March 20th and 21st .   For the 5th
year now, Austin will be hosting a fascinating and amazing BSides that you
have to attend to believe how lucky Austin is to have here.  See the
attachment or go learn more and *register* at www.BSidesAustin.com

Don't forget the chapter meeting coming on Tuesday, 2/25, 11:30-1 at
National Instruments.  Please RSVP for the lunch meeting at
owasp-austin-2014-february.eventbrite.com.   Everyone is welcome, whether
you are a member of OWASP or not.   We hope to see everyone there!

Till next time,
