Howdy folks!

Don't forget that the Security Professionals Happy Hour is this Thursday,
August 14th  from 5-7 PM at Sherlocks Baker Street Pub and Grill, 183 &
Burnet (we meet in the large room to the right as you walk in, normally on
the far side of the room.).  If you are an OWASP or ISSA member or just
interested in InfoSec, come out for drinks, good food, and some amazing
conversations.

Our sponsor this month is Trustwave!  They have generously decided to
provide an Apple TV as a giveaway.  Bring your business cards and drop them
in the jar to enter!  Must be present to win.

A word from our sponsor --
The Trustwave suite of application security solutions, delivered by an
expert team of application specialists, ensures that your application is
tested and reviewed thoroughly. The application security team uses manual
processes to test and review applications according to your needs. The
result is specific guidance that can significantly improve the security of
your applications and protect your business.
--

It is not not required, but if you want to RSVP --
https://aus-sec-happy-hour-2014-08.eventbrite.com

Hope to meet everyone there!
