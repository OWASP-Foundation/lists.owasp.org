Just a quick note to let the Austin Chapter know about an upcoming event:

(ISC)2 SecureSDLC day in Austin

Friday June 12 from 9 AM to 5 PM

Summary:
http://www.cvent.com/events/-isc-2-securesdlc-austin2015/event-summary-7702da885b9e4508ba878b4aad85c75c.aspx

Come see some home-town AppSec pro's speak on the theme “Software Security
– Moving from Prescriptive to Pragmatic.”

Speakers include:

   - Michael Howard
   - Mano Paul
   - Dan Cornell
   - Phil Beyer
   - James Wickett
   - Matt Tesauro (sorry, shameless self-promotion)

Agenda:
http://www.cvent.com/events/-isc-2-securesdlc-austin2015/agenda-7702da885b9e4508ba878b4aad85c75c.aspx

FYI:  I'll be speaking about using open source tools to construct your own
AppSec Pipeline to add DevOps and Automation to your internal security
program.  This is something we're actively doing where I work and I'll talk
about both the key requirements as well as how we've specifically created
our own AppSec Pipeline.  I'll also discuss where you can get these open
source tools/projects to super-charge your AppSec program.

Hope to see you there.
