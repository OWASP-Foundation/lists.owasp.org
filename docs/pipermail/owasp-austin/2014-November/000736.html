For those who are interested in the OWASP Austin Study Group, please read
on.

First, I appreciate those who took the time to submit their vote for the
next study group.  We had twice as many responses from last time, so that
helps.  Unfortunately, even with the higher number of responses, they were
very diverse which resulted in fractions of difference in the average
rankings.  However, to create a fair approach to taking everyone's input
into consideration, the next study group topic will be one receiving the
highest average ranking, which is "The Browser Hacker's Handbook".

"Instant Burp Suite Starter" came in a close second, followed by "Threat
Modeling: Designing for Security".

[image: Inline image 3]
With the top three being so close, perhaps we can discuss making those the
next two topics.

So, what is next?  First, getting the book, which can be found on Amazon or
Barnes and Noble:

   - The Browser Hacker's Handbook
      -
      http://www.amazon.com/Browser-Hackers-Handbook-Wade-Alcorn/dp/1118662091

      http://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/the-browser-hackers-handbook-wade-alcorn/1115807057?ean=9781118662090

For those of you who participated in the majority of meetings in the last
study group, I will contact you separately about the perk that we
previously discussed.

The first meeting would be Thursday next week, December 4th.  But if we
need another week to obtain the books, then it may get pushed out another
week.  I will send out an email to confirm.

Again, thanks for all your input and feedback, and I look forward to seeing
you at the next study group meeting.

For any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact me.

Happy Thanksgiving!
