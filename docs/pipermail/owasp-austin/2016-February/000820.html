Here are some reminders for this month's events:


*OWASP Austin Chapter Monthly Meeting*


Next Tuesday, February 23rd, James Wickett will be presenting *Rugged
DevOps Using Gauntlt*.  If you wish to attend and have not done so
already, *please RSVP
on Eventbrite <http://owasp-austin-2016-february.eventbrite.com/> by noon
this Friday, February 19th,* so the food order can be placed.

*RSVP *— http://owasp-austin-2016-february.eventbrite.com


For those who cannot attend the meeting but wish to listen via GoToWebinar,
you can register at the following link:

https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/121805370154086157



*Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour **sponsored by Rapid7*


The Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour is *tomorrow evening,
Thursday, February 18th*.  If you wish to attend but have not done so,
please *RSVP* at http://aus-sec-happy-hour-2016-02.eventbrite.com.


*OWASP Study Group*

The OWASP Study Group is finishing up the final several chapters of the
book "*Snapshot - Reading and Treating People Right the First Time*".
Discussions
are already underway for the next topic, and more information can be found
in the group discussion forum titled Future Study Group Ideas (Ongoing
Discussion Forum)
<http://myowasp.ning.com/groups/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/future-study-group-ideas-ongoing-discussion-forum>
on
the my.owasp.org site.
