About the presentation: 
"Biting the hand that feeds you" - A presentation on hosting malicious
content under well know domains to gain a victims confidence.
"Virtual World, Real Hacking" - A presentation on "Virtual Economies"
and game hacking. 
"Cover Debugging - Circumventing Software Armoring techniques" - A
presentation on advanced techniques automating and analyzing malicious
code. 
  
Plus 
Bring your Laptop we will be writing shellcode and provide an
application that is vulnerable to a buffer overflow and have the
audience use the shellcode they create to exploit the sample
application. 
The interactive session will use all free tools, so that everybody can
participate.
