Study Group Reminder: Wednesday, 2/8, from noon to 1pm. This time we will
be finishing Chapter Six: Attacking Authentication. We will go through the
various pieces involved in building a secure login mechanism.

Directions and details below. Hope to see you there! And remember, Study
Group does not build on previous sessions like a math class; each session
is modular, and you can't "fall behind." Come when you can and learn what
you will.

Thanks,
Ben Broussard


Directions and Times
 - When: Wednesdays from noon to 1pm.
 - Where: We have a room in Building A of the National Instruments
campus (the monthly OWASP meetings are in Building C). The drive has
directions to the buildings clearly marked. Once in Building A take the
elevator to the 2nd floor, go down the hallway to your left past the
restrooms and turn left down the small hallway directly after the
restrooms. Conference room 217 will be the second door on the right. You
will have internet access and we'll have a projector for examples. Feel
free to call me at (512) 944-2210 for details or directions.
