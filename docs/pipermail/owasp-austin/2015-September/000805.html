For those of you interested in participating in the OWASP Study Group, this
is a reminder that we will be starting up tomorrow (Thursday) to cover the
book titled "*Snapshot - Reading and Treating People Right the First Time*"
by Dan Korem. The book can only be purchased from the following online
store and only as hardcover (no electronic format). Cost of book is $34.95
retail.

   - Amazon -
   http://smile.amazon.com/Snapshot-Reading-Treating-People-Right/dp/098933581X/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1431191284&sr=8-1&keywords=dan+korem

We will use the
http://my.owasp.org/groups/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/study-group-2015-snapshot
discussion forum to share thoughts and have discussions on this book.
Please be sure to *sign up* for the site, *join* the OWASP Austin Study
Group, and *follow* the discussion forum so that you can get alerts on
posts to the forum.

We will begin tomorrow by doing a high level review of the contents and
decide on how best to break up the discussions (i.e. by chapter).  Also, we
will need to discuss upcoming dates which we may need to skip due to other
events.  For example, next Thursday (10/8) is InnoTech and later in the
month (10/22) is LASCON.  Most likely we may skip those meeting days and
continue the following week.  Any decisions will be posted on the Study
Group discussion forum.

Looking forward to seeing you tomorrow!

Tiana Chandler
OWASP Austin Training/Study Coordinator
