Hi All,

The Austin OWASP Study Group is starting up again. This time we will be
going through the Web Application Hacker's Handbook by Dafydd Stuttard and
Marcus Pinto. Note that this is the SECOND EDITION!

I was, unfortunately, not able to get free hard or soft copies for the study
 group. You can buy it on Amazon at
http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1118026470 for around $31. For our first
meeting it will not be necessary to have a copy, but it will be our text
for this session of Study Group, so get one if you plan to attend. If the
price tag will stop you from attending, talk to me and we'll see what we
can work out.

Our first meeting will be next Wednesday, 1/11, at the same time and place
as before. Specific directions and details are below.

Thanks,
Ben Broussard, CISSP

Directions and Times
 - When: Wednesdays from noon to 1pm.
 - Where: We have a room in Building A of the National Instruments
campus (the monthly OWASP meetings are in Building C). The drive has
directions to the buildings clearly marked. Once in Building A take the
elevator to the 2nd floor, go down the hallway to your left past the
restrooms and turn left down the small hallway directly after the
restrooms. Conference room 217 will be the second door on the right. You
will have internet access and we'll have a projector for examples. Feel
free to call me at (512) 944-2210 for details or directions.
