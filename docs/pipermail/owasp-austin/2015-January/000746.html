Do you have a unique project or skill that you want to share with the local
community?  Have you ever wanted to be a speaker at conference?  Did you
make it your new years goal to present at a conference?    Austin has
opportunities!  Coming up --

BSides Austin 2015 has their CFP open --
http://bsidestexas.blogspot.com/p/austin-2013.html  (please ignore the
'2013' in the URL)
or directly at --
http://sniperforensicstoolkit.squarespace.com/bsides-austin-cfp/

They are primarily looking for 'blue team' (or defender) presentations, but
they have another track for any and all infosec related presentations.
BSides is a great local conference put on by some of the best of our
community.  Check it out!

----
Other opportunities abound, just not all are publicized.   Occasionally
OWASP Austin is contacted by other local folks for speakers on application
security topics.  Besides the great list of local speakers we have from
chapter meetings and such, OWASP Austin is looking to compile an additional
list of local people that are comfortable and potentially available to
speak on application security topics.  Anyone interested in being
potentially available?  Please email me off-list at kyle.smith at owasp.org

Thank you,
-kyle
OWASP Austin, chapter leader
