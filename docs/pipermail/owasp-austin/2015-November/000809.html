Howdy Austin OWASP-ers!

The chapter is taking a short hiatus like we always do with the last two
months of the year, gotta get over LASCON somehow --  it was a great
conference!  LASCON keeps getting better every year!

While we aren't spearheading any activity this month, ISSA Austin is
coordinating a happy hour next week on November 12th!

*Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour sponsored by Austin ISSA (Capitol
of Texas Chapter)*

Please join us on Thursday, Nov 12 from 5:30 to 7:30 at the Park at the
Domain.  We’ll be on the covered side patio.  No corporate sponsor, no
presentation, just an opportunity to share a beer, a bite, and good
conversation with like-minded Security Professionals!
