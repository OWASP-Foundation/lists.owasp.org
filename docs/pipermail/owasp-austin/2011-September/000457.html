Austin OWASPers,

This year our chapter has worked out a deal with the Austin InnoTech 
conference to host an entire OWASP track at the conference.  Presentations 
will include:

Why Application Security Matters - Matt Tesauro
Architecting Secure Web Systems - Josh Sokol
The OWASP Top 10: How They Work and How to Protect Against Them - Ben 
Broussard
Rugged Dev: Building Reliability and Security Into Software - James 
Wickett

This is in addition to security talks on:

Living Below the Security Poverty Line - Wendy Nather
Protecting the Information Infrastructure: Why CIOs and CSOs are Becoming 
Mission-Critical Business Partners - Jay McLaughlin

For more details on any of these talks or presenters, select the "Security 
Track Highlights" option at the bottom of the "Event Details" menu located 
at http://www.innotechconference.com/austin/.  Wait, it gets better...  As 
part of our agreement with InnoTech, they have offered FREE passes for all 
of our members to attend the conference (a $30 value).  Simply go to their 
website, select "Register Now" and then select "INNOTECH ATTENDEE" and use 
the discount code "OWASP11".  A full day of cool new technology, hands-on 
demonstrations, and networking for free.  Sign up today!

Sincerely,

Josh Sokol (CISSP, CCNA, GWAS)
Information Security Program Owner
National Instruments
