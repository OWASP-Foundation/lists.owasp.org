Howdy Austin OWASPers!

The chapter team is doing its final push to bring you the best conference
NEXT WEEK!  The chapter meetings are currently on hiatus till next year,
but we will be back with some of the best speakers for next year!

----------------------------------------------------------------

LASCON LASCON LASCON LASCON LASCON

We hope to see everyone at LASCON, one of the best local security
conferences ever year!  October 23rd and 24th is the conference at the
Norris Conference Center.    Some of the speakers at the conference will be:

Martin E. Hellman - Stanford Professor and co-inventor of Public Key
Cryptography
Kelly Misata - Director of Communications and Outreach for The Tor Project
Chris Nickerson - Founder, Principal Security  Consultant of Lares
Consulting.. and co-host of the Exotic Liability Podcast
Matt Konda - Founder/Principal of Jemuari
Jeff Williams - Found and CTO of Contrast Security
Jim Manico - Instructor and Author of Secure Code Training
Marcus Carey - Security Research and Lead Developer at ThreatAgent.com
Zane Lackey - Founder/CSO at Signal Sciences
along with many others!

Sign up today for LASCON at -- http://lascon.org/register/

Did we mention there is training before the conference?  Training like --

Hands on Web and REST Testing:  Assessing Apps the OWASP Way (2 days!)
Defensive Programming for Enterprise Java Web-based Applications
Developer Security Training Bootcamp
Risk Management Like a Boss
and others!

Sign up for the LASCON training at -- http://lascon.org/training/
