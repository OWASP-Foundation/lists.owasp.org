Howdy OWASPers!

LASCON <http://lascon.org>2014 (October 23-24) is shaping to be a great
event again!   The key note speakers are set and many of the featured
speakers are already signed!  But the Call for Proposal is still available
for other opportunities for speakers.   The CFP will close on July 31st, so
hurry and submit your proposal.

Did you know that there is training available before the conference on
October 21-22?  The Call for Training is open for submissions.  AppSec,
Development (and language or framework), DevOps, Management, QA, Privacy
are example topics we are looking for for training events.  If you want to
propose a training event, submit it soon as the CFT closes on July 31st!
 (Use the same submission link as the CFP.)

If you want to attend LASCON, early bird registration end on July 31st!
 Hurry and get the early bird rates!

All this and more can be found at the LASCON site at lascon.org.

-kyle
