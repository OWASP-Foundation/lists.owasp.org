Security Assessments on Steroids:
Austin, TX Training Event

Please join Rapid7 on July 13th, 2011 from noon to 6pm at Idea City in Austin, TX for a half-day training session on accelerating assessments within your organization. The event will be kicked off by HD Moore, CSO of
Rapid7 and founder of the Metasploit project.

Other presenters include James (Egypt) Lee, Jonathan Cran, Tod Beardsley, Matt Buck and other members of the Austin-based Metasploit development team. This session will consist of short presentations that demonstrate how to improve your security assessment process by leveraging both open source and commercial tools in an automated fashion.

Topics include everything from managing a security program to advanced penetration testing techniques that can be used to identify security risks both internally and externally.

Session Agenda:
Noon-1:00pm: Registration, Lunch and Keynote Speaker
1:15-2:00pm: Security Program Management
2:15-3:00pm: Reconnaissance, Discovery and Enumeration
3:15-4:00pm: Vulnerability Identification and Verification
4:15-5:00pm: Exploitation and Island Hopping
5:15-6:00pm: Remediation and Reporting
Coffee and beverages will be available in-between sessions. We will be providing a local Austin favorite dessert at the end of the day.
Address:
GSD&M
Alpha Conference Room
828 West 6th Street, Austin, TX 78703
(512) 242-4736

Directions:
Please use the main entrance when you arrive at the GSD&M offices. You'll be directed to the Rapid7 event area from there.

If you would like to attend this unique Rapid7 training event on July13th, 2011, please RSVP with your contact information to Lindsey Angione at lindsey_angione at rapid7.com.
