OWASPers,

I just wanted to remind you all that the Lonestar Application Security 
Conference (LASCON) Early-Bird registration rate ends on Sunday. Currently 
you can register to attend LASCON for only $75 as an OWASP member or $125 
for a non-member which includes a year membership ($50 value).  However, 
as of Monday, August 1st, the price goes up by $50.  If you're planning on 
attending LASCON, now is the time to register before the price goes up. 

LASCON is going down on October 28, 2011 at the Norris Conference Center 
in Austin, TX.  This year we will have four to five tracks with topics 
including Audit, Architecture, Development, and Executive Management. 
That's right, bring your boss to LASCON!  The CFP for LASCON is still open 
(submissions welcome), but speakers include Jeremiah Grossman, John 
Steven, Rafal Los, Michael Howard, Joe Jarzombek, and many more.  We have 
a happy hour event immediately after the conference sponsored by Trustwave 
and the mechanical bull will be back.  We're looking forward to seeing you 
at LASCON!

http://www.lascon.org

Sincerely,

Josh Sokol & James Wickett
LASCON 2011 Co-Chairs
