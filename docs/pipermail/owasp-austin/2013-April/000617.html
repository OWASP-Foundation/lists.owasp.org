Agile Austin U

The Agile Austin U Foundations Course is an 8 week night course that is free to our community. The intent is to introduce the core concepts of agile, Scrum, Lean Software and Kanban. There will be two volunteer instructors for each class and dinner will be provided. Proof of Attendance will be provided for those interested in claiming PDU’s.

Schedule:

4/23 – Agile Values and Principles – David Hawks / Chris Brooks

4/30 – Scrum Overview and Roles – Kincade Park / Tracy Whitehill

5/7 – Agile Requirements and User Stories – Earl Everett / Ben Allums

5/15 – Release Planning and Estimation – Walter Bodwell / Yasser Farra

5/22 – Sprint Ceremonies – Lou Bailey / TBD

5/29 – Scrum Simulation – Jason Morillo / TBD

6/5 – Kanban and Lean SW Overview – Colin Turner / Ned Horvath

6/12 – Agile Technical Practices – Rick Grashel / Eric Stewart
