I'm surprised nobody has mentioned the CEH yet.  While certainly not the 
most notable security certification (I think that goes to the CISSP), the 
Certified Ethical Hacker does a very good job at focusing on specific 
security vulnerabilities, exploits, and tools.  It's great information, 
but is nowhere near as broad as the CISSP which is a bonus considering 
your stated interest.  Plus, it's far cheaper than the other 
certifications mentioned at roughly $500 for the exam.
