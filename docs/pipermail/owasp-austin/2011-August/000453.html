food are the ones on the RSVP list.  RSVP Here -->

We have completed the CFP's for LASCON 2011 speakers and should be

 Be sure to get there early to get your preview of the awesomeness of our

http://houstonseccon.com/ and look for the Registration link on the main 
menu.  Then send me a quick note if you're interested in carpooling to 
Houston like we did last year.
