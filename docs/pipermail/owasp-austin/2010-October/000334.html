Austin OWASPers,

As you all know by now, tomorrow (10/29/2010), our OWASP chapter will be 
hosting the first-ever OWASP Regional Conference in Texas.  The full 
schedule, presentation abstracts, and speaker bios are now up on the 
conference website for you to check out.  I wanted to let you guys know 
that we have a small handful (a couple dozen) of LASCON passes still 
available before we sell out.  The rate is now $200 for all conference 
passes (member, non-member, or student) and still includes a year of OWASP 
membership (a $50 value).  If you are planning on attending, it would be a 
good idea to get your pass today by registering at http://www.lascon.org. 
If passes do not sell out, we will be offering them at the registration 
table at the conference tomorrow.  See you at LASCON!

Sincerely,
