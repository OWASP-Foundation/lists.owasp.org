Austin OWASP Members,

Some of you already know this, but I am leaving Austin and heading back to
Atlanta to run Information Security at another company.

The security community in Austin is truly remarkable having world-renowned
security experts and I have been impressed over the past 3 years on the
level of sharing, collaboration, and the overall humility in this security
community. True to the spirit of Austin, our gatherings were laid back, ego
was checked at the door, and we were all better for having each other’s
company.

I am proud to have been part of this community.

Like my Dell Global Information Security teams, I wish you all the best and
know that I will see you again.

Regards,

Phil Agcaoili
