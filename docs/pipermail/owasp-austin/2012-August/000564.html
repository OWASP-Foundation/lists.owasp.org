*When* : Thursday, August 9th from 5-7 pm


*Where*: Sherlocks Baker Street Pub and Grill at the corner of 183 and
Burnet.

 *What is it?*: The Austin Security Professionals happy hour is a
monthly gathering of information security professionals from the Austin
area, heavily represented by OWASP and ISSA membership. It is a time to
enjoy some drinks and food provided by our sponsor, an to get to know
other InfoSec professionals. Come on down and hang out with a bunch of
hackers and geeks!

* There will be drawings for a $50 Fry's Gift Card an a Free External
Vulnerability Scan for up to 5 external IP addresses, so bring your
business cards!!*


*Our Sponsor: Slait Consulting
*

 

SLAIT Consulting is a MWOBE Information Technology Services company
headquartered in Virginia Beach, VA with 7 regional offices including 
Austin, TX; revenues of $135M a year and over 400 employees.  SLAIT
provides Security, Cloud and Staff Augmentation services.  Specific
security services include but not limited to: 

  * Security Policy Development
  * Security Awareness Training
  * Audit Preparation and Assistance
  * Security Survey Assistance
  * Vulnerability Scanning
  * Web Application Scanning
  * Security Risk Assessment
  * Regulatory Compliance Services
