Greetings OWASP Austin Community,

The Information Security Office at the University of Texas at Austin has an open position for an Information Security Analyst as part of our Risk Management team.  This team is the proactive side of the Information Security Office, performing network vulnerability assessments, application vulnerability assessments, and risk assessments for various departments, colleges, schools, and research units on campus.  A large part of our work involves assessing in-house and third-party applications and working with developers to remediate findings.  More information can be found here:
