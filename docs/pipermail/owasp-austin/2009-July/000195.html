James asked me to send this but I thought it might be of general interest.

Apart from DEFCON, USENIX runs some of the most interesting conferences
I've been to.  In particular, I really liked USENIX Security, but now
they have a variety of conferences on security; the Workshop on Offensive
Technologies (WOOT) has some excellent papers and there's another one,
the Workshop on Large-scale Exploits and Emergent Threats (LEET) that
looks good too.

Here's the best part; all of their proceedings are immediately available
online, for free:

http://www.usenix.org/publications/library/proceedings/

Happy reading...
