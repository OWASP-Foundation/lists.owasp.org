Topic:  Slowloris: A DOS tool for Apache
Slowloris was designed and developed as a low bandwidth denial
of service tool to take advantage of an architectural design flaw in
Apache web servers. It was quickly picked up and used by Iranian
government protesters. This speech will cover the technical issues
around the design flaw, and the events prior to, during and since the
release of the tool.
