Hey Austin OWASPers,

I wanted to let everyone know that we will be starting up a new study group
book next week and there is still plenty of time for you to make plans to
join us.  For this study group session, we will be working our way through
the "Wireshark 101: Essential Skills for Network Analysis" book by Laura
Chappel (ISBN: 1893939723).  The book is divided into 8 chapters with a
total of 46 labs between them plus a challenge at the end of each chapter.
It is full of pictures and uses pre-recorded packet captures for analysis
within Wireshark.  If you've ever wondered what your traffic looks like as
it flies around on a network, then this is the book for you.

This is a brand new study group meeting to discuss a brand new book.  We
will meet every Wednesday from 12-1 PM beginning next Wednesday, May 15 in
National Instruments, Building A, Room 217.  For the first session we will
be going through the process of installing Wireshark, talking about some
basic networking concepts, and maybe even performing our first packet
capture.  If you have any questions, you can ask them in the forum topic
dedicated to this study group here:

http://myowasp.ning.com/group/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/topics/study-group-wireshark-101

Get your book ordered today and we're all looking forward to seeing you
next week.  Take care!

~josh
