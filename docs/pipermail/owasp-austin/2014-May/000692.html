Hi!

For those of you interested in the next OWASP Austin Study Group, we are
going to start the first meeting on *Thursday, May 22nd*.  The agenda for
this first meeting is:

   - First 10-15 minutes we will only take a quick look at the book's table
   of contents and determine how best we can break up the chapters for future
   meetings.  At minimum we will plan out for the next few weeks.
   - For the rest of the meeting, *Josh Sokol has graciously offered to
   lead the group through a threat modeling exercise*.  Thank you Josh!

Information for the study group is on the myowasp.ning.com website:

   - The *OWASP Austin Study Group* main group page is
   http://myowasp.ning.com/group/austinowaspstudygroup
   - The discussion forum for the current study group is titled "*Study
   Group - The Practice of Network Security Monitoring - CURRENT*" and link
   is
   http://myowasp.ning.com/group/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/topics/study-group-the-practice-of-network-security-monitoring-current

To keep up with the current status of the study group, as well as receive
any notification of changes to scheduled meeting dates, I would encourage
you to create a Ning account, *join* the *OWASP Austin Study Group* group,
then *follow* the discussion forum.

If any questions about the study group, how to join/follow the group and
forum, or other concerns, please let me know.

Thanks,
Tiana Chandler
OWASP Austin Chapter - Training/Study Coordinator
