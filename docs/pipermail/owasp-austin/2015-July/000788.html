Hello OWASP Austin group!

For those of you who have been involved or are interested in the study
group, I wanted to send a note saying that we have finished our study of
the Browser Hacker's Handbook this past week.  It was a great turnout
throughout the time we spent on the book, with lots of insightful
discussions.

We are taking a little break from doing a book study, at least for the next
month. We have three presentations lined up so far, including one starting
tomorrow (Thursday, July 16th).  Please view the discussion forum
titled "*Study
Group - Individual Presentations (July/August 2015 - CURRENT*" (
http://myowasp.ning.com/groups/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/study-group-individual-presentations-july-august-2015-current)
on the my.owasp.org site for more details.  The schedule is subject to
change, especially for the few dates that are open (we hope to fill those
soon).

If you wish to attend and participate, I would suggest signing up for the
my.owasp.org (Ning) site, then joining the "OWASP Austin Study Group"
group, and lastly "follow" the Study Group - Individual Presentations
(July/August 2015 - CURRENT
<http://myowasp.ning.com/groups/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/study-group-individual-presentations-july-august-2015-current>
forum to ensure you receive any updates on this schedule.

Please let me know of any questions you have.

Thanks!

Tiana Chandler
OWASP Austin Training/Study Coordinator
