This email is to provide an update for this month's Austin Security
Professionals Happy Hour.  I had sent out the original notification that it

Here is the updated information:

*Where: *Mister Tramps Sports Pub and Cafe, 8565 Research Blvd, Austin TX
78758 (south-east corner of Hwy 183 and Ohlen Rd, just in front of the
Target store).  We have their large room reserved.


Google Map link:
