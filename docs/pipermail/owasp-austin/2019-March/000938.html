Hi all,

A new information security organization is coming to Austin. It is ICMCP (the International Consortium of Minority Cybersecurity Professionals) which supports women and minorities in information security. Even if you aren't coming to BSides Austin, please drop by to network! This is a great opportunity to get involved with the group.

More information about the event can be found here: bit.ly/icmcpatx<https://t.co/wswYsfFd2T>
and more information about the organization can be found here: https://www.icmcp.org/

Thanks!

Matt Pardo
ICMCP - Achieving<https://www.icmcp.org/>
Achieving the consistent representation of women and minorities in cybersecurity through programs designed to foster recruitment, inclusion and retention – one person at a time. Various industry groups recognize the female cyber workforce representation as approximately 11%. Only 6% percent of STEM workers are African American compared to an overall 11% of the US workforce.
