Hi David,

I have 7 new opportunities on my team here in HP. None of these positions are backfills. My team is a rapidly growing function that plays a strategic role for securing some of HP’s most important assets. Two of the openings are college hire level (i.e. within one year of graduation, one of the roles is for an experienced AppSec Engineer (i.e. 2+ years of experience or a CS Masters), 3 of the roles are senior AppSec Engineering positions and the other one is a AppSec Architect which is a Master level role (20+ years in a developer/security role). Below is a general JD for open roles:

This position will provide technical security leadership on programs and projects to groups throughout HP as a member and representative of the HP Global Cyber Security organization.  A qualified candidate will be responsible for security consulting in all phases of the software development lifecycle to worldwide teams of developers within HP.  This candidate will not only provide consulting services, but must also have the ability to write quality secure code in Java and/or .Net which can be leveraged either directly or via way of exposed web services to IT and business groups throughout HP.

While a strong security background is desired, a strong candidate with extensive history of writing code is more than acceptable if accompanied by a strong desire to build a security skill-set.  Only candidates who can demonstrate real-world experience writing in Java and/or .NET will be considered.  We are looking for individuals who can write core libraries in addition to leveraging them within custom applications.  It is expected that this individual will review code on a regular basis.

Job Responsibilities
• Participates as a virtual member of development teams.
• Performs analysis of complex functional and business requirements to determine security ramifications
• Leads teams through threat modeling exercises
• Prepares code examples for others
• Performs security code reviews
• Designs & write central services which enhance HP's security posture & which will be leveraged across HP
• Participates in cross-functional teams
• Participates in design activities as a security subject matter expert and provide mentoring and guidance to other developers on security topics
• Assists in the confirmation of false-positives & false-negatives generated from the HP Fortify suite of solutions
• Demonstrates technical leadership, and exerts influence outside of immediate team
• Develops innovative solutions to complex problems
• Contributes to strategic direction for teams
• Performs solution design
• Applies HP and 3rd party technologies and leads design of highly complex infrastructure and software solutions
• Independently implements end- user or enterprise infrastructure or services of significant complexity
• Integrates technical expertise and business understanding to create superior solutions for HP and customers
• Mentors and consults with team members and other organizations, customer and vendors on complex issues
• Presents secure coding concepts to worldwide HP development teams
