    September Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour (Sponsored by
    Mandiant)

This will be our final happy hour of the year due to AppSecUSA and the
holidays!

Please RSVP so have an idea how many to expect. There will be five 100$
Best Buy gift cards given away so bring your business cards. 


*RSVP:*http://www.eventbrite.com/event/4216720324

*When :* Thursday, September 13th from 5:00 to 7:00pm



*Where:* Sherlocks Baker Street Pub and Grill at the corner of 183 and
Burnet.


What is it?: The Austin Security Professionals happy hour is a monthly
gathering of information security professionals from the Austin area,
heavily represented by OWASP and ISSA membership. It is a time to enjoy
some drinks and food provided by our sponsor, an to get to know other
InfoSec professionals. Come on down and hang out with a bunch of hackers
and geeks!



*Our Sponsor: Mandiant*


Mandiant is the information security industry's leading provider of
advanced threat detection and incident response solutions and services.
Mandiant provides products, professional services and education to
Fortune 500 companies, financial institutions, government agencies,
domestic and foreign police departments and leading U.S. law firms.
