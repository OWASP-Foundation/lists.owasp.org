   Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour                                 
  Austin OWASP and Capitol of Texas ISSA would like to cordially extend an  
  invitation for you to attend our January Austin Security Professionals    
  Happy Hour sponsored by Rapid7.  The Happy Hour will be held on Thurday,  
  January 13, 2010 from 5 PM to 7 PM at Sherlock's Baker Street Pub.  We    
  will be in the party room located off to your right as you enter the main 
  doors.  Don't miss out on this excellent opportunity for you to enjoy     
  food and drinks while networking with fellow security professionals from  
  Austin and the surround areas.
