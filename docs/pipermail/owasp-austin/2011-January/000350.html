Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour
Austin OWASP and Capitol of Texas ISSA would like to cordially extend an
invitation for you to attend our January Austin Security Professionals
Happy Hour sponsored by Rapid7.  The Happy Hour will be held on Thurday,
January 13, 2010 from 5 PM to 7 PM at Sherlock's Baker Street Pub.  We will
be in the party room located off to your right as you enter the main doors.
Don't miss out on this excellent opportunity for you to enjoy food and
drinks while networking with fellow security professionals from Austin and
the surround areas.

As a special treat for all attendees of the first happy hour of 2011,
Rapid7 has graciously offered to sponsor a business card drawing for a FREE
iPad!!! Don't miss out.

Sherlock's Baker Street Pub
9012 Research Blvd
Austin, TX 78757
(512) 380-9443
http://www.sherlockspubco.com
The Austin Security Professionals Happy Hour is tonight from 5-7 PM at
Sherlocks sponsored by Rapid7.  Enter your business card for fantastic odds
at winning an iPad!
