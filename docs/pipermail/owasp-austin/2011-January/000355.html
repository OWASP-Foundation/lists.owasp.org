Hi All,

At the OWASP meeting today, I announced the creation of the weekly study
group. I collected emails from those who were present and interested, but if
you're interested and didn't get a chance to sign up yet then send me an
email. It will be every Wednesday from noon to 1pm starting 2/2/11 on the NI
campus but in a different building than the monthly OWASP meetings.

The study group will start off by working through the OWASP Testing Guide.
This will be a technical, in-depth look at web application attacks and
solutions. We will have a whiteboard and projector, and will discuss and
test examples. It will be geared towards developers and penetration testers.
If that interests you then send me an email and I'll add you to the list.

Thanks,
Ben Broussard
