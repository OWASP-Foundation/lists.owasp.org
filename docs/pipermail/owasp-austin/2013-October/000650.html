I wanted to remind anyone interested in joining us that the Web Application
Defender's Cookbook Study Group will be meeting tomorrow (Wednesday,
October 9) from 12-1 PM in Building A on the National Instruments campus in
room 217.  This is the same room that we have used for most of the past
study groups.  Once again, we will be using the MyOWASP site to coordinate
the group:

http://myowasp.ning.com/group/austinowaspstudygroup/forum/topics/study-group-web-application-defender-s-cookbook-modsecurity

In our first meeting last week we did a review of the way that the book is
laid out and set forth a plan to cover it.  For tomorrow's meeting,
attendees will be expected to have downloaded version 1.1.1 of the OWASP
Broken Web Applications project VM image.  Once downloaded, go ahead and
get it running in your VM of choice (Parallels, Virtualbox, or VMWare).
Hopefully everyone will have a chance to read chapter 1 (65 pages), but I
expect that much of our efforts for this next meeting will be in getting
everyone up and running on their VMs and maybe poking around there a little
bit.  Send me an e-mail if you have any questions.

Sincerely,

Josh Sokol
