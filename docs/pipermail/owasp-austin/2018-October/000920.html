Hello everyone!

Well, the year has been going by fairly fast, and *LASCON *(
https://lascon.org) is just a few weeks away. The LASCON Planning team is
busy putting the final touches on having everything ready.  As a reminder,
here are the dates:

   - Tuesday & Wednesday, October 23-24 -  pre-conference 2-day training
   - Thursday & Friday, October 25-26 - conference sessions

For those who have already registered, we really appreciate your interest
and are excited to seeing you all there.  For those of have not registered, *it
is not too late* to do so.

*Conference - *https://lascon.org/tickets/

Here are several reasons to attend LASCON:

   - We have some great speakers lined up, along with our three
keynotes (Shannon
   Lietz <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/ElHs/keynote-shannon-lietz>,
Jayson
   Street <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/ElHr/keynote-jayson-street>,
   and David A. Cass
   <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/GIPC/keynote-david-cass>)..See full
   schedule at https://lascon.org/schedule/.
   - CMD+CTRL CTF provided by Security Innovation
   - Networking with your peers
   - Retro video console games (we are renting 4 consoles)
   - and of course the mechanical bull!


*Pre-conference 2-DayTraining - *https://lascon.org/tickets/

We still have some spaces available for the 2-day training. At $550, you
would be getting great training at a great value. Other conferences charge
quite a bit more, but we strive to bring affordable training to you and our
local community. Of course many thanks goes to our great trainers who are
helping to make this happen.

Check out each one and sign up today!

   - AppSec Automation: DevSecOps, Pipelines, APIs and Getting Things Done
   Faster w/Matt Tesauro <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/GJIt>


   - Attacking Windows w/Powershell & Enterprise Active Directory Attack
   Playbook w/Aelon Porat <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/GJPK>


   - Container Security, Serverless and Orchestration Training w/Nithin R
   Jois <https://lascon2018.sched.com/event/GJLV>


*OWASP Top-10 Training (free) *-
http://lascon2018-free-training.eventbrite.com/

We are again providing an OWASP Top-10 Training that is offered free.
Thanks goes to Josh Sokol for leading the training.  The training will have
limited seating, so please sign up as soon as possible. There will be a
wait list available, so if you register and for some reason cannot attend,
please cancel your registration.
