?TRUST, SECURITY, AGILITY: Businesses Better Prepared for Tomorrow, Today?
conference line up released ISACA Ireland conference to be held in Dublin
Ireland on 23rd October 2015



Once again Croke Park will play host to an impressive line-up of thought
leaders and practitioners in the fields of information systems audit,
cybersecurity, risk, privacy, DevOps / Application Security & enterprise
governance from Ireland and across the globe on the 23rd Oct 2015. ?This
year?s conference theme is TRUST, SECURITY, AGILITY: Businesses Better
Prepared for Tomorrow, Today".  It will focus on helping our business
partners to be dynamic and faster in meeting their goals in a well-managed
way; against the backdrop of ever evolving risks and positive disruptive
technology challenges.? said Neil Barlow, Conference Chair.

Talks given at the conference will be under six themes: IT Assurance,
Cybersecurity/Information Security, Risk Management, Privacy Management,
DevOps/Application Security and Enterprise Governance.  Details of keynote
speakers and topics have yet to be released but already the conference
boasts a fantastic programme of non-commercial presentations relevant to
the profession:

? 8 security lessons from 8bit gaming

? A Case Study: Standard Bank's journey into security and DevOps.

? A Question of Trust

? Are you ready? The hitchhiker?s guide to the integration of privacy and
security

? Getting Started With GEIT

? How to Make Rubbish risk Decisions

? How to talk about IT Governance with your boss in the elevator?

? Increasing Your Audit Relevance Using COBIT 5

? Is Protecting the Balance Sheet Really Enough?

? Legal solutions to technical privacy problems.

? Making friends with Internal Audit!

? My Data My Responsibility

? Lessons Learned From Implementing One of the World?s First Cyber Defense
Management Regulations in Israel

? Role of Information Security Professional in tackling terror

? Shadow IT risk - empirical evidence from multiple case studies

? Threat Modeling: Finding Security threats Before They Happen

? Understanding Today's Mobile App Store Ecosystem and Why You're at Risk

? What we missed at the data centre audit

Sponsored by BCC Risk Advisory, Espion, ICON, Citi, Certification Europe,
Seven Technologies Group & Sysnet Global Solutions this one day event is
open to ISACA members as well as non-members.

This conference promises to offer excellent education opportunities, the
chance to network with fellow professionals, valuable insights into present
and future trends as well as a full day of CPEs for members of professional
bodies.
