Hey All,

For anyone who's interested, the OWASP OpenSAMM team are holding a summit
in Dublin on the 27th and 28th March 2015.

They have a great agenda for the user day:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_SAMM_Summit_2015#tab=User_Day

The project day will have interactive workgroups on integrating SAMM with
OWASP project; building out the first public SAMM baseline metrics
reference repository (details soon) and more! They will also be publishing
OpenSAMM v1.1 during the summit.

For more information visit the summit page at
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_SAMM_Summit_2015 or follow them on
twitter at https://twitter.com/owaspsamm
