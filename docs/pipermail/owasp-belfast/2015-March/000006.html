*OWASP Belfast Chapter Session - March 2015*

The next OWASP Belfast Chapter session will be taking place on Wednesday
18th March at the Citi building, 60 Sydenham Rd (beside the Odyssey). The
event will have 2 great talks on the current state of the security
industry, and as usual Pizza and soft drinks will be provided. There will
also be the opportunity to avail of some free OWASP merchandise.

Itinerary of events:

   - 6:30pm - Mark Schloesser from Rapid7 will discuss "Modern security
   scalability challenges"
   - 7:05pm - A representative of the PSNI Cyber Security Division will
   discuss upcoming Cyber Security initiatives specific to Northern Ireland
   - 7:40pm - Discussion and debate over Pizza and soft drinks

You can RSVP for the event at the OWASP Belfast Meetup group at
http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Belfast/

Sign-up for the OWASP Belfast mailing list for more information about this
session, and other security related topics, at
http://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-Belfast

OWASP Belfast would like to thank InvestNI for their sponsoring of this
event and the OWASP Belfast Chapter, and Citi for the use of their venue
for this event.
