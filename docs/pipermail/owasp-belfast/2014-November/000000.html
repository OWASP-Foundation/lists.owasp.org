Hi All,

We're pleased to announce the December session for OWASP Belfast will be at
7pm on Tuesday 9th December 2014 at The MAC in the center of town (behind
St. Anne's Cathedral).

This event is sponsored by InvestNI who will be providing pizza and beers
for all attendees.  There will be a few security presentations on the
night, including a special guest speaker who we will be able to announce on
the day of the event.  More details can be found on the OWASP Belfast wiki
page at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Belfast

This event is free to all participants, all you need to do is RSVP at
http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Belfast/events/218679545/ to reserve your
place.  Note that places are limited so please book early as we are
expecting this event to fill up quickly.

Note: CPE points will be available for attendance.

For directions to the venue go to https://themaclive.com/about/opening-hours


Thanks,

Gary Robinson,

(OWASP Belfast Chapter Leaders)
