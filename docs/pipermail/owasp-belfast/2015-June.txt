Innovative and thought provoking session proposals are sought for the ISACA
Ireland 2015 Conference to be held in the Croke Park Conference Centre on 23rd
Oct 2015.  The conference theme will be around delivering value and
empowering the business against the backdrop of today's dynamic world with
an apparently never-ending stream of highly publicised cyberthreats and
data breaches.  Proposals should engage an experienced audience of
Information Security, Assurance, Audit, Cyber, DevOps, Privacy, Governance,
Risk and Compliance professionals. Last year?s conference was a huge
success with 24 speakers from 12 countries sharing real-world examples,
'war stories', case studies, successes and failures as well as examples of
actual tools and insights on emerging issues across three streams with over
250 conference delegates.

:: Conference Streams ::
 - Information Systems Audit & Assurance
 - Cybersecurity
 - Risk Management
 - Privacy
 - Compliance / Governance
 - DevOps / AppSec

:: Submission Requirements ::
 - Closing date for submissions is 30th June 2015
 - Form to submit speaker proposal: https://goo.gl/i7nfwv
 - Presentations to be 25 minutes including Q&A
 - Proposals must be in English and should describe original work
 - Shortlisted speakers will be contacted by 31st July 2015
 - Speakers will be reimbursed their travel expenses as outlined on the
submission form
 - Commercially focused/vendor promotional proposals will not be considered.
 - ISACA membership is not required for submission
 - Contact conference at isaca.ie if you have problems accessing form or have
other questions
