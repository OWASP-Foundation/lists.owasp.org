CSIT Labs, the cyber security incubator which provides three months of engineering resource free, is open for applications for its 3rd cohort.

https://qub.us13.list-manage.com/track/click?u=74001ed540e2ac518e2579579&id=8e9fa0d20e&e=0b44565470


** Cohort 3 applications close on 3rd Dec
------------------------------------------------------------

CSIT Labs - https://qub.us13.list-manage.com/track/click?u=74001ed540e2ac518e2579579&id=a8f552f388&e=0b44565470 - the world's only incubator programme for cyber security start-ups which provides free engineering resource, will close for applications to its third cohort on 3rd December 2017. The incubator is delivered by the Centre for Secure Information Technologies (CSIT), the UK’s national Innovation and Knowledge Centre for cyber security.

The programme lasts 9 months and uniquely provides up to 3 man-months of funded engineering development resource. This will support start-ups in the field of cyber security develop demonstrators and minimal viable product, hastening their transition through to investment and market readiness. The engineering effort alone represents a £30,000 value to companies.

6 participant teams will receive:
* up to 3 man months (fully funded) engineering support for production/prototype/proof of concept technology
* A series of workshops to iterate and validate the proposed market offering
* Mentoring from a network of industry, government and venture capitalist representatives
* Exposure to the CSIT global network
* Promotion through existing business development activities e.g CyberUK, RSA San Francisco and Infosecurity Europe

There is no requirement to relocate to Belfast and there is no cost for programme participation. Those based outside Northern Ireland will be expected to fund their own travel and accommodation to the workshops and essential meetings. See website for further details.

CSIT Labs takes no equity so company cap tables stay simple when it comes to raising future investment.

Applications close on 3rd December 2017.
