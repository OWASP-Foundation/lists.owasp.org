>                                       * First, review the How to Host

>                                       * Gather a small team together
>                                         who are interested in working
>                                         the event.  Trust us, you

>                                         information (location,
>                                         tentative dates, possible
>                                         venues, costs, theme, etc)
>                                         about your event.  
>                                       * Prepare a rough budget
>                                         plan using the OWASP Budget
>                                         Planning Tool

>                                         together a short
>                                         presentation describing the

>                                         transportation, local culture
>                                         and more. In general, these
>                                         are presentations to 'pitch'
>                                         your city to the committee.
