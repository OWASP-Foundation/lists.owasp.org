Dear all,

OWASP AppSec Latam 2012 is just a few days away and we wanted to provide
some last minute details regarding the event!

*LOCATION*
AppSec Latam 2012 will be held in downtown Montevideo, Uruguay at the Antel
National Telco Company. The conference training and talks will be held in
the conference auditorium and interactive room, which are adjacent to the
Antel Tower.

*Antel National Telco Company*
Auditorio Torre de las Telecomunicacione Guatemala 1075
Montevideo 11800 Uruguay

Directions are available through: Google
Maps<https://maps.google.com/maps?q=Auditorio+Torre+de+las+Telecomunicaciones,+Guatemala+1075,+Montevideo+11800,+Uruguay&hl=es-419&ll=-34.899448,-56.179104&spn=0.098412,0.181789&geocode=FaOW6_0dt4qm_A&hnear=Auditorio+Torre+de+las+Telecomunicaciones,+Guatemala+1075,+Montevideo+11800,+Uruguay&t=m&z=13>



*TRAINING*
Training classes run from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM on Sunday and Monday (Nov 18 &
19) . There will be signs when you enter the building directing you to the
specific room for your assigned class.

If you haven't signed up for training, but would like to attend - No
problem!

We still have room in 2 great training courses:

   - *Developing Secure Java Applications with ESAPI, taught by Fabio
   Cerullo*
   - ***Hands on Web Application Testing: Assessing Web Apps the OWASP way,
   taught by Matt Tesauro*

Learn more about the courses at appseclatam.org (under the "Training" tab)

 *Click here<https://www.cvent.com/Events/Register/RegNumConfirmation.aspx?e=6e6cac65-3617-499a-a150-72e5c21b1ecf>
to
update your registration or arrive early on Sunday morning and we can
register you on-site.*


*CONFERENCE SESSIONS
*The main conference sessions take place next week Tuesday and Wednesday
(Nov 20 & 21).  Opening keynotes start at 9:00 am each day. Arrive early to
register, eat some breakfast, and network with other attendees.


*QUESTIONS?
*Contact the AppSec Latam Planning team at
appseclatam2012 en owasp.org<appseclatam2012 en owasp.org?subject=Question+regarding+AppSec+Latam>
