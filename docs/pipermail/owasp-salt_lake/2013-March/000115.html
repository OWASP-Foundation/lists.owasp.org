Front Range OWASP Conference

Join 300+ developers, security professionals, and cyber security industry
leaders for a day-and-a-half of world-class presentations, hacking
competitions, expert training, and collaborative Birds of a Feather
sessions.

SnowFROC 2013 offers four tracks designed to tackle cyber security from all
angles: technical, deep-dive, management, and legal. For the hands-on
crowd, a capture the flag hacking tournament will run in parallel to
conference proceedings.

The keynote speaker will be Neal Ziring, Technical Director of Information
Protection, NSA.

*When*
Conference proceedings: Thursday, March 28, 2013
Training and Birds of a Feather: Friday, March 29, 2013

*Where*
Denver Marriott City Center
1701 California Street
Denver, Colorado 80202 USA

*Attendee Registration*
Non-Member Registration  $115.00
*(One year of OWASP membership is included in the cost)*

OWASP Member Registration  $65.00
*(Register using the email address associated with your membership)*

Student Registration  $35.00
*(An active student ID or transcript is required)*

Event Registration<http://www.cvent.com/events/front-range-owasp-conference/event-summary-e25b04af59c84f4db33f51fb4fb6f5ec.aspx>
Additional SnowFROC
Information<https://owasp.org/index.php/Front_Range_OWASP_Conference_2013>

Come for the conference, stay for the winter sports.
