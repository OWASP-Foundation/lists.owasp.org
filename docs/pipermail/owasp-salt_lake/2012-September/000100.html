*Job Summary and Description:*

Accuvant, an IT security firm, is recognized as one of America’s fastest
growing companies and has been named to the Inc. 5000 list for 2007-2010.
Accuvant has been named Top Company by ColoradoBiz Magazine and Top
Technology Practice in Security by Everything Channel. Since 2002, more
than 3,900 organizations, including 65 of the Fortune 100 and 20 of the
largest U.S. Federal Agencies, have trusted Accuvant with their data
security challenges. Headquartered in Denver, Accuvant has offices in 36
cities across and United States, Canada and Europe.

*JOB SUMMARY:*
Principal Assessors are responsible for providing Accuvant’s clients with
world-class consulting services, focusing on the performance of security
assessments and penetration testing of application and enterprise
environments as well as security research and development of security
tools, processes and testing methodologies.

   - All pre-project planning and kickoff agenda items and associated
   activities
   - Provide confident leadership throughout the engagement process – from
   kickoff to post-engagement report
   - Monitor consultant project activities and effectively assist them with
   technical tasks and issues.
   - Conduct technical reviews within service line offerings
   - Provide consistent and professional deliverable documentation in a
   timely manner
   - On/Off-site client technical and project lead
   - Significant client interaction & project management throughout
   engagement
   - Improve engagement efficiency and accuracy
   - Provide thought leadership in the security space
   - Be recognized within Accuvant for one or more areas of technical
   expertise.
   - Lead communications of technical ideas throughout Labs organization
   - Refining and streamlining technical procedures for existing service
   lines
   - Review and approve modifications, developed by consultants, to
   procedures utilized in client engagements.
   - Provide training/mentoring to new or more junior members of the team.
   - Providing support for pre-sales client interaction including:
      - Qualification stage – qualification of scope and review of
      questionnaires
      - Proposal stage – technical language & effort
   - Quality review of data produced for:
      - Client engagements
      - White papers
      - Conference presentations
      - Automate engagements
   - Identify areas where code or processes could be implemented to
   streamline service line offerings
