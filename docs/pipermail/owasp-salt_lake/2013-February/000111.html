Everyone, our new UtahSec email list and google group is now live. Please
feel free to accept this invitation and join the group to keep up on
UtahSec meeting
announcements and related discussions.  To join the group, visit the
following page, log in with your google account, and click the join button.

https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/utahsec

Once you join, you can send emails to the list at
utahsec at googlegroups.comor by visiting the URL above and posting it
via the web interface.

Remember that this list will not be replacing each of the subgroup lists.
For example, the Salt Lake OWASP email will still be used for those
interested in web security topics and official OWASP communications.

We hope to see you one the list!
