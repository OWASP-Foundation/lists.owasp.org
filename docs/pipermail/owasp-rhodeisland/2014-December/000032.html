Hi all,

There will be no OWASP RI meeting in December but we will be back in
January with our schedule. You can see the upcoming schedule at
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/

January 21: Digital Forensics with Prof. Vic Fay-Wolfe, Chairman of the
Digital Forensics department at the University of Rhode Island

February 18: The Maturing Security Model with David Sherry, Chief
Information Security Officer at Brown University

March 18: Mining Ruby Gem Vulnerabilities for Fun and No Profit with Larry
Cashdollar, Customer Security Incident Response Engineer at Akamai
Technologies

April: Threat Intelligence and Open Source Intelligence with Stuart Gorton

You can see we have great topics and great speakers lined up into the
spring. You can get more information and RSVP for these at
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/ and
you can also get more information about the group and past talks at
http://www.provwebappsec.com. All meetings are at Swipely, 10 Dorrance
Street, Providence.

Have a great holiday season and we'll see you in January!

Patrick Laverty
