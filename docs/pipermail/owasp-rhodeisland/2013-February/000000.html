Hello all-

If you subscribed to the provwebappsec.com mailing list, I've taken the
liberty of subscribing you to the official OWASP-Rhode Island listserv.
This will be the only list used for meeting notifications, and that will be
the only purpose of the list. If you'd like to unsubscribe, you can do that
here: https://lists.owasp.org/mailman/listinfo/owasp-rhodeisland

Ok, now for the news. Two things, really quick:

The next meeting will be on Monday, March 4, 5:45 pm at Swipely in
Providence. Location information and a map is here:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Rhode_Island#Next_Meeting but the quick
directions is it's right off Wickenden, across from Al Forno.

The topic of our meeting will be some more hands-on hacking. We'll go over
a couple favorite topics like SQL injection and Cross Site Scripting and
try it against a vulnerable server, and we'll see how to guard against
these. We'll have a little fun with it and try making it a non-competitive
(read: educational) capture the flag game.

The only other thing is there will be a Security BSides conference in
Providence this summer. If you're interested in attending, it's only $20
admission. The full presenters list is available at
http://securitybsides.com/BSidesRI

That's it for now, we'll see you on March 4 at Swipely!

Thank you.

Patrick
