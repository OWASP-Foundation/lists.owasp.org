Hi all!

On April 15, OWASP RI will have Stuart Gorton in to talk about building a
threat intelligence program using open source intelligence (OSINT). You can
RSVP for this meeting either by responding to this email or at our
meetup.com page here:
http://www.meetup.com/Providence-Web-Application-Security-Meetup-OWASP-RI/

This meeting will be at 6:30 pm at Swipely, 10 Dorrance Street in
Providence. Pizza and drinks supplied by Swipely as well!

In May, we will be going on a road trip, to Congressman Jim Langevin's
office in Warwick, so he can tell us about the State of US Cybersecurity.
That will be on Monday, May 11.

We had a speaker for June but had to reschedule, so we have an opening
there. If you have a topic you'd like to present, or know someone who
would, come on out!

In other news, tickets are available now for BSides Boston:
www.securitybsides.com/Boston which is on May 9.

And if you're in the Boston area today, I'll be speaking on the topic of
Impostor Syndrome at OWASP Boston tonight at 6:30 pm, 150 Broadway,
Cambridge.

That's all for now, see you on April 15th!
