MINUTES OF THE FIRST UNOFFICIAL MEETING OF OWASP of 2009

 
 
Date: 1st Feb 2009.
 
Time: 3.00 P.M.
 
Venue: Brio Cafe in Crossroad Chennai.
 
ATTENDEES:           Mr. Chandrasekar Umapthy
Mr. Mohammed Zuber
Mr. Murgan Kuppuswamy
Mr. Rajkumar Pandian
Mr. Ramesh
 
ADOPTED AGENDA:  
 
Mr. Zuber welcomed everyone to the meeting.
 
Mr. Murgan Kuppuswamy gave a very interesting informal talk on how to
quantify Security in Business Sense and get Senior Management attention
towards it.
 
Mr. Rajkumar Pandian pointed out the difficulties faced by security
professional to make Business Owner's and Application Programmers
understand importance of Security in their Business Process and their
Applications
 
After collective brainstorming session we came to a conclusion that we
have to revitalize the Chennai Chapter and have a fixed schedule for
formal meetings
 
 
P.S: Refreshments for the Meeting is sponsored by Ramesh. We hope that
he would continues his generosity to next meetings also even in this
gloomy recession. (Ha ha . . . .)
