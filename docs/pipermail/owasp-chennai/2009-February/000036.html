Company Profile:

J.P. Morgan India is a leading financial services firm with global expertise
and reach. The firm's operations are based in Mumbai, Bangalore, Ahmedabad,
Chennai, Pune, Delhi and Kolkata. In India, J.P. Morgan is one of the few
fully integrated banks with leadership positions across investment banking,
transaction banking, asset management, private equity investments, and a
service organization that supports J.P. Morgan businesses from all over the
world. Local operations are linked to the world's major financial hubs of
New York, London and Hong Kong. J.P. Morgan's presence in the country dates
back to 1930, when the first office was opened in Mumbai. Since then J.P.
Morgan has grown considerably in scale and scope but the firm's commitment
to the core principle of doing "only first-class business...in a first-class
way" has never been stronger.
Job Title and Job Location:

IT Risk - Application Security Review (AVP)
Mumbai
Position reports to:
Head - IT Risk
Educational Qualifications:

• B.E/ B.Tech
• Professional certifications in Audit and Risk Management i.e., QiCA, MIIA,
CISSP, CISA, CISM or equivalent
Desired Work Experience:

• 8-10 yrs IT years with least 4 years in IT Risk, Penetration Testing,
Application Vulnerability Assessments

Job Requirements:

• Explicit understanding of Technology and Operational risk management as
well as knowledge and expertise in Information Technology (IT) Control
Policies, standards and guidelines in the Financial Services Industry.
• Understanding of the Application Security Assessment (ASA) risk management
review methodology, including Application Security Risk (ASR) reviews,
Penetration and AVAT vulnerability testing, external-facing system reviews.
• Personal Information (PI) reviews and deep dive/ targeted risk reviews at
application, operating system and database levels
• Working with Information Risk Management teams to plan, schedule and
execute the ASA risk review programme in a timely manner.
• Working with Application Development and technology teams to plan,
schedule, execute and finalise the ASA risk review programme levels.
• Working with internal and external groups to plan, schedule and execute
Penetration and AVAT vulnerability testing programme in a timely manner.
• Working with internal and external Audit groups to provide information,
expertise and opinion on risk and controls in areas reviewed as part of the
ASA review programme.
• Working with internal Technology teams to request information as well as
provide opinion on the state of controls at the operating system and
database levels.
• Scheduling and execution of ASA reviews, including data collection,
interviews, analysis, and opinion on effectiveness of controls in the areas
being reviewed.
• Ability to utilise past experiences and recommend viable solutions to all
key risk issues identified as part of the ASA review programme.
• Recording all findings, issues and observations within the Risk Assessment
System.
• Provision of metrics, trends analysis and reporting to management on a
monthly basis.
• Update and completion of all ASA reviews in the  risk management tool.
• Assurance that identified remediation plans are tracked and appropriately
closed with remediation validation.
• Awareness of industry wide laws and legal and regulatory requirements and
the potential impact on the ASA review programme.

Key Skills
Technical Skills

• Detailed knowledge and experience of key controls that should exist within
an application control environment.
• Explicit knowledge of the following Systems and associated Security tools
o        Operating Systems – Mainframe, Mid-Range, UNIX and Wintel
o        Security Tools – Pentasafe, Keon, E-Trust, ESM, RACF, ACF2
o        Databases – Oracle, Sybase, DB2, SQL
• Detailed knowledge and experience of:
o        Computer Assisted Audit Techniques (CAATs), e.g. ACL, IDEA
o        Programming/ Data Interrogation skills
o        Spreadsheets, e.g. Microsoft Excel Advanced, including Visual Basic

o        Databases, e.g. Microsoft Access Advanced

Professional Skills

• Excellent oral and written communication skills at all levels of
management
• Self-motivated and can work on own initiative
• Can work remotely from other team members
• Excellent analytical and decision-making skills
• Ability to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of processes and
procedures in order to add value to the Firm
• Can manage own time, budgets and resources in order to get tasks completed
in a timely manner
• Ability to produce quality reports and documentation in a timely manner
• Excellent leadership and negotiation skills
• Excellent understanding of IT Risks, Financial risks and IT Control
Policies, standards and guidelines.

Experience (Required)

• Information Technology risk management and Technology Audit experience or
equivalent
• Data sampling and analysis experience

Experience (Nice to have)

• Knowledge of Treasury Services business - products and operations
• Knowledge of JPMC IT Risk Management Policies, Standards and processes
• Knowledge and/or experience of the JPMC ASA Review Methodology
• Knowledge of the Phoenix Risk Assessment Tool.

Travel: Minimal
