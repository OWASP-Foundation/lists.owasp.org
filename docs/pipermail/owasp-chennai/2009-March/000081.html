I agree to Zuber & Siva's view that networking session was something that
was missing.

Good that Venki & Umapathy have atleast kickstarted some activity in OWASP
Chennai chapter & are planning to have more such events in the times to
come.

Looking forward to see more and more experts on the topic taking an
opportunity to present interesting sessions.

Though basic techniques & approach while developing/testing web applications
on various platforms would be same for most of the platforms, i would really
appreciate if experts touchbase upon security of platforms such as
Sharepoint, IBM Clearquest etc. I feel this might add value to both the
developers & testers.

Looking forward for an opportunity to present something interesting myself.
