I would like to thank the OWASP Chennai team for having arranged the
conference. It was nice to see a dynamic OWASP Chapter. I am sure OWASP
Chennai would scale and go on to host several world-class conferences. It is
a good start.

As I see, some of you have not had a chance to interact with me. I must
truly apologize for the absence towards the end. I had to leave for
Bangalore, as I had some prior engagements to keep.

I welcome any interaction from all of you. Please post your questions to me
through the community or my blog.

Also, if you want your emails to be added to my blog mail list, please let
me know.

I have put up the presentation on my blog. Anyone interested, or anyone who
missed the meet, feel free to see it and provide some feedback.

Thanks

Warm Regards
