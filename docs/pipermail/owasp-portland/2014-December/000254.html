I have a Mentor course coming up this spring and I invite you to join.

The course is the SANS Training Program for CISSP Certification. It is a
10 week mentored self-study. We will meet once per week to discuss the
security domains, go over any difficult topics and ask questions in a
small group.

The overall goal is to provide all the information enabling you to pass
the CISSP exam.

I am a 20 year veteran in IT and currently an Information Security
Architect for the City of Portland. I studied and passed my CISSP exam
in the spring of 2014.

Here is the link for the course. When you register, please put my name
in as a referral, under the "how did you hear about this event":
http://www.sans.org/instructors/brian-ventura [1]

If you have questions, please feel free to email me directly.

Look forward to seeing you at the next meeting.

Thank you.
