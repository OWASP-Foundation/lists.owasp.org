Hey Aaron,

That's a good idea.  Beaverton's room reservation system is all online
too, which makes it pretty easy to see what is available.  However, it
does seem like these rooms are quite popular...  Every date I've looked
at so far for our March meeting (based on a handful of preferred dates)
is booked. =\

So it sounds like we have 3-4 of us who can bring a projector now, so
that shouldn't be a big issue if we can get just one of them there to
any given meeting.  I did end up calling the Multnomah branches I
mentioned, and two of them said that they do have projectors for use.
The other one (Belmont library) said they have them, but they aren't
permitted for use with reserved rooms.  (Kind of odd.  I have to wonder
what the real library policy is and if some of the locations will change
their mind later.)

I've also put requests in to FreeGeek and Backspace Cafe, but haven't
heard back yet.

Thanks,
