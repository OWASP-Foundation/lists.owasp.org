Hi all,

For a while now the Portland hack-a-thon has been a random group of
geeks who get together each week at the east side Lucky Lab to help
each other on various coding projects.  (The "hack" in hack-a-thon
isn't meant to imply that it has anything to do with computer
security.  Just general computer geekery with a focus on programming.)

I have to admit I've never been to a hack-a-thon on the east side.
However, when I saw they are trying to get one started here in SW
Portland (one block from my house), I felt obliged to show up.  Seems
like a good informal way to meet people and help each other learn
about what other people are hacking on.

Anyway, I'll be hosting the SW hack-a-thon this Wednesday:
  http://calagator.org/events/1250463756

Feel free come on by.  If I am not totally distracted by interesting
discussions, I'll be working on my cryptanalysis toolkit, bletchley.
