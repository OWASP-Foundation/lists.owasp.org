Hey all,

Just wanted to drop a quick note to thank everyone who helped out with
this event.  I just sent off a preliminary list of vulnerabilities and
other findings to Ushahidi.  We found a total of 14 vulnerabilities.
Not bad at all considering there were about 9 of us on site (with two
Ushahidi developers joining remotely) and most folks didn't start
looking for issues until they arrived on Sunday.

I do plan on sending out all vulnerability details to all
participants, but I would like to give Ushahidi a bit of time to start
digesting the findings and get back to us with any feedback on them so
I can flesh out any technical details that I'm currently missing. For
those who did find flaws, you may be contacted directly with questions
about your findings.

Also, I should mention this FLOSSHack's prize winners!  The prizes
were $15 gift certificates for http://www.zerodayclothing.com/.  
(I know, it isn't the biggest incentive in the world, but I hope
future prizes will be more motivating.)  The prize for "best bug" will
go to Michael Freeman while the the prize for "most bugs" will go to
Kees Cook.  (I'll contact you guys once I get them in the mail.)


Thanks again and great work.
tim
