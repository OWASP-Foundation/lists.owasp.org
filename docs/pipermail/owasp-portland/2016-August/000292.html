Hi Portland OWASP -

Simple is recruiting for a Manager of Product Security
<http://grnh.se/j0cq12>.  This is a new position which will lead application

with both security engineering of the application stack, as well as baking
Security into product management processes. It will have lots of social

Also leading a small team of security engineers focused on pen testing, and

questions.  Also, save the date for Thursday, Aug 25th. We will be hosting
the OWASP meeting at the Simple office. Adam Baldwin will be giving a talk
on security considerations with Node.js
<http://calagator.org/events/1250470488>.  It should be a good topic, a
good speaker, and an opportunity to check out our new office space.
