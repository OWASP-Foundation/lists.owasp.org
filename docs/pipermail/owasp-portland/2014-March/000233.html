The Portland Chapter of the ISSA is pleased to extend a complimentary invitation to OWASP members to attend the INTERFACE-Portland<http://intelliclicksoftware.net/1j-1e-3O5> conference on March 13th, 2014 at the Oregon Convention Center.

INTERFACE is a single-day, IT conference addressing current advances in:

  *   Information Security
  *   Infrastructure (business continuance & disaster recovery)
  *   Enterprise Communications
With this invitation, you can attend INTERFACE at no cost. Furthermore, you are encouraged to share this invitation with your associates and extended professional network.

Benefits included with your invitation to INTERFACE-Portland 2014 are:

  *   20 CPE Accredited Educational Seminars*
     *   Earn up to 6 CPE Credit Hours
  *   Keynote Presentation
  *   Catered Lunch Buffet
  *   70+ Exhibitor Booths
  *   Hosted Afternoon Reception
*You are invited to attend the "The Reality of Social Engineering Attacks" panel session at 11:10 in Theater 3. OWASP's Tim Morgan will be one of the panelists in this program.

REGISTER: http://interfacetour.com/evites/pdx/issa.htm
DEADLINE: March 11th, 2014

We look forward to seeing you at INTERFACE-Portland!
