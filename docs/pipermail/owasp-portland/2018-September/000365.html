The University of Portland is seeking an adjunct instructor to teach our
Computer Systems Security elective this Spring semester.  The course is
scheduled to be taught on Tues and Thurs evenings from 4:10pm-5:55pm
starting on Jan 15 with a final exam to be given the first week of May.
Our campus is in North Portland.  (We are not Portland State University
downtown.)

Adjunct instructors should have a master's degree or long experience as a
computer security professional (10+ years).  Previous teaching experience
is preferred.

We can provide materials from the last time it was taught.  The instructor
is free to focus on appropriate topics with which she/he has expertise.

If you are interested or have questions please contact:
Andrew Nuxoll
Computer Science Program Chair
University of Portland
503-943-7688
nuxoll at up.edu
