Greetings!

The Portland OWASP chapter is seeking presenters and discussion leaders
for our chapter meetings in 2014.  Just about any security topic is
potentially fair game, but here are a few suggestions of the types of
things we would like:

* Any technical talks on specific classes of vulnerabilities
  (OWASP top 10 or similar)

* Your experiences in trying to apply OWASP principles in the real
  world

* Emerging threats or security risks associated with new technologies
  (HTML5, popular mobile paradigms, etc)

* Lead a discussion with the audience on controversial security topics

* Root cause analysis of why common development practices or platform
  constraints lead to software vulnerabilities


We hope to hold many more chapter meetings next year, so please don't
hesitate to submit your ideas.  Also, we could definitely use help in
spreading the word, so take a moment to think of anyone you know who
might be interested in speaking.

To submit a proposal, please email Tim and Adam the following:

* The title and abstract for each of the talk topics or discussions
  you are proposing.

* Your approximate calendar availability for 2014

* A short bio


Thank you!
tim
