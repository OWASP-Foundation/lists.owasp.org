Dear OWASP Friends,

Below is a message from the OWASP Foundation (the mothership) about the
upcoming board election. This election is for the global OWASP board, and
NOT for OWASP Los Angeles chapter.

If you have paid your OWASP membership fee of $50 before June 30, 2011, or
if you are a qualified honorary member, then you are eligible to vote.  You
will receive a confirmation email on July 19.

Click here <https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership/2011Election> to find
out who is running, how to vote and your membership status.  If you have
additional questions about the membership then please read the FAQ by
clicking here<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership#OWASP_Membership_Frequently_Asked_Questions_.28FAQ.29>
.

Please let me know if you have questions.
