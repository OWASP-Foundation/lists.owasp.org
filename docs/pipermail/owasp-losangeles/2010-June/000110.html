Dear OWASP Friends,

Our June monthly meeting will be on June 9, 7:30PM at Symantec offices
in Culver City.

The speaker is Brendan Bellina. Brendan is Identity Services Architect
and Manager of Enterprise Middleware Identity Management with
Information Technology Services at University of Southern California.
He will be speaking about Shibboleth
(http://shibboleth.internet2.edu/) implementation at USC. Shibboleth
System is a standards based, open source software package for web
single sign-on across or within organizational boundaries. I will send
out the abstract of the talk as soon as I get it.

Registration is open -- http://owaspla.eventbrite.com/.

We will order some food and accurate head count is appreciated.

We are having this month meeting on the second Wednesday, instead of
the regular third Wednesday, to avoid conflict with ISSA Summit
scheduled on June 16. You can still register for the summit at ISSA LA
website (http://www.issa-la.org/default.aspx).

I hope to see you on the 9th!
