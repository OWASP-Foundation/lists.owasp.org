Who should attend:

*Business Leaders: CEOs, COOs, CFOs, Managing Partners and other
non-technical decision makers with top-level management responsibility for
information security and for compliance with information privacy laws and
regulations, together with their Trusted Advisors.
*Technology Leaders: CIOs, Technical Managers and other IT
professionals with responsibility for maintaining the IT network and the
data it contains
*Information Systems Security Leaders: CISOs and other information
security practitioners having day-to-day responsibility for ensuring the
security of sensitive information, together with those responsible for
auditing systems security.
