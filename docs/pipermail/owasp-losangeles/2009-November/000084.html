*CIS PRESENTS: THE SSL/TLS MITM VULNERABILITY – Presentation and Q&A *

* *

The recent SSL/TLS man in the middle (MITM) vulnerability may have recently
popped up on your favorite security mailing list or RSS feed. You may have
read blogs or consulted your local security expert to determine how this
vulnerability impacts your organization or systems.



*CIS is excited to provide our community with the opportunity to learn from
and ask question of the individuals and organizations that discovered,
disclosed, and are mitigating this vulnerability.*



This Thursday, November 19th, at 10:00 AM PST, CIS is hosting a presentation
and Q&A session featuring the security researchers from PhoneFactor that
discovered the vulnerability as well as individuals from Juniper Networks,
ICASI, and Leviathan Security Group, who are central to the research,
disclosure, and mitigation process.



*Presenters and Panelists:*

Steve Dispensa, Marsh Ray of PhoneFactor, Inc.

Steven Muznik of Juniper Networks & ICASI

Frank Heidt, Mikhail Davidov from Leviathan Security Group



*Agenda:*

Welcome and Introduction

Vulnerability Disclosure Process

Vulnerability Technical Details

Exploitation and Mitigation Scenarios



Each segment will be followed by a Q&A session.


Date: Thursday, November 19, 2009

Time: 10:00 AM PST
