Hello all,

Please don't forget that we have monthly meeting tomorrow starting 7PM at
Symantec. We have two speakers and will serve gourmet Greek food, thanks to
our sponsor Mykonos Software. Please spread the word, and bring friends.

More info: http://www.owaspLA.org
RSVP: http://www.eventbrite.com/event/1025602605

For December, we are planning something different -- OWASP Christmas. It
will be on December 15.

We will have a dinner party at Tony P's in Marina del Rey. We will have a
brief technical talk and the rest of time will be spent on soliciting ideas
for 2011, networking and drinking (and eating of course).

For that, paid OWASP members (those who joined for $50/year and in good
standing) will get in free. It is also free for members who join (for
$50/year) that day (please join prior to the meeting and bring your
receipt). For the rest, there is $20 suggested donation.

Please let me know directly if you have questions/thoughts on this.

Safe holidays!

Tin
