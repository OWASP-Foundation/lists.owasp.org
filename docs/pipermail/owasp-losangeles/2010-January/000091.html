Fellow OWASP LA members,

I am pleased to announce that Richard Greenberg, CISSP, has agreed to
join me and Cassio as member of OWASP LA Local Chapter Board.

Richard is the CISO at the Los Angeles County Department of Public
Health. Prior to this appointment, he held the same position for the
County’s Department of Health Services. Richard has been a chief of
security operations, a director of IT, and project manger for the
private and public sectors. He is a member of the ISSA CISO Executive
Forum, Infragard, and (ISC)2.

With help of Richard, we plan to continue to deliver monthly meetings
with interesting topics and speakers. We are also planning to host an
AppSec conference in southern California, in conjunction with OWASP OC
chapter.

Please help me welcome Richard and let us know of any suggestions you
have to make OWASP LA more beneficial for all of us.

Best,
Tin

PS. Please don't forget to RSVP for January meeting by sending email to Cassio.
