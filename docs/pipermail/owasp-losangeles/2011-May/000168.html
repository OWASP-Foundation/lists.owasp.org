The ISSA Los Angeles Chapter invites you to attend the 3rd Annual
Information Security Summit.

Wednesday, June 15, 2011
7:30 am – 6:00 pm
UCLA Campus

Who Should Attend

Not just for the information security professional the Summit will
build on our chapter’s tradition of being the only educational forum
in Los Angeles specifically designed to encourage participation and
interaction among three vital information security constituencies:

Business Leaders: CEOs, COOs, CFOs, Managing Partners and other
non-technical decision makers with top-level management responsibility
for information security and compliance with information privacy laws
and regulations, together with their Trusted Advisors.

Technology Leaders: CIOs, Technical Managers and other IT
professionals with responsibility for maintaining the IT network and
the data it contains.

Information Systems Security Leaders: CISOs and other information
security specialists having day-to-day responsibility for ensuring the
security of sensitive information, together with those responsible for
auditing systems security.

Speakers Include:

        Marc Maiffret, CTO of eEye Digital
        Steve Lipner, Director of Security Assurance of Microsoft
        Roland Cloutier, CSO of ADP
        Gene Schultz, CTO of Emagined Security
        Jeremiah Grossman, CTO of WhiteHat Security
        Gary McGraw, CTO of Cigital, Inc.
        Francoise Gilbert, Founder/Partner of IT Law Group
        and
        Distinguished Keynote Speaker, Carl Terzian

For more information:

http://www.issa-la.org/2011-security-summit-home-page/
