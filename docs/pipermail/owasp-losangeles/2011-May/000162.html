Dear OWASPers,

It is unfortunate that our original speaker, Adam Shostack of
Microsoft, could not present at our meeting this month.

We do still have a meeting with a live presenter. I hope that the
topic will be of interest to security professionals and software
developers alike. We will have food as usual. Please bring your
developer and QA friends.

The following is the draft of the topic so you can register for the
meeting. We will update  the Wiki page with a more detailed
description once it is available.

Topic is about Automated Detection of Security Flaws in Ruby on Rails
Code (this is a working title and subject to change).

Speaker is Justin Collins. Justin is a Security Engineer at AT&T
Interactive and a PhD candidate in computer Science at UCLA. He wrote
and published Brakeman, security code analyzer for Ruby on Rails,
which is available on GitHub.

http://owasp-may2011.eventbrite.com/
