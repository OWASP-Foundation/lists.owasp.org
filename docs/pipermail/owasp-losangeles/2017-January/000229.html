​*The Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) Los Angeles Chapter is
teaming up with the Orange County, San Diego and Santa Barbara chapters to
bring you the fourth annual AppSecCalifornia.*

AppSec California combines a dynamic mix of training and education
delivered by some of the industry’s top experts in secure application
development and web application security. Information security
professionals, developers, and QA and testing professionals from around the
world will gather at the Annenberg Beach House where they will participate
in full-day trainings, enjoy invigorating and inspiring talks, engage in
thought-provoking conversations, and make new friends.

The event takes place January 23-25 on the beach in Santa Monica,
California at the Annenberg Beach House.A full day of training on various
subjects by expert trainers kicks off the conference on the 23rd. World
renowned speakers follow on days two and three.

*This year brings three very special Keynote Speakers: RSA CTO Zulfikar
“Zully” Ramzan, Cigital CTO Gary McGraw, and Acalvio Chief Security
Architect Chris Roberts.*

There will be four concurrent tracks addressing a variety of topics to
enhance knowledge. Our stellar lineup features: Jim Manico, Dan Cornell,
Scott Jenson, Neil Matatall, Arvind Mani, Jillian Karner, Bret Johnson,
Caroline Wong, Chenxi Wang, Roger Seagle, Marian Merritt, and Tin Zaw.

A full day of training kicks off the conference on January 23rd: Secure
Coding Bootcamp for the Web - Jim Manico; Real World Red Team Attacks -
Peter Kim; OWASP Top 10 - Exploitation and Effective Safeguards - David
Caissy; and The Best TLS Training in the World - Scott Helme.

A great opening party spills onto the beautiful deck of the landmark Marion
Davies House on day two, as conference goers network, drink, and eat as
they listen to the waves and gaze at the stars.

We hope to see you there!​
