Hi Everyone,



I write to confirm our first OWASP Los Angeles meeting is on for Tuesday
August 19th 7:00PM at Symantec Culver
City<http://maps.google.com/maps?q=900+Corporate+Pointe,+90230&ie=UTF8&oe=UTF-8&t=h&z=16&iwloc=addr>.
I'm proud to announce our remote guest speaker will be Jeff Williams, CEO of
Aspect Security and chair of OWASP. Jeff will be talking about the OWASP
ESAPI project during his presentation titled "Don't Write Your Own Security
Code". For more information, please check out the Los Angeles OWASP
webpage<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Los_Angeles>.




In case you'd like to help spread the word in your company, feel free to
use this flyer<https://www.owasp.org/images/2/2a/OWASP_Los_Angeles_Meetings.pdf>about
the meeting .



*IMPORTANT:* If you are planning to join the meeting, please confirm your
presence by going to this eVite
page<http://www.evite.com/pages/invite/viewInvite.jsp?inviteId=OYEBBRFNIHMESSRWPGAJ>and
add yourself to the invitation by clicking on "Invite More People" and
then confirming your attendance.



See you soon,

Cassio
