Hi Everyone,



Our next meeting will be Wednesday, October 21st 7:30PM at Symantec. Shankar
Subramaniyan will be presenting “Enabling Compliance Requirements using
Information Security Management System (ISMS) Framework (ISO27001)” and
sharing his vast experience running large projects in this area for over 11
years.  For more information about the talk visit
http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Los_Angeles



November and December promise to be great months for OWASP LA. After a tour
in Europe with McGraw presenting BSIMM, Brian Chess, CTO of Fortify, will be
coming to town to talk to us. The topic will be announced in our webpage in
the next couple of weeks.



And to end a great year, Michael Sutton, Vice President and security
research at Zscaler (also known for co-authoring the book “Fuzzing: Brute
Force Vulnerability Discovery”) will be presenting “Pulling the Plug:
Security Risks in the Next Generation of Offline Web Applications”. I’ve
seen parts of this presentation and I must say this is one you can’t miss!



Please don’t forget to RSVP by replying to this email. Pizza will be
provided.



Looking forward to seeing you,

Cassio
