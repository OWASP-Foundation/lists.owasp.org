HIMSS SoCal, in partnership with ISSA Los Angeles, ISSA Orange County,
ISACA Los Angeles, (ISC)2 Los Angeles, and OWASP Los Angeles invites
you to attend the 2nd Annual HITECH Security Forum, to be held at the
Center for Healthy Communities at the California Endowment near
downtown Los Angeles on January 24, 2012 from 7am to 1:30pm.

Join senior healthcare leaders as they discuss mobile and data
security, cloud computing, risk management, and legal and compliance
issues.  This half-day program will touch on many of the hot topics
both small and large healthcare systems are addressing today.

Registration Info

Registration will close Jan. 16, 2012

Member Registration (HIMSS, ISACA, ISSA, ISC2 and OWASP members): $50
Non-Member Registration: $65
Students: $20
