*Director, Compliance Office*

*AT&T Interactive*, a subsidiary of AT&T Inc, is an industry leader in
creating local search products that encourage consumers to discover and
engage with local businesses across three screens - online, mobile, and
TV. YP.com attracts over 36 million monthly online unique visitors. YP.com
is also accessed by millions of users each month via the mobile web. AT&T
Interactive consumer apps have a pre-activation presence or have been
downloaded through digital app stores on over 40 million mobile phones.

Helping advertisers grow their business, AT&T Interactive’s local ad
network, one of the industry’s most robust, distributes ads to a
distinguished list of top publishers whose presence spans online, mobile and
TV platforms. AT&T Interactive’s wide range of local ad products such as
premium listings, performance-based advertising, and search engine marketing
provides advertisers an opportunity to reach consumers who are looking for a
local business.

AT&T Interactive products are supported by more than 5,000 media consultants
from AT&T Advertising Solutions.

Join our dedicated and talented team of individuals all focused on creating
the best products in the marketplace. We currently have an opportunity for
an experienced Director, Compliance Office in our Glendale offices.
This individual would be responsible for ensuring that compliance plans,
policies, programs and schedules for business and finance data processing,
computer services, network communications, and information services are in
place and align with corporate goals and objectives under the guidance of
state and federal requirements.

*RESPONSIBILITIES:*

   - Assist senior management on strategic security systems controls and
   integrations in support of business goals and objectives.
   - Work with several departments analyzing existing physical,
   technological and administrative controls; work with department managers and
   leads to develop new or optimize existing processes to reduce risk,
   liability, provide recommendations, and assist in process improvement.
   - Review and analyze state and federal legislature to offer executive
   technical summaries relevant to business operations and product offerings.
   - Create gap assessment documentation using AT&T Policies, Standards.
   - Assist Sr. Compliance Leader to verify business unit’s compliance,
   provide recommendations, and work with departmental managers to maintain
   adherence to internal corporate governance requirements.
   - Assist technology, compliance, and other organizations on training
   programs, inbound and outbound separation process and protection of digital
   and physical assets.
   - Develop annual goals and objectives for the organization.
   - Develop and execute accurate and measurable initiatives to benefit the
   business while safeguarding employees and customers.

*QUALIFICATIONS:*

   - Minimum of 7 years of progressive professional experience involving
   information technology audit and compliance.
   - Strong working knowledge of security standards such as ISO 17799/27002,
   NIST best practices, or equivalent.
   - High-level understanding of server/database/application administration,
   access controls, security best practices, and system development life cycle.
   - Capabilities to handle multiple projects at the same time assigning
   appropriate priorities as set by Sr. Compliance Leaders and Executive
   Management.
   - One or more of the following certifications or equivalent demonstrated
   experience: Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP),
   Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security
   Manager (CISM).
   - BS or higher required.

We offer competitive salaries, excellent benefits including a matching
401(k), bonuses, and a casual yet high-energy work environment. Jobs are
located in Glendale, near downtown Los Angeles. Send us your resume today.

AT&T Interactive is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity Employer, and we
are committed to hiring a diverse and talented workforce. EOE/AA/M/F/D/V
