Topic: Senior Information Security Recruiter Panel

Panelists:

Tracy Lenzner

Tracy Lenzner, founder and CEO of LenznerGroup, is an established
thought leader in cyber security and technology risk recruitment. She
serves as a trusted advisor to senior leadership, professional forums
and alliances globally. Tracy is a requested contributor to industry
publications and conferences such as CSO Magazine, InformationWeek,
Computerworld, Network Computing, SC Magazine, IOMA, Information
Security, Computer Security Institute and the High Tech Crime
Investigation Association "HTCIA".

Jeff Snyder

Jeff Snyder earned a Marketing degree from the University of Kentucky
in 1988.  In 1990, Jeff joined the largest IT Search Firm in St.
Louis, MO where he became a top producer before leaving in 1994 to
start his own search firm.  In 1995, he completed his first retained
CIO search as well as his first information security search.
SecruityRecruiter.com was created in 2001 to wrap a brand around
Jeff's information security recruiting practice.  His first Corporate
Security search arrived in 2007.  Since then, Jeff has filled
information/cyber security, global compliance, risk management, audit,
privacy, high-level corporate physical security jobs and a number of
converged logical and physical security CSO level roles for Fortune
500 clients in the US and abroad.

Bob Valecka

Bob joined The InSource Group in 2008 after spending over 5 years as
the Dallas Fort Worth Branch Manager for the second largest IT
Contracting Company in the world. Currently, Bob manages Sales &
Delivery for The InSource Group's Strategic Accounts, as well as
overseeing new business development. Bob has a long and diverse
management and sales background in information technology,
telecommunications, and professional services. He began his career in
National Accounts at GTE (now Verizon). Over the course of 8 years
there, he was promoted through a succession of sales, operational, and
regional management roles. After GTE, Bob worked with multiple
start-up organizations in sales, strategy, product development, and
operations. Since 2002, Bob has worked exclusively in the IT Staffing
Industry. Bob holds a BA from Texas Christian University (TCU) and a
MBA from Southern Methodist University (SMU).

Abstract:

In Information Security, practitioners are always learning about
keeping the bad guy out and cleaning up if he should breach your
network. As a special evening event, ISSA Los Angeles presents a set
of Information Security recruiters from across the country who focus
on placing only the senior most Information Security management in
organizations. Our distinguished panel, moderated by ISSA Los Angeles
President Stan Stahl, will discuss what companies are looking for in a
leader today, how to build relationships with recruiters, and how to
build your skill set to be the next information security leader.

Vendor sponsor:

Websense, Inc., a global leader in unified Web security, email
security, and data loss prevention (DLP) solutions, delivers the best
content security for modern threats at the lowest total cost of
ownership to tens of thousands of organizations worldwide. Distributed
through partners and delivered as software, appliance and
Security-as-a-Service (SaaS), Websense helps organizations leverage
Web 2.0 and cloud communication, while protecting from advanced
persistent threats, preventing confidential data loss and enforcing
security policies.
