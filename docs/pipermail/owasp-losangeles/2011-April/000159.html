Dear OWASP Members,

I believe there was some confusion about the election for Board of Directors
(of the OWASP Foundation) I presented last night.

The election is for three seats on the GLOBAL OWASP Foundation Board of
Directors. To be eligible to vote, you must be a full OWASP member (who pays
$50/year dues). Hence, we were asked, by the OWASP Foundation, to drive up
membership.

You can get more information about the election at this URL.

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership/2011Election

This election is not related at all to OWASP Los Angeles Chapter Board of
Directors. Cassio, Richard and I will continue to serve at the same
capacities for the foreseeable future. At this point, we are not endorsing
any candidate for the GLOBAL OWASP election.

Please let me know if you have questions.
