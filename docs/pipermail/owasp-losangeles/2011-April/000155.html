*EARLY BIRD REGISTRATION EXTENDED UNTIL*

*Monday, April 18, 2011*

*Register NOW! *



http://www.issa-la.org/2011-security-summit-home-page/


Since this year's tax deadline was extended, ISSA LA has extended the Early
Bird Registration for this year's Summit to Monday, April 18th!



The Los Angeles Chapter of ISSA invites you to attend the one day Summit
being held once again at the beautiful historic UCLA Campus in Los Angeles,
CA.



*Who Should Attend*
*Not just for the information security professional,* the Summit will build
on our chapter's tradition of being the* only* educational forum in Los
Angeles specifically *designed to encourage participation and interaction*among
three vital information security constituencies:

   - *Business Leaders: CEOs, COOs, CFOs, Managing Partners*and other
   non-technical decision makers with top-level management responsibility
   for information security and compliance with information privacy laws and
   regulations, together with their Trusted Advisors.
   - *Technology Leaders: CIOs, Technical Managers and other IT
   professionals* with responsibility for maintaining the IT network and the
   data it contains.
   - *Information Systems Security Leaders: CISOs* and other information
   security specialists having day-to-day responsibility for ensuring the
   security of sensitive information, together with those responsible for
   auditing systems security.
   -

**
*We look forward to seeing you there!*


*Date: Wednesday, June 15, 2011
*
*Time:* 7:30am - 6:00pm

*Location:* UCLA Campus



Register before April 18th to take advantage of the early bird discount.
