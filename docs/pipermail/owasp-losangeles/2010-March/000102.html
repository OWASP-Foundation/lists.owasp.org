> *Topic:*  *Fundamental Practices and Tools to Implement a Security
> Development Lifecycle**
> Speaker:*  *Cassio Goldschmidt, Senior Manager, Product Security, Office
> of the CTO, Symantec Corporation*
>
> *Abstract*
> Implementing an effective security development lifecycle program is both a
> costly and arduous endeavor. This presentation will  demonstrate how to
> lower the costs associated with this project by taking advantage of the high
> quality tools freely available on the internet and the fundamental practices
> employed by technology giants who are members of SAFECode such as EMC,
> Juniper, Oracle, Microsoft, Symantec, Nokia, SAP and Symantec. Topics
> include The Security Development Lifecycle, reasons to  implement a Security
> Development Lifecycle program, SAFECode, and the free tools that can be
>  used in each phase of the Security DevelopmentLifecycle.
>
> *Speaker Bio**
> *Cassio Goldschmidt is senior manager of the product security team under
> the Office of the CTO at Symantec Corporation.  In this role he leads
> efforts across the company to ensure the secure development of software
> products.  With over 13 years of technical and managerial experience in the
> software industry, Cassio is internationally known for leading the OWASP
> chapter in Los Angeles.  Cassio represents Symantec on the SAFECode
> technical committee and (ISC)2 in the development of the CSSLP
> certification. He holds a bachelor degree in computer science from
> Pontificia Universidade Catolica do Rio Grande Do Sul, a master's degree in
> software engineering from Santa Clara University, and a master's of business
> administration from the University of Southern California.
