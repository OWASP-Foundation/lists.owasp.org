Hi Cassio,



I’m helping my good friend Ron Perris to get some of the local IT community
into the PeopleSpace location in Irvine for the Orange County Code School’s
2nd Student Showcase.  I’ve included an ICS and some information on the
event below.



[image: Orange County Code School]

*Orange County Code School Student Showcase 2015 - #2*

The Orange County Code School (OCCS) would like to invite you to join us
for our 2nd student showcase of 2015.

OCCS is an immersive full-stack JavaScript school that will be wrapping up
its eighth week of classes on Friday, June 5th, and we are excited for the
opportunity to share with the local IT community all of the hard work our
students have been putting in.

We'll be hosting an invite-only gathering at PeopleSpace, located at 1691
Kettering St., Irvine CA 92614, for IT professionals of all levels to come
in and have our students showcase their web development projects.

The event will start at 6:00PM and is free to attend. Feel free to invite /
bring any fellow IT colleagues along as we assist to highlight the work of
the next generation of Software Engineers right here in Orange County.


Please accept the attached calendar invite to save the date. Bring yourself
and we'll bring the food, drinks, and web projects made by our students.

See you then!

OCCS
