For those interested, please apply online at intel.com/careers; job code is
700402


 Job Description
   Systems Programmer - 700402
 Description
 **** Job Description: This job code is only for use by College Recruiting
for screeing question purposes. ****

This is an exciting opportunity; we are looking for a passionate
professional that enjoys working with leading edge technologies.
As a member of the IT Engineering group in our Guadalajara Site, you'll be
collaborating with global teams in order to define, plan and implement the
next generation enterprise systems that will keep Intel's information
secure and readily available regardless of devices or platforms used.
You'll collaborate with an equally talented team and become the subject
matter expert in your field. If you believe you have what it takes to take
on this challenge, you are on top of the latest trends on Information
Systems such as Cloud Computing, Open Source, McAfee Enterprise Security
then we'd like to hear from you.

 Qualifications
 You must have a Bachelor, Masters or Ph.D. of Science degree in
Engineering, Information Systems, Computer Science, Mathematics or related
field with at least 5 years of professional experience.

Must have skills:

- English language proficiency (spoken, written) and Excellent
Communications skills are essential for consideration
- Knowledge and experience with platform protection security software,
vulnerability scanner and host based intrusion protection capabilities
- Familiarity with overall networking concepts/architecture/design; this
would include ACL/FW basics
- Understanding of engineering principles, methodologies and practices
- Understanding of product packaging and deployment practices
- Ability to understand a broad range of security capabilities and
articulate clear plans, strategies, and direction to management, business
partners and stakeholders
- Familiarity with McAfee Enterprise portfolio of products, ePO, Deep
Defender, Vulnerability Scanner, etc.
- Database and reporting skills (Microsoft SQL Server) and developing
reports using SQL Server Reporting Services
- Strong analytical and problem solving skills to troubleshoot and resolve
network/operating system security issues.
- Project Management experience, negotiation and influence skills, Business
Acumen
- Ability to work in a team environment and balance and prioritize work.
- Great Attitude, Proactive and not afraid of taking over new challenges.

Nice to have skills:

- Experience with .NET programming and scripting languages a plus.
- Security background/certifications beneficial (CISSP, GSEC, etc.)
- Familiar with product testing methodologies a plus.
- Knowledge of Cloud Computing environment
- Knowledge and experience working with Open Source Environments

 Job Category: Engineering
 Primary Location: Mexico-Mexico, Guadalajara
 Full/Part Time: Full Time
 Job Type: Experienced
 Regular/Temporary: Regular
 Posting Date: Oct 29, 2012
Apply Before: Oct 29, 2013

 Business Group Intel's Information Technology Group (IT) designs, deploys
and supports the information technology architecture and hardware/software
applications for Intel. This includes the LAN, WAN, telephony, data
centers, client PCs, backup and restore, and enterprise applications. IT is
also responsible for e-Commerce development, data hosting and delivery of
Web content and services.
