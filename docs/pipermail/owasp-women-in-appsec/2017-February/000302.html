Hello Everyone,

This is a reminder that we have the February WIA call tomorrow at 4pm EST.
The call information is included at the bottom of this email. The agenda
has been updated (
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1RrUIrAgdL5llFXrZF0z4mmoGFPwN6uFKsscxQPXabDk/edit?usp=sharing).
Please let me know if you want to add anything to it.

We will be discussing (and hopefully voting on) short and long-term goals
as well as determining the priority of those goals. These are the goals
that were proposed at January's meeting:

   1. Increase visibility of WIA
      1. Wiki Page
      2. Twitter
      3. Other
   2. Plan/execute consistent events/branding at global conferences
   3. Increase # of women speakers at global conferences
      1. Include talks about career and life experiences, i.e. life/career
      after kids
   4. Increase # of women speakers at chapter meetings
   5. Provide training for women speakers
      1. Writing proposals
      2. Creating slides
      3. Giving talks
   6. Increase # of speakers from other fields
   7. Reach out to women at the university level to encourage them to
   pursue a career in application security
   8. Reach out to women in high school
      1. Possible partnership with Cynja
   9. Reach out to women in the community to encourage them to pursue a
   career in application security
   10. Create a webinar series
   11. Sponsor a Lean-in circle
      1. Help women build business and tech skills
      2. Hold working sessions to help build weaknesses

I look forward to speaking with you all tomorrow!
