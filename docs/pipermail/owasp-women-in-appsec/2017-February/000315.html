Hi Bev,

It's required by the OWASP Committees 2.0 Operational Model (
https://owasp.org/index.php/Governance/OWASP_Committees).  Specifically, it
says:

    "The committee will then conduct a vote on the applicant, via the same
medium"

with "medium" being the email list.

Interestingly, it doesn't appear to require votes be public for any other
decisions, just those related to adding new Voting Members.  It does
require discussion be public and archived publicly, but is silent on
decisions that are made.  I would think for transparency, we'd want all
votes public anyway though.



- Bil
