Dear colleagues:



There is just ONE DAY LEFT to nominate outstanding young researchers for
the Paula Kantor Award. We are so pleased that beginning this year, the
award will come with a US$2500 prize, thanks to a generous endowment from
Paula Kantor’s parents. If you work with a young woman (under 40) doing
outstanding field research in the field of gender equity, we urge you to
nominate them for this award.



More information on eligibility and the nomination process can be found here
<https://www.icrw.org/news/icrw-announces-third-annual-paula-kantor-award/>.
