Hello everyone,

I hope you are all well. There is a free hacking event for women taking
place in DC next month in case you or anyone you know is interested in
attending! Here's the info:

*In honor of Women’s History Month and in celebration of pioneering women
hackers, HackEd, The Hackerground and partners, invite you to a day of fun
and hacking!  *

*Join us for an all women’s team based “Capture the Flag” (CTF) event.  The
winning team takes home a $1000 cash prize.  Ladies of all levels of
experience are welcome!  If you don’t have a team we will introduce you to
one.*

*When: Sunday, March 18th from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM*
*Where:  2231 Crystal Dr #1000, Arlington, VA 22202  (10th Floor)*

*This event is free! Lunch and snacks will be provided. Register today
at https://hackeducate.com/WRTW_GirlsCTF.html
<https://hackeducate.com/WRTW_GirlsCTF.html> *
