Some interesting ideas were discussed during the meeting about involving
more ladies into appsec.

Where I can contribute here is, hopefully , we are organising a bug bounty
event for OWASP projects during Appsec US.

Right now, I'm coordinating with some OWASP projects and setting up a VM
hosting web applications protected by some OWASP defender project libraries
who are doing an amazing work.
The bounty is to help strength their security and improve their quality.
This is an on going Bug Bounty  that has started with OWASP ZAP and it will
be further launch for other projects.

With this in mind I have submitted an activity for appsec US and I would be
more than happy to have ladies visit us during this activity, and I can
explain them more about what we do , activity such as pen testing, learn
about Owasp projects and what exactly is bug hunting, which are quite well
know activity for Appsec professionals

Let me know if this is something you might be interested to get ladies pass
by, as a female in appsec, I'll be more than glad to show them and let them
try out things if they want to
