Hi Team,

Through OWASP Bangalore chapter we are planning an ambitious event  OWASP
Seasides(http://www.owaspseasides.com). This will be a free of cost event

evening talks and awesome networking parties, great food and drinks. Mark
the dates, 27-28 February to 1-2 march 2019.

supporters. Special thanks to Martin Knobloch and Netherlands chapter. I
