Today is the last day to nominate yourself for one of our leadership
roles.  You can see the current list of candidates here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Women_In_AppSec#tab=2017_ELECTIONS

Note that at the request of the two candidates running for the Volunteer
Coordinator, I've split the role into two, one for North America and one
for Europe.  This allows a local person to be in the same time zone with
volunteers.  If anyone is interested in one of those roles, or for another
region, please let me know.
