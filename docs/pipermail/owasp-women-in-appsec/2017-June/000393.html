I've added an elections tab to the WIA wiki, which you can view here:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Women_In_AppSec#tab=2017_ELECTIONS

It lists the candidates for the upcoming election and the timeline.  A few
key things to note:

1. You have until tomorrow to let me know if you want to be considered for
any of the positions.  The first four positions require you be a Voting
Member, the last two are open to any WIA member.  You can either email me
and I'll add you to the wiki, or you can add yourself to the wiki.

2. I'll send out an email to all Voting Members on Saturday and ask that
they select one candidate per position.  You'll have eight days to respond
with your vote.  At the end of the eight days, I'll tally the ballets and
announce the winners.  In the case of one person winning multiple roles,
they'll pick the role they want, and the runner-up of the other role will
be declared the winner.

3. We only have a single candidate for Chair, and no candidates for the
Media Relations Coordinator.  Consider stepping up!


- Bil
