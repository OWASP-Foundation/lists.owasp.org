Hi all,

Some really good ideas, it's great to see so many.

Given the time constraints, i think it would be good to keep it simple this
year and not to overwhelm the attendees/ ourselves. It takes a lot of time
to organise each event.

Why not run a drinks/ food reception ( evening) and invite local women in
tech/ code groups ( we did this in the EU and it went great). We could hit
up some inspirational speakers to speak at this event. It should be open to
between 100-150 people.

We should also have a WIA committee meeting in person to speak about plans
etc for the next year (maybe dial in details for those who are not
attending in person).

What does everyone think, might be a good way to break the new roles in
while still having a really successful event. Then aim for bigger and
better next year (with a lot more time to plan).

My two cents.

Owen
