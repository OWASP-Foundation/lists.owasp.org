Hello WIA,

Below are the results from the 2017 elections.  The following people will
serve the remainder of the 2017 year in their positions.  We'll hold
elections again later this year for the 2018 calendar year.

Congratulations to the incoming leadership team!

We do have two candidates that won multiple positions, Cathy Hall for
Secretary and Treasurer, and Loredana Mancini for Vice Chair and Volunteer
Coordinator of Europe.  Since neither had an alternative candidate, I'll
let the incoming leadership team decide how they want to resolve it (e.g.
let them hold multiple positions, or try to fill a vacate position, etc.).

Until it's resolved, I'll hold off on updating the wiki with the elected
candidates.

Thanks and congrats!

- Bil
