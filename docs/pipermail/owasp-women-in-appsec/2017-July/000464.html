I think funds from anywhere would be good to get for WIA. I understand your
comment about being self sufficient but in the end that means hitting up
sponsors.

In the end the more money we have the more we can do, like sending people
to speak at major conferences or even to day events like what was run in
Dublin last year. We should also maybe look in to getting SWAG for sending
to these events (Stickers are always good)

We might need to gauge the effort required to apply for the funds.
