Hi all,

On our list there is a plan to schedule a WIA Committee & Mentors Committee
roundtable, face to face meeting. After that meeting, we can have a WIA
Committee meeting, if you like. I doubt that the WIA & Mentors meeting will
take more than 30 to 45 minutes. We can have a networking break for 15
minutes and then WIA afterwards.

Please let me know what comes up with the Doodle Poll and will add send out
calendar invitation with gotomeeting details.

I think that it is a good idea to Mentor the new officers until they are
ready to do things on their own. I'm currently mentoring Tanya. Perhaps
Bil, Owen and others could mentor some of the other officers between now
and then.

We will be able to set up a WIA person at the registration table at
AppSecUSA. Who wants to do that? Do we want to print out some kind of
program insert about the WIA activities for AppSecUSA?

I think that AppSecUSA should cover most of WIA expenses as part of the
event budget. What do you think? They have in the past.

Best wishees,
Bev
