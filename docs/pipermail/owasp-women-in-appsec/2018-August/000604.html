*Hi All,*


All Day DevOps is back!


*What:* *All Day DevOps* <http://www.alldaydevops.com/>*, Live Online*

*When: October 17, 2018 (24 hours)*

*Where: From your desktop, laptop, or mobile device*

*Free Registration: Click* *here*
<https://www.alldaydevops.com/all-day-devops-2018-register>


On October 17th, we will be supporting the live online All Day DevOps
<http://www.alldaydevops.com/> conference. This is a 24 hour event with 5
simultaneous tracks, delivering 100 sessions in 38 time zones. Session
tracks include Cloud Native Infra and Monitoring, DevSecOps and Automated
Security, CI/CD - Continuous Everything, SRE - Site Reliability
Engineering, and Cultural Transformation.


*Featured speakers* include Cindy Healy, Chris Roberts, Chris Short,
Courtney Kissler, Marc Cluet, and Colin Wynd. Registration is free. Full
details are located at *AllDayDevOps.com*.

Let us know if you have any questions.
