Hello!


Women in AppSec is hosting two events at AppSec EU 2017
<https://2017.appsec.eu/program/women-in-appsec>. We’re looking for men and
women to contribute as mentors for both events.

This is the commitment we’re asking for from mentors:

   -

   A picture and bio for the website
   -

   A time commitment of two hours between the two events
   -

      30+ minutes at the networking event
      -

      1½ hours at the mentoring breakfast


We are appreciative of everyone who is willing to mentor women who are
interested in beginning or furthering their careers in the AppSec industry.
As a token of our thanks we are offering mentors 25% off their conference
tickets.

Interested? Please fill out this form
<https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSflgAAGXZQG-9H2sB8q8Bt6qNdUKQaRcmoj9Ith9bO3OjM6IA/viewform?usp=sf_link>.
If you have any questions please contact us at euwiaevents at owasp.org.

Best,

The AppSec EU WIA Planning team
