>>>>>>>>> -----------------------------------------
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *OWASP Women in Application Security (AppSec) Program*
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *Seeking Sponsorship*
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> The OWASP Foundation, in recognition of value to both
>>>>>>>>> organizations and society, is working to support and enhance programs that
>>>>>>>>> increase the participation of women in the field of application security.
>>>>>>>>> As part of this effort, OWASP is currently seeking funds to sponsor women
>>>>>>>>> from North America to attend  OWASP AppSec USA to be held in New York City
>>>>>>>>> in November of 2013.
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *Objectives*
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> OWASP’s current program objective is to encourage female students
>>>>>>>>> at both the undergraduate and graduate levels who are dedicated to a career
>>>>>>>>> in information security and/or application development, to expand their
>>>>>>>>> skills and pursue application security. Attendance for two women to the
>>>>>>>>> OWASP NYC AppSec USA 2013 conference, and at least one of the in-depth
>>>>>>>>> training sessions, will be the award for this year's winners. The Women in
>>>>>>>>> AppSec Program was successfully launched at AppSec USA in 2011 this year's
>>>>>>>>> event hopes to build on that trend and further engage women in the
>>>>>>>>> community.
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *Sponsorship Needs*
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> The OWASP Women in AppSec Committee is currently leading the
>>>>>>>>> effort to seek sponsors for this program.  Our goals are to raise funds to
>>>>>>>>> sponsor at least two attendees:
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>>  - $3,000 for recipient #1 to cover conference fee, training fee,
>>>>>>>>> travel and accommodation.
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> -  $3,000 for recipient #2 to cover conference fee, training fee,
>>>>>>>>> travel and accommodation.
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *Sponsor Opportunities: What do our sponsors receive? *
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> All OWASP Women in AppSec sponsors will receive the following
>>>>>>>>> benefits for contributing funds to this program:
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> - Sponsors mention in the AppSec USA Program
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> - Logo, company name, and link to company website will be added to
>>>>>>>>> the AppSec USA website in the Sponsors Page<http://appsecusa.org/2013/sponsors/>
>>>>>>>>> .
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> *Past Winners*
>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> Should we add a section here with quotes from our past
>>>>>>>>> winners? What do you all think?
>>>>>>>>>

>>>>>>>>>
>>>>>>>>> If you have questions, or if you wish to contribute to the
>>>>>>>>> program, please e-mail Samantha Groves at:
