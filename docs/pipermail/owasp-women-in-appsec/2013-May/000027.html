OWASP Women in Security Grant Program

For the second time, the OWASP Foundation is gathering sponsors for a
program to increase the participation of women in the field of application
security. OWASP will use funding to sponsor women from around the United
States to attend the OWASP AppSec USA 2013 conference taking place in New
York City.

Our focus is to encourage female professionals and students to attend
AppSec USA 2013, and to advance their career in the information security
field.

If you have questions regarding the grant program, please e-mail:
appsec_usa_women_in_security at lists.owasp.org.
