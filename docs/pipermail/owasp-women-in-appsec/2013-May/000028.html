OWASP Women in Application Security (AppSec) Program 


The OWASP Foundation supports and enhances programs to increase the
participation of women in the field of application security recognizing the
value to organizations and society.  OWASP is seeking funding at this time
to sponsor women from North America to attend an OWASP AppSec USA 2013
<http://appsecusa.org/2013/training/> training course and the OWASP AppSec
USA 2013  <http://appsecusa.org/2013/about/> conference talks to be held in
New York City in November of 2013. 

 

Current Objective

 

OWASP's current program goal is to encourage female students at both the
undergraduate and graduate levels who are dedicated to a career in
information security and/or application development to expand their skills
and pursue application security.  Attendance for two women at OWASP NY
AppSec USA 2013, an advanced application security conference, and at least
one of the in-depth training sessions, has been established as a success at
the last NY AppSec conference and this effort aims to continue and build
upon that trend.  


About the Current Program


 

The OWASP Foundation Women in AppSec program provides merit-based funding
for women to attend OWASP AppSec USA 2013 in New York. 

 

Each applicant must be a woman that meets our eligibility criteria below: 

 

.       Is a part- or full-time student at an accredited four-year college
or university or graduate program in the United States.

.       Is involved in an information technology degree and/or professional
information systems position.

.       Have an interest in pursuing application security in an information
security and/or application development role.

 

Sponsorship Opportunities 


The OWASP Women in AppSec Committee is currently leading the effort to seek
sponsors for this program.  At a minimum, our goals are to raise funds to
cover for two attendees: 

.       $2,400 for recipient from outside the area for travel and lodging
and/or 

.       $300 for recipient from in the NY metro area for local travel
expenses
