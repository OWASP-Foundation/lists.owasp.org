Dear Samantha,

This looks great, thank you!

A few comments:

I think that the objectives should match what is on the website to avoid
any confusion or misunderstandings in the award sponsors, awardees, and
award guidelines for the selection committee.

Has the value on the AppSec website placement been determined? I'm
concerned because I think that the actual value is going to be more than
the sponsor amount. It seems to me that the valuation is rather low.
Shouldn't a value be assigned to the different sponsorship benefits?

Also, will the sponsors be mentioned on social media? Facebook? Twitter?
Mailing list announcements, etc? If so, the value should be much greater
than $3000, more like $6000, don't you think?

Just my two cents worth at this point, but that is what "jumped out" at me
at first glance. Thank you and best wishes.

Sincerely,
Bev

> -----------------------------------------
>
> *OWASP Women in Application Security (AppSec) Program*
>
> *Seeking Sponsorship*
>
> The OWASP Foundation, in recognition of value to both organizations and
> society, is working to support and enhance programs that increase the
> participation of women in the field of application security.  As part of
> this effort, OWASP is currently seeking funds to sponsor women from North
> America to attend  OWASP AppSec USA to be held in New York City in November
> of 2013.
>
> *Objectives*
>
> OWASP’s current program objective is to encourage female students at both
> the undergraduate and graduate levels who are dedicated to a career in
> information security and/or application development, to expand their skills
> and pursue application security. Attendance for two women to the OWASP NYC
> AppSec USA 2013 conference, and at least one of the in-depth training
> sessions, will be the award for this year's winners. The Women in AppSec
> Program was successfully launched at AppSec USA in 2011 this year's event
> hopes to build on that trend and further engage women in the community.
>
> *Sponsorship Needs*
>
> The OWASP Women in AppSec Committee is currently leading the effort to
> seek sponsors for this program.  Our goals are to raise funds to sponsor at
> least two attendees:
>
>  - $3,000 for recipient #1 to cover conference fee, training fee, travel
> and accommodation.
>
> -  $3,000 for recipient #2 to cover conference fee, training fee, travel
> and accommodation.
>
> *Sponsor Opportunities: What do our sponsors receive? *
>
> All OWASP Women in AppSec sponsors will receive the following benefits for
> contributing funds to this program:
>
> - Sponsors mention in the AppSec USA Program
>
> - Logo, company name, and link to company website will be added to the
> AppSec USA website in the Sponsors Page<http://appsecusa.org/2013/sponsors/>
> .
>
> *Past Winners*
>
> Should we add a section here with quotes from our past winners? What do
> you all think?
>
> *Additional Information*
>
> If you have questions, or if you wish to contribute to the program, please
> e-mail Samantha Groves at: appsec_usa_women_in_security at lists.owasp.org
