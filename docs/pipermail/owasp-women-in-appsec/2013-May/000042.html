>>> OWASP Women in Application Security (AppSec) Program****
>>>
>>> ** **
>>>
>>> The OWASP Foundation supports and enhances programs to increase the
>>> participation of women in the field of application security recognizing the
>>> value to organizations and society. OWASP is seeking funding at this time
>>> to sponsor women from North America to attend an OWASP AppSec USA 2013 training
>>> course <http://appsecusa.org/2013/training/> and the OWASP AppSec USA
>>> 2013 conference talks <http://appsecusa.org/2013/about/> to be held in
>>> New York City in November of 2013.****
>>>
>>>  ****
>>>
>>> *Current Objective*****
>>>
>>>  ****
>>>
>>> OWASP’s current program objective is to encourage female students at
>>> both the undergraduate and graduate levels who are dedicated to a career in
>>> information security and/or application development, to expand their skills
>>> and pursue application security. Attendance for two women to the OWASP NY
>>> C AppSec USA 2013 conference, and at least one of the in-depth training
>>> sessions, will be the award for this year's winners. The Women in
>>> AppSec Program was successfully launched at AppSec USA in 2011 this year's
>>> event hopes to build on that trend and further engage women in the
>>> community.**
>>>  About the Current Program****
>>>
>>> ** **
>>>
>>> The OWASP Foundation Women in AppSec program provides merit-based
>>> funding for women to attend OWASP AppSec USA 2013 in New York.****
>>>
>>>  Each applicant must be a woman that meets our eligibility criteria
>>> below:****
>>>
>>>  ****
>>>
>>> ·      Is a part- or full-time student at an accredited four-year
>>> college or university or graduate program.****
>>>
>>>         Or
>>>
>>                        Is involved in information security and/or application
>> development in a professional role.
>>
>>> ·       AND Has an interest in exploring application security in an
>>> information security and/or application development role.
>>>
>>>  ****
>>>

>>> seek sponsors for this program.  Our goals are to raise funds to
>>> sponsor at least two attendees:****

>>> and accommodation. ****
