Hello All,

Here is my draft of the letter I would like to send to chapters. Let me
know what you think.
---------------------------------

Hello OWASP X Chapter Members,

I just wanted to reach out and introduce myself. My name is Samantha
Groves, and I am the Projects Manager here at OWASP. This year, I am
helping organise the Women in AppSec
Program<http://appsecusa.org/2013/activities/owasp-women-in-application-security-appsec-program/>for
AppSec USA this year taking place in New York City.

The program objective is to encourage female students at both the
undergraduate and graduate levels, instructors, and professional
workingwomen who are dedicated to a career in information security and/or
application development, to expand their skills and pursue application
security.

The OWASP Women in AppSec Committee is currently leading the effort to seek
sponsors for this program, and this is why I am reaching out to you today.
We are seeking $6,000 to cover conference fee, training fee, travel and
accommodation for 2 participants, and I was wondering if your Chapter had
any funds available to help sponsor this program. There is no minimum
amount required to help fund the initiatives as any little bit helps us get
closer to our program goals.

If you are interested in sponsoring this initiative, please do reach out to
me and let me know of your interest.

Thank you for your time. I look forward to hearing from you soon.


Sam G.
