Hello ladies and men colleagues : 

Thanks Zoe for the introduction and thanks Fateh for the earlier mention too.

As Fateh mentioned I am one of the managers of a quite large (130 members) LI group for women in cyber security in the Middle East. The group has women members mainly from Egypt, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait and some other countries too. The group includes CISOs , security consultants, cyber security entrepreneurs , and young talent too still starting their careers many of whom specialize in application security and who I believe would be delighted to participate in OWASP local and global activities/initiatives 

If any of you ladies would like to join the group to announce OWASP women in appsec activities in the region or connect with many of the young ladies who specialize in application security please let me know ; or send a request to join; the group can be found from the below link:

Women in Cyber Security Middle East

Also, if you would like to sponsor or send any of the OWASP women in appsec from nearby countries to the women CTF that will take place in December at the Cairo security camp , please reach out to the camp organizer, Moataz Salah, CEO of cyber talents (copied) and the initiator of this women CTF. 

Looking forward to further collaboration. 

My best regards,

Abeer Khedr
Information security director
National Bank of Egypt
