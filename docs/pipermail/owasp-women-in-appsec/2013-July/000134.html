> Women in Application Security Program: Application Form
> The OWASP Foundation, in recognition of value to both organizations and
> society, is working to support and enhance programs that increase the
> participation of women in the field of application security.
>
> **OBJECTIVES**
>
> OWASP’s current program objective is to encourage female students and
> professionals to expand their skills and pursue careers in application
> security. Attendance for two women to the OWASP NYC AppSec USA 2013
> conference, and at least one of the in-depth training sessions, will be the
> award for this year’s winners. The Women in AppSec Program was successfully
> launched at in 2011. This year’s event hopes to build on that trend and
> further engage women in the community.
>
> Please use the form below to submit your application. The application
> deadline is Monday, September 09, 2013 at 5pm GMT. Contact Samantha Groves
