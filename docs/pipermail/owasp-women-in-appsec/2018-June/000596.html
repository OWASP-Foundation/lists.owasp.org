Hi All,

Under DSCI’s “Women in Security” initiative, they have partnered with
Target India for a special event to bring *an exclusive meet up for women,
by women pioneering in the fields of Information Security*.

This event is an expression of DSCI and Target India’s commitment to
achieve full potential of diversity in the field of cybersecurity. More
than 100 key women stakeholders from the security ecosystem, including
security leaders, professionals,  academicians and leading researchers are
expected to attend this meet. Please find below the event agenda and
registration link.

The event will be held at *Target Office* (@Embassy Manyata Tech Park,
Bangalore) on *12th June, 2018* from *1:00 PM – 5:30 PM.*

Registration link: http://bit.ly/iamswati

Please feel free to nominate women security professionals from your
organization and network to be a part of this inspiring event. They can
leverage this opportunity to hear from some of leading women professionals
in the security fraternity and also contribute with their mindshare to the
overall discussions.

Looking forward to meet you on June 12th !
