*Dear OWASP Women in AppSec community,The WIA Committee is opening
nominations for the role of Committee Treasurer. This Monday, June 4, Bev
Corwin, a long standing OWASP WIA co-founding advisor to the Committee’s
development, expressed her desire to retire her current interim role as WIA
Treasurer. We will collaboratively nominate and elect a new WIA Treasurer
from our various global communities.  Please note the following in
submitting names for nomination: - Submission deadline for nominations:
Friday June 22- OWASP global Committee Operational model, implemented as
the WIA Committee Governance Model:
https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Governance/OWASP_Committees
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Governance/OWASP_Committees>- Updates from
WIA Officer calls regarding Bev’s resignation as WIA Treasurer can be found
on the WIA Committee working document:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/17r1btFUoSwxxL1hLnTmse9MY5t_5fWWmAr032gR5qrU/edit?usp=sharing
<https://docs.google.com/document/d/17r1btFUoSwxxL1hLnTmse9MY5t_5fWWmAr032gR5qrU/edit?usp=sharing>Looking
forward to your nominations as we continue to expand as a global committee
of diverse application security leaders!Sincerely,OWASP Women in AppSec
Committee Officers*
