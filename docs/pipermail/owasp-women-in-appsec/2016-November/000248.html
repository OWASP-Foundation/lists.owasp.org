Title: OWASP WIA December Meeting

OWASP WIA December Meeting
Dec 05, 2016 at 5:00 pm - 6:00 pm EST

Agenda:

1. Status of conversion from Initiative to Committee.

2. AppSec USA 2017.  I've started a conversation with Laura Grau regarding  
getting space for WIA and would like to discuss the high-level plans.

3. Scope of the new committee.  Draft is here:  
https://docs.google.com/a/owasp.org/document/d/1-cDEmeiYpaRbqWpgHW3LIGTT_4zqtfpUn1wS-lSSOZE/edit?usp=sharing

4. Scope discussion: working with high schoolers.

5. Scope discussion: should we hardwire the below sentence into our scope,  
or just say that we'll work with other committees and inititatives?

	"Collaborate with OWASP Education Committee, Mentor Initiative (soon to be  
Committee) and Learning Gateway Project providing liaisons to attend  
regular meetings, and provide access for WIA members to participate and  
contribute to Learning Gateway Project user research and requirements  
development activities."

6. Other topics
