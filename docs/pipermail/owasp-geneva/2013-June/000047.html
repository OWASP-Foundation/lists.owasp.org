Hello,

Some information regarding the upcoming OWASP Geneva evening event on
June 25th

0. If you are in a hurry
-------------------------------
a) Registration: http://owaspeutourgeneva.eventbrite.com
b) Becoming a sponsor: http://owaspeutourgeneva.eventbrite.com
c) We have 3 conference slots left. Are you interested?
d) That's it. Thank you!



1. Registrations
-------------------------------
a) The registration system is now live and can be reached at:
http://owaspeutourgeneva.eventbrite.com/ (Sponsor tickets are indicated
in Euros, this is due to our online payment processor's limitations)

b) For participants who registered by email, please re-contact us if you
haven't received our confirmation message yet, or directly register on
the online registration form.


2. Evening Program
-------------------------------
a) Sebastien Deleersnyder (Belgium) will experience on setting up and
working with the OpenSAMM application security governance framework. The
presentation will be hosted in English and will tie in nicely with our
previous chapter meeting during which we heard about the BSIMM model.

b) There are still 3 conference slots left. Our objective is to reach a
50/50 distribution between local and international speakers. Please
contact us if you or someone around you has an interesting appsec
presentation to share at the next evening meeting.


3. Sponsors: easy access
-------------------------------
a) The OWASP EU Tour Geneva is free to all registered participants. We
reached 35% of our financial objective thanks to a first sponsor, which
is excellent news but also means we still need sponsors to cover 65% of
the remaining expenses.

Entry event corporate supporter option: CHF 250.-
Full OWASP worldwide corporate supporter option: CHF 5'000.-

If you would like to participate in financing the event, fast access to
the sponsorship options is enabled on the registration form:
http://owaspeutourgeneva.eventbrite.com/

Please contact us for any further information.
