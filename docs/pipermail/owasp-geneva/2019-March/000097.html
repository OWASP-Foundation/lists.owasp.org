re-Bonjour,

Dans le courant de ce mois, la Fondation OWASP va migrer son service de
listes de diffusion par email (mailman) vers celui proposé par Google
(Google Groups).

En tant que membre de la liste, vous recevrez automatiquement un
courrier (et ceci pour chaque liste OWASP à laquelle vous êtes
inscrit-e) vous offrant la possibilité de maintenir votre adhésion, ou
de la résilier.

Bien entendu, les listes de diffusion OWASP restent un _service gratuit_.

En cas de doute, le team se tiendra à votre disposition pour répondre à
vos questions.

Cordialement,

Antonio Fontes
OWASP Genève

On 02.03.2019 04:16, Matt Tesauro wrote:
> FYI: Full details will always be
> at https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Staff-Projects/Mailman-EOL
>
> _*What's the high level plan/overview?*_
>
>   * All inactive/unused lists have been archived and 139 remain on
>     lists.owasp.org <http://lists.owasp.org> as of March 1st, 2019
>   * For each of those remaining lists, a new Google Group will be
>     created with the same ownership as Mailman.  This will be complete
>     by March 8th, 2019
>   * On March 8th, we'll start sending emails to the remaining lists
>     letting your members know about the move to Google Groups and
>     providing instructions/links to opt into the new mail list on
>     Google Groups.
>       o Continue to send messages to lists.owasp.org
>         <http://lists.owasp.org> during the transition period
>   * We'll repeat that message to your list(s) 3 times ending on March
>     22nd, 2019
>       o This will be the last day that we cut over to Google Groups. 
>         Starting March 22nd, send to your new Google Group list to
>         keep in contact with your list's community
>   * March 24, 2019 - the Mailman service on lists.owasp.org
>     <http://lists.owasp.org> will be turned off
>   * March 27, 2019 - the Mailman list archives will be moved to a
>     static web host
>
> _*What should I tell my list members?*_
>
> You're welcome to share this email and the link to the wiki page to
> give your list members early warning of the upcoming changes.
>
> _*What will my new list address be?*_
>
> The format we're using for the Google Groups is the following
> [name]-[type]@owasp.org <http://owasp.org> so
>
>   * For projects, the Google Group would be like
>     testing-guide-project at owasp.org
>     <mailto:testing-guide-project at owasp.org>
>   * For chapters, the Google Group would be like
>     london-chapter at owasp.org <mailto:london-chapter at owasp.org>
>   * We also expect to have [name]-event at owasp.org
>     <mailto:event at owasp.org>, [name]-committee at owasp.org
>     <mailto:committee at owasp.org>, and other types gong forward
>
> _*My list is getting SPAM currently, what do I do?*_
>
> To be brief, there's going to be some amount of SPAM on the lists
> until after the migration - it couldn't be avoided for a whole bunch
> of reasons.  That said, you've got some choices:
>
>  1. Migrate your list early (see previous question above)
>  2. Make some changes to your list configuration to help reduce the
>     SPAM traffic.  Bjorn made a post
>     <https://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/owasp-leaders/2019-February/019695.html>
>     on the Leaders List  with some advice on setting to help out with
>     this including this screenshot
>     <https://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/owasp-leaders/attachments/20190222/1bbda485/attachment-0004.png>. 
>     I'd recommend the advice on that thread
>     <http://lists.owasp.org/pipermail/owasp-leaders/2019-February/019694.html>.
>  3. You can always turn on emergency moderation
>     <http://www.gnu.org/software/mailman/mailman-admin/node14.html>
>     for your list an only let legit posts through during the migration
>     period.
>
> *_To sum up:_*
> If you have questions, please reply to this Google Group
> (mailman-list-owners at owasp.org <mailto:mailman-list-owners at owasp.org>)
> - all correspondence will also be available via a browser
> at https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/?hl=en#!forum/mailman-list-owners
>
> Thanks for your patience and understanding as we make this improvement
> to our infrastructure.
>
> Cheers!
