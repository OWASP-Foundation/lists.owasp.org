This mail is regarding the yearly information security conference we have 
been organizing for the last four years. c0c0n 2011 - International

I would like to take the privilege of requesting your active

speakers at different levels. For more information about the event, 
please visit - http://www.informationsecurityday.com/c0c0n/ .
  
If you have any plans/ideas/suggestions that we can incorporate as part of this platform, 
please do let me know. Kindly ping me in case of any queries. Expecting 
your Co-operation with us in spreading the event message by forwarding 
the link to as much people as possible.
