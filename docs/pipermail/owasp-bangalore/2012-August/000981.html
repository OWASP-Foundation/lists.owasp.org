Announcing null/OWASP/Garage4hackers/SecurityXploded Bangalore August
meet-up on Saturday 11th August
2012

This meet is special. It was 4 long years ago when we had our 1st null
Bangalore monthly meetup. Apart from the regularly scheduled talks we
shall have a small celebration on the occasion of the 4th birthday of
null Bangalore.

The meet is divided into two parts, the monthly talks and the training
on Reverse Engineering. The Reverse Engineering training will start at
12:45 PM by the  SecurityXploded/Garage4Hackers team.


The schedule for this month's meet is as outlined below:

09:30 - 10:10: Web AppSec Basics - CSRF with Mutillidae - Riyaz
10:10 - 10:25: Introductions
10:25 - 10:40: News Bytes - Ram
10:40 - 11:15: Basics of Metasploit - Atul
11:15 - 11:35: Networking & Break
11:35 - 12:10: UI Redressing Attacks - Amol
12:10 - 12:30: Feedback & Topic discussion for next monthly meet
12:30 - 12:45: Celebration of the 4th Year of Bangalore Null Chapter
12:45 - 13:50: Exploit Development - Harsimran Walia

VENUE DETAILS
Thoughtworks, 2nd Floor, ACR Mansion,
147/f, 8th Main Road, 3rd Block, Koramangala,
Bangalore - 560034
Google Maps: http://goo.gl/bokSL
Landmark : Next to Satya's Bar and Mercure Hotel

+ If you are coming from From Inner Ring road get on to Ooty
Chocolates road and after a small crossroad this will be on the right
hand side.
+ If you are coming from the Raheja Residency road then take a left
turn at the small crossroad and this will be on your right hand side.
+ If you are coming from Koramanagala BDA complex take a right turn at
the small crossroad and this will be on your right hand side.
