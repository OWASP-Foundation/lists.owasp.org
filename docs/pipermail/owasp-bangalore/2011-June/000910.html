Greetings!

I am Shailendra Kadre from JP Nagar. I have just joined this mailing list.
So thought of just introducing myself to this group.

I am an IT professional with conventional ADMS back ground. 14 years of
experience in Application Development and Maintenance, Project and Program
management. New to the IT Security field but I am pretty serious about
it.Recently I have published a book by the name 'Going Corporate: A Geek's
Guide' in USA with Apress Inc. The book is on IT Operations and Delivery
Management.

I have two kids Neha (7) and Vivek (3). Neha is going to PSBB school in 3rd
standard and Vivel has just started schooling. My wife Meenakshi works for
an insurance company. Currently I am on my own trying to cut a niche for
myself in the field of InfoSec. I am guest lecturer in Operations Management
in a couple of Universities in Bangalore. My hobbies are photography,
travelling, reading and making new friends.

It will be great to know the other members of this group as the time passes
by. Some one recently told me, I can take some courses at OWASP site but

help me with that?
