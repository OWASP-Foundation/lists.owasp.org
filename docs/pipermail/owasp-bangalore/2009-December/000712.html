ClubHack2009
*India’s own International Hackers’ Convention*

*ClubHack* is proud to welcome you for India’s OWN, ONLY and ONE of its kind
international hackers’ convention on 5th, 6th and 7th December 2009 in Pune,
India.

Started in Dec 2007, this convention brings together hackers from all over
the country & some of the celebrated international hackers. The sole purpose
of this event is “making security a common sense”. Technical community in
India has already started naming *ClubHack* as India’s own version of
“Defcon” or “Hack in the box”.


December 2007, India witnessed its FIRST ever international hackers’
convention and 2008 was the sequel.

The IT hub of the country, Pune, provides perfect environment for this
unique convention.
The audience largely includes, C-level executives, Sys Admins, IT Managers,
Developers, QA Engineers, Lawyers, Cyber Crime investigators & “Ethical”
hackers.


In 2009, * ClubHack *is proud to announce alliance with UK Science and
Innovation Network, British High Commission in India and City University.

* ClubHack*2009 is a 3 day event with below mentioned high level agenda.

*5th Dec 2009:*
Technical talks - Paper presentation

 *6th Dec 2009:*
Workshops and hands-on trainings

 *7th Dec 2009:*
Roundtable conference on Indo-UK cyber security issues in association with
UK Science and Innovation Network, British High Commission in India and City
University London.



This event is all about learning and sharing your knowledge in the field of
network security which can not be achieved without your participation. You
will do the security community a big help by spreading the word to whomever
you know might benefit from this event.



We look forward for your support to make this convention a roaring success.



To register: http://clubhack.com/2009/registration
