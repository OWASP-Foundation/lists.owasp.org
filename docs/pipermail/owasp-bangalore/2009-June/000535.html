It was nice to see few new faces in this session. Good to see that this
community is growing...
And thanks Abhay for the writeup...

I will mention the list of addons and its download locations

*Cleaning our work area* (Increasing firefox screen size)
Personal Menu - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3895
Hide Caption - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9256
Faviconize Tab - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3780
TitleBar Tweaks - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/57

*Security Assessment Helpers*
XSS Me - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7598
SQL Inject Me - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7597
FoxyProxy - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2464
Live HTTP Headers - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3829
Domain Details - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2166
Tamper Data - https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/966

Its not over yet.... The session i had taken was just an Intro... Will show
demo on hell lots of security related addons in the coming the sessions....

So don't miss it... Lets turn the worlds best browser into an ultimate
hacking tool...
