Regarding web application testing,
web application hackers handbook covers almost everything. it is also
cheaper than the beginners guide.
http://www.amazon.in/The-Web-Application-Hackers-Handbook/dp/8126533404/

TCP IP Illustrated volume 2 is more into programming and not concepts.
Volume 1 is the concepts book that is good to understand the protocols.
Volume 2 is the programming edition and needs volume 1 to understand
the concepts.
Douglas Comer's Internetworking With TCP/IP are also regarded equally
good and follow a similar pattern.
