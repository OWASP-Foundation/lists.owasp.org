IntelleSecure is pleased to inform you that we will conduct a training
program for the "Certified Ethical Hacker" certification offered by the

Workshop for CISSP aspirants.
 
We would be much obliged if you could forward this information to your
friends and colleagues who would be interested in these certifications.
Thank you!

Our five-day Certified Ethical Hacker training program aims to enable each
participant to identify and correct exploits that make information systems
vulnerable to attack. We focus on providing the skills required to obtain
the CEH certification which will serve as a significant step in your career
development as an information security professional. Each participant will
work on the latest techniques and tools for penetration testing because the
best way to protect an organization from hackers is to be an expert ethical
hacker. The Ethical Hacking and Countermeasures course prepares candidates
for the CEH exam. The course focuses on hacking techniques and technology
from a defensive perspective. This class will immerse the student into an
interactive environment where they will be shown how to scan, test, hack and
secure their own systems. The lab-intensive environment gives each student
in-depth knowledge and practical experience with current security systems
and technologies. Participants will understand how hackers attack and
exploit the vulnerabilities of a system, expose system weaknesses and
uncover unknown vulnerabilities. They will learn to implement
countermeasures to secure all of these.
