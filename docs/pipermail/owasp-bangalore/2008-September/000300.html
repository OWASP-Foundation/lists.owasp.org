Hola everyone,

OWASP Bangalore Chapter met at BarCamp 7 on the day following the
Teacher's Day, i.e. September 6th. :)
It was attended by Hari, me, Parshuram, Peeyush, Sharmishtha, Ankit,
Sumit, Kishore, and some names that I can't recall now. Sincere
apologies.

Hari introduced the junta to OWASP using his presentation, which he
downloaded over the (pathetically) slow net connection. Since we could
not get a room, we had to host in the open. The presentation was
accompanied/followed by a lot of questions, and I'd like to believe
that we have successfully roped in a couple of new members. ;)
The good part, however, was the participation by some enthusiastic
college students. I am pretty hopeful that we can find out ways to
spread the movement via these spirited souls. More on that during the
meeting.

We have scheduled the next meeting on *October 5th (Sunday) at 9:00 AM*.
