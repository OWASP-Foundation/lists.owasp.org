Dear OWASP members,
Some time ago, I had shared on this mailing list about an IEEE
workshop that we are organizing in Bangalore on Dec 9th.
You can find the updated information at http://www.cosec-workshop.info/

Now, in addition to the already planned Academia talks (as shown on
the website) and a couple of Industry talks (being planned), we would
also like to do a hands-on tutorial session on a security tool (say
for pen-testing or disassembly - IDA Pro ? OllyDbg ? etc).

So, if anybody on this list would be interested in giving an Invited
tutorial at the workshop, then please do reply back. It should be
around 30-45 minutes, entirely technical, hands-on and most important
of all, it should NOT be a product promotion (sorry folks, but this
being a research-based workshop we usually discourage such
promotions).

Please do respond back at the earliest, and we can then discuss your
presentation topic and finalize on maybe 1 or at most 2 such tutorial
sessions.

thanks and regards
Samir Saklikar
