Hi All,

*Please note that the meet is on 16th March and at the same venue
(ThoughtWorks, Koramangala).*
*
*
*Also note that all null meets are free for anyone to attend. There are no
fees and/or registration. Just come with an open mind and willingness to
share and learn.*

The meet is as usual divided into two parts, the monthly talks and the
training on Advanced Malware Analysis. The Advanced Malware Analysis
training will start at 13:00 by the SecurityXploded/Garage4Hackers team.

The schedule for this month's meet is as outlined below:

09:30 - 10:00:  Basic HTML 5 vulnerabilities - Satish
10:00 - 10:15:  Introductions
10:15 - 10:35:  News Bytes - Narayana
10:35 - 11:10:  Routing for Beginners - Ram
11:10 - 11:45:  WEP Cracking with aircrack-ng - Prajal Kulkarni
11:45 - 12:05:  Networking and Break
12:05 - 12:40:  HackIM WebApp level Walkthrough - Amol Naik
12:40 - 13:00:  Feedback and Topic discussion for next month meet
13:00 - 14:20:  Anti Analysis Technique - Swapnil

VENUE DETAILS
ThoughtWorks, Ground Floor, ACR Mansion,
147/f, 8th Main Road, 3rd Block, Koramangala,
Bangalore - 560034
Google Maps: http://goo.gl/bokSL

Landmark : Next to Satya's Bar and Mercure Hotel
+ If you are coming from From Inner Ring road get on to Ooty
Chocolates road and after a small crossroad this will be on the right
hand side.
+ If you are coming from the Raheja Residency road then take a left
turn at the small crossroad and this will be on your right hand side.
+ If you are coming from Koramanagala BDA complex take a right turn at
the small crossroad and this will be on your right hand side.

See you all there!
