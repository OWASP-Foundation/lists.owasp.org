*Proposed Change to Corporate Membership Model*

Comment Period Open

The board voted to move to a tiered corporate membership model.  This
enables organizations to support OWASP at a variety of levels.  We are
still working on the details of the updated membership matrix.  We are
seeking the input and feed back from current and potential corporate
supporters on the proposed model.

Please take a few minutes to review the proposal and provide us with your
feedback.  You can email us at
support at owasp.org<support at owasp.org?subject=Membership%20Model%20Feedback>
