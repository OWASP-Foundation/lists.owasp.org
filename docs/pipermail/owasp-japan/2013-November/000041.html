Leaders,

What an amazing AppSecUSA event! I hope that many of you were there
and had the opportunity to experience it. Another congratulations and
thanks to all the hard work by everyone involved especially Tom
Brennan, Peter Dean, Sarah Baso, the entire NY/NJ chapter, the OWASP
operations team and the many volunteers who donated countless hours.

In addition to all of the AppSecUSA festivities, the OWASP Board also
held our extended board meeting on Friday. We’ve found that the longer
format (about 6-7 hours) is much more effective for us to get things
done. More details coming on all of the items covered during our
meeting.

In the interim I wanted to both welcome our new board members for 2014
and announce the roles that were elected within the board.

New Board Members joining in 2014, per general election:

Tobias Gondrom
Josh Sokol
Fabio Cerullo

OWASP Board 2014
Michael Coates - Chair
Tom Brennan - Vice Chair
Josh Sokol - Treasurer
Tobias Gondrom - Secretary
Eoin Keary
Jim Manico
Fabio Cerullo

Also many thanks to Sebastien Deleersnyder and Dave Wichers for their
many years of service on the OWASP board. While their involvement on
the board will be coming to a close at the end of 2013 I fully expect
we'll continue seeing them at many appsec and other owasp events.


Thanks everyone and we’re looking forward to a great OWASP 2014!
