Hi friends,

OWASP is providing a scholarship program for Women in Application
Security (AppSec).
Application Deadline is September 9th at 5pm GMT!
It allows you to come to the AppSecUS in November in New York!
The funding is very comprehensive and covers conference fee, training
fee, travel and accommodation.

If you are a woman and interested in Application Security, I recommend
to apply.
(Note: OWASP membership is not required to apply.)

http://appsecusa.org/2013/activities/owasp-women-in-application-security-appsec-program/

Good luck and all the best,

Tobias
