Hello dear OWASPers in Japan,

the next OWASP board elections are coming and below you find the 
interviews with the current candidates.
It's a block of three interviews. Some comments are quite interesting, 
some are controversial.
At least not everyone has the same position. So you have something to 
choose from. :-)
And in case your membership has expired. There is time until Sep-30 to 
renew. The Asia membership is with just USD20 for one year a great 
bargain! Just follow the link in the text below. .

All the best and have a great start into the week.

Tobias



Your 2015 Global Board Candidate interviews are finished and are now 
posted here <https://soundcloud.com/owasp-podcast>.

Voting will start next month.  To help you make informed decisions, 
please take some time and listen to 8 of the 9 candidates answers when 
they were asked a few of the top questions that were provided and voted 
on by the OWASP community.

Individuals who's membership 
<https://docs.google.com/a/owasp.org/spreadsheets/d/1Tu2MAdu1xNq8RTaqHWMSb_0qM_OE6aaVgKB54q_fQIs/edit?usp=sharing>is 
on file as of September 30, 2015 will receive a ballot to vote on who 
they feel are the best 4 candidates to sit on the OWASP Global Board of 
Directors. OWASP members, both paid and honorary will have 17 days 
staring October 7, 2015 and ending on October 23, 2015 to cast their vote.
Individuals who are not OWASP members are encouraged to join OWASP 
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership> *by September 30, 2015* to 
be sure you receive a ballot so you too can cast your vote in this years 
election.

Your help is requested and is greatly appreciated if you would share 
this message with your contacts, on your social media channels, etc. 
More information on this year election can be found here 
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/2015_Global_Board_of_Directors_Election>.
