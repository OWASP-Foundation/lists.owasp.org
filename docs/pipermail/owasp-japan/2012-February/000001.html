The final selection of training courses for the conference are:

Assessing & Exploiting Web Applications with Samurai-WTF by Justin Searle of Utilisec(2 day course)
Hack Your Own Code: Advanced Training for Developers by Mike Park of Trustwave Spiderlabs(2 day course)
Hands On Web Application Testing: Assessing Web Apps the OWASP Way by Matt Tesauro (2 day course)
Mobile Penetration Testing: Start to Finish with iOS Apps by Jason Haddix of HP (2 day course)
Mobile Applications and Security by Prashant Verma of Paladion Networks (1 day course)
OWASP for CISOs and Senior Managers by Tobias Gondrom (1 day course)
Building Secure Web Apps by Klaus Johannes Rusch of IBM (1 day course)
