Anthony D’Amato, Partner in eDelta Consulting will be speaking on the subject of IT Governance. Anthony started out as an Electrical Engineer where he made the leap to Public Accounting and ran several IT audit engagements. He later formed his own very successful consulting company. In the IT Audit community Anthony also participates in training and manager roundtable discussions. His opinions and expertise are a great source of information for those involved in IT Audit, Security and Governance.

The Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) is a worldwide not-for-profit charitable organization focused on improving the security of software. Our mission is to make software security visible, so that individuals and organizations worldwide can make informed decisions about true software security risks.
