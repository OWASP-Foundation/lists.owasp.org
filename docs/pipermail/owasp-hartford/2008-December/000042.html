LAST CHANCE TO REGISTER FOR THE  ISSA-CT HOLIDAY LUNCHEON AND DECEMBER EVENT.  WE WILL BE FOCUSING ON A SECURE FOCUS FOR 2009 ON DECEMBER 16TH.

 

Registration closes tomorrow, Friday December 12, at Noon. Limited seats are still available today. No Walk ins will be accepted the day of the event.  

Our Holiday luncheon is filled with great speakers such as:    

Attorney General Richard Blumenthal from the State of Connecticut who will discuss Identity Theft;

 Officer Matt Brodasky will  present Case Studies, Security Breach Ramifications, and Technical Employees Liabilities; and

 Ashwini Ahuja, Vice President, Governance, Risk & Compliance, SDG Corporation (an MGL Group company )will  provide a presentation on Information Security Governance

At:  The Hartford Club 

       46 Prospect Street Hartford, CT 06103

       For Directions:  www.hartfordclub.com 

       Registration starts at 10:30, Event starts at 11:00.

 Register Now at:  http://www.issa-ct.org/    to get one of those limited seats.

Go to the Top-Right corner of the web page and click on:  Holiday Event Luncheon and follow the link.

You, your peers, colleagues, and Management can enjoy this special event and earn CEUs for participation. 

This cost for the event is $35 per member/$50 per non member.  The fee must be paid at time of registration.  Full refund available when handled through the online registration process before 12:00 noon on Friday, December 12th.  Sister organizations like ACP, OWASP, ISACA, etc. are considered members for purpose of registration.  Bring your organization membership card to the event registration desk.

The details of the event are included in the biographies found on the ISSA-CT web site http://www.issa-ct.org/ where you will see why ISSA-CT believes this is a holiday event you do not want to miss.
