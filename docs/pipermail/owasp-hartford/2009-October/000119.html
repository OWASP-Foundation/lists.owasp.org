Job Opportunity for Presales Systems Engineer Cisco






Rouleau Search Associates 

lori at rouleausearch.com



Cisco Solutions Engineer - CT



Our Client is a premier systems integrator and a leading provider of network, systems and telephony solutions. Our Client represents the solutions involving the products of vendors including Cisco, EMC, Sun, HP and Microsoft. The winner of numerous industry and vendors awards, we are proud to hold Cisco's master specializations in both unified communications and security.



Our Client is seeking a presales engineer to provide technical support to the sales team in Connecticut area regarding primarily Cisco-based solutions. The solutions engineer needs to have skills and experience in developing bills-of-material and scopes-of-work; delivering product presentations and demonstrations; developing proofs-of-concept; evaluating customer infrastructure readiness. The candidate must have extensive post sales experience in the systems integration business and must already be an experienced presales engineer with excellent presentation and writing skills.



Duties and responsibilities include: accompanying account managers at customer meetings, determining appropriate solution sets to business problems; delivery of solutions; contributing to sales team effort by delivering technical presentations and demonstrations; developing proof-of-concept efforts; accomplishing follow-up efforts for client delivery as needed; developing and implementing checklist to determine client site-readiness for recommended technology; assisting in system analysis; identifying sales requirements by establishing a personal rapport with technical influencers and decision makers; preparing reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing information; maintaining technical knowledge by attending Cisco and other educational workshops and reviewing Cisco publications.



The candidate engineer must have extensive Cisco Unified Communications product line knowledge and experience alongside high level skills in routing & switching and wireless (Cisco security and data center a plus).



Certification as a CCNP or CCVP is required; CCIE is preferred. Candidates must have strong written and verbal communication skills; be client focused; service driven and able to multitask.



 This is an opportunity to join one of the most prosperous value added solution providers in the country.
