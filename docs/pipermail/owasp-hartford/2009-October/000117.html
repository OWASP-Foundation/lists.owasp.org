RSA, The Security Division of EMC, is the premier provider of security
solutions for business acceleration, helping the world's leading
organizations succeed by solving their most complex and sensitive
security challenges. RSA's information-centric approach to security
guards the integrity and confidentiality of information throughout its
lifecycle - no matter where it moves, who accesses it or how it is used.

RSA offers industry-leading solutions in identity assurance & access
control, data loss prevention, encryption & key management, compliance &
security information management and fraud protection. These solutions
bring trust to millions of user identities, the transactions that they
perform, and the data that is generated. For more information, please
visit www.RSA.com <http://www.rsa.com/>  and www.EMC.com
<http://www.emc.com/> .

SUMMARY

Work as a key contributor to the next generation security and
information event management platform. Provide technical leadership in
developing solutions to complex business and software engineering
problems requiring advanced database technology. Consult on and carry
out R&D projects requiring data storage and/or business intelligence
expertise, directing the work of other engineering and technical
personnel. Interact regularly with product architecture team and senior
management to build consensus regarding project & technical direction.
Assignments may include new products as well as upgrades, and
enhancements or fixes to existing products.

 

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

*         Assume the role of in-house expert for design, implementation
and technical oversight of physical storage solutions for all
application data.

*         Owns physical data models, metadata

*         Conceive and propose enhancements and guide their
implementation to a final product or system according to industry
standards

*         Provides technical leadership in accomplishing project goals.
Assist in development of detailed project plans and provide inputs to
manager regarding individual employee performance against project goals.

*         Provide consulting level support when necessary to assist
continuing engineering team to troubleshoot and resolve database-related
issues.

*         Research, evaluate and report on new technologies for
potential adoption into the product, taking product strategy and
direction into account.

*         Analyze product performance and scalability, and provide
recommendations on data layer performance optimization opportunities and
enhancements.

*         Writes architecture, function, and design specifications.
Code and debug complex software, often involving novel techniques.
Prepares diagnostic programs for evaluation of complex software, where
special tool building or advances techniques are required.

*         Creates, understands and enforces database development
standards around coding, naming , database access, migration,
replication, security, storage, availability

REQUIRED SKILLS

*         8-10 years of hands-on industry experience working with
enterprise-class database systems/applications with at least 4 years
filling the role of database technical lead or consultant.

*         5-10 years experience with SQL, design of physical data
schemas, stored procedures, etc.

*         Experience in storing, indexing and searching unstructured
data. Knowledge of Data Warehousing (i.e. ETL, ROLAP) and analytics a
plus.

*         Experience with Oracle 10g, Sybase (SQL Anywhere)

*         Experience developing highly-available, reliable,
load-balanced & distributed applications. Experience with database
replication.

*         Experience modeling database growth to develop database
capacity metrics and recommendations.

 

EDUCATION

*	B.S. in Computer Science, M.S preferred.
