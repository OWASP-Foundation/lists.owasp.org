Sr .Net Information Architect position for Travelers Insurance -Hartford,

Also need:

SUMMARY:

The primary function of this position is to provide architectural guidance,
technological recommendations and transition strategies that support the
development and implementation of initiatives based on Common Delivery,
Services Based Architecture, and Agility for complex and/or multiple
projects.  Focus is on the development of an end to end information
architecture strategy, playing a lead role on projects by providing the
appropriate data architecture and ensuring alignment with the overall data
management strategies. 

 

PRIMARY DUTIES:

-Provide strategic thinking, solutions and roadmaps while driving
architectural recommendations

-Adopt and communicate new concepts, ideas, techniques, best practices and
technology assistance

-Serve as an expert resource on matters of data architecture to business and
IT staff in all phases of data warehouse and data mart development,
implementation and support.

-Provide guidance to development application areas by applying knowledge of
data warehouse design, extract-transform-load (ETL) design and development.

-Create/maintain the data architecture to increase the robustness,
performance, and scalability of systems.

-Establish an architecture roadmap covering transactional, operational and
business intelligence.

-Contribute substantially to the software architecture, and provide
direction for the design and development of the data layer.

-Responsible for the development of logical data models and physical data
base designs applied across multiple computing environments (e.g., host
based, client server, distributed, and warehouse environments).

-Perform product evaluations, and Proof of Concepts.

-Research and document information architecture standards and best
practices.

-Work with various customers and prospects, understand and translate
business use cases and/or requirements into data models, analytics reports
and scenario models.

-Ensure that data dictionaries are maintained holistically by partnering
with Data Management.

-Participate in data definition and data management reviews.

-Provide leadership, guidance and mentoring.

 

WORK EXPERIENCE:

-Extensive experience in creating and maintaining end to end data
architectures.

-Extensive experience in logical, access, and physical data modeling.

-Extensive hands-on technical experience using data warehouse, business
intelligence and analytics products.

-Extensive experience with normalized and dimensional data modeling.

-Experience in data analysis and mapping.

-Expertise in the creation and usage of metadata.

-Working knowledge of modeling tools (e.g., Computer Associates (CA)
AllFusion: ERWIN Model Manager, IBM Rational Suite, Rational (RSA & RDA).

-Excellent communication and facilitation skills.

-Team oriented with excellent collaboration skills.

-Attention to detail, and a commitment to quality work.

-Exhibits leadership qualities

-Minimum of 3 years experience in people, product and/or project management.

-Experience working in a matrixed organization.

-Experience in Property Casualty insurance, especially Claims a plus.

 

OTHER:

Technical Knowledge

Possess a solid working knowledge of capabilities and direction of
technology, coupled with an in-depth knowledge of the technology required
and the needs of business environment necessary to support assigned
projects. Develop solutions to meet business needs that reflect a clear
understanding of the objectives, practices, and procedures of the
corporation, department and business unit.

 

Problem Solving & Decision Making

Demonstrate sound analytical and diagnostic skills dealing with issues that
are not readily defined and/or conflict with available information. Knows
where to obtain information needed to make the appropriate decisions.
Ability to break a problem down to manageable pieces and implement
effective, timely solutions.   Strong ability to identify the problem versus
the symptom.  Deal increasingly with problems that require involvement of
others to solve.  Has the ability to reach sound decisions quickly.
Carefully evaluates alternative risks and solutions before taking action.
Optimize the use of all available resources. 

 

Team Orientation

Maintains partnerships across the organization, and is able to influence
senior management, peers and staff through an inclusive style and
recognition of their abilities to achieve results.  The success of the team
is above own interest.

 

Self-Management

Manages functional objectives and priorities supporting multiple assigned
business units. Responsibilities are assigned with latitude for setting
priorities and decision-making using generally accepted guidelines. Results
are reviewed with next level manager for clarification according to
predefined objectives.

 

Commitment to Quality Result

Creates an atmosphere where business partner's issues are dealt with
professionally, and in a timely manner.  Suggests solutions that make sense
and are in line with future technology direction.  Communicates openly and
effectively in a manner consistent with the audience. Demonstrates an
adaptive style that is flexible and effective in gaining cooperation of
others.  Anticipates and responds to management requests for information
with timely, accurate findings.

 

Planning and Project Management

Develops operational plans and provides resource estimation for task
planning.  Proposes plans of action that are timely, realistic and positive.
Sets appropriate goals for projects, and monitors progress against the plan.
