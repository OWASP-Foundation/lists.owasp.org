Hello -
The ISC2 CT chapter, in partnership with CT ISACA has agreed to sponsor two one day programs October 23&24, 2014.  Oct 23rd will be focused on Business and Oct 24th will be a focus on technical security. The link <https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1nHKLhYzbI8IC6xJvExPd6HAU7lzEaoA73Ch6je5wxQ8/viewform?usp=send_form> will provide you with the available details. October as you know is cybersecurity awareness month.

We would like to invite you to submit your papers on the topic of your interest as a session speaker or a panelist of these programs. Your reputation would add luster to our program and help us to attract a large audience.   Your knowledge and your ability would help to ensure that the program will deliver value to the participants.

If you would be interested in being a session speaker or a panelist on any of the topics listed below, please submit your choice of topic by clicking on the form link on the website page.

[Track 1] Emerging News & Trends
[Track 2] Forensics
[Track 3] Risk Management
[Track 4] Application Security
[Track 5] Legal, Regulations & Standards

The information provided by you will be reviewed by the program committee for a final selection.
We are looking forward to hearing back from you.

Thank you,
ISC2 Program Committee
