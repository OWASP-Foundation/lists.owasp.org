For those attending today's event:

*Where*

Fairfield University DiMenna-Nyselius Library
5171, 1073 N Benson Rd Multimedia Auditorium – 101 · Fairfield, ct 06824
<https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=41.158764%2C-73.257360>

The location is the Library Multimedia Auditorium – 101 (Box 10 on the
attached map). Attendees can reach the auditorium by entering the library
from the main entrance and taking the stairs to the ground floor, left at
the stairs will take you to the auditorium.

Parking: People can park in the parking garage which is on the right as you
pull into campus from the main entrance

Day of Point of contact: William Reyor – wreyor at fairfield.edu
