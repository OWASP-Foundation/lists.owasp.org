Identity & Access Management User Group


George,

I’m excited to announce the first meeting of the Hartford/Springfield IAM User Group on June 14 at 3:00 PM at Fleming's Prime Steakhouse & Wine Bar<http://www2.radiantlogic.com/e/31062/maps-7FRUY9nWgXH2/818mn3/282654990>.

This is a free meeting of local IAM Professionals with drinks and appetizers provided. Networking, best practices and an open exchange of ideas between IAM professionals is the only agenda. Spots are limited and priority will be given to folks working directly in the IAM space. Come prepared to discuss ideas and find out best practices from some of the leaders in the IAM community.

Speaker at this user group:

  *   Eric Anderson, Information Security Architect at Health Care Service Corporation - Empowering Decisions. Now. Extensible Data...On Demand
The agenda, details and registration for the meeting are at meetup:
www.meetup.com/Hartford-SpringfieldIAM/events/231014245/<http://www2.radiantlogic.com/e/31062/ringfieldIAM-events-231014245-/818mmy/282654990>
*If you do not already have a Meetup.com profile, you’ll be asked to create one to complete registration for the event*

This event is free to the Hartford/Springfield IAM community. Join us<http://www2.radiantlogic.com/e/31062/Hartford-SpringfieldIAM-/818mn5/282654990> at this meeting to start networking and learning. And please feel free to pass this along to anyone interested in IAM.

I hope to see you there!
