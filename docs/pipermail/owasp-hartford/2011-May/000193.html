Greetings,
               I wanted to extend an invitation to the OWASP Hartford
chapter to BsidesCT happening June 11th. For those not aware, security
B-sides are infosec events free to the attendee that consist of high quality
workshops and presentations. We currently have a line up of about six
presenters that are covering topics including Pentesting, mobile malware,
and secure communication using ZRTP. However, we're currently seeking more
high quality presentations and have an open CFP page at
http://www.nesit.net/bsidesct-call-for-papers/ .

Also for organizations looking to increase awareness about about there
organization, service, or product we still have many opportunities for
sponsorship, for more information regarding sponsorship call or contact me
directly.

For information regarding Bsides, or BsidesCT please see
http://www.nesit.net/bsides-excon/  <http://www.nesit.net/bsides-excon/>and
http://www.securitybsides.com/
