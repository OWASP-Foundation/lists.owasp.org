The San Diego chapter of OWASP recently completed their Hacking Dojo:
http://jeromiejackson.com/index.php/the-news/76-Sush-and-Sake-Hacker-Doj
o-OWASP-San-Diego where they brought in several Web Application
Firewalls (WAF) along with two instances of WebGoat. One instance of
WebGoat was behind the WAF which was in blocking mode and the other was
hanging off the AP. They allowed people to bring their laptops and learn
to break in realtime. 

So far, our chapter has been presentation-oriented. I would like to do
something similar for the second-half of the year if there is extreme
interest from the community...
