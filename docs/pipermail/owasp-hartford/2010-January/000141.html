Speakers include:

Mr. Hank Voight

CEO/Chief Technology Officer

Weston Software Group

Hank founded Weston Software, Inc. in 2007.  Prior to that, he worked at
Microsoft Corp. as a Security Architect for 10 years, during which he
led and managed multi-million dollar engagements based on the
integration and management of identities into Microsoft Active
Directory.  

The most public example of his work at Microsoft was the infrastructure
design, implementation and operation of MSNBC cable television station
and MSNBC internet-based properties.  He is recognized by Microsoft as a
security and identity and access management expert, and also as a
worldwide authority on integrating computers that run the Microsoft
Windows Operating system with systems and platforms offered by other
companies.  

Hank is acknowledged in the books ?Writing Secure Code? and the ?Windows
Security Resource Guide?, both published by Microsoft Press.  Weston
Software, Inc. is the second technology business that Hank has founded.
His first business, VEI Software, which developed accounting systems for
the first personal computers, was sold in 1989.

Mr. Bill Zack

Architect Evangelist

Microsoft Developer & Platform Evangelism

Bill Zack is an Architect Evangelist with Microsoft. He comes to this
role after serving as a Solutions Architect in the Financial Services
Unit of Microsoft Consulting Services.  His experience includes
developing, supporting and evangelizing .NET/SOA based frameworks used
to jump-start development projects for financial services companies.  

Prior to joining Microsoft he acted as a Consultant, Architect,
Administrator, Developer, and System Integrator. He has also authored
several computer books and white papers.  

He is the Founder and President of the New York chapter of the
International Association of Software Architects (IASA) and a member of
the IASA International Board of Directors He is also Co-Moderator of the
New York City .NET Developers Group, co-founder of the Windows Azure
User Group, founder and past president of the New York Enterprise
Windows User Group, and the founder and past president of several other
computer user groups.

He has been working with the Windows Azure Platform since before the
first public Community Technical Preview in 2008. He spends most of his
time these days working with customers who are leveraging Microsoft
cloud services in their applications.

Mr. Michael Ferris

Director Product Strategies for Cloud Computing

Red Hat Inc.

Michael Ferris is the Director of Product Strategy for Red Hat's Cloud
Computing initiatives where he is responsible for identifying and
designing emerging enterprise systems management and cloud offerings. 

Michael has more than 15 years of experience in enterprise software
development and management. He led product marketing and management
teams at Red Hat during the definition and introduction of the Red Hat
Enterprise Linux flagship product line. Most recently, Michael guided
the creation of Red Hat's enterprise security platform and cloud
computing offerings.

Prior to joining Red Hat in 2000, Michael led design, product
management, and development at both startup and enterprise software
firms including Relativity Technologies, Micro Focus, and Burl Software.


Michael holds a master's degree in Computer Science from Rensselaer
Polytechnic Institute and bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and
Mathematics from Vanderbilt University.
