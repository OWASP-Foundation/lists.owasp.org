FYI - there is a Hartford/Springfield IAM User Group meeting coming up. This is not an OWASP sponsored event, but I thought it would be of interest to those on our distribution that focus on Identity and Access Management.

Below is the description from the Meetup site. A link is provided to RSVP.

Your OWASP Hartford CT Leads:
James McGovern<mailto:James.mcgovern at owasp.org> & Alvin Fong<mailto:alvin.fong at owasp.org>

Hartford/Springfield IAM User Group

Thursday, March 30 at 3:00-6PM



We are pleased to announce the next Hartford IAM User Group Meetup! Please join us for food, drinks and technical content. Networking, best practices and an open exchange of ideas between IAM professionals is the only agenda.

https://www.meetup.com/Hartford-SpringfieldIAM/events/236143545/



Come prepared to discuss ideas and find out best practices from some of the leaders in the IAM community. Most importantly, Please recommend this event to your IAM friends and colleagues!

After introductory networking, the agenda for the meeting will include speakers:

Jim Cupps, IAM Architect at Liberty Mutual - Jim has over 15 years of experience directing and managing IT organizations with a combination of deep technical experience, coordination with executive management, and operational managerial experience.

Learn more from Jim about 'Risk in the Cloud'

Pranjal Naik, Manager, Cybersecurity Advisory Services of E&Y -Pranjal specializes in IAM and has assisted clients execute large-scale strategy, implementation and multi-year transformation programs.  She has a MS degree in Information Management and holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

Learn how E&Y is working with their customers to solve identity management issues, simplify sign on and drive a consistent user experience.
