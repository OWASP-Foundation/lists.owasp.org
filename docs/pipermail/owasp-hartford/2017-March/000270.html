Interested in *Hosting an OWASP event?!* -> We're looking to add additional
locations to host our 2017 programming. If you or your company would be
interested in hosting a chapter meeting, please contact me at
alvin.fong at owasp.org

*OWASP* Hartford *Marketing volunteer* -> We're looking for someone to help
us get the word out on OWASP Hartford events. No security experience
needed. If you or someone you know would be interested in helping us with
events, please reach out to me at alvin.fong at owasp.org

*New Initiatives **needing YOU!:*

·         *Penetration testing Lab* -> We're looking to build out a lab to
help our members enhance their hands-on application and network hacking
skills. If this is an effort you are interested in participating in, let us
know. No previous pen-testing experience needed. E-mail alvin.fong at owasp.org


·         *Academic Outreach* -> We're looking to host a session at UCONN
to engage with students that are interested in getting into Information
Security. If you are interested in presenting, or being a panelist, please
email alvin.fong at owasp.org

About the OWASP Foundation: The Open Web Application Security Project
(OWASP) <https://www.owasp.org/> is a 501(c)(3) worldwide not-for-profit
charitable organization focused on improving the security of software. Our
mission is to make software security visible, so that individuals and
organizations worldwide can make informed decisions about true software
security risks. Everyone is free to participate in OWASP and all of our
materials are available under a free and open software license. You'll find
everything about OWASP linked from our wiki and current information on our
OWASP Blog. OWASP does not endorse or recommend any product or service This
allows our community to remain vendor neutral with the collective wisdom of
the best minds in software security worldwide.
