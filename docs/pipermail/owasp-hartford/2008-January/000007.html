Figured I would give you a peak into our upcoming speakers who have
confirmed. In April, we will have Anton Chuvakin, Product Manager at
LogLogic and Jack Danahy, CTO of Ounce Labs. In September, we will have
Steven M. Christey of Mitre and Jeff Margolies of Accenture. For our
November event, we will have Jim Routh, CISO of the Depository Trust
Clearing Corporation (DTCC) and Nick Selby, Industry Analyst at the 451
Group.

We are also working on an all day event (TBD) to coincide with OWASP day
where we will have Grady Summers, CISO of GE as the keynote along with
speakers from Microsoft, BEA, EMC and the Hartford. We will be seeking
additional sponsorship for this event. We hope that area corporations
will be willing to send 10 to 20 IT employees each to this event. We are
even noodling making part of the event, more BarCamp style. For those
who are unfamiliar with BarCamps, see:
http://www.barcamp.org/TheRulesOfBarCamp

Help us make the Hartford CT chapter the best attended event on the
planet...

Cheers
