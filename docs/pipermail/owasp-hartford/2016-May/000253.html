Hello OWASP:

Thanks to everyone for taking the time out of their day to participate at our cyber security panel event. We had an excellent turnout both in person and remotely covering everything from Identity and Access Management, DevOps to PKI, API security, and security tools. Great information sharing and strategies for Cybersecurity challenges across industry. I did want to share some of the resources our panelists mentioned.

Security and technology training:
*       owasp.org - open security checklists and testing guides
*       Goodsecurityquestions.com - password reset and sign-in verification
*       Udemy - online technology courses
*       Udacity.com - online technology/programming training
*       Coursera.org - online university courses

Security technology:
*       RASP - Runtime Application Self-Protection: https://www.contrastsecurity.com/rasp

Hope to see everyone at the next OWASP session.
