The Los Angeles OWASP chapter leader would like you to know that his chapter is holding a two day event on the beach (literally) on January 27th and 28th. It will be held at the Annenberg Community Beach House: See: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Loci904axEQ

Only $50 for OWASP members (https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Individual_Member)  to attend. They have lined up noted speakers such as Jeremiah Grossman, Gene Kim and Jack Mannino.
