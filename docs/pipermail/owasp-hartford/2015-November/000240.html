I wanted to introduce myself as the new co-lead for the Hartford Chapter of OWASP. We have an upcoming meet-up this Friday on Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC). Mark Coderre, CISO of OpenSky, will be joining us to talk about GRC, risk methodologies, and information sharing.

Date/Time: Friday, November 13, 2015 – 11:30am to 1pm
Location: Travelers Central Row Meeting Room #2
GPS Address: 45 Central Row, Hartford, CT, 06103 (entrance is between Dunkin Donuts and CVS Pharmacy)

About Mark Coderre:
Mark Coderre is an Information Security Executive with over 25 years of experience protecting information in Healthcare and Insurance. He is now a National Practice Director with OpenSky Corporation focused on CISO Advisory services, Internet Identity, Healthcare, GRC and Risk Management. During his career with Aetna in Hartford, Connecticut, Mark served as Executive Director of Security Strategy and Risk Management. Mark has experience building teams and relationships across IT functions including business analysts, architecture, planning, reporting, program management, portfolio management, research & development, infrastructure, development and business continuity.
