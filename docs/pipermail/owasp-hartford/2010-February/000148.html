For our last meeting in 2009, I commited to making our chapter even more
open than before. One of the things that I wanted to do in 2010 was to
give members of the chapter a say in future speakers. I have enabled our
chapter homepage (http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Hartford) with a
feature that will allow interested parties to propose speaking topics.
Likewise, I will also require a few participants to help govern
submissions.
