Have you ever had a security question that you needed answered quickly
but couldn't find your local neighborhood security architect to provide
a perspective? There is now an online forum for solving this challenge.
Many of the members of the OWASP community at large worldwide will be
available to answer your questions. You may notice that this particular
site eschews fugly registration and avoids the whole debacle around
using IDs and passwords. 

 

Even if you don't have a question yourself, but want to make yourself
smarter regarding what others are asking, it is worthy of checking out.
Please visit: http://security.stackexchange.com/
