Here is the google translate version of an announcement by the 
"Franco-German initiative on internal security in Europe".

https://translate.google.co.za/translate?sl=auto&tl=en&js=y&prev=_t&hl=en&ie=UTF-8&u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.interieur.gouv.fr%2FActualites%2FL-actu-du-Ministere%2FInitiative-franco-allemande-sur-la-securite-interieure-en-Europe&edit-text=

The crux is the second-last paragraph starting with "They propose" and 
specifically

    At European level, it would tax the uncooperative operators to
    remove illegal content or decrypt messages as part of
    investigations.

or in normal English, the French are proposing that the EU Commission 
require telecom operators to have a key to decrypt messages and hand 
over that key on demand.
