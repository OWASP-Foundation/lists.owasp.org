The CyberCrime and CyberSecurity Bill was based on a Policy Framework
already approved by cabinet in 2012. It has only recently been
de-classified for the public to review: National Cybersecurity Policy
Framework: http://www.gov.za/sites/www.gov.za/files/39475_gon609.pdf.
Also for those who wish not to go to a .gov.za server:
https://www.dropbox.com/s/o4d0nh2fn2sfbfr/39475_gon609.pdf?dl=0.

Ironically this has been done 4 days after comments on the CyberCrimes
Bill closed. This should give some indication to everyone on how the SSA
is handling this matter. Clearly want to fas-track and rubber stamp this
bill.
