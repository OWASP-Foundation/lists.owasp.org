Hi Everyone

The next OWASP CPT meeting will be this Wednesday and Liam Smit will be
covering "Incident Response Primer". Here follows a description of the talk:
> Hi
>
> The fifth official meeting of the Cape Town chapter of OWASP (Open Web
> Application Security Project) is being held at 7pm on Wednesday the
> 14th of October at VOSS.
>
> The meeting is titled: 
> Incident Response Primer
>
> About the talk:
>
> Incident response entails planning and putting in place structures to
> detect and respond to incidents. This talk will cover the basics of
> incident response. E.g. incident response plans, incident response
> teams, etc.
>
> Questions will be posed [ and perhaps even answered ;-) ]. E.g. Why
> these things can be useful, goals, considerations. 
>
>
> Space is limited so sign up:
> http://www.meetup.com/OWASP-Cape-Town-Chapter-Meetup/events/224921164/
>
> There is also informal chatting after the main talk.

Thanks
