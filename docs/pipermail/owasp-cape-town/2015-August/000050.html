> Hi Everyone
>
> We are excited to announce our next OWASP Cape Town event with a guest
> speaker Dr Evan Summers.
>
> Here is a summary of the next talk:
>
> "The PCI DSS
> <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Payment_Card_Industry_Data_Security_Standard> (Data
> Security Standard) provides information security objectives for the payment
> card industry. Its recommendations are common sense, e.g. use a firewall to
> secure your network, regularly scan your network for vulnerabilities, and
> monitor your network for intrusions.
>
> We will consider and discuss PCI DSS as a useful security standard for
> everyone (i.e. beyond credit card processing companies) to improve the
> security of our IT applications, systems and networks.
>
> About The Speaker:
>
> Dr Evan Summers (@evanxsummers) is a Principal Consultant at Saratoga.
> Currently he works with the IOL digital media team at World Wide Creative,
> using Node.js, ReactJS and Redis to build websites for Independent Media.
> Last year this team produced a “new” IOL mobi site
> <http://mobi.iol.co.za/#%21/section/top>, and currently they are building
> a new responsive site for IOL."
>
