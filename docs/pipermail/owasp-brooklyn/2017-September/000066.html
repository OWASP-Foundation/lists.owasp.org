*National Cyber Security Awareness Month Global Launch Events*

National Cyber Security Awareness Month (NCSAM) is just a few days away!

For the first time, the Department of Homeland Security is coordinating
with the Organization of American States and the National Cyber Security
Alliance to present a *NCSAM Global Launch Event.*

Please join us on Tuesday, October 3 in Washington, DC to hear
cybersecurity experts from governments and industry across the globe
discuss international coordination around cybersecurity.

   - *Date:* Tuesday, October 3, 2017
   - *Time:* 11:00 a.m.–7:00 p.m. ET
   - *Location:* Organization of American States, Hall of the Americas, 200
   17th Street NW, Washington, DC
   <https://maps.google.com/?q=the+Americas,+200+17th+Street+NW,+Washington,+DC&entry=gmail&source=g>

Visit the event website
<https://www.eventbrite.com/e/global-launch-of-cyber-security-awareness-month-registration-36905655810>
for the full agenda and registration.  Space is limited and an RSVP is
required, so please be sure to register if you plan to attend in person.

Can’t make it to DC? Follow along virtually!  The event will be live
streamed <https://livestream.com/oas2/live> starting at 12:00 p.m. ET.
Along with the live stream (12–6 p.m. ET), see below for a full social
media schedule for the day.

*NCSAM Global Launch Social Media Activities*

   - *9:30*–*10:15 a.m. ET: Twitter Q&As with Global Launch speakers.  *Use
   #AskCyberAware to submit questions to our Global Launch speakers or follow
   along with the Q&A.

   - *11:00*–*11:30 a.m. ET: Live, 30-minute #ChatSTC Twitter Chat.  *Follow
   #ChatSTC to view the chat, and include #ChatSTC and #CyberAware in your
   Tweets to be part of the conversation.

   - *1:00*–*5:30 p.m. ET: Live Periscope and Facebook Live mini
   interviews.  *View live interviews from U.S. and international industry
   and government representatives on facebook.com/staysafeonline
   <http://www.facebook.com/staysafeonline> and periscope.tv/staysafeonline
   <http://www.periscope.tv/staysafeonline>.
