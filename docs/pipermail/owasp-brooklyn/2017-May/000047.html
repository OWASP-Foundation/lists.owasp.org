*Pre- and post-conference events at no additional charge.*

*STIX 2.0 Workshop for ISACers*
FS-ISAC, NH-ISAC, and OASIS will host an informal STIX 2.0 workshop on Monday,
20 June. This will be your opportunity to speak directly with committee
members about the pain points STIX 2.0 addresses and provide input on the
specifications.

*Cybersecurity Standards User Council Forum*
Alexander Foley, VP at Bank of America, will chair an open forum on Tuesday,
20 June afternoon for executives in the financial services, healthcare,
manufacturing, government, and technology sectors. The forum is an
opportunity for the non-vendor community to collaborate on business
requirements, mobilize support for vertical specializations, and promote
adoption of common best practices relating to cybersecurity standards. Open
to all.

*OASIS Member Meeting and Reception*
Connect with OASIS board, members, and staff on Thursday evening (22 Jun)
for a special networking event. This is your chance to learn about OASIS’
plans for the future, honor member accomplishments, get technical work
updates, and provide feedback.
