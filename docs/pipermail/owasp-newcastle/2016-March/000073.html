This sounds like Campus North would be an ideal venue. They are used (for free) by NE-Bytes and the SharePoint User Group(NE) and its a good City centre location walk-able from all main transport hubs, and with lots of parking nearby.

"Hire Campus North - We ensure the region's not-for-profit tech meetups receive free space for their events and support community events such as hack weekends."
