Hello all,

Maturity models are a good way of benchmarking your secure development
practises against other people. BSIMM <https://www.bsimm.com/>is a very
popular model and of course, OWASP has its flagship OpenSAMM
<https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_SAMM_Project> project.

If you are assessing yourself against BSIMM or OpenSAMM and need a tool to
track progress against multiple teams, you could give this new project a
test drive:

https://github.com/owasp/Maturity-Models

The team would welcome any feedback you might have!
