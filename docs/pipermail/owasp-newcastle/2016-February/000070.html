Hello all,

I'd like your opinion on an email that came by the OWASP leaders mailing
list - see below. As a chapter, we have funds (not a huge amount, but we
are doing OK). We could use some of them to help this student chapter in
Ukraine, but I wanted to see what the views of our community are, since the
funds are for your benefit really.

If you have a view, one way or the other, let me know by replying either to

events.*
