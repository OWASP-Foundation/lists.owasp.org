Cyber Security Information Sharing Partnership (CiSP) is a Government sharing portal. Through which Cyber Security companies and Government Agencies can share cyber threat information and advice : https://www.cert.gov.uk/cisp/

Dave Lloyd (signacure.co.uk) the Regional CiSP Coordinator, has worked together with Durham Police. To create possibility for small and startup business to form a group on the CiSP Portal.So that may work together and share information with each other. As well as receive cyber threat advice from Government Agencies.

In order to start the network, there will be a free Event at the Montgomery Suite, Stadium of Light on the 10th of December from 8am to 5pm. Why not pop in and get registered for CiSP ?
