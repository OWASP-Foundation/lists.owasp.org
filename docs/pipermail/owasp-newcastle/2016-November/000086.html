Hi All,

I’m a volunteer and track leader for the free world-wide virtual conference
event called All Day DevOps, http://www.alldaydevops.com/.  There is a
round 50 sessions or so and around 8000 registered attendees from around
the globe so far.  I’m looking for some assistance to help me organize the
track sessions on the day of the event.  Collect questions from the
audience and present them to the speaker.  Keep speakers on schedule.
Communicate concerns to track leaders if there are any technology
problems.  Track moderators can work from anywhere there is an Internet
connection and it’s quiet.  The event is all day but I’m looking to keep
volunteer moderators commitment to a 4hr time block.  It’s my desire to
keep volunteer moderators experience light and positive.  Moderators don’t
need to be located in USA and there are local friendly times you can help.

If you or someone in your chapter has never spoke at a conference or helped
to organize a conference this could be an interesting opportunity for the
resume.  Send me a note if your interested.

Regards,
Milton Smith

*This is not an OWASP event but it’s a free non-marketing type event with a
full security track.
