Hi OWASP Members,

I am working to bring together a group of volunteers to participate in
developing and presenting material related to online safety for children and
parents.  This effort is going to be coordinated with various organizations
in Canada, and will be focused initially on the Greater Vancouver Regional
District and Victoria, with an expanding focus based on success.

If you are interested in participating, or would like to be kept updated as
we move forward, please send me an email.  Note that there will not be an
official mailing list for this until the project moves forward, and anyone
interacting with the organization may be expected to go through some degree
of background checks to ensure compliance with specific legal requirements.

If you are interested in participating in this effort, or you would like
additional information, please reply to me with your name, the city you live
in, and a number you can be reached at.

Regards,
Yvan Boily
