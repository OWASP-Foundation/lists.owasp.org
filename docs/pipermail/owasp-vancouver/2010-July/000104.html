*OWASP Chapter Meeting Announcement*

I am pleased to announce our second meeting of 2010!  As previously
announced on the mailing list, we will have the meeting on July 22nd, and
Martin Kyle of Sierra Systems has offered to host the meeting.
*
Date & Time: *Thursday, July 22, 2010 @ 5:30pm

*Location: *
Sierra Systems
1177 West Hastings Street, Suite 2500
Vancouver, BC V6E 2K3

*First Topic :* Risk Assessment Methodologies
*Second Topic :*  Cloud Technologies and Risks

The meeting will feature two presentations, with a 15 minute break between
them.  Following the second presentation, we can open the meeting up for
discussion of how to grow the chapter, and future areas of interest.

*Risk Assessments (Erasmus Dong) *- a high level presentation on risk
management and risk assessment methodologies with focus on OWASP Threat Risk
Modelling.

*Cloud Technologies and Risks (Yvan Boily)* - A brief overview of Cloud
technologies, and a discussion of some of the key areas of risk when
considering Cloud technologies.

*About Erasmus Dong*
A new member of the Vancouver OWASP community, Erasmus has been working in
information technology for over a decade in areas of operations,
infrastructure, and information security risk in government and financial
services.

*About Yvan Boily*
Yvan Boily works for a global Financial Institution and was the former
chapter lead for the Winnipeg chapter of OWASP.  He has worked in various
roles in IT Security, primarily focused on Application Security and Security
Testing.  Working for the last 8 years in IT Security, Yvan  has held roles
in provincial governemnt, independant contracting, and as a consultant for a
dedicated IT security firm.
