Security Consultant

development methodologies (e.g., Waterfall, RUP and Agile)

o	Ability to interface with clients, utilizing consulting and negotiating
skills

and delivery timelines, and to move swiftly from concepts and theory to
action

o	Ability to define and lead a project, and execute tasks consistently
o	Ability to collaborate with, mentor and guide project team members
o	Ability to provide leadership as an SME (Subject Matter Expert) in one
or more domains
•	Communication Skills
o	Ability to articulate thoughts, deliver presentations and training,
written and verbal
•	Demeanor
o	Creativity, drive, enthusiasm and commitment to professional services
Education and Experience

experience
•	5+ years enterprise software architecture, security and development
lifecycle experience

technologies
