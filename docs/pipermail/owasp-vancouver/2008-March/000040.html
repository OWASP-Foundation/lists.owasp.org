Hello everyone,

I had gotten some questions as to whether or not attending an OWASP local
chapter meeting, or conference, counted towards CPE credits for individuals
that have CISSP certification.  I called ISC2, and found out that both the
chapter meetings and conferences can be used to obtain credits.

I would like to ask the chapter leaders to mention this at your next chapter
meeting.  For those individuals that would like to take advantage of this,
they just need to get a certificate signed, and then enter the hours through
the ISC2 website.  I have downloaded the certificate to the website at:

https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Image:%28ISC%292_CPE_certification_form.doc

I would suggest that chapter leaders print out a few of these before each
chapter meeting.

Thanks!
