Ping Identity:

788 Beatty Street

Suite 200

Vancouver, BC  V6G 2M1

Getting here:

We are located at Beatty/Robson beside BC Place; two blocks South of the
Stadium skytrain station.

Parking/Arrival details:

There is meter parking on the street in front of our office or lot parking
at Library Square.  When you arrive, please take the elevator to the 2nd
floor and you will see our suite on the right hand side.  Buzz the intercom
and one of us will greet you once you are buzzed into our suite.
