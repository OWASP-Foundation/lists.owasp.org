All:

Just wanted to give everyone a heads-up that the OWASP AppSec 2006
conference will be held on October 17th and 18th in Seattle (with a
training day on the 16th).  More information can be found here:
<http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSec_Seattle_2006>

Denim Group folks will be giving two of the presentations (!)  I will be
presenting on sprajax and how it can be used to test AJAX security, and
Keith Landrus (Denim Group's Director of Technology) will be presenting
on integrating security into Agile development methodologies.  See the
agenda here:
<http://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_AppSec_Seattle_2006/Agenda>

The conference are $450 with discounts for students and paid OWASP
members.  That is  _very_ reasonable for a conference such as this.
Hopefully the San Antonio chapter will be well represented.

Thanks,

Dan
