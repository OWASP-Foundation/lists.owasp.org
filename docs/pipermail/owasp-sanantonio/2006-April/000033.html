All:

Folks in San Antonio who are interested in web application security
might want to check out the Texas Regional Infrastructure Security
Conference (TRISC) (http://www.trisc.org/)  I will be giving a 3-hour
workshop on database security on Wednesday May 17th and John Dickson
will be presenting on the organizational challenges of creating secure
software on Tuesday May 16th.

This is a 3-day conference (May 15th - May 17th) in Houston, TX and the
cost is pretty reasonable (~$500).  Given the low cost, the speaker list
for this conference is fantastic - independent of John and I presenting
:)  Caleb Sima (founder of SPI Dynamics) is speaking about web
application hacks and Ira Winkler (ex-NSA) is giving a talk titled
"Everything You Wanted to Know About Taking Down the Infrastructure, But
Were Afraid To Ask"  I have seen Ira speak before and he is very
entertaining and informative.

Thanks,
