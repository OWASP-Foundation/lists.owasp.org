All:

The next OWASP San Antonio meeting will be held tomorrow from 11:30
until 1:00 at the San Antonio Technology Center. Full details are below.

This month we are fortunate to have VoIP security expert Mark Collier
from SecureLogix talking to the group about the SIP protocol used in
VoIP communications and how it is similar to the HTTP protocol for web
applications. He will also be giving an overview of VoIP security in
general.

As always the meeting is free. Feel free to bring a lunch, and cookies
and Cokes will be provided. Hope to see everyone there!

Thanks,

Dan

San Antonio OWASP Chapter: February 2008 Meeting
Topic: The SIP Protocol and VoIP Security
Presenter: Dan Cornell
Date: February 27th, 11:30am - 1:00pm
Location:
San Antonio Technology Center (Web Room)
3463 Magic Drive

Abstract:
Mark Collier will be discussing the SIP protocol used in VoIP
communications and how it is similar to the HTTP protocol used in web
applications. He will also be giving an overview of VoIP security in
general.

Bio:
Mark Collier leads hardware and software product engineering,
development, test and manufacturing. Previously, Mr. Collier was with
the Southwest Research Institute for 14 years, where he contributed to
and managed a software research and development projects in a wide
variety of fields, including information warfare. Mr. Collier holds a
Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from St. Mary's
University.
