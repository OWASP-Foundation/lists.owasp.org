Austin OWASPers,

While there is still another week or so left for CFP submissions, the LASCON planning committee today voted on acceptance of the first batch of speakers and presentations for the conference.  Hopefully this preliminary list will get you guys as excited as I am about LASCON:
Presentation

Name

OWASP Top 10.  What is it all about?

Dean Bushmiller

Deconstructing ColdFusion

Chris Eng

HTTPS Can Byte Me

Robert Hasen and Josh Sokol

Dan Cornell

How I Met Your Girlfriend

Joe Jarzombek

Automating Web Testing Beyond OWASP Webscarab Using Python

Brad Causey

Michael Howard



That's two presentations from this years BlackHat conference, several published authors, and the Director for Software Assurance in the National Cyber Security Division of the Department of Homeland Security just to name a few, and that's only the preliminary round of acceptances.  The complete list and schedule won't be out for a couple of weeks, but we're seeing a steady stream of registrations and passes may not last that long.  At only $50 for OWASP members and $100 for non-members (includes a year of OWASP membership) for a full day of training, this should be a no-brainer.  Register for LASCON today and be sure to tell your friends and co-workers to get their passes before they sell out!

Conference Website: http://www.lascon.org<http://www.lascon.org/>

We'd also like to thank Gemalto, WhiteHat Security, Splunk, and Impact Security LLC for being Gold Sponsors of LASCON 2010!
