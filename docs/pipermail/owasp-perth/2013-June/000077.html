Hey All,

Firstly I'm sorry I wasn't able to attend any of the recent Jim Manico
sessions that were held last month, from all accounts it sounds like they
were excellent!

Also thanks again to Bankwest for helping with hosting the events.

For those who haven't yet joined OWASP, and perhaps you've been uhmming and
ahhing, they've just announced their (American) Summer Membership Drive.
And for the first time they're now offering new special lifetime
memberships.

Regardless, if you become a paid member in the first 10 days of June you go
in the running for a bunch of awesome stuff, including full conference
passes to BH Vegas or OWASP's AppSec USA.

You can read more about it here:
http://owasp.com/index.php/Summer_2013_Membership_Drive

If you have any other questions, don't forget to let us know on this list.
