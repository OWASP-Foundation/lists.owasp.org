The ninth AusCERT conference will take place from Sunday 16th to Wednesday
19th May 2010 followed by two days of workshops. The venue will be RACV
Royal Pines on the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia.

Benjamin Mosse and Paul Therialt are both presenting at AusCERT2010 and both
have previously presented at the OWASP Australian Conferences which AusCERT
supported in the past.  For more program details please visit
http://conference.auscert.org.au/conf2010/

OWASP (Individual and Organizational Supporters) members CAN REGISTER AT THE
FOUR PLUS DELEGATE LEVEL. This is equal to a 27% discount off the single
EARLY BIRD delegate price. (17% OF THE NORMAL PRICE) Please put the initials
"10OWASP" in the "General Registration Comment" field when registering.

If you are planning to attend AusCERT2010 and are not yet an OWASP Member
then making the investment to become an OWASP Member today would pay for
itself with this discount.  Further information on becoming an OWASP Member
is available from http://www.owasp.org/index.php/Membership
