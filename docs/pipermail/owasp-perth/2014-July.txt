Fellow security professional,

You are invited to participate in the 3rd Breakpoint Security Conference in Melbourne.

Breakpoint brings together 14 world-class security experts and trainers to share their knowledge on a full range of offensive and defensive security topics. Breakpoint provides 2 days of learning opportunities and networking with industry professionals, and will cover topics that relate to critical security issues and emerging threats.

For more information please find attached the Breakpoint 2014 brochure and justificaton letter or please visit the Breakpoint website: http://www.ruxconbreakpoint.com

If you specialise in the IT Security industry then you do not want to miss this event.

Speakers

    * Neel Mehta (Google) - TLS Under Siege: A Bug Hunter???s Perspective
    * Stefan Esser - iOS8: Containers, Sandboxes, and Entitlements
    * Mike Bond - EMV Security: Cloning, Skimming & Shimming - A Practical Guide
    * Mathew Solnik - Global Scale Mobile Exploitation: Rise & Fall of the Control Protocol
    * Corey Kallenberg - Extreme Privilege Escalation on Windows 8/UEFI Systems
    * Brian Gorenc - Thinking Outside the Sandbox: Violating Trust Boundaries
    * Dr Thomas Holt - Stolen Data Markets: An Economic and Organizational Assessment
    * Joe FitzPatrick - NSA Playset: PCIe

Additional speakers to be announced soon. Speaker lineup is subject to change.

Training Sessions

    * Saumil Shah - The Exploit Laboratory: Introduction To Exploit Development
    * Louis Nyffenegger & Luke Jahnke - Advanced Web Hacking
    * Kayne Naughton - Open Source Intelligence and Automating Intelligence Collection


If you would like to register for this event, please visit our website. Alternatively, you can reply to this email or call Chris, our registrations manager, on +61-407-848-737.

We look forward to seeing you there,
Breakpoint 2014
