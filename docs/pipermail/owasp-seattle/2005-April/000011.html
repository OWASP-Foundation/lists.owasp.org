For tomorrow night's meeting at Las Margaritas, there is free parking
available in the parking lot behind the restaurant and the meeting room is
the first room on the left when you enter the door from the parking lot.
If entering from the front door, the room is down the stairs.

I am planning to have the appetizers served at 6:15pm and the food will
include the following items buffet style:

* Carnitas Vegetarianas (fajita vegetables with black beans and rice)
* Veggie enchiladas
* Cheese quesadillas
* Platter with quesadillas, tacos, buffalo wings, and chimichangas
* Nachos Idaho (potato skins)
* Soft drink pitchers
* Beer pitcher & red wine

Las Margaritas is located at 437 108th Ave NE in downtown Bellevue near
the corner of 4th & 108th. Thanks to those who have already sent an RSVP.
If you have questions, email or call me at 425-922-1320. See you Thursday
night.
