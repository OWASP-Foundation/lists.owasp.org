The wiki does not contain enough information on current issues,
solutions, and best practices to be very useful to the common person.
Additionally, little work goes into updating existing entries with
links to other parts of the site.  I mean, check out the Java project.
Most of the topic areas are at 0%.

Here is an example of help needed: http://www.owasp.org/index.php/LDAP_injection

No examples, no links or best practices on mitigation, no links to
other ldap pages on the owasp wiki, no links to papers.

I think we should adopt a topic once a every other month and do the following:

  1. Flesh the topic out with good content
  2. Add links to current information, papers, etc
  3. Cross-link with other pages on the owasp wiki
  4. Create or verify mitigation solutions for: Java, .NET, and PHP
