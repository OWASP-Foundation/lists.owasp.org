Hi folks, a quick update on progress towards getting the OWASP Seattle Chapter a vibrant local software security community. 

There has been a great response and over 20 people have mailed directly to say they want to attend the first meeting. I have had offers for hosting the first event from KPMG and Google (and a few other options as potentials) so I am hopeful we can get regular meetings at great locations. 

I am working with KPMG to lock on the date and location and with our first speaker(s). At the first event we can do a poll for future topics, speakers. 

Look for an update with a date, location and topics sometime next week. 

Cheers!
